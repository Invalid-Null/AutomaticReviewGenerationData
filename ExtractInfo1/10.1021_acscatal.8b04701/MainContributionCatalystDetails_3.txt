- The catalysts studied are vanadium oxide catalysts supported on alumina. 

- The active species is vanadium (V).

- No oxidizing agent is present in the feed.

- The main scientific contributions are:

1) Correlating the p-band center of surface oxygen to dehydrogenation activity. Surface oxygens with p-band centers close to the Fermi level have higher activity.

2) Showing that monomeric VOx favors acetone formation, while polymeric VOx and crystalline VOx favor propylene formation. This provides guidance on selecting the VOx structure for optimal selectivity.
