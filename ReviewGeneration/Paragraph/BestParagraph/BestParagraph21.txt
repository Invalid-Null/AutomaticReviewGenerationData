It demonstrates that extra-framework Ga+ sites are formed regardless of Si/Al ratio. While reaction rates decrease over time, propylene selectivity remains stable at 96% when the Ga/Al ratio is properly tuned.
        
        Furthermore, the research published in [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024] explores the effects of tuning TS-1 crystal size from 100 nm to 5.7 μm. Smaller sizes introduce mesoporosity, improving diffusion and avoiding coking. This significantly enhances conversion, selectivity and catalyst lifetime. 
        
        Finally, the article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808] investigates mesoporous MFI zeolites. The mesoporous structure and abundant silanol groups are shown to facilitate intermetallic alloy nanoparticle formation.
