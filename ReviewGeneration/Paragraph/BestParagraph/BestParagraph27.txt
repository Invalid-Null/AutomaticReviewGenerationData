The experiments were conducted at a synchrotron radiation facility on fresh and spent Co@Si-BEA catalysts. According to the FT-EXAFS and WT-EXAFS spectra of the spent catalyst, there was still a single signal corresponding to the first shell Co-O bond with an almost unchanged coordination environment (CN = 5.7 ± 0.3), confirming that the incorporated Co2+ cations acting as predominant Co active sites were structurally stable against the reaction [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726].
