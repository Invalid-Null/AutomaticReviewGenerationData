Based on the details extracted from the references, several mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over various metal oxide catalysts. 

On chromium oxide catalysts, active Cr3+ sites are generated in situ from Cr6+ precursors during reaction. Isolated Cr3+ sites with coordination unsaturation are proposed as most active in Cr/SBA-15, while oligomeric Cr species are more active in Cr/Al2O3 [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]. Mononuclear Cr3+ sites with two ligand vacancies have also been suggested as active sites [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183].

On gallium oxide catalysts, propane dehydrogenation is proposed to occur via a heterolytic dissociation pathway over tetrahedral Ga3+ sites, rather than a redox mechanism [10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017, 10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]. Key steps involve dissociative adsorption to gallium hydride and alkoxide species. Isolated Ga+ sites in zeolites also activate propane via an oxidative addition, H-β elimination pathway [10.1021/jacs.2c03941].

On vanadia catalysts, a Mars-van Krevelen mechanism is widely proposed, involving lattice oxygen abstraction of H from propane, followed by catalyst reoxidation. Isolated V5+ sites reduce to V3+ during this cycle [10.1006/jcat.1999.2510]. Sub-monolayer VOx structures help avoid overoxidation compared to bulk V2O5 [10.1002/anie.202005968]. 

On zirconia catalysts, coordinatively unsaturated Zr4+ sites neighboring oxygen vacancies activate propane's C-H bonds. Subsequent β-H elimination forms propene after a Zr-propyl intermediate [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012]. Introducing Ga changes the mechanism from stepwise to concerted [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002].

Additionally, H-assisted formate/carboxyl pathways are described for indirect catalysts with rWGS activity [10.1021/acscatal.2c01374].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The information amassed reveals crucial structural motifs conferring activity for propane dehydrogenation over metal oxides - isolated cationic sites neighboring oxygen vacancies demonstrate particular competence. While considerable strides have illuminated predominant pathways and intermediates, atomic-level understanding of selectivity control warrants further exploration. Emerging operando spectroscopies probing transient moieties in tandem with computational modeling can offer novel insights. Beyond conventional metal oxides, burgeoning two-dimensional derivatives manifesting high concentrations of coordinatively unsaturated sites merit investigation.

根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,已经为多种金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢提出了几种机理场景。

在铬氧化物催化剂上,活性Cr3+位点是在反应过程中从Cr6+前体原位生成的。 Cr/SBA-15中的隔离Cr3+位点配位不饱和度更高时更为活跃,而Cr/Al2O3中的低聚体Cr物种更为活跃 [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]。 单核Cr3+位点与两个配体空位也被认为是活性位点 [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]。

在氧化镓催化剂上,丙烷脱氢被提出是通过异裂路径在四面体Ga3+位点上发生,而不是氧化还原机理 [10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017, 10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]。 关键步骤涉及吸附至氧化镓氢化物和烷氧化物物种。 沸石中隔离的Ga+位点也通过氧化加成、H-β消除途径激活丙烷 [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]。

在钒酸盐催化剂上,广泛提出了Mars-van Krevelen机理,其中晶格氧从丙烷中提取H,然后催化剂再氧化。  隔离的V5+位点在此循环中还原为V3+ [10.1006/jcat.1999.2510]。 亚单分子层VOx结构有助于避免过氧化,而不是块状V2O5 [10.1002/anie.202005968]。

在氧化锆催化剂上,配位不饱和的Zr4+位点邻近氧空位激活丙烷的C-H键。 在Zr-丙基中间体之后,进一步的β-H消除形成丙烯 [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012]。 引入Ga会将机理从步进改变为协同 [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。

此外,对具有rWGS活性的间接催化剂描述了H辅助的甲酸酯/羧酸途径[10.1021/acscatal.2c01374]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望: 聚集的信息揭示了赋予金属氧化物丙烷脱氢活性的关键结构基元——隔离的阳离子位点邻近氧空位展现出特别的胜任能力。 尽管对主导途径和中间体有了相当大的进步,但对选择性控制的原子级理解还有待进一步探索。 新兴的共位光谱技术检测瞬态物种与计算建模相结合,可以提供新的见解。 除了常规金属氧化物之外,新兴的二维衍生物表现出高浓度的配位不饱和位点值得研究。
