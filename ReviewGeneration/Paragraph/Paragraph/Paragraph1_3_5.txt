 <English>

Propylene is a crucial chemical feedstock utilized for producing a diverse range of products including polypropylene, acrylonitrile, propylene oxide, acetone, etc [1,2]. Conventional production pathways via steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking struggle to satisfy the rapidly rising global demand [3]. Hence, propane dehydrogenation has attracted attention as an alternative and propitious route by leveraging abundant propane reserves unlocked by the shale gas boom [2,4,5]. 

Propane dehydrogenation is an economically feasible and eco-friendly means of generating propylene to mitigate impending supply deficits [6]. As elucidated, "the nonoxidative propane dehydrogenation (PDH) technique has emerged as a high-value-rising and promising alternative to traditional propylene production techniques" [7]. Furthermore, Industry has already actualized and commercialized the PDH route via processes like Catofin and Oleflex [8].

The ever-growing applications and consumption of propylene derivatives encompassing polypropylene, propylene oxide, etc coupled with favorable propane economics presage the ascent of PDH as a sustainable pathway for large-scale propylene manufacturing [5,9].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Capitalizing on propane's abundance, viability, and price advantage, PDH innovation is primed to unlock new value creation opportunities. Beyond catalyst and process optimizations, emerging areas like biomass-based propane production and electrolytic PDH leveraging renewable energy could expand PDH's sustainability quotient. Furthermore, greenhouse gas capture/utilization technologies paired with PDH may pave the path to net-zero emissions. Exploring these interdisciplinary intersections will likely take center stage in future PDH advancements.

<Chinese>

丙烯是用于生产各种产品(包括聚丙烯、丙烯腈、丙烯醇、丙酮等)的关键化学原料[1,2]。常规的蒸汽裂解和催化裂解生产途径难以满足全球需求的快速增长[3]。因此,丙烷脱氢引起了人们的关注,作为一种利用丰富的丙烷储量(由页岩气繁荣解锁)的替代和有利途径[2,4,5]。

丙烷脱氢是一种经济可行和环保的丙烯生产方式,以缓解即将到来的供应短缺[6]。正如阐明的那样,“非氧化丙烷脱氢(PDH)技术已经出现为一种高价值上升和有前途的替代传统丙烯生产技术”[7]。此外,工业已经通过Catofin和Oleflex等工艺实现和商业化了PDH途径[8]。

丙烯衍生产品(包括聚丙烯、丙烯醇等)的不断增长的应用和消费,再加上丙烷的有利经济条件,预示着PDH作为大规模丙烯制造的可持续途径的崛起[5,9]。

[1], [2], [3], [4], [5], [6], [7], [8], [9]  

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 依托于丙烷的丰富性、可行性和价格优势,PDH创新准备好解锁新的价值创造机会。除了催化剂和工艺优化之外,新兴领域如生物质丙烷生产和利用可再生能源的电解PDH可以扩大PDH的可持续性。此外,与PDH相结合的温室气体捕集/利用技术可能铺平实现净零排放的道路。探索这些交叉学科的交汇点可能会成为未来PDH进步的核心。
