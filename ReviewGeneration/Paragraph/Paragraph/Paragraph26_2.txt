One study by Wang et al. This demonstrated precise localization of the active phase within the microporous support.

In another investigation, Zheng et al. [10.1038/s41467-021-23426-z] utilized aberration-corrected HAADF-STEM to visualize atomically dispersed platinum nanoclusters (mean size 1 nm) coordinated with sulfur-doped carbon supports. The images evidenced strong metal-support interactions restricting mobility and growth of the ultrafine platinum species. 

Advanced electron tomography methods were also implemented by Paredes et al. [10.1021/acscatal.1c04092] to construct three-dimensional visualizations of sub-2 nm PtZn clusters firmly anchored within microporous zeolite channels. This substantiated the isolated bimetallic entities were restricted from migration or aggregation.

Collectively, these electron microscopy-based findings have uncovered distinct metal-support interfacial sites governing the stabilization of isolated atoms or ultra-small clusters. The insights posit opportunities to engineer specific locations for localizing the active component and potentially tuning the coordination environment. Wang等人[10.1021/jacs.2c02636]的一项研究采用了集成差分相差STEM(iDPC-STEM)技术,揭示了错位的{(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2}四面体离子,均匀地嵌入MFI骨架的T1(7)和T3(9)位点。这证明了活性相对微孔支撑体的精确定位。

在另一项调查中,Zheng等人[10.1038/s41467-021-23426-z]利用球差校正HAADF-STEM可视化了以原子分散的铂纳米簇(平均大小1 nm)与硫化碳支撑体配位。该图像证据了强大的金属-支撑体相互作用限制了超细铂物种的迁移和生长。

Paredes等人[10.1021/acscatal.1c04092]还实施了先进的电子断层扫描方法,构建了嵌入微孔沸石通道中的亚2 nm PtZn簇的三维可视化结果。这证实了这些隔离的双金属体实体不会发生迁移或聚集。  

总体而言,这些基于电子显微镜的发现已经揭示了不同的金属-支撑体界面位点,这些位点决定了隔离原子或超小簇的稳定性。这些见解提出了为本地化活性成分及潜在调控其配位环境的特定位置提供机会。这些静态隔离位点是否可以在工作条件下动态转化,这仍是一个悬而未决的问题。未来的工作还可能集中于扩展先进的原位显微技术,以跟踪反应轨迹中的界面演变。
