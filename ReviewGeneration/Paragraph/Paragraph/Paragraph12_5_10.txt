Based on the details extracted from the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH).

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most successful and widely used catalysts for commercial PDH processes [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. An alumina-supported Pt catalyst is considered crucial for its ability to selectively activate C-H bonds while minimizing C-C cleavage [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. Data shows Pt/Al2O3 modified with ALD metal oxide overcoats increased PDH yield and selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. Other studies found small Pt clusters highly dispersed on supports like Ge-UTL zeolite [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7] and Zn-exchanged ZSM-5 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631] exhibited excellent catalytic performance. Adding promoters like 1 wt% boron to Pt/γ-Al2O3 increased stability by reducing coking [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. 

Investigations into the effect of Pt cluster size determined a volcano-shape relationship, with Pt2 clusters demonstrating exceptionally high intrinsic activity akin to magic clusters [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]. Evaluations have also been conducted on PtSn bimetallics [DOI: 10.1016/S1385-8947(02)00158-4, DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119], PtIn bimetallics [DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2017.05.058], and PtGaPb trimetallics [DOI: 10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9] to elucidate geometric and electronic effects on activity.

Besides Pt, supported Ir, Pd [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002], and Ru [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667] catalysts have been briefly studied but found less suitable than Pt owing to lower intrinsic activity or rapid deactivation issues.

Overall, prevalent insights establish Pt-based catalysts, especially nanoclusters stabilized on tailored supports, as promising materials for continued PDH advancements [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631].

Key discernments surrounding controlled Pt nanocluster synthesis methods, tailored supports, and strategic promoters set the foundation for engineering optimized catalysts.

基于文件提取的详细信息,已有几项研究探索了负载单金属Pt、Pd和Ir催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

报道Pt基催化剂是商业PDH过程中最成功和最广泛使用的催化剂之一[DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。报道氧化铝支持的Pt催化剂由于其选择性激活C-H键而最小化C-C键的断裂能力,被认为是至关重要的[DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。 研究结果表明,通过ALD金属氧化物涂层改性Pt/Al2O3可提高PDH产率和选择性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。 其他研究还发现,小的Pt簇高度分散在Ge-UTL沸石[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]和锌交换的ZSM-5 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]等载体上,显示出优异的催化性能。 在Pt/γ-Al2O3中添加1 wt%硼促进剂可通过减少焦炭沉积来提高稳定性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。

关于Pt簇大小效应的研究确定了火山形关系,其中Pt2簇表现出异常高的本征活性,类似于魔簇[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。 已经对PtSn双金属[DOI: 10.1016/S1385-8947(02)00158-4, DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119],PtIn双金属[DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2017.05.058]和PtGaPb三金属[DOI: 10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9]等催化剂进行了评价,以阐明几何和电子效应对活性的影响。

除了Pt之外,也简要研究了负载的Ir、Pd [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]和Ru [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]催化剂,但由于本征活性较低或快速失活问题,它们被认为不如Pt适合。

总的来说,主流的见解将Pt基催化剂(特别是稳定在定制支撑体上的纳米簇)确立为PDH持续进步的有希望材料[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]。
