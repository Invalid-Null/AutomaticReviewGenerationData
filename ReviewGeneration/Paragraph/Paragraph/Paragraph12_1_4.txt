Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH).

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts. For example, Pt/Al2O3 is a vital component of the commercial Oleflex process due to Pt's ability to activate C-H bonds while minimizing C-C bond cleavage [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]. One study showed an alumina-supported Pt catalyst maintained 58% propane conversion for 25 hours, exhibiting excellent stability compared to other reported catalysts [10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044]. The addition of metal oxide overcoats (e.g. Al2O3, TiO2) via ALD to supported Pt catalysts has also been found to increase PDH yield and selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391].

Investigations into the effects of promoters such as S, B, and Ca have revealed enhanced selectivity, activity, and stability of supported Pt catalysts. Predosing Pt/Al2O3 with H2S improved initial propene selectivity to 98%, while adding 1 wt% B promoter increased selectivity to 98% over 12 hours [10.1006/jcat.1994.1333, 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548].

Beyond Pt, supported Pd, Rh, and Ir are intrinsically active for C-H bond activation but less studied for PDH. One recent study showed a SiO2-supported RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst exhibited higher activity, 100% propene selectivity and coking resistance compared to a standard Pt/Al2O3 catalyst [10.1126/science.abg8389].

Overall, while Pt-based catalysts have seen the most commercial success, opportunities remain to develop improved supported metal catalysts through approaches like promoters, metal oxide additives, and novel supports. Continued research into Pd, Rh, and Ir alternatives may also uncover viabilities.

根据文件提供的细节,已经有几项研究探索了用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)的受支持的单金属Pt、Pd和Ir催化剂。

Pt基催化剂被报道是最广泛使用和商业上最成功的PDH催化剂之一。例如,Pt/Al2O3是商业Oleflex工艺的重要组成部分,这是由于Pt能够激活C-H键而最小化C-C键的断裂[10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。一项研究表明,一种受氧化铝支持的Pt催化剂在25小时内保持了58%的丙烷转化率,表现出比其他报道的催化剂更好的稳定性[10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044]。通过ALD向受支持的Pt催化剂添加氧化铝或二氧化铽等金属氧化物外壳也被发现可以增加PDH的收率和选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。

对促进剂如S、B和Ca的作用的研究揭示了受支持Pt催化剂的选择性、活性和稳定性的增强。用H2S预处理Pt/Al2O3可以将初始丙烯选择性提高到98%,而添加1 wt% B促进剂可以将12小时的选择性提高到98% [10.1006/jcat.1994.1333, 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。

除Pt之外,受支持的Pd、Rh和Ir对C-H键有内在的活化能力,但对PDH的研究较少。最近的一项研究表明,一种SiO2支持的RhCu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂比标准Pt/Al2O3催化剂表现出更高的活性、100%丙烯选择性和抗焦炭性[10.1126/science.abg8389]。

总的来说,虽然Pt基催化剂在商业上取得了最大的成功,但通过促进剂、金属氧化物添加剂和新型载体等方法开发改进的受支持金属催化剂仍有很大的机会。继续研究Pd、Rh和Ir等替代品也可能发现其可行性。

综合理解与未来展望:考虑到文件中讨论的进步和发现,我们有机会探索新兴领域和创新方法。未来的研究努力可能会集中在单原子合金催化剂上,这已显示出超过商业Pt标准的前景。此外,利用促进剂和金属与载体之间的相互作用来稳定活性金属位点,防止团聚和焦炭也可能开辟新的契机。在PDH定制的氮化硼或MXene等新型载体材料的开发中也存在机会。通过继续推动科学界限,并在化学工程学科之间杂交工具,开拓先锋金属基PDH催化剂可能是可行的。
