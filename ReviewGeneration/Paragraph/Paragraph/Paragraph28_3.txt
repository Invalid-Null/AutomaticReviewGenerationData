However, a couple of references do provide some insights on reactors. One study by Garcia-Garcia et al. This configuration allows hydrogen removal through the membrane while also regenerating the catalyst by coke combustion in situ. Experimental data shows the membrane reactor increases conversion but also coking rates compared to just TZFBR, indicating potential for further optimization.

Another article by Coronas et al. [10.1016/S1383-5866(01)00085-5] evaluates silica-based membrane reactors for improving propene yields in propane dehydrogenation by continuous hydrogen removal. Results indicate the membrane reactor outperforms fixed beds given sufficient hydrogen flux and selectivity, although accelerated catalyst deactivation remains a challenge.

Overall the collected studies offer limited insights on reactors for propane dehydrogenation, relying predominantly on simple fixed bed systems. The exploration of membrane reactors and fluidized beds demonstrates potential advantages but also areas needing further development, presenting opportunities for innovation in reactor design and catalyst materials tailored for these systems.

 Coronas等人的另一篇文章[10.1016/S1383-5866(01)00085-5] 评估了二氧化硅基膜反应器在丙烷脱氢反应中通过持续去除氢气来改善丙烯收率。结果表明,在足够的氢气通量和选择性的条件下,膜反应器优于固定床反应器,尽管催化剂失活加速仍然是一个挑战。

总的来说,收集的研究对丙烷脱氢反应的反应器提供了有限的见解,主要依赖简单的固定床体系。 膜反应器和流化床的探索表明具有潜在的优势,但也有需要进一步开发的领域,为这些系统的反应器设计和催化剂材料的创新提供了机会。
