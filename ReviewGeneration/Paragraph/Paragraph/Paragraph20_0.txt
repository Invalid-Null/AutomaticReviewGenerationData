※※※ Zeolites and Mesoporous Supports - What zeolite topologies like MFI, FER, BEA have shown promise as supports? ※※※

Based on the details provided in the file, zeolites with MFI, BEA, and CHA topologies have shown promise as supports for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts:

MFI Zeolites:
- Silicalite-1 (purely siliceous MFI) provides excellent stabilization for ultrasmall PtZn clusters that are highly active for PDH [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.02.019]. The 5.6 x 5.1 Å channels match well with propane size.  
- A hierarchical ferrosilicate MFI zeolite (H-Fe-S-1-n) anchors isolated Pt atoms leading to high dispersion and stability [10.1021/acscatal.2c05042]. Iron sites increase Pt-O bond strength.
- Mesoporous MFI nanosheets supported a PtZn alloy nanoparticle catalyst exhibiting outstanding durability for PDH [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808].

BEA Zeolites: 
- Pt stabilized on isolated Sn sites in dealuminated zeolite Beta showed excellent activity, selectivity and stability for PDH [10.1021/jacs.1c10261].
- Pt/Sn-Beta approaches 90% propylene selectivity near equilibrium conversion in PDH [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816].

CHA Zeolites:
- Ga-exchanged chabazite zeolites demonstrate 96% propylene selectivity in PDH, with isolated Ga+ sites identified as active centers [10.1021/jacs.2c03941].

The above examples showcase the promise of zeolites to anchor highly dispersed metal sites, stabilize small clusters, and confinement effects - leading to enhancements in activity, selectivity and stability for PDH catalysts.

※※※ 分子筛支撑材料 - MFI、FER和BEA等结构的分子筛展现了哪些应用前景?※※※

根据所提供的文件内容,MFI、BEA和CHA结构的分子筛作为丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂的支撑材料展现了应用前景:

MFI分子筛:  
- 纯硅MFI硅酸盐分子筛(硅微晶沸石)可提供出色的稳定性,固定超小型PtZn簇团,对PDH反应表现出高活性[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.02.019]。5.6 x 5.1 Å的细孔结构与丙烷分子大小匹配良好。
- 层状铁硅酸MFI分子筛(H-Fe-S-1-n)可锚定分离的Pt原子,获得高分散度和稳定性[10.1021/acscatal.2c05042]。铁原子增强了Pt-O键的强度。  
- 支持在介孔MFI纳米片层的PtZn合金纳米颗粒催化剂表现出杰出的稳定性[10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]。

BEA分子筛:
- Pt稳定在脱铝Beta分子筛的隔离Sn位点上,PDH活性、选择性和稳定性优异[10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。  
- Pt/Sn-Beta接近PDH反应平衡转化,丙烯选择性可达90%[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。

CHA分子筛:  
- 加载Ga的沸石(CHA)表现出96%的丙烯选择性,确定Ga+孤立中心是活性中心[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]。

上述例子展示了分子筛固定高度分散的金属活性位点、稳定小簇团以及空间限制效应等优势,这些都可提高PDH催化剂的活性、选择性和稳定性。
