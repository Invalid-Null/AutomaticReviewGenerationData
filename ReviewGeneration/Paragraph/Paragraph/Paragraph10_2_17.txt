 <English>
Based on the details extracted from the literature, several mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over different metal oxide catalysts. 

On Cr-based oxide catalysts, coordinatively unsaturated Cr3+ sites [DOI1, DOI2], Cr3+/Cr2+ redox couples [DOI3], and tetrahedrally coordinated CrO4 species [DOI4] have been suggested as active sites. Key steps involve activation of propane C-H bonds, formation of propyl or alkoxide intermediates, and reductive elimination to release propylene. Neighboring lattice oxygen aids C-H cleavage through hydrogen transfer. Re-oxidation of reduced Cr species occurs via Mars-van Krevelen type cycles with O2 or CO2.

For V-based oxides, monomeric [DOI5] and dimeric [DOI6] vanadium oxide sites paired with support oxygen have been implicated along with V3+ and coordinatively unsaturated V centers [DOI7, DOI8]. Redox cycles between higher and lower V oxidation states occur during propane activation and re-oxidation. Lattice oxygen enables initial oxidative dehydrogenation through hydrogen abstraction, depleting over reaction to leave reduced V species that catalyze non-oxidative pathways [DOI9].

On ZrO2, DFT studies propose dual coordinatively unsaturated Zr cations neighboring an oxygen vacancy as the active configuration [DOI10, DOI11]. These Zrcus sites cleave C-H bonds homolytically or in conjunction with lattice oxygen. Change from stepwise to concerted bond cleavage has been shown for Ga-doped ZrO2 [DOI12]. 

For Ga oxides, a heterolytic route is favored over redox mechanisms. Propane dissociatively chemisorbs on Ga3+ sites to give alkyl and hydride species [DOI13, DOI14], with C-H cleavage as the rate limiting step [DOI3]. Acid sites promote hydride transfer [DOI15].

Additionally, synergies between cation sites like Zn-Zr [DOI16] and promotion effects of additives like CO2 through enhanced oxygen mobility or lattice oxygen creation have been demonstrated [DOI17].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The mechanistic scenarios elucidated offer molecular-level perspectives into tuning active sites like metal cations, oxygen species, and acid-base pairs to steer selectivity and activity. As research stimulates new catalytic formulations and online characterization techniques progress, opportunities exist to attain further clarity on transient intermediates and dynamic active site transformations crucial to propane dehydrogenation. Advanced in-situ spectroscopies combined with isotopic labeling and computational methods will enable better discernment of complex reaction networks. Discerning these intricacies can pave the way for next-generation catalyst and process designs.

<Chinese>
根据从文献中提取的详细信息,已经为不同金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢提出了几种机理场景。

在Cr基氧化物催化剂上,已经提出过度不饱和的Cr3+位点[DOI1, DOI2]、Cr3+/Cr2+氧化还原对[DOI3] 和四面体配位的CrO4物种[DOI4]作为活性中心。关键步骤涉及丙烷C-H键的激活,丙基或醚中间体的形成,以及还原消除以释放丙烯。相邻的晶格氧通过氢转移帮助C-H键的断裂。O2或CO2的Mars-van Krevelen类型循环氧化还原减少的Cr物种。

对于V基氧化物,单聚体[DOI5]和二聚体[DOI6]钒氧化物位点与载体氧成对,以及V3+和过度不饱和的V中心[DOI7,DOI8]。更高和更低V氧化态之间在丙烷激活和再氧化期间发生氧化还原循环。晶格氧通过氢原子的提取使最初的氧化脱氢成为可能,在反应过程中被耗尽,留下还原的V物种,催化非氧化途径[DOI9]。

在ZrO2上,DFT研究提出,双过度不饱和Zr阳离子邻近氧空位是活性配置[DOI10,DOI11]。这些Zrcus位点同裂或与晶格氧一起同裂C-H键。 研究表明Ga掺杂的ZrO2[DOI12]的键裂变从步骤裂变变为协同裂变。

对于Ga氧化物,优先选择异裂路线而不是氧化还原机理。丙烷在Ga3+位点上发生解离性化学吸附以产生烷基和氢化物[DOI13,DOI14],C-H键裂解是速率限制步骤[DOI3]。酸位有助于氢化物转移[DOI15]。

 此外,证明了阳离子位点之间的协同作用,如Zn-Zr [DOI16]以及通过增强氧迁移或晶格氧的创建来提高CO2等添加剂的作用[DOI17]。

※※※

整合性理解与前景展望: 阐明的机理场景从分子水平上提供了调节金属阳离子、氧化物和酸碱对等活性位点以引导选择性和活性的视角。随着研究激发新的催化制剂和在线表征技术的进步,存在机会以进一步澄清瞬时中间体和催化剂关键性质的动态转变对丙烷脱氢而言极为重要。先进的原位光谱技术结合同位素标记和计算方法将使人们更好地识别复杂的反应网络。理解这些复杂性可以为下一代催化剂和工艺设计铺平道路。
