 <English>

The article with DOI 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726 [1] discusses using dealumination to generate isolated silanol groups in the zeolite Beta (BEA) support, and then immobilizing isolated Co2+ cations at these sites via vacuum impregnation.

Another approach using dealumination to create isolated single sites is described in the study with DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103 [2]. Here, dealuminated zeolite Beta (named SiBeta) contains vacant Al sites and SiOH nests that can anchor and isolate active metal sites. Examples include incorporating Ga species into the SiBeta framework to achieve single Ga sites stabilized by the zeolite, and confining Co compounds in hydroxyl nests of dealuminated SiBeta.

The article with DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636 [3] utilizes a ligand-protected hydrothermal synthesis method to construct isolated Co-motifs with a distorted tetrahedral structure of {(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2} that are homogeneously embedded into the T1(7) and T3(9) sites of the MFI framework. Advanced STEM and spectroscopy techniques confirm the isolated nature and exact structure of the single Co sites. 

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The current literature demonstrates viable pathways for synthesizing isolated single metal sites in zeolitic supports, including dealumination to generate anchoring points combined with selective metal incorporation. However, widespread implementation could benefit from protocols that are more facile and scalable without compromising site isolation or catalyst longevity. Exploring alternative zeolite composites with intrinsic isolated sites, dynamic structuring techniques, and computational screening of optimal sites could uncover new horizons. Ultimately, progress in this burgeoning area may inspire innovations like customized zeolites with designed single site architectures for specialized reactions.

<Chinese>

DOI为10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726的文章[1]讨论了使用除铝来产生分离的硅醇基团在分子筛Beta(BEA)支撑体中,然后通过真空浸渍固定分离的Co2+阳离子在这些位点上。  

使用DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103 [2]的另一项研究描述了一种类似的方法,即用除铝方法在分子筛Beta中创建单独的位点。这里,除铝后的硅Beta分子筛包含空铝位点和SiOH巢,可以锚定和隔离活性金属位点。例子包括将Ga物种并入SiBeta框架以达到被分子筛稳定的单个Ga位点,以及将Co化合物限制在除铝SiBeta的羟基巢中。  

DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636 [3]的文章利用配体保护的水热合成方法构建了扭曲四面体结构的{(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2}的隔离Co基团,均匀嵌入MFI框架的T1(7)位点和T3(9)位点。先进的STEM和光谱技术确认了单个Co位点的隔离性质和确切结构。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:现有文献展示了在分子筛支撑体中合成隔离的单个金属位点的可行途径,包括除铝以产生锚定点结合与选择性金属掺入。但是,为了实现大规模应用,这些方法仍需更加简易、可扩展而又不损害位点的隔离性或催化剂的稳定性。探索具有固有隔离位点的另类分子筛复合材料、动态构效技术以及计算筛选最佳位点的方法可能会开启新的契机。最终,这一新兴领域的进展可能会激发创新思维,例如为特定反应自定义设计单个位点构型的分子筛。
