 <English>

Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) is an important industrial process for producing propylene, a key building block for chemicals and polymers. Metal oxide catalysts have been explored for PDH, with chromium (Cr) and gallium (Ga) based oxides identified as promising options. Here is a review of metal oxide catalysts for PDH focusing on Cr2O3, Pt-Sn, and Ga2O3:

Cr-based catalysts, especially Cr2O3, are among the most extensively studied metal oxide catalysts for PDH. CrOx is the key component in the commercial CATOFIN PDH process by CB&I [Placholdo_Of_DOI]. Supported Cr2O3 catalysts like Cr2O3/SiO2 and Cr2O3/Al2O3 have shown good catalytic activity, with yields up to 30% reported [Placholdo_Of_DOI]. Acidity of Cr2O3 is important, with higher surface acid site density linked to improved initial PDH activity [Placholdo_Of_DOI]. Doping Cr2O3 with promoters like Pt and using supports like SiO2 to modulate metal-support interactions has been found effective for enhancing propylene selectivity [Placholdo_Of_DOI]. However, deactivation from coke formation remains an issue. 

Among metal oxides, Pt-Sn catalysts have demonstrated high intrinsic activity and reasonable stability for PDH. The PtSn system forms intermetallic compounds, which improve propylene selectivity and stability compared to monometallic Pt [Placholdo_Of_DOI]. Supported PtSn catalysts like PtSn/Al2O3 and PtSn/SBA-15 synthesized by coimpregnation show 40-50% propane conversion initially with ~90% propylene selectivity [Placholdo_Of_DOI, Placholdo_Of_DOI]. The Pt-Sn combination lowers barriers for propylene desorption while suppressing deep dehydrogenation pathways.

Gallium oxide, especially the β-Ga2O3 polymorph, has emerged as a promising PDH catalyst. β-Ga2O3 exhibits higher initial activity than Cr2O3 with CO2 co-feed, linked to its surface acidity [Placholdo_Of_DOI]. Supported β-Ga2O3/SiO2 also outperforms supported Cr2O3 and PtSn catalysts for propylene productivity [Placholdo_Of_DOI]. Modulating Ga dispersion by supporting on oxides like SiO2 and ZrO2 gives promising results. However, Ga catalysts experience rapid deactivation.

While certain metal oxides show promise for PDH, instability arising from coking and phase changes remains a key challenge. Developing promoters, supports, and synthesis strategies to boost propylene selectivity while stabilizing the active phase will be critical moving forward. More in-depth surface characterization and mechanistic studies can also help advance metal oxide catalysts for PDH.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and findings from the literature, metal oxide catalysts represent a promising yet challenging area for propane dehydrogenation. Though Cr and Ga-based oxides show strong potential, their instability underscores the need for innovative approaches to enhance redox and coking resistance. Beyond compositional tuning, utilization of dynamic techniques like operando spectroscopy during PDH could offer novel structure-performance insights to guide optimization. Looking forward, expanding the library of model catalysts beyond Cr, Ga, and Pt-Sn allows exploration of new oxide compositions tailored for PDH. Machine learning methods integrating catalyst descriptors with PDH outcomes also hold promise to accelerate discovery. With smarter design enhanced by mechanistic comprehension, metal oxide catalysts have an exciting future in selective, stable PDH.

丙烷脱氢(PDH)是生产丙烯的重要工业过程,丙烯是化学品和聚合物的关键基础化学品。人们已经探索了金属氧化物催化剂用于PDH,铬(Cr)和镓(Ga)基氧化物被认为是有前景的选择。这里对PDH的金属氧化物催化剂进行综述,重点关注Cr2O3、Pt-Sn和Ga2O3:

Cr基催化剂,特别是Cr2O3,是PDH最广泛研究的金属氧化物催化剂之一。CrOx是CB&I商业CATOFIN PDH工艺的关键组分[Placholdo_Of_DOI]。受支持的Cr2O3催化剂如Cr2O3/SiO2和Cr2O3/Al2O3表现出良好的催化活性,报道了高达30%的收率[Placholdo_Of_DOI]。Cr2O3的酸度很重要,表面酸位的较高密度与改善的初始PDH活性相关[Placholdo_Of_DOI]。用Pt等促进剂掺杂Cr2O3并使用SiO2等载体来调控金属载体相互作用已被发现能有效提高丙烯选择性[Placholdo_Of_DOI]。但是,来自焦炭形成的失活仍然是一个问题。

在金属氧化物中,Pt-Sn催化剂表现出高的固有活性和合理的PDH稳定性。PtSn系统形成金属间化合物,提高丙烯选择性和稳定性,与单金属Pt相比[Placholdo_Of_DOI]。像PtSn/Al2O3和PtSn/SBA-15等受支持的PtSn催化剂通过共浸渍合成最初表现出40%-50%的丙烷转化率,丙烯选择性约为90%[Placholdo_Of_DOI,Placholdo_Of_DOI]。Pt-Sn组合降低丙烯脱附的能垒,同时抑制深度脱氢途径。

氧化镓,特别是β-Ga2O3多晶型,已经成为一个有前途的PDH催化剂。与Cr2O3相比,β-Ga2O3在CO2共饲料存在下表现出更高的初始活性,这与其表面酸度有关[Placholdo_Of_DOI]。受支持的β-Ga2O3/SiO2也优于受支持的Cr2O3和PtSn催化剂,产丙烯活性更高[Placholdo_Of_DOI]。通过支持在SiO2和ZrO2等氧化物上调控Ga分散度得到了有前景的结果。但是,Ga催化剂经历快速失活。

虽然某些金属氧化物对PDH显示前景,但来自焦炭形成和相变的不稳定性仍然是一个关键挑战。开发促进剂、载体和合成策略以提高丙烯选择性并稳定活性相将是未来的关键。更深入的表面表征和机理研究也可以帮助推进金属氧化物催化剂用于PDH。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:考虑到文献的进展和发现,金属氧化物催化剂在丙烷脱氢方面具有巨大的前景和挑战。尽管Cr和Ga基氧化物显示了巨大的潜力,但它们的不稳定性凸显了需要创新方法来提高Redox和抗焦炭性。除了组成调控,像PDH时的原位分光等动态技术的利用,可以提供新的构效关系理解来指导优化。展望未来,拓展Cr、Ga和Pt-Sn之外的模型催化剂组分库,允许探索量身定制的新型氧化物组成。整合催化剂描述符与PDH结果的机器学习方法,也有望加速发现。通过更智能的设计加上机理理解的提升,金属氧化物催化剂在选择性稳定PDH中拥有令人振奋的前景。
