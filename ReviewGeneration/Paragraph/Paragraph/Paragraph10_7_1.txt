Based on the details extracted from the literature, various mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over different metal oxide catalysts. 

On Cr-based catalysts, coordinatively unsaturated Cr3+ sites [10.1016/S1385-8947(02)00158-4], Cr3+ species with two coordinative vacancies [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183], and Cr6+/Cr3+/Cr2+ redox cycles [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999] have been suggested as active sites. Reaction steps may involve hydrogen abstraction by lattice oxygen [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814], generation of alkyl species like chromium propyl [10.1021/acscatal.6b00893], and subsequent conversion to propylene. 

For VOx catalysts, monomeric [10.1021/acscatal.8b04701], dimeric [10.1021/acscatal.8b04701], and bulk V2O5 [10.1002/anie.202005968] sites have been proposed as active, depending on VOx structure. Mars-van Krevelen [10.1006/jcat.2004.01.023, 10.1021/jacs.9b09235] and redox mechanisms [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.02.028] have been suggested, involving lattice oxygen participation.

On Ga/zeolite catalysts, monovalent Ga+ sites [10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.004] and Ga+H+ pairs [10.1021/acscatal.1c01497] catalyze propane dehydrogenation via alkyl intermediates. Heterolytic dissociation pathways have also been proposed on supported Ga oxides [10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017, 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999].

Additionally, coordinatively unsaturated Zr (Zrcus) sites [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012, 10.1002/anie.201508731] and Zn [10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074] sites have been identified as active. Reaction mechanisms may change from stepwise on ZrO2 to concerted pathways on Ga-ZrOx [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. Synergistic effects between Zn, Zr, and Ti sites have also been described [10.1021/acscatal.0c01580].
※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The detailed mechanistic scenarios described showcase the diversity of active sites and reaction pathways that have been uncovered on different metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, from Cr and V centers to Ga and Zn sites. While redox, Mars-van Krevelen, and heterolytic dissociation routes have been proposed, opacity around the specific nature of critical intermediates presents opportunities to further disentangle fundamental steps using advanced spectroscopic and computational techniques. 

Additionally, mechanistic descriptors identified, such as coordinative unsaturation or proximity between heterolytic pairs, provide guiding principles for rational design of optimized multifunctional sites. However, tuning active site speciation and maintaining stability under harsh dehydrogenation environments remains an outstanding challenge. Addressing these fundamental and applied facets related to activity, selectivity and longevity will be imperative as the field progresses.

根据从文献中提取的详细信息,已经为不同金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢提出了各种机理方案。

在Cr基催化剂上,协同地不饱和的Cr3+位点[10.1016/S1385-8947(02)00158-4]、两个配体空位的Cr3+物种[10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]以及Cr6+/Cr3+/Cr2+氧化还原循环[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]被认为是活性位点。反应步骤可能涉及晶格氧的氢原子提取[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]、产生烷基物种(如铬丙基)[10.1021/acscatal.6b00893]以及随后的转化为丙烯。

对于VOx催化剂,单体[10.1021/acscatal.8b04701]、二聚体[10.1021/acscatal.8b04701]和块状V2O5 [10.1002/anie.202005968]位点已被证明是活性位点,这取决于VOx的结构。 Mars-van Krevelen[10.1006/jcat.2004.01.023,10.1021/jacs.9b09235]和氧化还原机理[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.02.028]也已被证明是可能的,涉及晶格氧的参与。


在Ga/分子筛催化剂上,单价Ga+位点[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.004]和Ga+H+对[10.1021/acscatal.1c01497]通过烷基中间体催化丙烷脱氢。 支撑的Ga氧化物上也提出了杂裂解途径[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017,10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。


此外,协调不饱和Zr (Zrcus)位点[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012,10.1002/anie.201508731]和Zn [10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074]位点也被确定为活性位点。 在Ga-ZrOx上,反应机制可能从ZrO2上的逐步途径改变为协奏途径[10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。 Zn、Zr和Ti位点之间的协同作用也有描述[10.1021/acscatal.0c01580]。


※※※
综合理解与未来展望:所描述的详细的机理方案展示了在不同金属氧化物催化剂上用于丙烷脱氢的活性位点和反应途径的多样性,从Cr和V中心到Ga和Zn位点。虽然已提出氧化还原、Mars-van Krevelen和杂裂解途径,但对关键中间体的本质的不透明度为利用先进的光谱学和计算技术进一步解开基本步骤提供了机会。


此外,确定的机理描述符,如协调不饱和或杂质对之间的接近度,提供了针对优化的多功能性地点合理设计的指导原则。然而,在严苛的脱氢环境下调节活性位点的生成和保持稳定性仍然是一个悬而未决的挑战。 解决与活性、选择性和稳定性相关的这些基本和应用面向问题,这对该领域的进步将是必不可少的。
