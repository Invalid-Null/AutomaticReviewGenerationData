Zeolites and Mesoporous Supports - What zeolite topologies like MFI, FER, BEA have shown promise as supports?

Based on the details provided in the file, zeolites with MFI, BEA, and CHA topologies have shown promise as supports for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts. 

MFI zeolites like silicalite-1 and ZSM-5 provide excellent supports to stabilize isolated metal sites and sub-nanometer clusters that are highly active for PDH. As described in [10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4], [10.1021/acscatal.2c05042], and [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.02.019], the MFI framework helps anchor atomically dispersed Pt sites and encapsulate ultrasmall PtZn and PtSn nanoclusters, leading to high propane conversion around 40-45% and propylene selectivity above 99%. The rigid microporous structure and weak metal-support interactions of MFI zeolites prevent agglomeration and facilitate high metal dispersion. Hierarchical MFI zeolites with mesoporosity mitigate diffusion limitations, as shown for a PtZn alloy catalyst achieving remarkable stability over 30 days onstream [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808].


For the BEA zeolite, studies like [10.1021/jacs.1c10261] and [10.1016/j.jechem.2020.08.058] demonstrate that dealuminated zeolite Beta provides an optimal support for single Pt atoms stabilized in Zn nests inside the micropores. The resulting Pt/Zn-Beta catalysts balance high propane conversion around 30% with 90% propylene selectivity at near equilibrium levels. The BEA topology offers a 3D pore network and sites for metal anchoring.

As described in [10.1021/jacs.2c03941], Ga-containing CHA zeolites with chabazite structure serve as efficient PDH catalysts reaching 96% propylene selectivity. Isolated Ga+ active sites exchanged into the CHA framework reversibly form GaHx species that catalyze selective propane dehydrogenation with initial conversion over 40%.

In summary, MFI, BEA, and CHA zeolites have shown excellent potential as catalyst supports for PDH, owing to advantageous properties like high surface area, thermal stability, tunable acidity, ability to disperse and stabilize isolated metal sites, and confinement effects that prevent agglomeration while permitting reactant/product diffusion.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings extracted from the file highlight promising research directions in developing zeolite-supported catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. While MFI, BEA and CHA topologies have demonstrated good performance, there may be opportunities to explore other zeolite frameworks like FER or *STO that balance activity, metal anchoring, and accessibility. Strategies like hierarchical design and framework modification could help address diffusion limitations. Stabilizing single atoms and sub-nanometer clusters appears crucial, so further work is needed on customizing zeolites to enable precise control over active site speciation and isolation. Broader implementation may require extending these specialized synthesis procedures to commercial feasibility. Advances in operando spectroscopy can offer additional insights into metal-zeolite interactions and deactivation phenomena.

分子筛支持 - MFI、FER、BEA等结构的分子筛作为支持体表现如何?

根据文件提供的详细信息,MFI、BEA和CHA结构的分子筛已被证明是丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂的有前景的支持体。

硅沸石-1和ZSM-5等MFI分子筛提供了优秀的支持体来稳定单个金属位点和亚纳米级团簇,这些位点和团簇对PDH高度活性。如[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4]、[10.1021/acscatal.2c05042]和[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.02.019]所述,MFI框架有助于锚定原子分散的Pt位点和封装超小PtZn和PtSn纳米簇,从而实现了约40-45%的丙烷转化率和99%以上的丙烯选择性。 MFI分子筛的刚性微孔结构和弱金属-支持体相互作用可防止团聚并有助于高度分散金属。 分层MFI分子筛具有介孔性,可缓解扩散限制, 正如PtZn合金催化剂在30天内保持显着稳定性的结果所示[10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]。

对于BEA分子筛,如[10.1021/jacs.1c10261]和[10.1016/j.jechem.2020.08.058]的研究表明,除铝Beta分子筛提供了优秀的支持体,可将单个Pt原子稳定在微孔内的Zn巢中。 所得到的Pt/Zn-Beta催化剂在约30%的丙烷转化率和90%的丙烯选择性达到接近平衡水平。 BEA结构提供了三维孔道网络和金属锚定位点。

如[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]所述,含镓CHA分子筛作为PDH催化剂实现了96%的丙烯选择性。 CHA框架中交换的隔离Ga+活性位点可逆地形成GaHx种,催化选择性丙烷脱氢,初始转化率超过40%。

总之,由于MFI、BEA和CHA分子筛具有高比表面积、热稳定性、可调酸度、分散和稳定单个金属位点的能力以及防止团聚的约束效应等有利特性,它们在PDH催化剂的支持体方面展现了巨大的潜力。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:文件中提取的结果突出了在开发分子筛支持的丙烷脱氢催化剂方面的有前景的研究方向。 尽管MFI、BEA和CHA拓扑结构表现出良好的性能,但是在活性、锚定金属和可访问性方面取得平衡的其他分子筛框架(如FER或*STO)也具有探索的机会。 分层设计和框架修饰等策略可以帮助解决扩散限制。 稳定单原子和亚纳米级团簇似乎至关重要,因此需要进一步研究定制分子筛以准确控制活性位点的种类和隔离。 更广泛的实现可能需要将这些专门的合成方法扩展到商业可行性。 operando光谱的进步可以提供关于金属-分子筛相互作用和失活现象的额外信息。
