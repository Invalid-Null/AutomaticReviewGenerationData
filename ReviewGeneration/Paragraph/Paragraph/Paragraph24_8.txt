Based on the details extracted from the file, the article investigates catalyst preparation methods aimed at optimizing active phase dispersion. Specifically, it prepared Co@Si-BEA catalysts with Co loadings from 0.1-5 wt% by vacuum impregnation and showed the 0.1-0.5 wt% samples had the highest intrinsic activity due to well dispersed isolated Co2+ sites. It also showed the dealuminated Co@Si-BEA catalyst produced less graphitic coke than non-dealuminated Co@BEA, indicating better dispersion. 

The preparation methods focused on controlling the Co loading and dealuminating the zeolite to optimize the dispersion of isolated Co2+ sites and minimize aggregation. This optimized dispersion resulted in enhanced catalytic performance. The characterization data revealed key structure-activity relationships that will help guide further development of propane dehydrogenation catalysts [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726].

※※※
The findings showcase how tailored catalyst synthesis techniques can effectively tune active phase dispersion, thereby regulating selectivity and stability. As research endeavors uncover novel compositions and preparation strategies, opportunities emerge to extend such precision-engineering approaches across broader catalyst families. Moreover, advanced operando spectroscopies now allow tracking dispersion dynamics during reaction. Coupling these in-motion insights with high-throughput computations could rapidly screen promoters/additives that maintain optimized dispersion states. Such cutting-edge methodologies may spearhead rational design of robust catalysts for propane dehydrogenation and beyond.

根据文件提取的详细信息,该文研究了旨在优化活性相分散的催化剂制备方法。具体而言,它通过真空浸渍法制备了Co质量分数为0.1-5 wt%的Co@Si-BEA催化剂,结果显示0.1-0.5 wt%的样品由于存在良好分散的隔离Co2+位点而具有最高的固有活性。它还表明,脱铝Co@Si-BEA催化剂比未脱铝Co@BEA产生的焦炭更少,这表明其分散性更好。

制备方法集中在控制Co加载量和对分子筛的脱铝以优化隔离Co2+位点的分散和最小化聚集。这种优化的分散导致了催化性能的提高。表征数据揭示了关键的结构-活性关系,这将有助于进一步开发丙烷脱氢催化剂 [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。

这些发现展示了如何通过定制的催化剂合成技术有效地调节活性相的分散,从而调控选择性和稳定性。随着研究工作揭示新的组成和制备策略,出现了将这种精密工程方法扩展到更广泛催化剂家族的机会。此外,先进的原位光谱现在可以跟踪反应过程中的分散动力学。将这些动态见解与高通量计算相结合,可以快速筛选维持优化分散状态的助剂/添加剂。这种尖端方法论可能会引领丙烷脱氢和其他反应的稳定催化剂的合理设计。
