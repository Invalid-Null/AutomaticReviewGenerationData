Based on the details extracted from the file, one study utilizes integrated differential phase contrast scanning transmission electron microscopy (iDPC-STEM) to directly image isolated cobalt (Co) motifs embedded within the MFI zeolite framework at the T1(7) and T3(9) sites [DOI: 10.1021/jacs.2c02636]. This reveals the location and coordination environment of the active Co species. 

Another study uses high-resolution HAADF-STEM paired with integrated differential phase contrast (iDPC) imaging to precisely pinpoint the localization of subnanometric platinum (Pt) clusters confined within the sinusoidal channels of MFI zeolite [DOI: 10.1038/s41563-019-0430-4]. This demonstrates a strong preference for Pt cluster stabilization within these confined micropores versus other locations.

One article employs atomic-resolution HAADF-STEM and EDS mapping to determine the detailed atomic structure of Pt-yttrium (Pt-Y) intermetallic compound nanoparticles supported on mesoporous zeolite [DOI: 10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4]. Images and analysis reveal the nanoparticles exhibit ordered Pt3Y phases with alternating Pt and Y columns. 

Another study utilizes aberration-corrected HAADF-STEM to directly visualize isolated Pt atoms dispersed in copper (Cu) nanoparticles on alumina [DOI: 10.1038/s41467-018-06967-8]. This confirms strong metal-support anchoring to stabilize single Pt sites. 

One report applies HR-HAADF-STEM paired with iDPC imaging and automated analysis to quantify the percentage of Pt-tin (PtSn) clusters formed within zeolite micropores during reduction treatments [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7].

根据文件中提取的详细信息,一项研究利用集成差分相差扫描透射电子显微镜(iDPC-STEM)直接成像了嵌入MFI沸石框架中的T1(7)和T3(9)位点的隔离钴(Co)活性位点[DOI: 10.1021/jacs.2c02636]。这揭示了活性Co种的位置和配位环境。

另一项研究使用高分辨HAADF-STEM配合集成差分相差(iDPC)成像技术,精确定位了限域在MFI沸石正弦微通道中的亚纳米级铂(Pt)簇[DOI: 10.1038/s41563-019-0430-4]。这表明Pt簇稳定优先发生在这些受限的微米孔隙中。

一篇论文运用原子分辨HAADF-STEM和EDS元素映射技术确定了负载在介孔沸石上的铂-钇(Pt-Y)金属间化合物纳米粒子的详细原子结构[DOI: 10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4]。图像和分析显示纳米粒子呈现有序的Pt3Y相,具有交替的Pt和Y原子列。

另一项研究利用球差校正的HAADF-STEM直接可视化了分散在氧化铝载体上铜(Cu)纳米粒子中的单一Pt原子[DOI: 10.1038/s41467-018-06967-8]。这确认了强大的金属-载体锚定作用来稳定单一Pt位点。

一份报告应用HR-HAADF-STEM配合iDPC成像和自动化分析技术定量确定还原处理过程中形成在沸石微米孔中的Pt-锡(PtSn)簇的百分比[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7]。
