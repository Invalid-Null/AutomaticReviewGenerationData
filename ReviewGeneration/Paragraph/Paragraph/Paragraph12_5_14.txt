Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have evaluated supported mono-metallic platinum (Pt), palladium (Pd), and iridium (Ir) catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts. For example, Pt/Al2O3 catalysts are vital components of the Oleflex process due to Pt's superior ability to activate paraffinic C-H bonds while minimizing C-C bond cleavage [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]. One study showed an alumina-supported Pt catalyst prepared by the colloid method had increasing propane consumption rates with higher H2 content, although propylene production rates depended on exposed Pt surfaces [10.1021/acscatal.0c03381]. Other work found that modifying Pt/γ-Al2O3 with 1 wt% boron promoter reduced coke formation 3-fold and boosted propylene selectivity from 90% to 98% over 12 hours [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548].

There is some evidence that strong metal-support interactions in certain Pt catalysts influence PDH performance. One example synthesized Pt4 clusters confined inside Ge-UTL zeolite channels via Pt-O-Ge linkages. This catalyst exhibited high activity, near 98% propylene selectivity and exceptional stability [10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. However, few studies systematically explore these effects.

Data also shows supported Pd, Rh, and Ir catalysts activate C-H bonds for PDH, but are limited by availability and cost [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. One study compared a SiO2-supported RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst to a standard Pt/Al2O3 catalyst for PDH. Under kinetic conditions, the RhCu SAA displayed higher activity and 100% propylene selectivity versus 80% for Pt, with resistance to coking [10.1126/science.abg8389].

Overall, Pt-based catalysts dominate commercial PDH processes, with some recent work elucidating the impacts of promoters and metal-support interactions on performance. Yet opportunities remain to design optimized catalysts and further understand catalytic mechanisms.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The insights disclosed in the file highlight remarkable progress made in developing selective and stable supported metal catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, especially Pt-based materials. However, most current systems still gradually deactivate, motivating work on novel promoters and supports to mitigate issues like metal sintering and coking while unlocking superior selectivity. Recent advances also underscore vast possibilities to isolate and stabilize finely dispersed metal sites. Future undertakings might therefore concentrate on crafting single atom alloy catalysts or strategically confining ultra-small metal clusters in selectively crafted microporous architectures. Such endeavors can spur the imaginative design of next-generation catalysts that push selectivity limits through exquisite active site engineering. In tandem, operando spectroscopic techniques combined with kinetic and computational modeling will elucidate complex mechanistic pathways occurring at catalyst-reactant interfaces, engendering knowledge to ratchet optimization efforts.

根据文件提供的详细信息,已有几项研究评估了载体支持的铂(Pt)、钯(Pd)和铱(Ir)单金属催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

已有报道表明,Pt基催化剂是最广泛使用和商业上最成功的PDH催化剂。例如,由于Pt优异的烷烃C-H键激活能力和较低的C-C键裂解活性,Pt/Al2O3催化剂是Oleflex工艺的重要组成部分 [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。一项研究表明,通过胶体法制备的Al2O3支持Pt催化剂,其丙烷消耗速率随H2含量的增加而增加,尽管丙烯产率依赖于暴露的Pt表面[10.1021/acscatal.0c03381]。其他工作发现,用1 wt%硼掺杂Pt/γ-Al2O3能使焦炭形成量降低3倍,12小时丙烯选择性从90%提高到98% [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。

有些证据表明,某些Pt催化剂中的强金属支撑体相互作用会影响PDH性能。一个例子是将Pt4簇封装在UTL分子筛上的Ge位点,通过Pt-O-Ge连接。该催化剂表现出高活性、近98%的丙烯选择性和异常稳定性[10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。然而,很少有研究系统地探索这些效应。

数据还显示,载体支持的Pd、Rh和Ir催化剂可激活PDH的C-H键,但受可获得性和成本的限制[10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。一项研究将SiO2支撑的RhCu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂与标准的Pt/Al2O3催化剂进行了PDH比较。在动力学条件下,RhCu SAA催化剂显示出比Pt更高的活性和100%的丙烯选择性,Pt为80%,且RhCu催化剂抗焦炭[10.1126/science.abg8389]。

总体而言,Pt基催化剂主导了商业PDH工艺,最近的一些工作阐明了络合物和金属支撑体相互作用对性能的影响。但优化催化剂设计和深入理解催化机理仍有机会。

综合理解与未来展望:文件披露的信息突出了在开发用于丙烷脱氢的选择性和稳定的载体金属催化剂方面取得的显着进展,特别是Pt基材料。然而,当前大多数体系仍会逐渐失活,促使研究开发新型助剂和载体,以减轻金属烧结和焦化等问题,同时释放卓越的选择性。最近的进展也强调了隔离和稳定高度分散的金属活性中心的巨大可能性。因此,未来的工作可能会集中在制备单原子合金催化剂或有选择性地限制超小金属簇在微孔体系内。这些努力可以促进下一代催化剂的富有想象力的设计,通过精细的活性中心工程来推动选择性的极限。与此同时,作用条件下的光谱技术结合动力学和计算建模,将阐明发生在催化剂-反应物界面上的复杂机理路径,提供知识以推动优化工作。
