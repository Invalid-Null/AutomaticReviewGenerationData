     Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Several studies have investigated the effect of alloying Pt with secondary metals like Sn, Ga, and Zn to modulate the performance for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). Adding these promoters can significantly enhance catalytic activity, selectivity towards propylene, and stability compared to monometallic Pt catalysts.

Pt-Sn catalysts are the most widely studied systems for PDH, with the commercial Oleflex process utilizing PtSn/Al2O3 catalysts [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. Alloying Pt with Sn helps decrease the size of Pt ensembles, alter the electron density, and minimize coke formation and hydrogenolysis reactions, thereby improving propylene selectivity [10.1016/j.cej.2010.09.018, 10.1002/anie.201507119]. Initial C3 conversion with PtSn/Al2O3 increased up to a Sn:Pt ratio of 1.5, then decreased at higher ratios [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]. Pt3Sn1 is considered the optimal active phase [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]. PtSn clusters confined in MFI zeolites also demonstrated excellent stability from ensemble and electronic effects [10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7].

Pt-Ga catalyst systems similarly demonstrated improved dispersion, activity close to equilibrium conversion, selectivity >90%, and stability over monometallic Pt, attributed to the formation of Pt-Ga intermetallic compounds [10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2021.120731]. Isolated Pt-Zn sites stabilized by SiOZn-OH groups in zeolite Beta achieved high conversion, selectivity and stability, with Zn helping anchor isolated Pt atoms [10.1021/jacs.1c10261]. PtZn intermetallic nanoparticles encapsulated in zeolites also showed high productivity up to 17 molC3H6/molPt/s [10.1021/acscatal.1c04092].

In summary, alloying Pt with promoters like Sn, Ga and Zn has demonstrated vast improvements in catalytic performance for propane dehydrogenation compared to monometallic Pt catalysts. The synergistic electronic, ensemble and stability effects imparted through alloy formation allows modulation of the critical activity-selectivity-stability tradeoffs.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The promising advancements in developing highly selective and stable Pt alloy catalysts for propane dehydrogenation reactions, as elucidated in the above studies, pave the pathway for continued innovations in this sphere. Building on the promising Pt-Sn, Pt-Ga and Pt-Zn bimetallic systems, future efforts might focus on optimization of alloy compositions and nanostructures to help break activity-selectivity tradeoffs. Stabilizing uniform sub-nanometric alloys and single-atom catalysts in zeolites could unlock maximal ensemble control. Operando spectroscopy techniques can offer invaluable atomic-level insights into the dynamic structure-performance interplay during reactions. Coupled with kinetic studies and computational modeling, research endeavors might uncover novel phenomena governing alloy catalysts, potentially transforming propane dehydrogenation processes. Explorations of new synergistic alloy combinations could also unlock hitherto unknown possibilities. Integration of emerging catalyst characterization tools with high-throughput computational and experimental screening can accelerate these discoveries. Ultimately, crossing traditional boundaries through interdisciplinary catalyst design can actualize superior alloy catalysts for dehydrogenation reactions, enabling major sustainability strides.

     双金属和合金催化剂——Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

几项研究已调查了合金化Pt与Sn、Ga和Zn等辅助金属对丙烷脱氢(PDH)性能的调节效果。 添加这些助剂可以显著提高催化活性、对丙烯的选择性和稳定性,相比单金属Pt催化剂。

Pt-Sn催化剂是PDH研究最广泛的体系,商业Oleflex工艺使用PtSn/Al2O3催化剂[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。  Pt与Sn合金化有助于减小Pt集团大小,改变电子密度,最大限度地减少焦炭形成和氢裂解反应,从而提高丙烯选择性[10.1016/j.cej.2010.09.018, 10.1002/anie.201507119]。 PtSn/Al2O3的C3转化随Sn:Pt比的增加首先上升至1.5,然后在更高比值下降[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]。 Pt3Sn1被认为是最佳活性相[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]。 采用MFI分子筛限域的PtSn簇也表现出了优异的稳定性,这是合金效应和电子效应的结果[10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7]。

Pt-Ga催化体系同样表现出比单金属Pt更好的分散性、接近平衡转化的活性、大于90%的选择性和稳定性,这归因于Pt-Ga金属间化合物的形成[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2021.120731]。 硅氧锌羟基团稳定的隔离Pt-Zn位点在Beta沸石中实现了高转化率、选择性和稳定性,Zn有助于锚定隔离Pt原子[10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。 封装在分子筛中的PtZn金属纳米粒子的产量也高达17 molC3H6/molPt/s [10.1021/acscatal.1c04092]。

总的来说,与单金属Pt催化剂相比,使用Pt与Sn、Ga和Zn等助剂形成合金可以极大地改善丙烷脱氢的催化性能。 通过合金形成授予的协同电子、集团和稳定性效应允许调节关键的活性-选择性-稳定性折衷。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 正如上述研究所阐明的那样,开发高选择性和高稳定性Pt合金催化剂用于丙烷脱氢反应的前景广阔,为该领域的持续创新铺平了道路。 在前景广阔的Pt-Sn、Pt-Ga和Pt-Zn双金属体系基础上,未来的工作可能会聚焦于优化合金组成和纳米结构,以帮助突破活性-选择性权衡。 在分子筛中稳定均匀的亚纳米合金和单原子催化剂可以实现最大程度的集团控制。Operando光谱技术可以提供宝贵的原子水平见解,以了解反应过程中动态的结构-性能互动。 结合动力学研究和计算建模,研究工作可能会发现治理合金催化剂的新现象,这可能会改变丙烷脱氢工艺。 对新的协同合金组合的勘探也可能打开迄今未知的可能性。 催化剂表征工具与高通量计算和实验筛选的整合可以加速这些发现。 最后,通过跨学科催化剂设计跨越传统界限,可以实现用于脱氢反应的卓越合金催化剂,从而实现重大可持续性进步。
