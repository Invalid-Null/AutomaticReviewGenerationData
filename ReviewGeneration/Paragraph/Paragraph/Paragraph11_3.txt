	<English>
		The article with DOI 10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63202-4 explores metal-support interfacial effects in Cr2O3/Al2O3 catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. It shows tuning the calcination temperature of the alumina support modulates acidity, affecting catalyst stability and coking resistance. The rod-shaped nanostructure and rough surface of alumina enhances Cr2O3 dispersion and stability. Lower support acidity gives better anti-coking ability [10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63202-4].
		
		Another study on VOx/Al2O3 catalysts reveals phosphine modification weakens the metal-support interaction, facilitating propylene desorption and enhancing selectivity/stability [10.1016/j.apcatb.2020.119089].

		※※※
		The findings showcase the pivotal role of strategic metal-support interplays in tuning the microenvironment and electronic properties of active sites. This enables targeted modulation of catalyst performance parameters. Further research could focus on high-throughput computational screening of novel support compositions and morphologies to identify optimal metal-support combinations. In-depth spectroscopic analyses should shed light on modifications in active site geometric and electronic structure engendered by support interactions.

	<Chinese>
		DOI为10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63202-4的文章探讨了Cr2O3/Al2O3催化剂中金属载体界面的效应用于丙烷脱氢。它表明调节氧化铝载体的焙烧温度可以调节酸度,从而影响催化剂的稳定性和结焦抗性。氧化铝的棒状纳米结构和粗糙表面可以增强Cr2O3的分散和稳定性。载体酸度更低可以获得更好的抗焦炭性能[10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63202-4]。
		
		另一项关于VOx/Al2O3催化剂的研究表明磷化物修饰削弱了金属与载体之间的相互作用,促进了丙烯的解吸,提高了选择性和稳定性[10.1016/j.apcatb.2020.119089]。

		※※※
		研究结果凸显了战略性的金属-载体相互作用在优化活性位点的微环境和电子性质中的关键角色。这可以有针对性地调控催化剂性能参数。未来的研究可以侧重于高通量计算在筛选新型载体组成和形貌以识别最佳的金属-载体组合。深入的光谱分析可以阐明载体相互作用导致的活性位点几何和电子结构的改变。
