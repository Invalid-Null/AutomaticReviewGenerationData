 <English>

The article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939] presents microkinetic models of propane dehydrogenation using DFT-derived parameters. It discusses rate equations, surface coverages, turnover frequencies, selectivities, and rate-controlling steps.

Additional microkinetic modeling efforts are presented in the article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889], which uses the CATKINAS package to perform microkinetic simulations based on parameters from DFT calculations. This allows evaluation of the kinetics and practical applicability of single-atom catalysts under industrial conditions.

The study with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916] shows how microkinetic modeling on Pt alloy surfaces has evaluated propane first dehydrogenation barriers as a proxy for activity, along with a selectivity descriptor comparing propylene desorption and dehydrogenation. This reveals a useful trade-off relationship for identifying optimal catalysts.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The microkinetic modeling efforts discussed provide valuable insights into propane dehydrogenation mechanisms, kinetics, and catalyst screening. However, there are opportunities to build on this work by incorporating more complex models and exploring broader catalyst spaces. Emerging areas such as machine learning and high-throughput computations could be integrated with microkinetic models to rapidly predict kinetics over vast catalyst formulations and operating conditions. This data-driven approach may reveal entirely new structure-activity relationships to guide the design of next-generation propane dehydrogenation catalysts.

<Chinese>

带DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939]的文章使用DFT derived参数呈现丙烷脱氢的微观动力学模型。它讨论了速率方程、表面覆盖率、周转频率、选择性和速控步骤。

额外的微观动力学建模工作呈现在DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]的文章中,它使用CATKINAS软件包根据DFT计算的参数进行了微观动力学模拟。这使评估单原子催化剂在工业条件下的动力学和实际适用性成为可能。

DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916]的研究显示微观动力学模型评估了Pt合金表面上丙烷第一脱氢障碍作为活性的近似值,以及丙烯脱附和脱氢的选择性描述符。这揭示了识别优化催化剂的有用折衷关系。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:讨论的微观动力学建模工作对丙烷脱氢机理、动力学和催化剂筛选提供了有价值的洞见。但是,通过结合更复杂的模型和探索更广泛的催化剂空间,有机会在这项工作的基础上进行扩展。新兴领域如机器学习和高通量计算可以与微观动力学模型集成,快速预测广泛催化剂配方和操作条件下的动力学。这种数据驱动方法可能会揭示全新的结构-活性关系,以指导下一代丙烷脱氢催化剂的设计。
