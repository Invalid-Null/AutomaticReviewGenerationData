Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts: How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Pt-Sn alloy catalysts have been widely studied for propane dehydrogenation, with PtSn/Al2O3 being used commercially in the Oleflex process [Placeholder_of_DOI102]. Adding Sn as a promoter helps increase propylene selectivity and stability compared to monometallic Pt catalysts by modifying the electronic properties of Pt sites [Placeholder_of_DOI101, Placeholder_of_DOI106]. Specifically, Sn addition increases the electron density on Pt which facilitates selective C-H bond activation over C-C bond breaking [Placeholder_of_DOI101, Placeholder_of_DOI119]. Sn also produces a “drain-off” effect where it helps remove coke precursors from Pt sites, suppressing deactivation [Placeholder_of_DOI101]. However, too much Sn can also decrease activity by diluting Pt sites [Placeholder_of_DOI118, Placeholder_of_DOI120]. An optimal Pt:Sn ratio around 2-3:1 maintains good activity and stability [Placeholder_of_DOI120]. 

Pt-Zn catalysts have also shown promise for propane dehydrogenation. Isolating single Pt atoms within Zn-modified zeolite frameworks maximizes selectivity and stability while achieving high mass-specific activity [Placeholder_of_DOI111]. The strong Pt-Zn interaction in isolated PtZn4-6 sites leads to exceptional performance. Moreover, forming intermetallic PtZn alloys can reduce the Pt ensemble size compared to disordered PtZn, enhancing thermal stability [Placeholder_of_DOI117]. PtZn intermetallic alloy catalysts approach equilibrium conversion for propane dehydrogenation [Placeholder_of_DOI133].

For Pt-Ga catalysts, the formation of intermetallic Pt-Ga bonds in nanoalloy clusters suppresses side reactions like hydrogenolysis and isomerization [Placeholder_of_DOI138]. This leads to over 99% propylene selectivity while maintaining high activity in certain Pt-Ga catalysts [Placeholder_of_DOI119, Placeholder_of_DOI138]. The Pt-Ga configuration also resists coking and sintering, contributing to stable performance [Placeholder_of_DOI138].

In summary, alloying Pt with promoters like Sn, Zn and Ga allows tuning the selectivity and stability of propane dehydrogenation through electronic, ensemble and geometric effects. Each promoter interacts uniquely with Pt to balance conversion and selectivity. But excess promoter addition can also hinder activity by diluting Pt sites. Determining optimal Pt:promoter ratios and configurations is key to designing high-performance propane dehydrogenation catalysts.

Integrative Understanding and Prospective Outlook: The studies investigated demonstrate that Sn, Zn and Ga serve as promising alloying promoters for Pt-based propane dehydrogenation catalysts, helping overcome limitations in conversion, selectivity and stability compared to monometallic Pt. 1) Exploring novel promoters beyond Sn, Zn and Ga to unlock additional activity and selectivity benefits

4) Developing novel synthetic strategies to precisely control Pt-promoter nanoalloy configurations and metal-support interfaces critical for ideal ensemble and ligand effects

双金属和合金催化剂:Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

Pt-Sn合金催化剂已经被广泛研究用于丙烷脱氢反应, PtSn/Al2O3被商业上使用在Oleflex工艺中 [引用文章DOI编号102]。添加Sn作为助剂有助于提高丙烯选择性和稳定性,相比于单金属的Pt催化剂,这是通过修改Pt位点的电子性质实现的 [引用文章DOI编号101,106]。 具体来说,Sn的加入增加了Pt上的电子密度,这促进了选择性C-H键活化而不是C-C键断裂 [引用文章DOI编号101,119]。 Sn还产生“排出”效应,它有助于从Pt位点去除焦炭前体,抑制脱活性 [引用文章DOI编号101]。但是,过量的Sn也会通过稀释Pt位点而降低活性 [引用文章DOI编号118,120]。 Pt:Sn比率为2-3:1可以保持良好的活性和稳定性 [引用文章DOI编号120]。

Pt-Zn催化剂在丙烷脱氢反应中也展现出了前景。隔离Pt单原子在Zn修饰分子筛框架内可以最大化选择性和稳定性,同时实现高的质量比活性[引用文章DOI编号111]。 PtZn4-6隔离位点中的强Pt-Zn作用导致了卓越的性能。 此外,形成PtZn金属间化合物可以降低Pt团簇的大小,相比无序的PtZn合金增强了热稳定性[引用文章DOI编号117]。 PtZn金属间化合物催化剂接近丙烷脱氢的平衡转化[引用文章DOI编号133]。 

对于Pt-Ga催化剂,纳米合金簇中的铂镓金属间键的形成抑制了如氢裂解和异构化等副反应[引用文章DOI编号138]。这导致某些Pt-Ga催化剂在保持高活性的同时,丙烯选择性高达99%以上[引用文章DOI编号119,138]。 Pt-Ga配置还可以抵抗结焦和烧结,有助于保持稳定的性能[引用文章DOI编号138]。

总之,用助剂如Sn、Zn和Ga合金化Pt允许通过电子效应、配位效应和几何效应调节丙烷脱氢的选择性和稳定性。 每种助剂都以独特的方式与Pt相互作用,在转化率和选择性之间达到平衡。 但是,过量的助剂也会通过稀释Pt活性位点而抑制活性。 确定Pt与助剂的最佳原子比例和配置对于设计高性能丙烷脱氢催化剂至关重要。

※※※  

综合理解与未来展望:调查的研究表明,Sn、Zn和Ga作为Pt基丙烷脱氢催化剂的有前景的合金化助剂,它们有助于克服单金属Pt在转化率、选择性和稳定性方面的局限性。 尽管某些组分如PtSn、PtZn和PtGa达到了卓越的性能,超过了平衡值,未来的研究可以集中在:

1) 探索Sn、Zn和Ga之外的新型助剂,以解锁额外的活性和选择性好处

2)通过先进的光谱技术与计算建模相结合,阐明助剂效应的机理,以实现理性设计

3) 研究多助剂Pt催化剂(例如PtSnZn)和优化助剂水平,以最大限度地发挥协同效应  

4) 开发新颖的合成策略以精确控制纳米Pt-助剂合金配置和金属与载体界面的关系,这些都是实现理想的配位和配体效应的关键

5) 在工业相关条件下评估长期催化剂稳定性,以及在再生循环后的催化剂性能

6) 考虑到性能指标和材料可获得性,进行工艺经济分析来指导催化剂组成的优化

通过不断推进Pt-助剂催化剂调控的基础理论研究,以及评估实际应用的可行性,有效将丙烷转化为丙烯的远景有望不久实现。
