Based on the details extracted from the file, several studies evaluate supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Specifically, Pt/alumina catalysts are widely studied, as Pt has superior ability to activate C-H bonds while minimizing C-C cleavage important for selective PDH [10.1002/anie.201507119, 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. Data shows Pt/alumina maintains stability over 12 hrs when promoted with 1% boron [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. Pt on magnesium aluminate spinel also demonstrates high initial activity before deactivating due to Pt agglomeration accelerated by sulfur [10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]. Adding ALD metal oxide overcoats to Pt/alumina extrudates increases PDH yield and selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391].

Besides alumina, Pt has been evaluated on various supports like SBA-15 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005], zeolites [10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008, 10.1038/s41467-022-32842-8], and boron nitride [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]. Data shows dealuminated BEA zeolite stabilizes highly dispersed Pt2+ cations that serve as primary active sites [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]. And Pt/BN demonstrates higher stability than Pt/alumina.

Characterizations reveal Pt particle size impacts performance. 0.9-1.6 nm clusters on alumina nanosheets show good PDH activity [10.1002/anie.201507119]. While large ensembles are less selective, isolated Pt sites and dimers maintain selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.2c01631, 10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9].

Besides Pt, Ru and Rh have been explored. Monometallic Ru nanoparticles show very low 4.5% propylene selectivity, far inferior to Pt and Ru phosphides [10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]. And RhCu single atoms on silica exhibited higher activity, 100% selectivity, and coking resistance compared to a Pt/alumina benchmark [10.1126/science.abg8389].

Several studies also investigate effects of promoters. Sulfur addition greatly improves Pt/alumina selectivity [10.1006/jcat.1994.1333]. And Sn and In promote Pt activity and stability [10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044, 10.1016/jcat.1997.1531]. While Sn lowers intrinsic activity of small Pt clusters, benefiting selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The analysis of supported metal catalysts for PDH reveals promising pathways forward. Isolated Pt sites stabilized on tailored microporous supports demonstrate impressive intrinsic activity. And promoters like Sn, In, and S further enhance performance through geometric, electronic, and coking inhibition effects. Translation of these fundamental insights to practical extrudate and fluidized bed systems could enable a new generation of robust supported metal catalysts. Beyond traditional supports, exploring novel 2D materials like MXenes and hexagonal boron nitride could unlock additional active site-support synergies. And expanding the library of selective metals and promoters through combinatorial and high-throughput experimentation and screening presents an exciting direction. Overall there exists great potential to design optimized supported metal catalysts that approach the selectivity of Cu while minimizing sensitivity to coking and poisoning.

根据文件提取的详细信息,几项研究评估了Pt、Pd和Ir单金属受支撑催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

具体来说,广泛研究了Pt/氧化铝催化剂,因为Pt有优异的C-H键活化能力,同时最小化了PDH选择性所需的C-C键断裂[10.1002/anie.201507119, 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。数据显示,添加1%的硼可使Pt/氧化铝在12小时内保持稳定[10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。 Pt在镁铝酸钙菱MgAl2O4上也表现出高初始活性,之后因硫促进的Pt团聚而失活[10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]。 在Pt/氧化铝挤出催化剂上添ALD金属氧化物涂层可提高PDH产率和选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。

除氧化铝外,还评价了Pt在各种载体上的性能,例如SBA-15 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005]、分子筛[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008,10.1038/s41467-022-32842-8] 和氮化硼[10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]。数据显示除铝硅分子筛稳定高度分散的Pt2+阳离子作为主要活性位点[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。 Pt/BN比Pt/氧化铝表现出更高的稳定性。

表征结果显示Pt颗粒大小会影响性能。 氧化铝纳米片上0.9-1.6 nm的簇表现出良好的PDH活性[10.1002/anie.201507119]。 而大的配合物选择性较低,隔离的Pt位点和二聚体保持选择性[10.1021/acscatal.2c01631,10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9]。

除Pt外,还探索了Ru和Rh。 单金属Ru纳米粒子的丙烯选择性非常低,仅为4.5%,远低于Pt和Ru磷化物[10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]。 RhCu单原子材料担载在二氧化硅上比Pt/氧化铝基准催化剂表现出更高的活性、100%的选择性和抗炭化性能[10.1126/science.abg8389]。

几项研究还调查了促进剂的效应。 添加硫可大大提高Pt/氧化铝的选择性[10.1006/jcat.1994.1333]。 Sn和In可提高Pt的活性和稳定性[10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044, 10.1016/jcat.1997.1531]。 而Sn降低了小Pt簇的固有活性,提高选择性[10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。

※※※
PDH金属催化剂的分析揭示了前进的有前途的路径。 稳定在定制微孔载体上的隔离Pt位点展示了令人印象深刻的固有活性。 Sn、In 和S等促进剂进一步增强了性能。 将这些基础见解转化为实际挤出和流化床系统,可以实现新一代稳定的金属载体催化剂。除传统载体外,探索MXenes和六方氮化硼等新型2D材料,可以解锁额外的活性位点-载体协同效应。 通过组合和高通量实验,扩大选择性金属和促进剂的范围,呈现一个激动人心的研究方向。 总的来说,设计优化的金属载体催化剂在接近Cu的选择性的同时尽量减少对积炭和中毒的敏感性,潜力巨大。
