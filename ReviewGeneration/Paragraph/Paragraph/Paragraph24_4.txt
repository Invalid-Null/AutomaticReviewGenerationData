 <English>

Based on the details extracted from the file, the article investigates Co@Si-BEA catalysts prepared by vacuum impregnation with varying cobalt loadings from 0.1-5 wt%. The 0.1-0.5 wt% samples showed the highest intrinsic activity due to well dispersed isolated Co2+ sites. Dealuminated Co@Si-BEA also produced less graphitic coke than non-dealuminated Co@BEA, indicating better dispersion [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726].

The preparation methods enable optimizing active phase dispersion by controlling metal loading during impregnation and incorporating dealuminating treatments. Lower cobalt loadings lead to isolated Co2+ sites, while higher loadings cause aggregation. Dealumination further improves dispersion by altering support interactions.

※※※

The findings showcase the intricate interplay between active phase loading, support properties, and textural characteristics in modulating dispersion. While vacuum impregnation furnishes simplicity, emerging preparation techniques could unlock additional facets. For instance, targeted fragmentation strategies may engineer zeolite substrates with defect sites to disperse active motifs. Moreover, surface organometallic routes can graft isolated metal complexes. Exploring these avenues alongside operando spectroscopic monitoring could uncover structure-activity motifs to advance catalyst design.

<Chinese>

根据文件中提取的详细信息,该文章研究了通过真空浸渍法准备的Co含量为0.1-5 wt%的Co@Si-BEA催化剂。0.1-0.5 wt%的样本由于存在良好分散的隔离Co2+位点而表现出最高的固有活性。除铝Co@Si-BEA也产生比非除铝Co@BEA更少的石墨烯焦炭,这表明更好的分散 [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。

制备方法通过控制浸渍过程中的金属负载量和引入除铝处理来优化活性相的分散。较低的钴负载量导致隔离的Co2+位点,而较高的负载量会导致聚集。除铝进一步改进了分散,方法是改变了支持体的相互作用。

※※※

这些发现展示了活性相负载量、支持体性质和组织结构之间复杂的相互作用在调节分散方面的精细配合。虽然真空浸渍法提供了简单性,但新兴的制备技术可能会解锁额外的方面。例如,目标断裂策略可以设计具有缺陷位点的沸石基质以分散活性基团。此外,表面有机金属路线可以接枝隔离的金属复合物。结合操作光谱监测探索这些途径可能会发现结构-活性基团,以推进催化剂设计。
