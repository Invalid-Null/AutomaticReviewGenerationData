 <English>

Several studies have utilized density functional theory (DFT) calculations to elucidate propane dehydrogenation mechanisms and pathways over various catalysts. Gao et al. [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816] performed calculations showing Zn-O-Zn sites in methanol synthesis catalysts possess lower apparent activation barriers (1.81 eV) compared to single Zn2+ sites (2.14 eV), matching experiments. Additionally, their computations revealed Pt's d-band center shifts based on electronic modifications, correlating with propylene selectivity in Pt-transition metal catalysts. 

Gutiérrez et al. [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008] connected DFT-derived microscopic dehydrogenation mechanisms to macroscopic catalytic behavior through microkinetic models. Their models utilized DFT-calculated descriptors - adsorption energies and barriers - to capture structure-activity relationships. Calculations employed the BEEF-vdW functional in VASP to screen transition metal catalyst surfaces.

Wang et al. [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889] studied single-atom Pt catalysts for propane dehydrogenation using spin-polarized DFT, determining reaction energies and barriers. Their plane-wave basis set calculations in VASP examined stability and reactivity trends across coordination numbers and supports. The computations provided insights into the structures and feasibility of these specialized catalysts.

Multiple works have also applied DFT to analyze conventional Pt and Pt-Sn catalysts. Studies by Matera et al. [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916] traced selectivity descriptors from binding energies, while microkinetic models highlighted propane C-H cleavage as the pivotal initial step. Their methodology delivered a rotational assessment augmenting experimental breakthroughs.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: DFT techniques have furnished descriptive perceptions into propane dehydrogenation pathways over assorted catalysts. Building on these advances, an opportunity exists to implement more rigorous computational methodologies such as hybrid functionals or correlated wavefunction approaches to acquire enhanced qualitative precision. Kinetic Monte Carlo simulations could also assist in bridging microscopic electronic structures to macroscopic kinetics.

Furthermore, machine learning frameworks predicated on accrued DFT reference data may facilitate extensive screening of novel single-atom and heterogenous catalysts. By deducing underlying correlations linking geometric, orbital and band parameters to reactivity, such models could accelerate the identification of optimal catalyst compositions and configurations.

<Chinese>

几项研究利用了密度泛函理论(DFT)计算来阐明各种催化剂上的丙烷脱氢机理和途径。Gao等[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816] 的计算显示,在甲醇合成催化剂中,Zn-O-Zn位点的表观激活栏垒(1.81 eV)低于单个Zn2+位点(2.14 eV),与实验结果一致。此外,他们的计算揭示了Pt的d带中心根据电子修饰而改变,与Pt-过渡金属催化剂中的丙烯选择性相关。

 Gutiérrez等[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008] 通过微动力学模型将基于DFT的微观脱氢机理连接到催化剂的宏观行为。他们的模型利用DFT计算的描述子——吸附能和势垒来捕获结构-活性关系。计算中在VASP中采用BEEF-vdW泛函来筛选过渡金属催化剂表面。

 Wang等[10.1021/acscatal.3c00889] 利用自旋极化DFT研究了丙烷脱氢的单原子Pt催化剂,确定反应能和势垒。他们在VASP中的平面波基组计算检查了不同配位数和载体上的稳定性和反应性趋势。这些计算为这些专门催化剂的结构和可行性提供了见解。

 多项工作也将DFT应用于分析常规Pt和Pt-Sn催化剂。Matera等[10.1021/acscatal.1c01916]的研究从结合能追溯选择性描述符,而微动力学模型则强调丙烷C-H键的裂解是关键的初始步骤。他们的方法提供了一个补充实验突破的全面评估。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: DFT技术为各种催化剂上的丙烷脱氢途径提供了描述性的认识。在这些进展的基础上,有机会实施更严格的计算方法,如杂化泛函或相关波函数方法,以获得更高的定性精度。动力学蒙特卡罗模拟还可以帮助桥接微观电子结构到宏观动力学。

 此外,基于累积的DFT参考数据的机器学习框架可以促进对新颖的单原子和异质催化剂的广泛筛选。通过推断出链接几何、轨道和带参数与反应性的基本相关性,这样的模型可以加速确定最佳催化剂成分和配置。
