The article investigates catalyst preparation methods aimed at optimizing active phase dispersion by using vacuum impregnation to prepare Co@Si-BEA catalysts with Co loadings from 0.1-5 wt% [Placeholder_Of_DOI1]. It shows the 0.1-0.5 wt% samples have the highest intrinsic activity due to well dispersed isolated Co2+ sites.
