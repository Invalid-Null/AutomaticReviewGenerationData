 <English>
Based on the information extracted from the file, several studies have evaluated supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Specifically, alumina-supported Pt catalysts have been extensively explored, with one study showing 1 wt% boron addition can decrease coke formation 3-fold and increase propylene selectivity from 90% to 98% over 12 hours [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. Atomic layer deposition (ALD) of metal oxide overcoats on Pt/Al2O3 has also been found to increase PDH yield and selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. Detailed characterizations reveal small Pt clusters around 1.3 nm dispersed on Al2O3 nanosheets [10.1002/anie.201507119]. Adding Sn and forming Pt-Sn alloy phases enhances conversion and selectivity compared to monometallic Pt, attributed to geometric and electronic effects [10.1021/acscatal.1c00639, 10.1126/science.abg7894].

Besides Pt, supported Pd, Rh, and Ir are mentioned to have high intrinsic activity, but their higher cost has limited industrial use [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. A supported RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst was shown to outperform Pt/Al2O3 for PDH, with doubled propylene selectivity (100% vs 80%), lower light-off temperature, and coking resistance [10.1126/science.abg8389].

Overall, while Pt dominates commercial PDH catalysts, new compositions and nanostructures for other noble metals may provide opportunities to enhance catalytic performance.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings underscore Pt's efficacy for PDH arising from optimized CH activation and CC cleavage selectivity. However, stability issues due to coking and sintering necessitate modified compositions (Pt-Sn) or promoters (B) to prolong lifetime. Beyond platinum, tailoring other noble metals at the atomic level, like single atom alloy RhCu, opens alternative design pathways to potentially supersede Pt. Fundamental studies probing metal-support interfacial effects, site requirements, and deactivation mechanisms will inform knowledge-based advances. Operando spectroscopies mapping surface evolution may illuminate optimization routes. Adaptive machine learning models classifying structure-activity descriptors could accelerate screening and discovery too. Commercial drivers around propane supply and propylene demand could further spur innovation across reaction and catalyst engineering.

根据文件提取的信息,已有几项研究评价了Pt、Pd和Ir单金属负载型催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

具体而言,已广泛探索了氧化铝支持的Pt催化剂,一项研究表明1 wt%硼的添加可以使焦炭形成减少3倍,并将丙烯选择性从90%提高到12小时内的98% [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。在Pt/Al2O3上沉积金属氧化物原子层覆层也被发现可以增加PDH产率和选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。详细��征揭示了尺寸约为1.3 nm的Pt簇均匀分散在Al2O3纳米片上[10.1002/anie.201507119]。与单金属Pt相比,添加Sn并形成Pt-Sn合金相可提高转换率和选择性,这是因几何和电子效应导致的[10.1021/acscatal.1c00639, 10.1126/science.abg7894]。

除Pt外,还提到 supported Pd、Rh和Ir具有高的固有活性,但其更高的成本限制了工业应用[10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。一项研究显示,与Pt/Al2O3相比,supported RhCu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂的PDH性能更好,丙烯选择性提高一倍(100% 对 80%),光 off 温度更低,且抗焦炭[10.1126/science.abg8389]。

总的来说,尽管Pt主导商业PDH催化剂,但其他贵金属的新组成和纳米结构可能提供提高催化性能的机会。


※※※
综合理解与未来展望: 这些发现强调了Pt用于PDH的有效性,这源于 CH 激活和 CC 断裂选择性的优化。 然而,由于焦炭和烧结导致的稳定性问题,需要改性组成(Pt-Sn)或促进剂(B)来延长使用寿命。 在铂之外,像单原子合金RhCu那样在原子水平上定制其他贵金属,为可能超越Pt打开了另一种设计途径。 基础研究探究金属-支持体相互作用、位点要求和失活机制将为知识驱动的进步提供信息。 表征反应过程的光谱技术可能会揭示优化路线。 自适应机器学习模型对结构-活性描述子的归类也可以加速筛选和发现。 丙烷供应和丙烯需求的商业驱动因素还可以进一步激发反应和催化剂工程方面的创新。
