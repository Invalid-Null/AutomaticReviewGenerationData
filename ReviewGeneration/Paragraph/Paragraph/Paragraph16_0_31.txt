Based on the in-depth details extracted from the file, several studies have evaluated Pt alloy catalysts including Pt-Sn, Pt-Ga, and Pt-Zn for propane dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.3c00548, 10.1016/j.jechem.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]. Key insights into how alloying modulates the performance are summarized below:

Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Pt-Sn
- Adding Sn as a promoter improves selectivity to propylene and stability, while decreasing initial activity [10.1016/j.jechem.2014.02.084, 10.1002/anie.201507119]. This is attributed to electronic and ensemble effects where Sn increases electron density on Pt and blocks some sites.
- Pt3Sn1 and Pt2Sn1 are considered the most active phases, with optimal Sn/Pt ratios around 1-1.5. Higher Sn content further reduces activity [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016, 10.1016/j.cej.2017.09.035].
- On acidic supports like zeolites, adding Sn helps neutralize acid sites and prevent secondary cracking reactions, boosting propylene selectivity [10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62750-5, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816].

Pt-Ga 
- Pt-Ga shows high initial activity close to equilibrium conversion, with excellent stability and >90% propylene selectivity at temperatures as low as 450°C [10.1016/j.apcatb.2021.120731, 10.1002/anie.202107210].
- The Pt-Ga structure suppresses side reactions like hydrogenolysis and coke formation that quickly deactivate Pt catalysts [10.1002/anie.202107210, 10.1021/jacs.8b05378].

Pt-Zn
- Isolating Pt atoms within Zn-zeolite frameworks maximizes activity and stability, attributed to single atom PtZn4-6 sites [10.1021/jacs.1c10261]. 
- Intermetallic PtZn clusters also show high thermal stability and propylene selectivity >95% over a broad temperature window [10.1016/j.chempr.2020.10.008].

In summary, alloying modulates Pt's geometric and electronic properties, altering adsorption strengths and ensemble sizes to improve selectivity and stability while reducing overcracking for propane dehydrogenation. Each promoter has unique effects that depend on variables like composition, support interactions and preparation method.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The studies reveal Pt-Sn, Pt-Ga and Pt-Zn alloys enable remarkable propane dehydrogenation performance unattainable by monometallic Pt catalysts. However, sub-optimal results are also reported in some cases, suggesting opportunities to further optimize these systems. Future research might explore novel supports to stabilize specific active sites, or utilize operando spectroscopy techniques to elucidate dynamic structure-performance correlations during reaction. Applying data-driven approaches could also accelerate discovery of enhanced bimetallic and multi-metallic combinations. Broader adoption of earth-abundant metals like Fe, Co and Ni as dehydrogenation catalysts is another emerging area offering sustainability benefits compared to Pt alloys.

基于文件中提取的深入细节,几项研究评估了Pt合金催化剂,包括Pt-Sn、Pt-Ga和Pt-Zn,用于丙烷脱氢[10.1021/acscatal.3c00548,10.1016/j.jechem.2014.02.084,10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。 合金化如何调节性能的关键见解概述如下:

双金属和合金催化剂 - 将Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

Pt-Sn
- 添加Sn作为助剂提高了丙烯的选择性和稳定性,同时降低了初始活性[10.1016/j.jechem.2014.02.084,10.1002/anie.201507119]。这归因于Sn增加Pt上的电子密度和堵塞一些活跃位点的电子效应和配位效应。
- Pt3Sn1和Pt2Sn1被认为是最活跃的相,Sn/Pt比率最佳在1-1.5。更高的Sn含量进一步降低活性[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016,10.1016/j.cej.2017.09.035]。
- 在酸性支持如分子筛上的,添加Sn有助于中和酸性位点和预防次生裂解反应,提高丙烯选择性[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62750-5,10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。

Pt-Ga
- Pt-Ga显示接近平衡转化的高初始活性,在低至450°C时具有优异的稳定性和>90%的丙烯选择性[10.1016/j.apcatb.2021.120731,10.1002/anie.202107210]。
- Pt-Ga结构抑制氢解和焦炭形成等副反应,这些副反应会快速失活Pt催化剂[10.1002/anie.202107210,10.1021/jacs.8b05378]。

Pt-Zn
- 将Pt原子隔离在Zn-分子筛框架内可最大化活性和稳定性,这归因于单原子PtZn4-6活跃位点[10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。
- 互化合物PtZn簇也显示了在宽温度范围内>95%的高热稳定性和丙烯选择性[10.1016/j.chempr.2020.10.008]。

总之,合金化调节Pt的几何和电子性质,改变吸附强度和配位数以提高选择性、稳定性,同时减少超裂解用于丙烷脱氢。每种助剂都有其独特的影响,这取决于组成、支持体相互作用和制备方法等变量。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:这些研究表明,Pt-Sn、Pt-Ga和Pt-Zn合金催化剂实现了单组分Pt催化剂无法达到的杰出丙烷脱氢性能。但是,在某些情况下也报道了次优结果,这表明有机会进一步优化这些系统。未来的研究可能会探索新型支撑体来稳定特定的活跃位点,或者利用操作表征技术来阐明反应过程中动态的结构-性能相关性。应用数据驱动方法也可以加速发现增强的双金属和多金属组合。以Fe、Co和Ni等丰富金属为基础的脱氢催化剂也是一个新兴领域,与Pt合金相比,它提供了可持续性方面的好处。
