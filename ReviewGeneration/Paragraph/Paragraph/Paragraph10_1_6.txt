 <English>
Based on the details extracted from the literature, mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over several metal oxide catalysts, with coordinatively unsaturated metal cation (Mcus) sites often implicated as active sites. 

For ZrO2, DFT calculations suggest two Zrcus sites located at an oxygen vacancy activate propane via homolytic C-H bond cleavage [DOI1]. Experimental activation energies agree, supporting Zrcus sites as active regardless of ZrO2 crystal phase. Over Cr/ZrO2, neighboring Zrcus and CrOx sites facilitate C-H bond cleavage in propane and propyl intermediates [DOI2]. For Ga-ZrOx, introducing Ga changes the mechanism from stepwise over ZrO2 to concerted, lowering the barrier [DOI3].

Isolated V5+ sites in VOx/MOx oxides catalyze oxidative dehydrogenation using lattice oxygen. As lattice oxygen depletes, reduced V3+ sites take over, catalyzing non-oxidative dehydrogenation [DOI4, DOI5]. Mars-van Krevelen cycles also operate for VOx, with lattice oxygen abstracting H from propane, the reduced VOx re-oxidizing in O2 or CO2 [DOI6, DOI7].  

Ga2O3 activates propane heterolytically, with Ga3+ sites dissociating C-H bonds. Subsequent H transfer to Pt facilitates H2 formation [DOI8]. Heterolytic dissociation also occurs over supported Ga2O3, forming gallium hydride and alkoxide intermediates [DOI9]. Over zeolitic Ga+, an alkyl mechanism proceeds via propyl and propenyl-Ga intermediates [DOI10, DOI11].

Several other pathways are also proposed, like Cr3+/6+ redox cycles [DOI12], chlorine-assisted routes over CeO2 [DOI13], and metal cation hydride formation from H2 dissociation facilitating dehydrogenation [DOI14].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The studies reveal crucial mechanistic details and active site requirements for propane dehydrogenation over metal oxides. Coordinatively unsaturated metal sites adjacent to lattice oxygen generally activate C-H bonds in propane. Isolated cationic sites often catalyze oxidative dehydrogenation, with their reduction prompting non-oxidative routes. Re-oxidation by O2 or CO2 completes Mars-van Krevelen cycles. 

Several questions remain unaddressed. Elucidating complete reaction networks from propane to desired/undesired products will aid selective catalyst design. Operando spectroscopy can reveal functioning surface intermediates. Multifunctional sites that tune selectivity warrant exploration. Extending these mechanistic principles to other light alkanes presents new opportunities. Advancing fundamental kinetics can accelerate translating academic insights into viable processes.

根据从文献中提取的详细信息,己经为几种金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢提出了机理场景,与此过程中协同不饱和金属阳离子(Mcus)位常被认为是活性位点。

对于二氧化锆,DFT计算表明,位于氧空位的两个锆 Mcus 位通过同裂C-H键激活丙烷[DOI1]。实验激活能证实了这一点,支持无论锆氧化物的晶体相如何,Mcus位都是活性位点。在Cr/ZrO2上,相邻的锆Mcus位和CrOx位促进了丙烷和丙基中间体中的C-H键的断裂[DOI2]。对于 Ga-ZrOx,引入 Ga 将 ZrO2 上的机理从步进改变为协同作用,降低了屏障[DOI3]。

VOx/MOx 氧化物中的孤立 V5+ 位利用晶格氧催化氧化脱氢。随着晶格氧的耗尽,还原的 V3+ 位接管,催化非氧化脱氢[DOI4,DOI5]。 Mars-van Krevelen 循环也适用于 VOx,晶格氧从丙烷中吸出 H,还原的 VOx 在 O2 或 CO2 中再氧化[DOI6,DOI7]。

Ga2O3 通过杂裂活化丙烷,Ga3+位解离C-H键。随后的 H 转移到 Pt 有利于 H2 的形成[DOI8]。杂质裂解也发生在受支持的 Ga2O3 上,形成镓氢化物和醇盐中间体[DOI9]。在沸石型 Ga+ 上,烷基机理通过丙基和丙烯基-Ga 中间体进行[DOI10,DOI11]。

还提出了几种其他途径,如 Cr3+/6+ 氧化还原循环[DOI12]、氧化铈上的氯辅助途径[DOI13]和从 H2 解离中形成的金属阳离子氢化物有利于脱氢[DOI14]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:这些研究揭示了金属氧化物上丙烷脱氢的关键机理细节和活性位点要求。 与晶格氧相邻的协调不饱和金属位通常激活丙烷中的 C-H 键。 孤立的阳离子位通常催化氧化脱氢,其还原促使非氧化脱氢。 氧化还原循环通过 O2 或 CO2 完成。

几个问题仍未解决。阐明从丙烷到期望/不期望产物的完整反应网络将有助于选择性催化剂设计。 操作波谱学可以揭示表面中间体的功能。 调谐选择性的多功能位点值得探索。 将这些机理原理扩展到其他轻烷类呈现新的机会。 促进基本动力学可以加速将学术见解转化为可行的过程。
