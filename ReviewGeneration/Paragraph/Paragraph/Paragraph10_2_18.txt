Based on the details extracted from the literature, propane dehydrogenation mechanisms have been proposed over various metal oxide catalysts. 

Over Cr-based oxides such as CrOx/ZrO2, Cr/ZSM-5, and Cr/SBA-15, active Cr3+ sites are generated in situ during the reaction [DOI1, DOI2]. Isolated Cr3+ species with coordination unsaturation are identified as the active sites in silica-supported catalysts, while oligomeric Cr oxides show higher activity in alumina-supported catalysts [DOI2]. The proximity of Cr3+ cations to Brønsted acid sites also plays a key role in some zeolitic catalysts [DOI3].  

For V-based oxides like VOx/ZrO2, VOx/TiO2, and MoVO, Mars-Van Krevelen type redox mechanisms are widely proposed, involving lattice oxygen in selective alkane activation, followed by catalyst reoxidation by O2 or CO2 [DOI4, DOI5]. Sub-monolayer VOx nanostructures help minimize overoxidation pathways [DOI6]. Specific reaction steps often involve CH bond activation via hydrogen abstraction by lattice oxygen species.

Over Ga/ZSM-5 and supported Ga2O3 catalysts, heterolytic dissociation mechanisms are suggested, leading to gallium hydride and alkoxide intermediates from propane dissociation [DOI7, DOI8]. Subsequent β-H elimination results in propylene formation. The proximity of Ga3+ Lewis acid sites with Brønsted acid sites facilitates key hydride extraction steps after propane activation on the Ga species [DOI3].

For tetragonal ZrO2, DFT calculations have identified coordinatively unsaturated Zr4+ sites neighboring oxygen vacancies as the active sites responsible for homolytic CH bond cleavage [DOI9]. Similar sites may exist on related oxides like GaZrOx which show a change in mechanism from stepwise over ZrO2 to concerted over GaZrOx [DOI10].   

Several other oxides like CeO2, Cr2O3, and MnOx in conjunction with promoters also show mechanistic promise for oxidative dehydrogenation routes, with emphasis on tuning surface oxygen species to minimize nonselective overoxidation [DOI11, DOI12].

※※※

An integrated understanding of these mechanistic scenarios over diverse metal oxide catalysts aids in the identification of promising compositions and architectures for selective dehydrogenation as opposed to undesired combustion or cracking. The advent of in situ spectroscopy techniques has also offered unprecedented glimpses into transient surface intermediates and transformations during alkane dehydrogenation over oxide surfaces. 

Building on these fundamental advances, future research directions may involve optimization of known oxide catalysts by tuning oxidation states or engineering oxygen mobility through compositional modifications or morphological design. Operando characterization methods with enhanced space and time resolution could also elucidate new mechanistic facets over mixed and complex oxide systems. From a broader perspective, these dehydrogenation mechanisms may provide clues to unlock related C-H activation pathways over metal oxides.

<Chinese>

根据从文献中提取的详细信息,在各种金属氧化物催化剂上已经提出了丙烷脱氢机理。  

在CrOx/ZrO2、Cr/ZSM-5和Cr/SBA-15等Cr基氧化物上,反应过程中原位生成活性Cr3+位点[DOI1,DOI]。在硅载体催化剂中,配位不饱和的隔离Cr3+种被确定为活性位点,而在氧化铝载体催化剂中,低聚Cr氧化物显示出更高的活性[DOI2]。 在一些分子筛催化剂中,Cr3+阳离子与Brønsted酸位点的邻近也起关键作用[DOI3]。  

对于VOx/ZrO2、VOx/TiO2和MoVO等V基氧化物,广泛提出了Mars-Van Krevelen型氧化还原机理,其中包括选择性烷烃激活中的晶格氧,然后催化剂被O2或CO2再氧化[DOI4,DOI5]。 子单层VOx纳米结构有助于最大限度地减少过氧化途径[DOI6]。 特定的反应步骤通常涉及晶格氧物种通过氢去除作用起始CH键的活化。  

在Ga/ZSM-5和载体Ga2O3催化剂上,提出了解离机理,导致丙烷解离形成氧化镓和烷基中间体[DOI7,DOI8]。 随后β-H消除导致丙烯形成。 Ga3+路易斯酸位点与Brønsted酸位点的邻近有利于丙烷在Ga物种上的活化后关键的氢提取步骤[DOI3]。  

对于正方四面体ZrO2,DFT计算确定了与氧空位相邻的配位不饱和Zr4+位点作为负责均裂CH键活化的活性位点[DOI9]。 相关氧化物如GaZrOx可能存在类似位点,其在ZrO2上呈逐步机理,在GaZrOx上呈共裂机理[DOI10]。

CeO2、Cr2O3和MnOx以及助剂的几种其它氧化物也表现出氧化脱氢途径的机理前景,强调调节表面氧物种以最小化非选择性过氧化[DOI11,DOI12]。  

※※※  

对这些多种金属氧化物催化剂的脱氢机理的综合理解有助于识别出用于选择性脱氢而非望不到的燃烧或裂化的有前景的组合和结构。 原位光谱技术的出现也提供了瞬时表面中间体和过渡态的前所未有的视角,在氧化物表面上进行烷烃脱氢。   

在这些基础进展的基础上,未来的研究方向可能涉及通过组成修饰或形态设计来优化已知氧化物催化剂,调整氧化态或工程氧的迁移。 增强空间和时间分辨率的原位表征方法也可以阐明新型机理面貌,mieshuo xīn xíng jī yì miào mó,涉及混合氧化物和复合氧化物系统。 从更广阔的视角来看,这些脱氢机理可能为解锁相关的C-H活化途径中的氧化物提供线索。
