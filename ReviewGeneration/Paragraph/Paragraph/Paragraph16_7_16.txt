Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Several studies have evaluated the effect of alloying platinum with secondary metals like tin (Sn), gallium (Ga), and zinc (Zn) for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) [10.1021/acscatal.3c00548, 10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]. Adding these promoters helps modulate the catalytic performance of Pt in different ways.

Alloying Pt with Sn has been widely studied for PDH. The addition of Sn serves to increase propene selectivity and stability compared to monometallic Pt catalysts. For example, Pt-Sn/Al2O3 was found to achieve higher propane conversion and propene selectivity over Pt/Al2O3 [10.1016/j.jje.2017.09.035]. DFT calculations suggest Sn helps remove carbonaceous deposits from Pt active sites. The optimal Sn/Pt ratio was found to be around 1-1.5, beyond which excess Sn poisoned sites [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]. Pt3Sn and Pt2Sn1 sites are considered the active phase [10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]. Sn addition also helps dispersion of Pt particles. However, some studies note initial activity is lower for Pt-Sn versus Pt, indicative of a tradeoff between stability and activity [10.1016/j.jcat.2018.09.006]. 

For Pt-Ga catalysts, key findings show Ga helps suppress side reactions like coke formation and improves selectivity [10.1002/anie.202107210]. The Ga atoms isolate Pt sites, altering the adsorption properties. Pt-Ga intermetallic clusters supported in zeolites or on metal oxides like Al2O3 displayed enhanced stability and conversion approaching equilibrium levels [10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2021.120731].

PtZn catalysts formed intermetallic structures which helped reduce Pt ensemble size and improve PDH performance. Ordered PtZn alloys achieved >95% propylene selectivity over a broad temperature range with excellent stability, surpassing reference PtSn catalysts [10.1016/j.chempr.2020.10.008]. PtZn sites embedded in the cages and channels of zeolites also limit sintering and improve conversion [10.1021/acscatal.1c04092]. An optimal Zn/Pt ratio of around 4:1 balanced activity and stability [10.1021/jacs.1c10261].

In summary, adding secondary metals like Sn, Ga and Zn helps tune the geometric and electronic properties of Pt sites to achieve enhanced activity, selectivity and stability for PDH compared to monometallic Pt catalysts. Formation of intermetallic Pt alloy clusters plays a key role in many optimized catalyst formulations. However, balancing stability improvements against potential activity losses requires identifying optimal secondary metal content.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The detailed insights uncovered in these studies highlight the remarkable improvements in catalytic performance enabled by alloying Pt with promoters like Sn, Ga and Zn for propane dehydrogenation. Key synergies arise from geometric and ligand effects that help isolate Pt sites and remove coke precursors. 

However, further work might explore ternary compositions like PtGaZn or focus on optimizing oxygen storage capacity. Additionally, more in-situ characterization studies during PDH could offer dynamic insights to guide synthetic design. Machine learning approaches leveraging existing data on composition-performance relationships may also accelerate discovery of optimized Pt alloy formulations and structures. Ultimately, scaling up specialized preparation methods poses challenges, but continued developments promise to enable cleaner propylene production.

双金属和合金催化剂 - Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

几项研究评估了将铂与锡(Sn)、镓(Ga)和锌(Zn)等辅助金属合金化用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应的效果[10.1021/acscatal.3c00548, 10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。加入这些助剂有助于以不同方式调节Pt的催化性能。

Pt与Sn合金催化剂在PDH反应中已被广泛研究。添加Sn有助于提高丙烯选择性和稳定性,相比单金属Pt催化剂。例如,Pt-Sn/Al2O3比Pt/Al2O3获得更高的丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性[10.1016/j.jje.2017.09.035]。 DFT计算表明,Sn有助于除去Pt活性位点上的碳质沉积物。最佳的Sn/Pt比率约为1-1.5,超过该比例,过量的Sn会中毒活性位点[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]。 Pt3Sn和Pt2Sn1被认为是活性相[10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]。 Sn的添加也有助于Pt颗粒的分散。但是,一些研究指出Pt-Sn的初始活性比Pt低,这表明稳定性和活性之间存在权衡[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.09.006]。

对于Pt-Ga催化剂,关键发现表明Ga有助于抑制副反应如焦化反应,并提高选择性[10.1002/anie.202107210]。 Ga原子隔离Pt位点,改变吸附特性。支撑在分子筛或Al2O3等金属氧化物上的Pt-Ga金属间化合物簇显示出增强的稳定性和接近平衡水平的转化率[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2021.120731]。

PtZn形成的金属间化合物有助于减小Pt团簇尺寸,改善PDH性能。 有序PtZn合金实现了>95%的丙烯选择性,在宽温度范围内具有优异的稳定性,优于参比PtSn催化剂[10.1016/j.chempr.2020.10.008]。嵌入在分子筛孔道和笼中的PtZn位点也限制烧结,提高转化率[10.1021/acscatal.1c04092]。 约4:1的最佳Zn/Pt比可以平衡活性和稳定性[10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。

总之,添加Sn、Ga和Zn等辅助金属有助于调谐Pt位点的几何和电子特性,使其相比单金属Pt催化剂实现更高的PDH活性、选择性和稳定性。 在许多优化的催化剂配方中,Pt合金团簇的形成起关键作用。 但是,在改善稳定性的同时平衡潜在的活性损失需要确定辅助金属的最佳含量。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:这些研究中揭示的详细见解突出表明,通过将Pt与Sn、Ga和Zn等助剂合金化可以显着改善丙烷脱氢催化性能。 几何和配体效应产生的关键协同作用有助于隔离Pt位点并去除焦炭前体。

然而,未来的工作可能会探索PtGaZn等三元组合,或者专注于优化氧储能容量。 此外,PDH反应过程中进行的更多原位表征研究可以提供动态信息来指导合成设计。 权衡现有组成-性能关系数据的机器学习方法也可以加速发现Pt合金配方和结构的优化。 最终,扩大专业制备方法的规模具有挑战性,但持续的发展有望实现更清洁的烯生产。
