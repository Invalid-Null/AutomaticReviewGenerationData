Based on the details extracted from the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH).

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most successful and widely used catalysts for commercial PDH processes [DOI1]. An alumina-supported Pt catalyst is noted to be vital for PDH due to Pt's ability to selectively activate C-H bonds while minimizing C-C bond cleavage [DOI2]. Multiple studies have provided data and insights on the performance of supported Pt catalysts, including Pt/Al2O3 [DOI3, DOI4], Pt/SBA-15 [DOI5], Pt/zeolites [DOI6], and Pt/boron nitride [DOI7]. Key factors influencing Pt catalyst stability and selectivity include the addition of promoters (e.g. Sn, S, B) [DOI8, DOI9], metal-support interactions [DOI7], and isolation of single Pt sites [DOI10]. 

Besides Pt, supported Pd, Rh, and Ir are briefly noted to exhibit high intrinsic activity, but there is minimal specific data on their performance [DOI11]. One study directly compares a supported RhCu single atom alloy catalyst to a standard Pt/Al2O3 catalyst, finding superior activity, 100% propene selectivity and coking resistance for the RhCu catalyst [DOI12].

Overall, Pt-based catalysts remain the predominantly studied and utilized mono-metallic catalysts for commercial PDH processes. However, emerging studies on novel supports and promoter effects for Pt, as well as comparative data for other noble metals, suggest opportunities to further enhance mono-metallic catalyst stability, selectivity and cost-effectiveness.

Present research trends coupled with rapid expansions in materials synthesis & characterization techniques could enable breakthroughs in designing optimized mono-metallic catalysts that balance activity, selectivity, and longevity at reduced precious metal loadings.

根据文件提取的详细信息,已经有几项研究探索了载体支持的单金属催化剂Pt、Pd和Ir用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

文献报道,Pt基催化剂是最成功和广泛使用的商业PDH催化剂之一 [DOI1]。载体支持的Pt催化剂由于Pt能够选择性激活C-H键而最小化C-C键的断裂,这对PDH来说至关重要 [DOI2]。多个研究已经提供了有关载体支持Pt催化剂性能的数据和见解,包括Pt/Al2O3 [DOI3, DOI4]、Pt/SBA-15 [DOI5]、Pt/分子筛 [DOI6] 和Pt/氮化硼 [DOI7]。影响Pt催化剂稳定性和选择性的关键因素包括添加促进剂(例如Sn、S、B) [DOI8, DOI9]、金属与载体的相互作用 [DOI7]以及单一Pt位点的隔离 [DOI10]。

除Pt以外,文献简要提到载体支持的Pd、Rh和Ir表现出高的固有活性,但关于其性能的数据很少 [DOI11]。一项研究直接比较了载体支持的RhCu单原子合金催化剂和标准的Pt/Al2O3催化剂,发现RhCu催化剂具有更高的活性、100%丙烯选择性和抗焦炭性 [DOI12]。

总的来说,Pt基催化剂仍然是商业PDH工艺中研究和利用最广泛的单金属催化剂。然而,Pt的新型载体和促进剂效应的新兴研究,以及其他贵金属的比较数据,表明有机会进一步提高单金属催化剂的稳定性、选择性和经济效益。
