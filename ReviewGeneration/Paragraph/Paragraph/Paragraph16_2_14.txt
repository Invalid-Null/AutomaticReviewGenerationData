Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Supported PtSn catalysts have been widely studied and utilized commercially for propane dehydrogenation, with the PtSn/Al2O3 system at the core of the UOP Oleflex process [Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.cej.2010.09.018, Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.jechem.2021.06.002]. Adding Sn as a promoter enables significant improvements in propene selectivity, stability, and regeneration capability over monometallic Pt catalysts. This stems from electronic and ensemble effects where Sn increases the electron density on Pt sites while limiting the number of contiguous Pt sites for alkane activation [Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.jcat.2018.09.006, Placeholder_of_10.1007/s11244-015-0464-3]. A Pt:Sn ratio around 2:1 to 3:1 balances activity and selectivity [Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016, Placeholder_of_10.1016/S0920-5861(96)00061-0]. Beyond this, excess Sn leads to over-suppression of dehydrogenation turnover [Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]. 

Investigations have also honed in on Pt-Ga and Pt-Zn alloy catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. Pt-Ga intermetallic compounds help mete out contiguous Pt sites to achieve exceptional propylene selectivity (>95%) over a broad 520-620°C range [Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.chempr.2020.10.008]. Isolated Pt stabilized on SiOZn-OH nests in the channels of desilicated BEA zeolite enables persistent 115 s-1 site-time yields [Placeholder_of_10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]. Anchoring Pt-Zn moieties (CN ≈ 3.5) on the external surface of SPP affords remarkable 160 s-1 productivity unmatched by Pt/SPP or PtZn-DeAlBEA analogues [Placeholder_of_10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]. Encapsulated PtZn nanoclusters in hierarchically structured MFI and Beta zeolites allow breaking diffusion limitations to actualize near-equilibrium conversion [Placeholder_of_10.1021/acscatal.1c01808, Placeholder_of_10.1021/jacs.1c10261].

In consolidation, strategic additions of Sn, Ga, or Zn alter the electron density, coordination environment and contiguous ensemble size of Pt to enable remarkable propane dehydrogenation performance. Future designs should aspire to strike the optimal balance between initial activity, selectivity and longevity by fine-tuning the relative composition, coordination motifs and diffusion landscape.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Alloying Pt with secondary metals has shown tremendous promise to overcome limitations of monometallic Pt catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. However, uncovering explicit structure-performance correlations remains an open quest, especially regarding the coordination chemistry and motif-specific turnover capabilities. Advancements in operando spectroscopic techniques combined with theoretical modeling may illuminate these intricacies. An emerging horizon is extending such bimetallic constructs to photocatalysis and electrocatalysis realms to enable renewably-driven alkane activation.

双金属和合金催化剂 - Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

支持在Al2O3上的PtSn催化剂已经被广泛研究和商业利用,UOP公司的Oleflex工艺就是以PtSn/Al2O3体系为核心。Sn的添加作为助剂可以通过电子效应和组合效应显著提高单金属Pt催化剂的丙烯选择性、稳定性和再生能力。Sn增加了Pt活性位上的电子密度,同时限制了Pt连续位的数量。Pt与Sn的摩尔比约为2:1到3:1时,催化剂的活性和选择性之间达到平衡。超过这个比例,过量的Sn会过度抑制脱氢反应。

Pt-Ga和Pt-Zn合金催化剂也被研究用来进行丙烷脱氢反应。Pt-Ga金属间化合物有助于分散连续的Pt位点,在520-620°C的广泛温度范围内实现了异常的高丙烯选择性(>95%)。被隔离且稳定在脱硅BEA分子筛的SiOZn-OH巢穴中的Pt可以达到115 s-1的持续位点时间产率。将Pt-Zn基团(配位数≈3.5)锚定在硅酸铝球状物表面可以获得160 s-1的产率,超过了Pt/硅酸铝球状物或PtZn-脱硅BEA分子筛的性能。分别被包裹在分层MFI和Beta分子筛中的PtZn纳米团簇允许打破扩散限制,接近平衡转化率。

总的来说,Sn、Ga或Zn的战略添加可以改变Pt的电子密度、配位环境和连续配位体大小,实现卓越的丙烷脱氢性能。未来的设计应该通过精细调节相对组成比、配位基团和扩散结构来实现最佳平衡的初始活性、选择性和稳定性。

※※※

综合理解和未来展望:用第二种金属与Pt合金化已经显示出巨大的前景来克服单金属Pt催化剂在丙烷脱氢中的局限性。然而,确定明确的结构-性能相关仍然是一个悬而未决的课题,尤其是与配位化学和特定基团的周转能力有关。先进的原位光谱技术结合理论模型可能会揭示这些复杂性。一个新兴的领域是将这种双金属构造扩展到光催化和电催化领域,以实现可再生驱动的烷烃激活。
