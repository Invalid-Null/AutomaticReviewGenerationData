Zeolites and Mesoporous Supports - What zeolite topologies like MFI, FER, BEA have shown promise as supports?

Several studies have evaluated zeolite supports like MFI, FER, and BEA structures for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts. MFI and BEA topological zeolites in particular have demonstrated promise, which may be attributed to their relatively facile synthesis procedure, tunable acidity, and moderate pore size [Placeholder_of_DOI1].

MFI zeolites such as silicalite-1 and ZSM-5 provide ideal supports to anchor isolated Pt atoms or sub-nanometric Pt alloy clusters, leveraging strong metal-support interactions. Encapsulation of these ultrafine species within the microporous MFI structure suppresses undesirable CC bond cleavage while retaining high activity for propane dehydrogenation [Placeholder_of_DOI2]. Hierarchical MFI zeolites with secondary mesoporosity have also been effective in minimizing diffusional constraints. PtZn alloy nanoparticles supported on mesoporous MFI nanosheets exhibited outstanding durability over prolonged reaction periods [Placeholder_of_DOI3].  

Zeolite BEA, particularly in dealuminated forms, has served as a versatile support matrix to disperse Pt, stabilizing the metal as isolated atoms or clusters coordinated to framework heteroatoms like Zn or Sn. Optimization of factors like Pt loading, Zn content, and dealumination level in PtZn/BEA systems has enabled remarkable equilibrium propane conversions near 30-40% with propylene selectivities over 90% [Placeholder_of_DOI1].

While MFI and BEA have taken precedence, other zeolite topologies like FAU and CHA have received limited attention thus far. Continued research into specialized zeolite structures or hierarchical variants may uncover additional suitable supports for next-generation PDH catalysts with augmented stability and minimized deactivation tendencies.

分子筛支持材料——哪些 MFI、FER 和 BEA 等沸石结构表现出具有应用前景的支持材料?

几项研究已经评估了MFI、FER和BEA等沸石结构作为丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂的支持材料。尤其是MFI和BEA拓扑分子筛由于其相对简易的合成程序、可调节的酸性和适中的孔径表现出了应用前景[占位符_基于_DOI1]。  

如硅酸盐-1和ZSM-5等MFI沸石可提供理想的支撑基质以锚定孤立的Pt原子或亚纳米Pt合金簇,利用强大的金属-支撑相互作用。这些超细物种封装在MFI微孔结构中可抑制不desired CC键断裂而保持PDH反应的高活性[占位符_基于_DOI2]。具有二级介孔的分层MFI沸石也有效地减少了扩散限制。负载在介孔MFI纳米片层上的PtZn合金纳米颗粒在长周期反应中表现出优异的稳定性[占位符_基于_DOI3]。

沸石BEA,特别是其除铝形式,可作为优异的支撑基质分散Pt,将Pt稳定为配位于框架杂原子(如Zn或Sn)的孤立原子或簇。通过优化Pt负载量、BEA系列催化剂中的Zn含量和除铝度等因素,可实现卓越的平衡丙烷转化率近30-40%,丙烯选择性超过90% [占位符_基于_DOI1]。

尽管目前MFI和BEA占主导地位,但对其他沸石拓扑结构(如FAU和CHA)的关注还比较有限。继续研究特殊的分子筛结构或分层变体可以发现更优良的PDH催化剂支持体系,以实现增强的稳定性和尽可能减少失活倾向。
