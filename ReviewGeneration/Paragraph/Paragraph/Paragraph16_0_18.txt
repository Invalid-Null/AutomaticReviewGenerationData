Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) is an endothermic catalytic process to produce propylene, an important petrochemical feedstock. Platinum-based catalysts exhibit high activity but suffer from rapid deactivation and low selectivity.[Placeholder_of_DOI1] Alloying platinum with post-transition metals like tin, gallium, or zinc has emerged as an effective strategy to enhance the catalytic performance in terms of activity, selectivity and stability.[Placeholder_of_DOI2] 

Several studies have demonstrated that incorporating tin as a secondary metal modulates the geometric and electronic properties of platinum active sites in Pt-Sn catalysts. The addition of Sn helps decrease the size of Pt ensembles and increases electron density of Pt, which improves propene selectivity by selectively activating C-H bonds over C-C bonds.[Placeholder_of_DOI3] DFT studies suggest Pt3Sn1 configuration is optimal, with excess Sn leading to over-dilution of active sites.[Placeholder_of_DOI4] Supported Pt-Sn catalysts like Pt-Sn/Al2O3 and Pt-Sn/MFI zeolite show significantly higher propene selectivity (>99%) and stability compared to monometallic Pt catalysts.[Placeholder_of_DOI5, Placeholder_of_DOI6]

Investigations on supported Pt-Ga nanoparticles reveal Ga incorporation greatly enhances thermal stability and resistance to coking. The Pt-Ga structure suppresses side reactions leading to coke precursors. Pt-Ga intermetallic alloy catalysts achieve high propane conversion close to equilibrium limits at mild temperatures (≤500°C) with exceptional longevity.[Placeholder_of_DOI7]

Pt-Zn catalysts synthesized by stabilizing Pt atoms within SiOZn-OH nests in the framework of zeolite Beta produce isolated PtZn4-6 sites upon activation. These single-site PtZn catalysts approach thermodynamic equilibrium conversion levels (up to 63h) with remarkable stability.[Placeholder_of_DOI8] 

In summary, alloying platinum with secondary metals like Sn, Ga and Zn alters the geometric and electronic properties of Pt active sites, leading to optimized ensemble configuration and ligand effects. This augments propene selectivity, activity and stability - the key criteria for viable propane dehydrogenation catalysts.

双金属和合金催化剂 —— Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

丙烷脱氢(PDH)是一种内吸热催化过程,用于生产丙烯,这是一种重要的石化原料。铂基催化剂表现出高活性,但遭受快速失活和低选择性。 [Placeholder_of_DOI1]用锡、镓或锌等后过渡金属合金化铂已经成为一种有效的策略,以提高催化性能,如活性、选择性和稳定性。[Placeholder_of_DOI2]

几项研究表明,在Pt-Sn催化剂中掺入锡作为第二金属可以调节铂活性位点的几何和电子性质。 Sn的添加有助于减小Pt聚集体的大小,增加Pt的电子密度,这通过选择活化C-H键而不是C-C键来提高丙烯的选择性。 [Placeholder_of_DOI3] DFT研究表明Pt3Sn1配置是最佳的,过量的Sn会导致活性位点过度稀释。 [Placeholder_of_DOI4] 支持的Pt-Sn催化剂,如Pt-Sn/ Al2O3和Pt-Sn/ MFI沸石,显示出比单金属Pt催化剂高得多的丙烯选择性(>99%)和稳定性。 [Placeholder_of_DOI5, Placeholder_of_DOI6]

对支持Pt-Ga纳米粒子的研究表明,Ga掺入大大提高了热稳定性和抗结焦性。 Pt-Ga结构抑制了导致焦炭前体的副反应。 Pt-Ga金属间化合物催化剂在温和温度(≤500°C)下实现了高达平衡限度的丙烷转换率,并且持久性异常。 [Placeholder_of_DOI7]

通过稳定Pt原子包裹在沸石β中SiOZn-OH巢中合成的Pt-Zn催化剂在活化后形成隔离的PtZn4-6位点。这些单位点PtZn催化剂接近热力学平衡转化水平(直到63小时),稳定性卓越。 [ Placeholder_of_DOI8]

总之,用锡、镓和锌等第二金属合金化铂改变了Pt活性位点的几何和电子性质,导致优化的配位体配置和配体效应。这增强了丙烯的选择性、活性和稳定性——这是可行丙烷脱氢催化剂的关键标准。
