Based on the in-depth details extracted from the files, a few notable techniques enable synthesizing isolated single metal sites in zeolitic supports for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts:

Dealumination of zeolites like beta or ZSM-5 creates vacant lattice sites and hydroxyl nests that can anchor isolated metal species. The study [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103] discusses dealuminating zeolite beta with nitric acid, then stabilizing single cobalt sites within the generated hydroxyl nests. Similarly, atomically dispersed platinum anchored to silanol groups in dealuminated zeolite BEA exhibits activity and stability for PDH [10.1021/acscatal.2c01631].

Direct hydrothermal zeolite synthesis with metal-ligand precursors facilitates building isolated metal sites into the framework. [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034] exemplifies trapping zinc cations coordinated to ethylenediamine within the micropores of silicalite-1. Additives like Cu enhance the formation of exclusively isolated zinc sites in S-1, verified by EXAFS [10.1021/acscatal.2c00928].

High-temperature hydrogen reduction converts species like zinc methyl and zinc hydroxide in the pores of ZSM-5 into isolated Zn^2+ stabilized at cation sites [10.1021/cs200441e]. Ga-CHA needs 700°C reduction to form tetrahedrally coordinated, single Ga+ sites reactive for alkane activation [10.1021/jacs.2c03941].

Advanced microscopy and spectroscopy techniques clearly verify the structure of isolated metal sites. Scanning transmission electron microscopy elucidates the location of single platinum atoms anchored to framework germanium in dealuminated zeolite UTL [10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. X-ray absorption fine structure spectra in [10.1021/acscatal.2c00928] confirms Cu addition creates exclusively isolated zinc sites in S-1.

B型或ZSM-5型分子筛的除鋁处理产生空的晶格位点和羟基巢,可以锚定隔离的金属物种。 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]的研究讨论用硝酸处理沸石B型β产生空的晶格位点和羟基巢,然后稳定β型分子筛中单个钴位点。类似地,锚定在除铝分子筛BEA的硅醇基团上的原子分散铂表现出高活性和稳定性[10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]。

直接的水热分子筛合成与金属-配体前驱体有助于将隔离的金属位点建立到支架中。 [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034]例子描述了在硅沸石-1微孔中困住与乙二胺配位的锌阳离子。添加剂如Cu增强了S-1中完全隔离锌位点的形成,这是EXAFS证实的[10.1021/acscatal.2c00928]。

高温氢还原将如ZSM-5孔中的锌甲基和锌氢氧化物转化为稳定在阳离子位点的离子Zn^2+ [10.1021/cs200441e]。 Ga-CHA需要700°C还原才能形成四面体配位的单Ga+位点,这些位点能活化烷烃[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]。

先进的显微镜和光谱技术明确证实了隔离金属位点的结构。 扫描透射电子显微镜阐明了除鋁UTL型分子筛中锚定在结构锗上的单个铂原子的位置[10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。 [10.1021/acscatal.2c00928] 中的X射线吸收细微结构谱证实Cu的添加产生S-1中完全隔离的锌位点。
