Based on the details extracted from the references, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) faces considerable thermodynamic constraints due to being a highly endothermic process. As stated in [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103], the standard enthalpy change (ΔH298) is +124.3 kJ/mol, necessitating high temperatures of 500-600°C to achieve reasonable conversion levels [10.1021/acscatal.1c01924, 10.1021/acscatal.0c01455]. At lower temperatures, the positive Gibbs free energy makes the reaction unfavorable [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. To shift the equilibrium, low propane partial pressures or co-feeding of oxidants like O2 and CO2 can be utilized, providing exothermic oxidation reactions to improve thermodynamic conversion [10.1002/advs.202207756].

Regarding kinetics, propane dehydrogenation likely involves C-H bond activation as the rate determining step based on calculated barriers around 0.7 eV [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]. Dehydrogenation competes with other pathways like hydrogenolysis, cracking to form C2 hydrocarbons, and coke formation, which lead to catalyst deactivation [10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01916]. These side reactions are structure-sensitive, favored on adjacent Pt ensembles, whereas dehydrogenation can occur on isolated metal sites [10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019]. 

Developing a detailed mechanistic picture of these elementary steps could aid selective catalyst design, but gaps remain in understanding coke formation and other secondary transformations [10.1021/acscatal.5b01423]. Apparent activation energies have been estimated as 180-200 kJ/mol for dehydrogenation and 159-174 kJ/mol for cracking over chabazite catalysts [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]. Overall, progress has been made in elucidating the kinetic and thermodynamic factors influencing propane dehydrogenation, but opportunities exist to explore emerging computational, spectroscopic, and synthetic techniques to gain further quantitative insights.

根据参考文献中提取的细节,丙烷脱氢(PDH)由于是一个高度放热的过程,因此面临相当大的热力学限制。如[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]中所述,标准焓变(ΔH298)为+124.3 kJ/mol,需要500-600°C的高温才能达到合理的转化水平[10.1021/acscatal.1c01924, 10.1021/acscatal.0c01455]。在更低的温度下,正的吉布斯自由能使反应不利[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。 为了移动平衡,可以利用低丙烷偏压或氧化剂如O2和CO2的共饲,提供放热的氧化反应来提高热力学转换[10.1002/advs.202207756]。

关于动力学方面,丙烷脱氢可能涉及C-H键活化作为速率限制步骤,根据计算的势垒约为0.7 eV [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]。脱氢与其它途径如氢裂解、裂解形成C2烃和焦炭形成竞争,这将导致催化剂失活[10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01916]。这些副反应对相邻的Pt配合物敏感,而脱氢可以发生在隔离的金属位点上[10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019]。

开发这些基元步骤的详细机理图片可以帮助选择性催化剂设计,但焦炭形成和其他次要转化的理解还存在差距[10.1021/acscatal.5b01423]。在沸石催化剂上,脱氢和裂解的表观活化能分别估计为180-200 kJ/mol和159-174 kJ/mol [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]。总的来说,在阐明影响丙烷脱氢的动力学和热力学因素方面取得了进展,但是还有机会利用新兴的计算、光谱和合成技术获得更多的定量洞见。
