	<English>
		Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) is an endothermic process (ΔH298K = 124.3 kJ/mol) that requires high temperatures (>500°C) to achieve sufficient equilibrium conversion, as detailed by references [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103] and [10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019]. The thermodynamic constraints pose challenges, with side reactions like coke formation and hydrogenolysis becoming prevalent at optimal PDH temperatures [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916]. Attempts to improve performance must balance between kinetic activity, which favors coordinatively unsaturated sites, and selectivity, where isolated metal sites mitigate over-dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.5b01423]. Certain catalysts kinetically stabilize sites, for instance molecular sieves preventing sintering [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816], or alter thermodynamic limitations via oxidative routes [10.1002/advs.202207756]. Reaction kinetics have been probed through apparent activation energies - PDH barriers range 180-200 kJ/mol on zeolites [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]. Further mechanistic insights could elucidate structure-sensitive steps and strategic catalyst optimizations.
		※※※
		With recent advancements elucidating thermodynamic and kinetic intricacies, an integrated approach leveraging computational screening and microkinetic modeling appears promising. This could systematically assess tradeoffs, identify optimal sites balancing activity against selectivity, and enable rational catalyst design. Broader possibilities exist in solar thermochemical routes, where renewable heating overcomes equilibrium limitations.
	</English>
	<Chinese>
		丙烷脱氢(PDH)是一个吸热过程(ΔH298K = 124.3 kJ/mol),需要高温(>500°C)才能达到足够的平衡转化率,详见参考文献[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]和[10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019]。热力学限制带来挑战,最佳PDH温度下会出现共ם形成和氢化裂解等副反应[10.1021/acscatal.1c01916]。为提高性能,必须在催化活性(青睐无配体位点)与选择性(隔离的金属位点减少过度脱氢)之间取得平衡[10.1021/acscatal.5b01423]。某些催化剂在动力学上稳定位点,例如,分子筛防止烧结[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816],或者通过氧化途径改变热力学限制[10.1002/advs.202207756]。已经用表观活化能探究了反应动力学--沸石上PDH屏障在180-200 kJ/mol [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]。进一步的机理认识能阐明结构敏感步骤和战略性催化剂优化。
		※※※
		随着最近在热力学和动力学复杂性方面的进展,一种集成的方法,利用计算筛选和微观动力学建模,似乎前景光明。这可以系统地评估权衡,识别平衡活性和选择性的最佳位点,并实现理性的催化剂设计。在太阳能热化学途径中存在更广阔的可能性,可再生加热克服平衡限制。
