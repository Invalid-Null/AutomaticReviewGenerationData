Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and successfully commercialized PDH catalysts, employed in the UOP Oleflex process [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]. Experiments show supported Pt/Al2O3 catalysts can achieve relatively high initial activity but are prone to rapid deactivation. Adding promoters such as boron [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548] or applying ALD overcoats [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391] have been found to enhance Pt/Al2O3 stability and selectivity. Other studies demonstrate isolated Pt sites anchored to supports via Pt-O bonds give improved PDH performance compared to larger Pt clusters [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. Pt particle size effects are also reported, with a volcano-shaped dependence where Pt2 clusters on Al2O3(110) display the highest theoretical activity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671].

Fewer details are furnished on supported Pd or Ir catalysts. One brief mention states Pd and Ir are intrinsically active for C-H bond activation but expensive alternatives to Pt [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. A Rh-Cu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst showed significantly higher activity, lower light-off temperature, 100% propene selectivity and coking resistance compared to a standard Pt/Al2O3 catalyst [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]. This underscores the potential of SAAs as highly selective dehydrogenation catalysts.

In summary, Pt-based catalysts, especially Pt/Al2O3, have been most extensively studied for PDH. Key findings demonstrate promoting Pt, stabilizing isolated sites, and controlling particle size can optimize stability and selectivity. Further work exploring composition, promoters and metal-support interfaces provides opportunities to enhance commercial Pt catalysts. Highly selective SAAs also emerge as promising alternatives.

※※※

Integrative Understanding and Prospective Outlook: Taking into consideration the advancements and findings discussed in the file, there lies an opportunity to explore emerging fields and innovative methodologies for designing optimized PDH catalysts. Although Pt-based catalysts currently dominate, single atom alloy (SAA) systems have shown intriguing capability to circumvent issues like sintering and over-hydrogenation. By stabilizing solitary Rh, Ir or other transition metal sites on supports via strong metal-support bonding or embedment in protective matrices, SAAs could overcome limitations plaguing metal nanoparticles while maximizing C-H activation and olefin selectivity. 

Machine learning techniques moreover hold promise to rapidly screen combinations of metals, ligands and supports compared to traditional trial-and-error approaches. Such high-throughput computations built on accumulated data for existing catalysts may reveal synergistic compositions and architectures. Operando spectroscopies coupled with DFT will lend crucial atomic-level insights to guide this design process. Ultimately, progress at the metal-support interface is vital—the capability to synthesize and characterize isolated sites, tune electronic structure and control interactions between metals and supports paves the way for transformative advances in selective alkane dehydrogenation.

根据文件提供的详细信息,已经有几项研究探索了受支撑的单金属Pt、Pd和Ir催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

报道称,Pt基催化剂是应用于UOP Oleflex工艺中最广泛使用和商业化成功的PDH催化剂之一 [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。实验表明,受支撑的Pt/Al2O3催化剂可以达到相对较高的初始活性,但易于快速失活。添加促进剂如硼[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]或应用ALD涂层[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]被发现可以增强Pt/Al2O3的稳定性和选择性。其他研究表明,通过Pt-O键固定在支撑体上的隔离Pt位点比较大的Pt簇提高了PDH性能[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。 Pt颗粒大小效应也有报道,火山形依赖关系 where Pt2 簇在Al2O3(110)上显示出最高的理论活性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。


关于受支撑的Pd或Ir催化剂的详细信息比较少。一处简要提到Pd和Ir在C-H键激活上具有内在活性,但比Pt昂贵[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。 一种Rh-Cu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂与标准Pt/Al2O3催化剂相比,显示出显著更高的活性、更低的起动温度、100%丙烯选择性和抗焦炭性[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。这凸显了SAA作为高选择性脱氢催化剂的潜力。


总的来说,Pt基催化剂,尤其是Pt/Al2O3,是PDH中研究最广泛的。关键发现表明,促进Pt、稳定隔离位点和控制颗粒大小可以优化稳定性和选择性。 探索组成、促进剂和金属支持体界面提供了进一步增强商业Pt催化剂的机会。 高度选择性的SAA也作为有前景的替代催化剂出现。


※※※

综合理解与展望:考虑到文件中讨论的先进成果和发现,有机会探索新兴领域和创新方法来设计优化的PDH催化剂。 尽管目前以Pt基催化剂为主,但单原子合金(SAA)系统显示了克服烧结和过度氢化等问题的有趣能力。 通过强大的金属支撑体键合或嵌入保护矩阵来稳定Rh、Ir或其他过渡金属的单个位点,SAA可以克服限制金属纳米颗粒的局限同时最大化C-H活化和烯烃选择性。


此外,机器学习技术有望比传统的试错法更快速地筛选现有催化剂累积的数据的金属、配体和载体组合。这样的高通量计算可能会揭示协同组合和体系结构。 联合DFT的原位光谱技术将为这一设计过程提供关键的原子层次的洞察。 最终,界面处的进步至关重要——合成和表征隔离位点、调谐电子结构以及控制金属与载体之间的相互作用的能力为选择性烷烃脱氢的转型进步铺平了道路。
