The article with DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816ates tuning zeolite Si/Al ratio by dealumination to create silanol nests in Beta zeolites, which enhances metal dispersion and stabilization [Placeholder_of_DOI1]. 

Another study with DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c03941 evaluates propane dehydrogenation over CHA zeolites with varying Si/Al ratios. It shows that extra-framework Ga+ sites are formed regardless of Si/Al ratio between 5-25. While rates decrease over time, propylene selectivity remains stably around 96% when Ga/Al ratio is properly tuned [Placeholder_of_DOI2].

Furthermore, an investigation on Ga/H-ZSM-5 reveals a complex dependence of propane dehydrogenation rate on both Si/Al and Ga/Al ratios. Increasing Ga loading first increases then decreases the rate, with the optimal Ga/Al ratio depending on the Si/Al ratio. This indicates interpreting reactivity on a single Si/Al ratio sample would be incomplete [Placeholder_of_DOI3]. 

In addition, a report with DOI 10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024 studies modulating TS-1 crystal size from 100 nm to 5.7 μm. Smaller crystals introduce mesoporosity, enhancing diffusion and avoiding coking. This significantly improves catalytic conversion, selectivity and lifetime [Placeholder_of_DOI4].

Moreover, mesoporous MFI zeolite nanosheets are found to promote intermetallic alloy nanoparticle formation, boosting propane dehydrogenation performance [Placeholder_of_DOI5].

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The investigations reveal tuning zeolite properties like Si/Al ratio, crystal size and mesoporosity can significantly impact propane dehydrogenation performance. Strategies like dealumination, metal doping and crystal size control introduce critical defects, active sites and diffusion pathways. Future efforts may focus on combining multiple tuning strategies to optimize catalyst design. Operando spectroscopy could also elucidate dynamic structure-function relationships. Extending these principles to other reactions and zeolite topologies harbors exciting potential.

此外,对Ga/H-ZSM-5的研究表明,丙烷脱氢反应速率与Si/Al比和Ga/Al比都有复杂的依赖性。Ga负载量的增加会先增加然后降低反应速率,最佳Ga/Al比取决于Si/Al比。这表明仅根据单一Si/Al比的样本解释反应活性是不完整的[Placeholder_of_DOI3]。

此外,介孔MFI分子筛纳米片被发现可以促进金属间化合物纳米粒子的形成,提高丙烷脱氢性能[Placeholder_of_DOI5]。
