Metal Oxide Catalysts - What metal oxides like Cr2O3, Ga2O3 have shown promising propane dehydrogenation activity?

Among metal oxide catalysts studied for propane dehydrogenation, gallium oxide (Ga2O3) and chromium oxide (Cr2O3) have demonstrated promising catalytic activity [DOI1, DOI2]. 

Investigations reveal Ga2O3 exhibits higher propylene yield compared to Cr2O3 and other metal oxides like Fe2O3, Al2O3, SiO2 [DOI2]. A study comparing Ga2O3 polymorphs finds β-Ga2O3 displays maximum initial activity owing to its high surface acidity density, although deactivation issues remain [DOI1]. Adding Zr to Ga2O3 forms Ga2O3-ZrO2 mixed oxides with increased Lewis acid sites and maintained propane conversion [DOI2]. Supported Cr2O3 catalysts, particularly on SiO2, also achieve reasonable propylene selectivity, attributed to weaker metal-support interactions [DOI2]. Doped Cr2O3 systems like Pt-Cr2O3 demonstrate enhanced stability and conversion [DOI3].

Thus key factors imparting promising propane dehydrogenation functionality to Ga2O3 and Cr2O3 catalysts include high surface acidity, specific polymorphs in case of Ga2O3, weak substrate interactions for supported Cr2O3, and strategic dopants. However realizing extended catalyst lifetimes continues to pose an operational challenge. Overall, these emerging explorations underline the potential of rationally tailored Ga2O3 and Cr2O3 formulations as efficient and sustainable propane dehydrogenation catalysts.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: With metal oxides like suitably designed Ga2O3 and Cr2O3 presenting appreciable propane dehydrogenation activity rivaling conventional metallic catalysts, ongoing research might concentrate on elucidating degradation pathways and stabilizing these catalysts via compositional and architectural optimizations. Exploring synergistic combinations between Ga2O3, Cr2O3 and dopants like Pt also appears promising. Translating these advances to structured reactors that prevent overheating and coking could enable commercially relevant propane conversions. Broader horizons include expanding the library of selective and stable metal oxide catalysts through high-throughput theoretical and experimental screening.

金属氧化物催化剂 - 什么金属氧化物如Cr2O3、Ga2O3表现出有前景的丙烷脱氢活性?

在研究过的用于丙烷脱氢的金属氧化物催化剂中,氧化镓(Ga2O3)和氧化铬(Cr2O3)表现出了有前景的催化活性[DOI1,DOI2]。

研究表明,与Cr2O3和其他金属氧化物如Fe2O3、Al2O3、SiO2相比,Ga2O3表现出更高的丙烯产率[DOI2]。一项对Ga2O3多晶体的比较研究发现,β-Ga2O3由于其高的表面酸度密度而显示出最大的初始活性,尽管存在脱活问题[DOI1]。向Ga2O3中添加Zr形成Ga2O3-ZrO2混合氧化物增加了Lewis酸位,同时保持了丙烷转化率[DOI2]。受载的Cr2O3催化剂,特别是负载在SiO2上的,也实现了合理的丙烯选择性,这被归因于较弱的金属与载体间相互作用[DOI2]。掺杂的Cr2O3系统如Pt-Cr2O3表现出增强的稳定性和转化率[DOI3]。

因此,赋予Ga2O3和Cr2O3催化剂有前景的丙烷脱氢功能的关键因素包括高表面酸度、Ga2O3的特定多晶体、受载Cr2O3的弱基质交互作用以及战略掺杂剂。然而,实现延长的催化剂使用寿命仍然构成操作挑战。总的来说,这些新兴的探索彰显了经过理性设计的Ga2O3和Cr2O3配方作为高效和可持续的丙烷脱氢催化剂的潜力。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望: 随着像适当设计的Ga2O3和Cr2O3这样的金属氧化物呈现出可与常规金属催化剂媲美的丙烷脱氢活性,正在进行的研究可能会集中在阐明降解路径并通过组成和体系结构的优化来稳定这些催化剂。探索Ga2O3、Cr2O3与掺杂剂如Pt之间的协同效应也很有前景。将这些进步转化为结构反应器,防止过热和结焦,可以实现商业相关的丙烷转化率。更广阔的前景包括通过高通量的理论和实验筛选来扩大选择性好、稳定性高的金属氧化物催化剂库。
