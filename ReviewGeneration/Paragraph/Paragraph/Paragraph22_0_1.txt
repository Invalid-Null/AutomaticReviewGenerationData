Based on the in-depth details extracted from the file, some key techniques discussed for synthesizing isolated single sites in zeolitic supports include:

Dealumination to Generate Defect Sites
Dealuminating zeolites using methods like acid leaching creates vacant lattice sites and silanol nests that can stabilize isolated metal species. For example, dealuminated Beta zeolite (SiBeta) confines cobalt complexes within the silanol nests [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103].

Anchoring Metals Using Framework Heteroatoms 
Incorporating metals like titanium into the zeolite framework provides anchor sites to disperse and isolate impregnated metal clusters. This is seen with PtZn species stabilized by framework Ti in TS-1 zeolite [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816].

Ion Exchange Methods
Exchanging zeolite protons with soluble metal precursors allows incorporating isolated metal ions at the cation sites. An illustration is using zinc nitrate solution to ion exchange H-ZSM-5, forming isolated Zn2+ sites [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05898].

Hydrothermal Synthesis with Ligand Additives
Using chelating organic ligands like ethylenediamine to complex metals during zeolite crystallization hinders precipitation. This enables trapping isolated metal ions like Zn2+ within the zeolite pores [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034].

Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD)
CVD of molecular metal precursors can precisely introduce isolated metal species onto zeolite hydroxyl groups. For example, CVD of dimethylzinc incorporates [ZnCH3]+ motifs that are reduced to Zn2+ sites [DOI: 10.1021/cs200441e].

High Temperature Reduction
Subjecting metal-loaded zeolites to high temperature H2 reduction converts extraframework metal oxides to isolated cationic sites. This occurs for Ga2O3 particles in CHA zeolite reduced to Ga+ at 700°C [DOI: 10.1021/jacs.2c03941].

The methods above demonstrate some promising techniques discussed in the references to achieve isolated single metal sites dispersed on zeolitic supports.

基于文件提取的深入详细信息,讨论合成分离于分子筛载体上的单一活性位点的一些关键技术包括:

除铝生成缺陷位点
使用酸洗等除铝方法创造空位点和硅醇巢,这可以稳定隔离的金属物种。例如,在硅醇巢内限制住钴配合物的除铝β沸石(SiBeta)[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]。

使用框架杂原子锚定金属
将诸如钛之类的金属掺入分子筛框架中,为分散和隔离的金属簇提供锚定位点。这可以在TS-1分子筛中看到PtZn物种被框架Ti稳定[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。

离子交换法
用可溶性金属前体交换分子筛质子,使得隔离的金属离子掺入在阳离子位点中。一个例证是用硝酸锌溶液交换H-ZSM-5,形成被隔离的Zn2+位点[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05898]。

有配体添加剂的水热合成
在分子筛结晶过程中使用螯合有机配体如乙二胺络合金属,抑制沉淀。这使得像Zn2+之类的被隔离金属离子困于分子筛孔内[DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034]。

化学汽相沉积(CVD)
分子金属前体的CVD可以精确地将被隔离的金属物种引入分子筛上的羟基基团。例如,二甲基锌的CVD掺入可以还原成Zn2+位点的[ZnCH3]+基团[DOI: 10.1021/cs200441e]。

高温氢气还原
使金属负载型分子筛经受高温H2还原,将过量的金属氧化物转化为被隔离的阳离子位点。这发生在CHA分子筛中的Ga2O3颗粒,在700°C时还原成Ga+ [DOI: 10.1021/jacs.2c03941]。

上述方法展示了参考文献中讨论的一些很有前景的技术,以实现分子筛载体上被分散的单一金属位点。
