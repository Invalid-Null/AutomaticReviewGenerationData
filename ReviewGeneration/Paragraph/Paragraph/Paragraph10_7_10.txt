Based on the details extracted from the references, proposed mechanistic scenarios for propane dehydrogenation over different metal oxide catalysts include:

On CrOx/ZrO2, two neighboring coordinatively unsaturated Zr (Zrcus) sites located at an oxygen vacancy are responsible for breaking C-H bonds in propane homolytically to form propylene [DOI1]. For Ga-doped ZrO2, the addition of Ga changes the pathway from stepwise over ZrO2 to concerted over GaZrOx via simultaneous cleavage of two C-H bonds, lowering the barrier [DOI2]. Isolated tetrahedral Ga3+ sites in Ga2O3 catalysts are also described as active for dehydrogenation [DOI3].  

VOx/support catalysts are proposed to follow a Mars-van Krevelen mechanism, with lattice oxygen abstracting H from propane, leaving behind reduced V centers that regenerate by reacting with O2 [DOI4, DOI5]. At lower lattice oxygen levels, V3+ sites catalyze subsequent non-oxidative pathways [DOI6]. Isolated Zn2+ on ZrO2 and synergies between Zn, Zr, and TiO2 are also suggested in Zn/ZrO2/TiO2 [DOI7].

For CrOx/Al2O3 and CrOx/SiO2, generated Cr3+ sites, especially those with coordination number >4, are responsible for propane activation [DOI8]. On MoVO mixed oxides, lattice oxygen enables oxidative dehydrogenation to propylene, while depleted lattice oxygen leaves V3+ sites that catalyze non-oxidative routes [DOI9].※※※

With various mechanistic scenarios proposed spanning different active sites like isolated cations, cation-oxygen pairs, and synergies between components, future work can further consolidate understanding into generalized descriptors. More operando studies can also shed light on evolving reaction networks. Broader incorporation of computational screening and predictive modeling can guide discovery of multifunctional systems.

根据从参考文献中提取的细节,在不同金属氧化物催化剂上进行丙烷脱氢所拟议的机理场景包括:

在CrOx/ZrO2上,两个位于氧空位的键合不饱和Zr (Zrcus)位点负责同质裂解丙烷中的C-H键以形成丙烯[DOI1]。 对于Ga掺杂的ZrO2,Ga的添加使机理从ZrO2上的逐步转变为GaZrOx上的协同转变,通过同时断裂两个C-H键来降低屏障[DOI2]。 Ga2O3催化剂中的四面体Ga3+孤立位点也被描述为用于脱氢活性的[DOI3]。

建议VOx/支撑催化剂遵循Mars-van Krevelen机制,其中晶格氧摘除H从丙烷中脱离,留下被还原的V中心,通过与O2反应进行再生[DOI4,DOI5]。 在较低的晶格氧水平下,V3+位点催化后续的非氧化路径[DOI6]。 Zn/ZrO2/TiO2中的ZrO2上被隔离的Zn2+以及Zn、Zr和TiO2之间的协同作用也被建议[DOI7]。

对于CrOx/Al2O3和CrOx/SiO2,生成的Cr3+位点尤其是那些协调数大于4的位点负责丙烷的激活[DOI8]。 在MoVO混合氧化物上,晶格氧使丙烷发生氧化脱氢生成丙烯,而耗尽的晶格氧则留下V3+位点,催化非氧化途径[DOI9]。※※※

随着在不同的活性位点(如被隔离的阳离子、阳离子-氧键合对以及组分之间的协同作用)上拟议出各种机理场景,未来的工作可以进一步整合理解到广义描述子。 更多的反应过程研究也可以阐明演变的反应网络。 对计算筛选和预测建模的更广泛结合可以指导发现多功能体系。
