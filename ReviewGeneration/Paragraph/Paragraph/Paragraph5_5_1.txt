Based on the detailed analysis from the references, DFT calculations have been applied in several studies to gain insights into propane dehydrogenation mechanisms and pathways over different catalysts.

For example, DFT results revealed that Zn-O-Zn sites in one catalyst had lower apparent activation barriers (1.81 eV) compared to single Zn2+ sites (2.14 eV), explaining the better catalytic performance [DOI1]. Calculations also showed how the d-band center of platinum correlates with propylene selectivity [DOI2]. 

More broadly, DFT energies and structures mapped out potential energy surfaces, while microkinetic modeling helped relate computational insights to macroscopic catalytic behavior. This led to structure-activity-selectivity relationships, as well as discovery of scaling relations between key parameters like binding energies [DOI3].

Specific implementations have utilized plane-wave DFT functionals to screen many transition metal catalysts, while others employed programs like VASP to optimize geometries and calculate barriers associated with critical reaction intermediates [DOI6, DOI7].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The rapid expansion of computational catalysis techniques provides exciting opportunities to accelerate discovery of next-generation propane dehydrogenation catalysts. Rather than trial-and-error experimental approaches, computational screening, scaling relations and microkinetic models can systematically identify promising materials. Continued cross-pollination between theory and experiment will likely uncover additional fundamental insights that enhance performance. Emerging areas like machine learning and in situ characterization may also contribute advanced perspectives. Ultimately, these innovations might culminate in the rational design of optimized catalysts boasting industrially-compatible conversion, yield and stability under mild, sustainable conditions.

根据参考文献的详细分析,DFT计算已在几项研究中应用于获得丙烷脱氢机制和途径的见解。

例如,DFT结果显示,一种催化剂中的Zn-O-Zn位点比单Zn2+位点具有更低的表观活化能垒(1.81 eV对2.14 eV),这解释了更好的催化性能[DOI1]。计算还显示铂的d带中心与丙烯选择性的相关性[DOI2]。

更广泛地说,DFT能量和结构映射了可能的能量面,而微动力学建模有助于将计算洞察与宏观催化行为联系起来。这导致了结构-活性-选择性关系以及关键参数(如结合能)之间缩放关系的发现[DOI3]。

具体实现采用平面波DFT泛函对许多过渡金属催化剂进行了筛选,而其他一些采用像VASP这样的程序来优化几何形状和计算与关键反应中间体相关的屏障[DOI6,DOI7]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 计算催化技术的快速发展为加速发现下一代丙烷脱氢催化剂提供了令人兴奋的机会。计算筛选,缩放关系和微动力学模型可以系统地识别出有前景的材料,而不是试错的实验方法。理论和实验之间的持续交叉授粉可能会发现额外的基本见解,以增强性能。新兴领域如机器学习和原位表征也可能贡献先进的视角。 最终,这些创新可能会导致在温和的可持续条件下,具有工业兼容转换率,产率和稳定性的优化催化剂的理性设计。
