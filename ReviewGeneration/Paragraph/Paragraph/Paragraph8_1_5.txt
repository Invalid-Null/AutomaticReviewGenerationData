 <English>
Metal oxide catalysts, especially chromium, gallium, and tin oxides have been explored as promising options for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) due to their potential for high activity, selectivity, and stability. Some key findings on metal oxide catalysts for PDH are:

- Chromium oxide (Cr2O3) catalysts, especially supported Cr2O3 and CrOx, have shown promise for PDH. Cr-based catalysts are among the most active for this reaction, and CrOx is a component in the industrial CATOFIN PDH process [1.1]. Cr2O3/SiO2, Cr2O3/Al2O3, and α-Cr2O3 have demonstrated PDH activity [1.2]. Doping Cr2O3 with Pt can further improve performance [1.3]. Modeling studies have provided insights into PDH mechanisms on Cr2O3 [1.4]. However,Cr2O3 catalysts still suffer from poor stability issues [1.5].  

- Gallium oxide (Ga2O3), especially the β-Ga2O3 polymorph, has been identified as a promising PDH catalyst. Among metal oxides, Ga2O3 exhibits the highest propylene yield, attributed to appropriate surface acidity [2.1]. Supported β-Ga2O3/SiO2 and γ-Ga2O3/SiO2 also show good PDH activity [2.2]. The initial PDH activity correlates with surface acid site density, highest on β-Ga2O3 [2.3].

- Tin-modified platinum (PtSn) catalysts have been commercialized for PDH, utilizing intermetallic PtSn nanoparticles. The PtSn alloy improves propylene selectivity and stability compared to Pt catalysts [3.1]. Supported PtSn/Al2O3, PtSn/SBA-15 have shown high propane conversion (~40%) and propylene selectivity (>90%) [3.2, 3.3].

- Weak metal-support interactions can improve propylene selectivity for metal oxides like Cr2O3/SiO2 [4]. Mixed oxides like Ga2O3-ZrO2 with high Lewis acidity also show promise [4].

- Despite progress, there are still opportunities to further improve stability and optimize synthesis methods. More mechanistic studies could provide insights to guide the design of superior metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation [1.3, 1.4, 2.3, 4].

金属氧化物催化剂,尤其是铬、镓和锡氧化物,由于其具有高活性、选择性和稳定性的潜力,已被探索为丙烷脱氢(PDH)的有前景的选择。关于PDH金属氧化物催化剂的一些关键发现是:

- 铬氧化物(Cr2O3)催化剂,尤其是支持的Cr2O3和CrOx,已显示出PDH的前景。Cr基催化剂是这种反应最活性催化剂之一,CrOx是工业CATOFIN PDH工艺中的组分[1.1]。Cr2O3/SiO2、Cr2O3/Al2O3和α-Cr2O3表现出PDH活性[1.2]。用Pt掺杂Cr2O3可以进一步提高性能[1.3]。建模研究提供了Cr2O3上PDH机理的见解[1.4]。然而,Cr2O3催化剂稳定性仍然差[1.5]。

- 氧化镓(Ga2O3),特别是β-Ga2O3多形,已被确定为一种有前途的PDH催化剂。在金属氧化物中,Ga2O3显示出最高的丙烯产率,这归因于其适当的表面酸度[2.1]。支持的β-Ga2O3/SiO2和γ-Ga2O3/SiO2也显示出良好的PDH活性[2.2]。PDH初始活性与表面酸位密度相关,β-Ga2O3上最高[2.3]。

- 锡改性铂(PtSn)催化剂已被商业化用于PDH,利用间金属PtSn纳米粒子。PtSn合金相比Pt催化剂提高了丙烯选择性和稳定性[3.1]。支持的PtSn/Al2O3、PtSn/SBA-15显示了高的丙烷转化率(~40%)和丙烯选择性(>90%)[3.2,3.3]。

- 弱的金属支撑相互作用可以提高诸如Cr2O3/SiO2之类的金属氧化物的丙烯选择性[4]。如Ga2O3-ZrO2等混合氧化物具有高路易斯酸度也显示出前景[4]。

- 尽管取得了进展,但仍有改进稳定性和优化合成方法的机会。更多的机理研究可以提供见解来指导设计优异的丙烷脱氢金属氧化物催化剂[1.3,1.4,2.3,4]。

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: 
Based on the promising results reported for metal oxide catalysts like Cr2O3, Ga2O3, and PtSn in propane dehydrogenation, there are opportunities to further optimize these materials and gain fundamental insights to guide the design of superior catalysts. Key future research directions may involve:

- Controlling synthesis methods to optimize metal oxide phase, morphology, porosity, and metal-support interactions to maximize selectivity, activity and stability.

- In-depth characterization studies to reveal the nature of active sites and crystallite surfaces that promote desired C-H bond activation while suppressing undesired cracking reactions.

- Mechanistic investigations using spectroscopic methods and computational modeling to reveal PDH reaction networks and rate-determining steps over metal oxide surfaces.

- Design of bifunctional metal oxide catalysts combining redox and acid/base sites to improve performance through synergy between different sites.

- Exploring novel supports and metal oxide compositions to discover new highly selective and stable PDH catalysts beyond existing options.

- Techno-economic analyses guiding scale up and commercial deployment of the most promising metal oxide PDH catalyst formulations.

Overall, progress has been made on metal oxide catalysts for PDH, but further optimizations rooted in mechanistic understanding could enable transformational enhancements in catalyst efficiency, unlocking the full potential of these materials.
