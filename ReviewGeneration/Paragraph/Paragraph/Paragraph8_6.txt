		Based on the details extracted from the references, some promising metal oxide catalysts evaluated for propane dehydrogenation include chromium oxide (Cr2O3), tin-modified platinum (PtSn), and gallium oxide (Ga2O3) polymorphs [10.1021/acscatal.2c05286, 10.1021/acscatal.1c02621, 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Cr-based catalysts demonstrate high activity for the CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation reaction, with CrOx being a key component in industrial CATOFIN processes [10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]. Supported PtSn catalysts are also commercially relevant, achieving up to 40% propane conversion and 92% propylene selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]. Among Ga2O3 polymorphs, β-Ga2O3 exhibits the highest initial activity for propane dehydrogenation due to abundant medium-strength acid sites [10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]. Composite metal oxides like Cr2O3/SiO2 and Ga2O3-ZrO2 have additionally shown promising performance [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999].
		※※※
		Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The insights gained on tuned Cr, PtSn, and Ga oxide catalysts highlight the promise of selectively tailoring multifunctional sites and metal-support interfaces to further elevate conversion, yield, and stability. An interdisciplinary approach harnessing advanced characterization and computational modeling may uncover optimal configurations. Translating the fundamental principles to design optimized nanoscale architectures and reaction environments could catalyze breakthroughs. Broader horizons yet unexplored include bifunctional catalysts coupling dehydrogenation with value-added chemical transformations in cascade or hybrid processes.

		根据参考文献中提取的详细信息, 一些被评估为丙烷脱氢反应有前景的金属氧化物催化剂包括: 铬氧化物(Cr2O3)、锡修饰的铂(PtSn)和氧化镓(Ga2O3)多形[10.1021/acscatal.2c05286, 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639, 10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]。 Cr基催化剂表现出高的CO2辅助氧化脱氢活性, CrOx是工业CATOFIN过程中的关键组分[10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]。 支持的PtSn催化剂也具有商业相关性, 可达到40%的丙烷转化率和92%的丙烯选择性[10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]。 在Ga2O3多形中, β-Ga2O3由于大量中等强度的酸中心而表现出最高的丙烷脱氢初始活性[10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]。 Cr2O3/SiO2和Ga2O3-ZrO2等复合金属氧化物也表现出有前景的性能[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。

		综合理解与未来展望: 对调控的Cr、PtSn和氧化镓催化剂性能的理解凸显了精确调控多功能位点和金属支撑体界面以进一步提高转化率、收率和稳定性的前景。 采用先进的表征和计算建模的跨学科方法可能会发现最优配置。 将这些基本原理转化为设计优化的纳米尺度体系结构和反应环境, 可能会催生突破。 更广阔的未被开发的前景包括双功能催化剂, 它可以串联脱氢反应与增值化学转化, 或者在串联反应或混合反应中进行耦合。
