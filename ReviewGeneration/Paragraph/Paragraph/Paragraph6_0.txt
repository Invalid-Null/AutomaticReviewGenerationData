	<English>
		Microkinetic modeling efforts have elucidated the propane dehydrogenation kinetics and practical applicability of various catalysts, utilizing parameters from DFT calculations \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00939, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00889\]. The models simulate rate equations and surface coverages to determine turnover frequencies, selectivities, and rate-controlling steps [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939]. Descriptors based on formation energies have also been employed to screen and rank the predicted catalytic activity [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]. On Pt alloy surfaces, propane’s first dehydrogenation barrier was evaluated as a proxy for activity, while propylene’s desorption and dehydrogenation barriers served as a selectivity descriptor [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916].
		※※※
		With the advent of high-throughput computational screening and microkinetic models grounded in electronic structure calculations, there exist bountiful opportunities to explore novel compositions, facet-engineered surfaces, and single-atom catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. Elucidating reaction mechanisms and kinetic parameters on such materials can unravel performance-structure correlations to guide optimal catalyst design. Beyond homogeneous catalysts, future efforts might also examine the coupled kinetics between metal active sites and acidic supports in bifunctional systems.

	<Chinese>
		微动力学建模工作利用DFT计算的参数阐明了各种催化剂的丙烷脱氢动力学和实际适用性 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]。这些模型模拟速度方程和表面覆盖率以确定周转频率、选择性和速控步骤 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939]。还使用基于形成能的描述符来筛选和评估预测的催化活性 [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]。在铂合金表面上,丙烷的首次脱氢垒被评估为活性的代用指标,而丙烯的解吸和脱氢垒用作选择性描述符 [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916]。
		※※※
		随着高通量计算筛选和基于电子结构计算的微动力学模型的出现,存在大量的机会来探索新的组成、切面工程表面和单原子催化剂用于丙烷脱氢反应。阐明这些材料上的反应机理和动力学参数可以揭示性能-结构相关性,以指导优化催化剂设计。除同相催化剂外,未来的工作还可能检查双功能体系中金属活性位点和酸性载体之间的耦合动力学。
