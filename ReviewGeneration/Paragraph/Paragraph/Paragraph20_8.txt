 <English>
	Propane dehydrogenation catalysts utilizing zeolite supports have shown promise due to the high surface area, ordered micropores and adjustable acidity of zeolites. Three of the most studied zeolite topologies for propane dehydrogenation are MFI, BEA and FER.

MFI zeolites, with a framework composition of ZSM-5 or silicalite-1, have been extensively explored as supports to encapsulate sub-nanometric metal clusters for propane dehydrogenation. A study by [10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7] revealed MFI confines 0.5-0.6 nm PtSn clusters within its sinusoidal 10R channels, governing their structure-activity profile and achieving 45.3% propane conversion with &gt;99% propylene selectivity at 550°C. The MFI pore structure facilitates formation of highly dispersed PtZn nanoparticles as seen in [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808], imparting outstanding stability. Incorporation of Fe and ZnO dopants into MFI has also been effective, generating isolated Pt anchoring sites to prevent agglomeration [10.1021/acscatal.2c05042]. The evidence demonstrates MFI is a versatile platform to modulate active site isolation and dispersion.

BEA, with a three-dimensional porous network, facilitates access to metal sites. [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816] showed a Pt/Zn-Beta catalyst operated at 30% propane conversion and 90% propylene selectivity close to equilibrium levels. The confinement effect of BEA stabilized PtSn clusters [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527], achieving high activity and negligible deactivation over 100 hours. These studies confirm BEA topology provides an optimal balance of confinement versus accessibility.

Additionally, CHA [10.1021/jacs.2c03941], FAU [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527] and SUZ-4 [10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62750-5] zeolites have demonstrated potential, ascribed to the isolated active sites hosted in their frameworks.

In summary, zeolite-encapsulated propane dehydrogenation catalysts with isolated metal sites anchored within MFI, BEA and other topological frameworks have shown enhanced catalytic performance. The zeolitic architecture governs critical facets of activity, selectivity and stability. Further explorations optimizing zeolite properties like acidity, dopant identity and morphology are worthwhile to fully realize their potential.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The insights divulged in the references highlight zeolites as emerging supports to activate propane while mitigating catalyst deactivation. Although substantial advancements have been attained, actualizing commercial viability necessitates surmounting incremental hurdles regarding long-term stability under industrial conditions. Attaining a detailed mechanistic comprehension of deactivation pathways would unlock targeted strategies to further enhance robustness. An interdisciplinary effort coupling advanced characterization techniques with sophisticated simulations and kinetic modeling is imperative. Furthermore, certain recent works have ventured into crafting hierarchical zeolites boasting improved mass transfer. Expanding these Proof-of-Concept endeavors through rational material design could pave the way for exceptionally durable and kinetically facile catalysts on the brink of commercial adoption.

		采用分子筛作为支撑的丙烷脱氢反应催化剂由于分子筛的高比表面积、有序的微孔结构和可调节的酸性而显示出良好的应用前景。最常用于丙烷脱氢反应的三种分子筛拓扑结构是 MFI、BEA 和 FER。
		
		组成为 ZSM-5 或硅沸石的 MFI 型分子筛广泛应用于锚定亚纳米金属簇用于丙烷脱氢反应。[10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7]的研究揭示 MFI 的正弦波 10R 通道限制 0.5-0.6 nm PtSn 簇的结构和性能,在 550°C 下实现 45.3% 的丙烷转化率和&gt;99% 的丙烯选择性。MFI 的孔道结构有助于形成高度分散的 PtZn 纳米颗粒,如[10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]所示,实现卓越的稳定性。 MFI 中掺杂 Fe 和 ZnO 元素也发挥了良好作用,产生分离的 Pt 锚定位点以防止团聚[10.1021/acscatal.2c05042]。这些证据表明,MFI 是调控活性位点的隔离和分散的多功能平台。
		
		BEA 的三维多孔网络结构使金属位点的通透性得到改善。 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]展示 Pt/Zn-Beta 催化剂的丙烷转化率为30%,丙烯选择性接近热力学平衡的90%。BEA 的限域作用稳定了 PtSn 簇[10.1021/acscatal.9b03527],实现了高活性和100小时内可忽略的失活。这些研究确认 BEA 拓扑结构在限域作用和可通透性之间提供了最佳平衡。
		
		此外,CHA [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]、FAU [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527] 和 SUZ-4 [10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62750-5] 分子筛也表现出潜在前景,归因于其框架内部的隔离活性位点。
		
		总的来说,采用封装金属隔离活性位点的分子筛用于丙烷脱氢催化剂可以获得增强的催化性能。分子筛结构控制活性、选择性和稳定性的关键方面。进一步优化分子筛酸度、掺杂剂和形态等性质值得深入研究,以充分利用其潜力。

		综合理解和未来展望:引用文献的信息突出了分子筛作为新兴支撑剂的作用,可激活丙烷同时减少催化剂的失活。尽管在这方面已经取得很大进展,但要实现商业应用还需要克服稳定性方面的障碍。对失活机理形成详细的理解将有助于采取有针对性的策略来进一步提高催化剂的耐用性。结合先进的表征技术、精细的模拟计算和动力学建模对失活机理形成深入的理解至关重要。此外,一些最新的工作开始探索分层分子筛以改善物质转移。通过合理的材料设计将这些概念验证的工作拓展至更高层次,可望实现异常耐用和动力学性能出色的商业化催化剂。
