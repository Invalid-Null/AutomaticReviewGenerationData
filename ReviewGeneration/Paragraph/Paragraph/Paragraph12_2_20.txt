Based on the details provided, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]. For example, Pt/Al2O3 maintains high initial activity for PDH, although it deactivates over multiple cycles due to metal agglomeration accelerated by sulfur [DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]. Adding promoters such as boron [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548] and metal oxide overcoats [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391] are shown to enhance Pt/Al2O3 stability and selectivity. Optimizing the dispersion of Pt clusters has also improved performance, with isolated Pt atoms [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631] and Pt2 clusters [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671] demonstrating high intrinsic activity. However, Pt deactivates readily due to coking, as revealed by TPO studies of spent catalysts [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361].

Besides Pt, a few studies have explored Pd- and Ir-based PDH catalysts. Pd, Rh, and Ir are noted to exhibit high intrinsic rates for C-H bond activation, but practical use is limited by cost considerations [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. Supported RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalysts, interestingly, outperformed Pt/Al2O3 benchmarks, demonstrating far higher productivity and 100% propylene selectivity [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]. Further studies are warranted to optimize these systems.  

In summary, Pt-based catalysts constitute the primary industrial PDH catalysts reported thus far, although opportunities exist to enhance stability via promoters and metal-support interactions. Emerging Pd, Rh, and Ir systems also display encouraging intrinsic kinetics. Continuing R&amp;D into tuneable single atom alloy and cluster catalysts may unlock additional gains.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering significant strides in characterization and computational techniques [e.g. DOI: 10.1038/s41467-018-07502-5, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671], opportunities abound to achieve greater atomic-level precision in designing selective and stable supported metal PDH catalysts. For instance, recent findings on isolated sites [DOI: 10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9] and geometric motifs [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631] provide invaluable guides to target specific configurations predicted to minimize coking and over-hydrogenation. Additionally, further probing metal-support interplays [DOI: 10.1002/anie.202005968] through advanced interface-sensitive tools promises to inform rational improvements via tailoring strong anchoring environments. Such fundamental mechanistic insights, coupled with high-throughput and operando experimentation, may galvanize the next wave of innovations to meet propene supply needs efficiently and sustainably.

根据所提供的详细信息,已有几项研究探索了丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应中受支撑的单金属Pt、Pd和Ir催化剂。

文献报道,Pt基催化剂是最广泛使用和商业上最成功的PDH催化剂之一[DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]。例如,Pt/Al2O3表现出高初始PDH活性,尽管它在多周期反应中会因金属凝聚的加速而失活[DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]。加入助剂如硼[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]和金属氧化物顶层涂料[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]已被证明可以增强Pt/Al2O3的稳定性和选择性。优化Pt簇的分散状态也改进了催化性能,其中隔离的Pt原子[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]和Pt2簇[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]表现出高的固有活性。然而,Pt很容易因共烷化而失活,这在TPO研究中得到证实 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]。

除Pt外,也有少数研究探索了Pd和Ir基PDH催化剂。文献指出Pd、Rh和Ir表现出高的C-H键断裂的固有反应速率,但实际应用受成本考量的限制[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。有趣的是,RhCu单原子合金(SAA)受支撑催化剂的表现远优于Pt/Al2O3基准,示出更高的生产力和100%丙烯选择性[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。有必要进一步优化这些系统。

总的来说,迄今为止报道的主要工业PDH催化剂是Pt基催化剂,尽管通过助剂和金属-载体相互作用进行稳定性增强仍有机会。新兴的Pd、Rh和Ir系统也显示出令人鼓舞的固有反应动力学。继续研发可调节的单原子合金和簇催化剂可能会带来额外的收益。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到显著的表征和计算技术进步[如 DOI: 10.1038/s41467-018-07502-5、DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671],我们有大量机会在设计选择性高和稳定的受支撑金属PDH催化剂方面实现更高的原子水平精确度。例如,关于隔离活性位点[DOI: 10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9]和几何结构模式[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]的最新发现为锁定特定配置提供了宝贵指导,这些配置被预测可以最大限度地减少共烷化和过度氢化。 此外,通过先进的界面敏感工具深入研究金属-载体相互作用[DOI: 10.1002/anie.202005968]有望提供合理改进途径,即通过调控强韧的锚定环境。这种基础机制见解再结合高通量和原位实验,可能催生下一波创新,以高效和可持续的方式满足丙烯供应需求。
