Based on the insights extracted from the file, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts have been extensively studied using X-ray based techniques to probe changes in metal oxidation states and coordination environments during various catalyst treatment steps as well as under reaction conditions. 

X-ray absorption spectroscopy (XAS), including XANES and EXAFS, has been widely utilized to track the changes in oxidation state and coordination geometry of active species like Cr, V, Ga and Zn during reduction or oxidation pretreatments. For example, in situ XANES revealed changes in chromium speciation from isolated Cr(VI) sites to reduced Cr(III) species during initial PDH reaction stages over Cr-MCM-41 catalysts [10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014]. Another study showed partial reduction of framework Zn(II) centers in Zn/H-ZSM-5 zeolites during high temperature H2 treatment, associated with decreasing Zn-O coordination [10.1038/s41586-021-03923-3].

In addition, X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS) has offered valuable insights into catalyst compositional changes and evolution of specific metal oxidation states under different environmental exposures. Quantitative analysis of XPS spectral components made it possible to calculate relative abundances of various metal valence states like Cr(III)/Cr(VI) in Cr/Al2O3 [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.01.005], V(IV)/V(V) in V/Si-Beta [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63444-3], or Sn(II)/Sn(IV) in PtSn/Al2O3 [10.1016/j.cej.2014.09.107] catalysts during treatment in H2, oxidizing, or reaction atmospheres.


Notably, continuous advancements in in-situ/operando XAS and XPS techniques under near-ambient pressure conditions now permit tracking metal oxidation state changes during actual propane dehydrogenation reactions. For instance, Pt L-III edge XANES uncovered periodic oxidation-reduction cycling of Sn as it segregates from Pt nanoparticles during coke burn-off and subsequent H2 regeneration over PtSn/Al2O3 catalysts [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018]. Another operando NAP-XPS study on Zn/H-ZSM-5 catalysts directly confirmed partial reduction of exchanged Zn(II) sites to active Znδ+ species under dehydrogenation temperatures [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The wealth of information revealed by X-ray based probes highlights their power in unraveling catalyst compositional and electronic structure changes governing activity, selectivity and stability during propane dehydrogenation. Building on these advancements, continued application of operando XAS, XPS and emerging techniques like HAXPES could further our mechanistic comprehension by connecting transformation phenomena to actual catalytic performance attributes. 

Moreover, correlative microscopy examinations coupling X-ray nano-probes with spatial imaging modalities can map oxidation state gradients over individual catalytic particles with nanometer resolution. These multi-parametric techniques blending electronic sensitivities with real-space imaging may illuminate degradation and restructuring processes previously hidden from view. Future research harnessing such correlative inner workings could inform rational design paradigms aimed at tuning and optimizing catalytic functions by targeting specific redox transitions associated with PDH activation and deactivation pathways.

基于从文件中提取的信息,丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂已经广泛地使用X射线技术来探测金属氧化态和配位环境在各种催化剂处理步骤以及反应条件下的变化。

X射线吸收光谱(XAS)技术,包括XANES和EXAFS,已经被广泛应用于跟踪Cr、V、Ga和Zn等活性物种在还原或氧化预处理过程中的氧化态和配位几何的变化。例如,原位XANES发现Cr-MCM-41催化剂上的Cr从被隔离的Cr(VI)物种在PDH初期反应阶段转变为还原态的Cr(III)物种[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014]。另一项研究表明,Zn/H-ZSM-5沸石载体框架结构中的Zn(II)中心在高温H2处理过程中发生了部分还原,与Zn-O配位数的减少有关[10.1038/s41586-021-03923-3]。  

此外,X射线光电子能谱(XPS)提供了催化剂组成变化和不同环境条件下金属氧化态演变的有价值信息。 XPS谱峰的定量分析使计算Cr(III)/ Cr(VI)在Cr/Al2O3 [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.01.005]、V(IV)/V(V)在V/Si-Beta [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63444-3]或PtSn/Al2O3 [10.1016/j.cej.2014.09.107]催化剂在H2、氧化或反应气氛中的相对丰度成为可能。


值得注意的是,原位/在线XAS和XPS技术在近大气压条件下的不断进步现在允许在实际丙烷脱氢反应过程中跟踪金属氧化态的变化。例如,Pt L-III边缘的XANES发现Sn在PtSn/Al2O3催化剂的焦炭燃烧和随后的H2再生期间发生的周期性氧化-还原循环[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018]。另一项在线NAP-XPS研究直接确认了反应温度下载体上交换的Zn(II)位点可部分还原为活性Znδ+物种 [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034]。


※※※

综合理解与未来展望: X射线技术揭示的大量信息突显了它们在阐明催化剂组成和电子结构变化方面的强大作用,这些变化支配着丙烷脱氢过程中的活性、选择性和稳定性。 建立在这些进展的基础上,原位XAS、XPS和新兴技术如HAXPES的继续应用可以通过连接转化现象与实际催化性能之间的联系来进一步加深我们的机理认识。


此外,相关显微技术可以在几个纳米的分辨率下映射催化剂粒子表面的氧化态梯度。这些多参数技术融合了电子敏感性和真实空间成像,可以揭示以前隐藏在视野之外的退化和重组过程。 未来利用这种相关的内在工作机制的研究可以指导设计理性的范式,目的是通过目标丙烷脱氢活化和失活途径相关的特定氧化-还原转变来优化催化功能。
