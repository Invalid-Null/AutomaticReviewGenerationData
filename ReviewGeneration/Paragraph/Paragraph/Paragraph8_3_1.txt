Metal Oxide Catalysts - What metal oxides like Cr2O3, Ga2O3 have shown promising propane dehydrogenation activity?

Propane dehydrogenation is an important industrial process for producing propylene, a key building block for polymers and chemicals. Metal oxides are promising catalysts for this reaction due to their high activity, moderate cost, and regenerability. Recent studies have explored various metal oxides for propane dehydrogenation, providing insights into their activity and mechanistic functioning.

Chromium oxide (Cr2O3) is one of the most extensively studied metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. CrOx/Al2O3 and CrOx/SiO2 catalysts have demonstrated catalytic activity at 723-823 K [DOI1]. Doping Cr2O3 with Pt has been found to improve catalytic performance [DOI2]. The reduced α-Cr2O3(0001) surface shows activity in kinetic Monte Carlo and microkinetic modeling studies of propane and butane dehydrogenation [DOI3]. Overall, Cr-based catalysts are among the most active for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane [DOI4]. However, Cr2O3 catalysts still suffer from relatively low selectivity and poor stability.

Gallium oxide (Ga2O3), especially in the beta polymorph, has emerged as a very promising metal oxide catalyst. Among different metal oxides, Ga2O3 exhibits the highest propylene yield, followed by Fe2O3 and Cr2O3 [DOI5]. The catalytic performance correlates strongly with surface acidity, with β-Ga2O3 having the highest initial activity [DOI6]. Supported β-Ga2O3/SiO2 also shows good propane conversion and propylene selectivity [DOI7]. The dehydrogenation activity is attributed to Ga2O3's abundance of medium-strength acid sites. Overall, Ga2O3 demonstrates superior propane dehydrogenation ability compared to Cr2O3. 

Other metal oxides like Pt-Sn, V2O5, and ZrO2 have also been studied for propane dehydrogenation, often as supports or promoters with metals like Pt. Pt-Sn catalysts form intermetallic alloys that improve selectivity and coke resistance [DOI8]. While metal oxides have shown promising activity, bimetallic Pt catalysts currently exhibit the best propane dehydrogenation performance. Further work is needed to optimize metal oxide catalysts through composition, morphology, and support effects.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The reviewed studies highlight the potential of metal oxides, especially Cr2O3 and Ga2O3, as viable alternatives to conventional propane dehydrogenation catalysts. Although not surpassing noble metals yet, tailored metal oxides present an opportunity to achieve good catalytic performance for affordable propylene production. Advances in nanoscale characterization and computational modeling will likely accelerate future innovation. An integrative approach combining theory and experiments to probe structure-activity relationships could unravel active sites and mechanistic pathways. Novel synthesis techniques may also yield enhanced metal oxide catalysts. There is much scope for research on tuning metal oxide catalysts via multimetallic compositions, solid solutions, and interface engineering with supports. Overall, the field still has rich scope for innovation to realize highly selective, stable and low-cost metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation.

金属氧化物催化剂 - Cr2O3、Ga2O3等金属氧化物催化剂在丙烷脱氢反应中的应用前景

丙烷脱氢是产生丙烯的重要工业过程,丙烯是重要的聚合物和化学品基础原料。由于金属氧化物催化剂具有高活性、适中的成本和可再生性,使其成为丙烷脱氢反应非常有前景的催化剂。最近的研究探索了各种金属氧化物在丙烷脱氢反应中的应用,为理解它们的活性和作用机理提供了见解。

铬氧化物(Cr2O3)是丙烷脱氢反应研究最广泛的金属氧化物催化剂之一。CrOx/Al2O3 和 CrOx/SiO2 催化剂在723-823 K条件下展现出催化活性[DOI1]。掺杂Pt能改善Cr2O3的催化性能[DOI2]。减量α-Cr2O3(0001)表面在丙烷和丁烷脱氢反应的动力学Monte Carlo和微动力学建模研究中表现出活性[DOI3]。总体而言,Cr基催化剂是CO2辅助氧化脱氢丙烷反应中最活性的[DOI4]。但是,Cr2O3催化剂仍然存在选择性较低和稳定性差的问题。

氧化镓(Ga2O3),特别是β晶型,已经成为非常有前途的金属氧化物催化剂。在不同金属氧化物中,Ga2O3表现出最高的丙烯产率,其次是Fe2O3,然后是Cr2O3 [DOI5]。催化性能与表面酸度高度相关,β-Ga2O3表现出最高的初始活性[DOI6]。负载的β-Ga2O3/SiO2也表现出良好的丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性[DOI7]。该脱氢活性源于Ga2O3表面的中等强度酸性位。总体而言,与Cr2O3相比,Ga2O3表现出优异的丙烷脱氢性能。

其他金属氧化物如Pt-Sn、V2O5和ZrO2也被研究用于丙烷脱氢反应,常作为Pt等金属的载体或助剂。Pt-Sn形成金属间化合物,提高选择性和抗焦炭能力[DOI8]。尽管金属氧化物表现出良好活性,但双金属Pt催化剂目前表现出最佳的丙烷脱氢性能。改进金属氧化物催化剂的成分、形貌和载体效应还需要进一步的工作。

※※※

综合理解与展望:综述研究强调了金属氧化物尤其是Cr2O3和Ga2O3作为传统丙烷脱氢催化剂的可行替代品的潜力。尽管还未超过贵金属,但经过设计的金属氧化物催化剂为实现经济高效的丙烯生产提供了机会。纳米尺度表征和计算建模的进步可能会加速未来的创新。结合理论和实验探究结构-活性关系的综合方法可能会揭示活性位点和机理途径。新合成技术也可能产生性能更优异的金属氧化物催化剂。调控金属氧化物催化剂的多金属组成、固溶体以及与载体的界面工程还有大量改进空间。总体而言,该领域在实现高选择性、高稳定性和低成本的金属氧化物丙烷脱氢催化剂方面还具有丰富的创新机会。
