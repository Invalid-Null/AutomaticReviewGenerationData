Based on the in-depth details extracted from the file, this review section will emphasize 'Catalyst Synthesis and Characterization - What preparation methods have enabled optimizing active phase dispersion?'.

Multiple studies have investigated catalyst preparation techniques aimed at optimizing the dispersion of active species for propane dehydrogenation. Co@Si-BEA catalysts with 0.1-0.5 wt% cobalt loadings prepared by vacuum impregnation showed the highest intrinsic activity due to isolated Co2+ sites [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]. Dealuminated Co@Si-BEA also produced less graphitic coke than non-dealuminated Co@BEA, indicating enhanced dispersion [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]. 

Using defects and holes to create dispersed 2D active sites in BN nanosheets was found to significantly boost propane dehydrogenation activity [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728]. Anchoring B active components on 3D porous structures or doping them into frameworks constitutes another effective strategy to construct dispersed sites [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728].

Multiple studies demonstrated the efficacy of Surface Organometallic Chemistry (SOMC) approaches in generating isolated metal centers. SOMC using siloxide or aluminate molecular precursors on dehydroxylated oxide surfaces allows crafting isolated sites in proximity to OH groups, enabling tailored bimetallic synthesis [10.1021/acs.accounts.9b00138]. Grafting metal alkyl species on supports produces sites with exceptional anti-reduction capabilities [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103].

Impregnation, ion exchange, and reductive treatments have enabled optimized dispersion of Ga, Zn, and In active phases in zeolites [10.1002/advs.202207756]. Ligand-protected hydrothermal approaches generate tetrahedral Co motifs in MFI, evading CoOx aggregation [10.1021/jacs.2c02636]. Varying impregnation solvents like ethanol over water prevents coking and enables dispersion [10.1016/S1872-2067(15)61042-7]. Sonication during wet impregnation breaks up Pt precursor salts, decreasing particle size [10.1016/j.ultsonch.2010.04.005].

Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) allows depositing highly dispersed metal oxides on supports without detectable crystals [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.01.005]. Strong interactions between promoters like Fe and active species such as Pt enable anchoring isolated atoms at multiple sites [10.1021/acscatal.2c05042].

Hydrothermal [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034] and sol-gel [10.1021/acscatal.3c00818] methods have successfully yielded small, uniform metal nanoparticles. Surfactant addition during synthesis improves uniformity [10.1021/acscatal.3c00818]. Metals introduced in situ avoid leaching and migration during calcination, retaining dispersion [10.1021/acscatal.1c01924].

To conclude, myriad preparation strategies have been developed to optimize active phase dispersion for propane dehydrogenation, employing techniques like SOMC, ALD, sonication, hydrothermal synthesis, and surfactant addition during preparation. These methods create isolated sites, atomically dispersed species, small uniform particles, and strong metal-support interactions. Further research could focus on extending these strategies to craft single site catalysts with maximal stability.

根据文件中提取的深入细节,本综述部分将强调“催化剂合成和表征——什么制备方法实现了活性相的优化分散?”。

多项研究调查了优化丙烷脱氢催化剂活性种类分散的催化剂制备技术。通过真空浸渍法制备的Co负载量为0.1-0.5 wt%的Co@Si-BEA催化剂由于其孤立的Co2+位点显示出最高的本征活性[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。脱铝Co@Si-BEA相比非脱铝Co@BEA也产生更少的石墨焦炭,这表明其分散性更强[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。

在BN纳米片中利用缺陷和空穴以创造分散的2D活性位点被发现可显著提高丙烷脱氢活性[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728]。将B活性组分锚定在3D多孔支撑上或掺杂入其框架中是构建分散位点的另一有效策略[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728]。

多项研究证明了表面有机金属化学(SOMC)方法在生成隔离金属中心方面的功效。使用硅醚或铝酸盐分子前体在脱羟基氧化物表面上的SOMC可以在接近OH基团的地方制造隔离位点,实现定制双金属合成[10.1021/acs.accounts.9b00138]。在支撑体上接枝金属烷基种可产生具有异常抗还原能力的位点[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]。

浸渍、离子交换和还原处理实现了分别在沸石中Ga、Zn和In活性相的优化分散[10.1002/advs.202207756]。配体保护的水热方法在MFI中产生四面体Co基团,避免了CoOx聚集[10.1021/jacs.2c02636]。改变浸渍溶剂如使用乙醇而不是水可防止结焦并实现分散[10.1016/S1872-2067(15)61042-7]。湿式浸渍过程中使用声波打断Pt前体盐,使颗粒尺寸减小[10.1016/j.ultsonch.2010.04.005]。

原子层沉积(ALD)技术允许在支撑体上沉积高度分散的金属氧化物而无可检测到的晶体[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.01.005]。促进剂如Fe与活性种类如Pt之间的强相互作用可使隔离的原子在多个位点上锚定[10.1021/acscatal.2c05042]。

水热[10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034]和溶胶凝胶[10.1021/acscatal.3c00818]方法已成功产生小的、均一的金属纳米颗粒。合成过程中添加表面活性剂改善均一性[10.1021/acscatal.3c00818]。原位引入的金属可避免钙化过程中的溶出和迁移,保持分散性[10.1021/acscatal.1c01924]。

总之,为优化丙烷脱氢的活性相分散,已经开发出了各种制备策略,应用了诸如SOMC、ALD、声波处理、水热合成和制备过程中添加表面活性剂等技术。这些方法创造了隔离位点、原子分散种类、小的均一颗粒以及强大的金属与载体间相互作用。进一步的研究可以集中在利用这些策略来制备具有最大稳定性的单一位点催化剂上。
