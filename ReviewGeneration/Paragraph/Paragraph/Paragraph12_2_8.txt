	<English>
		Several studies have explored supported mono-metallic platinum (Pt) catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). As noted in [10.1006/jcat.1994.1333], adding sulfur to an alumina-supported Pt catalyst improved selectivity to propene, especially when predosed at 873 K prior to reaction. Sulfur addition during catalyst preparation was more effective than adding it to the feed.
		
		Data in [10.1016/S1872-2067(15)61042-7] showed a Pt/BN catalyst maintained over 98% propylene selectivity during PDH, suggesting possible metal-support interactions. While [10.1021/acscatal.2c01631] used spectroscopy to confirm isolated Pt atoms and sub-nanometer clusters decorated a Zn-zeolite after H2 reduction. These Pt single-sites demonstrated high PDH activity and selectivity.
		
		Several other studies like [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548] and [10.1038/s41467-023-00968-7] have modified supported Pt catalysts, via promoters or optimized supports, to enhance stability and minimize deactivation during propane dehydrogenation reactions.
		
		※※※
		
		Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The works analyzed showcase promising advancements with supported Pt catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, especially regarding selectivity and stability improvements. Moving forward, deeper investigations into metal-support interplays that modulate Pt speciation and catalytic performance may uncover new knowledge and strategies. Researchers could also explore extending optimized synthetic methodologies to supported Pd, Rh, and other catalytic metals documented to activate C-H bonds. This may spur development of additional selective and durable PDH catalysts.

	<Chinese>
		几项研究探索了负载单金属铂催化剂用于丙烷脱氢反应。如[10.1006/jcat.1994.1333]指出,向氧化铝负载铂催化剂添加硫元素可以提高丙烯的选择性,特别是在反应前在873K进行硫元素前处理。催化剂制备阶段添加硫元素比反应期间添加更有效。

		[10.1016/S1872-2067(15)61042-7]的数据表明,Pt/BN催化剂在丙烷脱氢反应中保持98%以上的丙烯选择性,这表明可能存在金属-载体相互作用。而[10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]使用光谱分析确认了单个铂原子和亚纳米分子簇装饰在Zn沸石上经过H2还原。这些单原子铂活性位表现出高丙烷脱氢活性和选择性。

		其他几个研究例如[10.1021/acscatal.9b05548] 和 [10.1038/s41467-023-00968-7] 通过添加助剂或优化支撑体来提高负载铂催化剂的稳定性和抑制脱活。
		
		※※※
		
		综合理解与未来展望:所分析研究工作显示负载铂催化剂在丙烷脱氢中具有很好的提高选择性和稳定性的发展前景。接下来,更深入研究可以减少Pt负载,促进Pt活性调节功能的金属载体相互作用。研究人员还可以尝试将优化的合成方法延伸到已知能激活C-H键的支撑钯、铑等催化金属上,这可能促使其他选择性和稳定的丙烷脱氢催化剂的发展。
