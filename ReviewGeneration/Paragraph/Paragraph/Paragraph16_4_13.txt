Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

The addition of secondary metals such as Sn, Ga, and Zn has been widely explored to tune the catalytic performance of Pt-based catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). Alloying Pt with these metals brings about electronic and geometric alterations to the Pt active sites through ligand and ensemble effects, which modulate the adsorption behavior and transition states involved in the PDH mechanism [Placeholder_Of_DOI1].

Pt-Sn alloy catalysts are the most extensively studied system for PDH, and have been commercialized in the UOP Oleflex process. Incorporating Sn serves to depress hydrogenolysis and cracking reactions, improving selectivity towards propylene, while also minimizing deactivation from coking by removing coke precursors from Pt sites [Placeholder_Of_DOI2]. The formation of Pt-Sn intermetallic compounds like Pt3Sn and PtSn is considered crucial to attain optimal activity and stability. Though initial rates may be lowered, Sn inclusion greatly enhances stability by increasing propane dissociation barriers [Placeholder_Of_DOI3]. 

Investigations reveal that the highest initial conversion is achieved at Sn/Pt ratios around 1-1.5, beyond which excess Sn leads to loss of activity. But selectivity continues improving with higher Sn content [Placeholder_Of_DOI4]. The optimal balance depends on the support interaction; for instance, on zeolites a Pt/Sn ratio exceeding 1:1 maintains substantial rates unlike on reducible oxides [Placeholder_Of_DOI5].

双金属和合金催化剂 - Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

加入Sn、Ga和Zn等次要金属被广泛研究用于调节Pt基催化剂在丙烷脱氢(PDH)中的催化性能。与这些金属形成合金改变了Pt活性位点的电子和几何结构,通过配体和集成效应调控PDH机理中的吸附行为和转化状态[Placeholder_Of_DOI1]。

Pt-Sn合金催化剂是PDH中研究最广泛的体系,已经在UOP Oleflex工艺中商业化。加入Sn有助于抑制氢化裂解和裂解反应,提高向丙烯的选择性,同时通过从Pt位点除去焦油前体最小化脱活性[Placeholder_Of_DOI2]。 Pt3Sn和PtSn等Pt-Sn金属间化合物的形成对于实现最佳的活性和稳定性至关重要。 尽管初始反应速率可能会降低,但Sn的引入极大地增强了稳定性,方法是增加丙烷解离屏障[Placeholder_Of_DOI3]。

研究表明,Sn/Pt比率约为1-1.5时可以达到最高的初始转换率,超过此比例时,过量的Sn会导致活性损失。 但随着Sn含量的增加,选择性继续提高[Placeholder_Of_DOI4]。 最佳平衡取决于载体相互作用; 例如,在分子筛上,Pt/Sn比超过1:1可以维持可观的速率,而在可还原氧化物上则不行[Placeholder_Of_DOI5]。

与PtSn类似,Pt与Ga和Zn合金化也通过改变Pt的几何和电子性质来实现高丙烯选择性和抵抗脱活途径[Placeholder_Of_DOI6]。 Pt-Ga金属间化合物表现出异常的热稳定性,富含氧的Ga位点控制产物解吸,同时抑制焦炭生成。 Zn调节PtZn4集团的d带中心提高丙烷解离屏障,增强选择性[Placeholder_Of_DOI7]。
