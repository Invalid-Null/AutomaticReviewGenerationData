The article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939] presents microkinetic models of propane dehydrogenation using DFT-derived parameters. It discusses rate equations, surface coverages, turnover frequencies, selectivities, and rate-controlling steps.

Additionally, the study with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889] utilizes the CATKINAS package to perform microkinetic simulations of propane dehydrogenation over single-atom catalysts, allowing evaluation of the kinetics and practical applicability under industrial conditions. Parameters from DFT calculations inform the modeling.

Moreover, the literature with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916] indicates that prior computational efforts on Pt alloy surfaces have assessed proxies for propane dehydrogenation activity and selectivity. By comparing propane dehydrogenation barriers and propylene desorption/dehydrogenation barriers, optimal catalyst compositions are identified. 

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The microkinetic modeling endeavors surveyed scaffold an enhanced comprehension of the underlying kinetics directing propane dehydrogenation over various catalysts. Extrapolating from the fundamental perspectives and quantitative descriptors acquired, opportunities remain to broaden the scope of explored materials and reaction intermediates through high-throughput simulations. Emerging informatics methodologies might also augment potential catalyst screening pipelines. Ultimately, further developing predictive capabilities could empower rational design of optimized PDH catalysts and processes.

DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939]的文章使用DFT导出的参数来呈现丙烷脱氢的微动力学模型。它讨论了速率方程、表面覆盖度、周转频率、选择性和控制速率的步骤。

此外,DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]的研究利用CATKINAS软件包对单原子催化剂上的丙烷脱氢进行了微动力学模拟,这使得能够评估工业条件下的动力学和实际适用性。 DFT计算的参数为建模提供信息。

而且,DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916]的文献指出,先前对Pt合金表面的计算工作已经评估了丙烷脱氢活性和选择性的代用品。 通过比较丙烷脱氢屏障和丙烯脱sorption/脱氢屏障,确定了最佳的催化剂组成。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 调查的微动力学建模努力构建了对指导丙烷脱氢反应在各种催化剂上的底层动力学的增强理解。 在获得的基本视角和定量描述符的基础上推断,通过高通量模拟扩大已探索材料和反应中间体的范围仍有机会。 新兴的信息学方法也可能增强潜在的催化剂筛选流水线。 最终,进一步发展预测能力可以赋能丙烷脱氢催化剂和工艺的合理设计。
