Based on the detailed analysis from the references, some mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over specific metal oxide catalysts. 

On CrZrOx catalysts, two neighboring coordinatively unsaturated Zr (Zrcus) sites located at oxygen vacancies are proposed as the active sites responsible for breaking C-H bonds in propane homolytically to produce propene [10.1021/acscatal.9b05063]. Isolated Cr3+ species in Cr2O3 catalysts with coordination unsaturation are also suggested to catalyze dehydrogenation [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]. 

Mars-van Krevelen type redox mechanisms involving participation of lattice oxygen are described over VOx [10.1006/jcat.1999.2510, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023], MoVO [10.1021/jacs.9b09235], and other highly valent metal oxide catalysts [10.1021/acscatal.2c01374]. Subsequent reduction of the metal oxide and reoxidation by gaseous O2 or CO2 complete the catalytic cycle.

Heterolytic dissociative pathways preferentially breaking C-H over C-C bonds are proposed on gallium oxide surfaces, with rate-limiting dissociative propane adsorption [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001, 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017]. Alkyl and carbenium ion intermediates are described over Ga/zeolite catalysts [10.1021/acscatal.1c01497].

The specific nature of proposed active sites and mechanistic steps vary based on the metal oxide composition and structure. However, common themes of unsaturation, redox capability, and ability to break C-H bonds emerge as essential attributes.

※※※

An integrative perspective reveals that while substantial advances have been made in unraveling mechanistic scenarios over selected metal oxide catalysts, several gaps and opportunities remain. Many complex multifunctional mixed metal oxides lack mechanistic understanding. Extending computational studies in tandem with sophisticated spectroscopic probes of working catalysts can further bridge knowledge gaps. An integrated approach tuning composition, coordination, oxidation state, and metal-support interfacial sites provides an exciting playground for developing innovative catalysts and mechanistic hypotheses. By covering diverse chemical spaces and tolerance regimes, next generation catalysts can aspire beyond incremental gains towards disruptive process improvements.

根据详细分析的参考文献,针对某些金属氧化物催化剂已经提出了丙烷脱氢的一些机理情景。

在CrZrOx催化剂上,位于氧空位的两个相邻的非配位饱和锆(Zrcus)位点被认为是断裂丙烷中的C-H键以同解生成丙烯的活性位点 [10.1021/acscatal.9b05063]。在Cr2O3催化剂中,不饱和配位的Cr3+孤立物种也被认为催化脱氢[10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]。

涉及晶格氧参与的Mars-van Krevelen类型的氧化还原机理被描述在VOx [10.1006/jcat.1999.2510, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023], MoVO [10.1021/jacs.9b09235]和其它价态较高的金属氧化物催化剂上[10.1021/acscatal.2c01374]。随后的金属氧化物还原和气态O2或CO2的再氧化完成催化循环。

在氧化镓表面上优先断裂C-H而不是C-C键的异解离子机理,其中限速步骤为解离吸附的丙烷[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001, 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017]。在镓/分子筛催化剂上也描述了烷基和碳正离子中间体[10.1021/acscatal.1c01497]。

根据金属氧化物的组成和结构,所提出的活性位点和机理步骤的具体性质各不相同。然而,不饱和,氧化还原能力和断裂C-H键的能力作为基本属性出现的共同主题。

※※※

综合视角揭示,尽管在选定的金属氧化物催化剂上对机理情景有了实质性的进展,但仍有几个差距和机会存在。许多复杂的多功能性混合金属氧化物缺乏机理的理解。扩展计算研究与精细光谱技术的工作催化剂的研究可以进一步弥补知识差距。通过调控组成、配位、氧化态和金属载体界面的综合方法为开发创新型催化剂和机理假说提供了激动人心的空间。通过覆盖不同的化学空间和容差体系,新一代催化剂可以超越渐进增益,向颠覆性的过程改进迈进。
