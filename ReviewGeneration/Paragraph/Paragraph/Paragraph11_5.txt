	<English>
		The study detailed in reference [10.1021/acscatal.2c00878] systematically investigates the effects of different oxide supports on propane dehydrogenation activity over VOx catalysts. It reveals that VOx/ZrO2 exhibits over 4 times higher activity compared to other supports, attributed to weaker VOx-ZrO2 interactions leading to lower electron density on bridge oxygen atoms and enhanced C-H bond activation capability. Reference [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726] explores metal-support interfacial effects in Co-BEA zeolite catalysts, showing isolated Co2+ species anchored on dealuminated sites selectively activate C-H over C-C bonds due to strong Co-support interactions. These principles help elucidate how tuning metal-oxide interplay affects dehydrogenation activity and selectivity.
		※※※  
		The findings showcase opportunities to rationally design optimized metal-support interfaces through controlled deposition of isolated metal sites, crystal facet engineering, defect integration etc. that strike the right balance between metal-support bonding and desired catalytic functionalities. 
		There also lies scope for multi-scale characterization techniques coupled with in-situ spectroscopy and DFT to obtain fundamental molecular-level understanding of these interfacial phenomenon that dictate catalyst stability and selectivity over extended operation. Elucidating these structure-property relationships can unlock breakthroughs in minimizing carbon deposition while maximizing propylene yield.

	<Chinese>
		参考文献[10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]系统地研究了不同氧化物载体对VOx催化剂丙烷脱氢活性的影响。结果表明,与其他载体相比,VOx/ZrO2的活性提高了4倍,这是由于VOx与ZrO2的相互作用较弱,导致桥氧原子上的电子密度降低,C-H键活化能力增强。参考文献[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]探讨了Co-BEA沸石催化剂的金属-载体界面效应,结果表明,在经过除铝后与载体相互作用的隔离Co2+物种可以选择性地活化C-H键而不是C-C键。这些原理有助于阐明调节金属-氧化物相互作用如何影响脱氢活性和选择性。
		※※※
		研究结果展示了通过受控沉积隔离金属活性位、晶体面工程、缺陷整合等方式合理设计优化的金属-载体界面,在金属载体键合与所需催化功能间取得平衡。
		还有利用多尺度表征技术结合原位光谱和DFT,在分子水平上深入理解决定催化剂稳定性和选择性的界面现象,这些界面现象在长周期运行中发挥重要作用。阐明这些结构-性能关系,可以实现突破以最大限度地减少炭沉积同时最大化丙烯收率。
