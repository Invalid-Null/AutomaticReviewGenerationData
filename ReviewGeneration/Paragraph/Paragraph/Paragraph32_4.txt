Based on the details provided in the file, several articles summarize remaining challenges and knowledge gaps in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysis research [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103, 10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1, 10.1021/acscatal.1c02621, 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.01.030, 10.1021/acscatal.1c05724, 10.1021/acscatal.1c00331, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005, 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. 

Major challenges highlighted include the need for alternative non-precious metal catalysts to replace expensive platinum-based industrial catalysts [10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1], lack of understanding of the intrinsic effects of catalyst modifications like oxygen vacancies on performance [10.1021/acscatal.1c02621], developing effective catalysts for selective propane oxidation as most work has focused on more reactive alkanes [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.01.030], definitively characterizing the identity and structure of highly active sites like gallium species [10.1021/acscatal.1c05724], issues with rapid catalyst deactivation from coke formation [10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005, 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014], and need for improved energy efficiency, catalyst stability and overcoming equilibrium limitations on conversion [10.1021/cr5002436].

Specific future research directions identified include exploring single atom and confined cluster catalysts [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331], using computational screening and microkinetic modeling to predict active sites [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816], developing well-defined model catalysts to systematically derive catalytic functions [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014], and utilizing operando spectroscopy to track working catalysts.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The detailed analysis reveals that while significant progress has been made, propane dehydrogenation catalysis still faces substantial gaps in fundamental scientific understanding and technical performance limitations. Tackling these gaps will require expansive perspective across multiple disciplines. 

Computationally screening novel sites in silico before targeted synthesis allows exploring vast phase space unattainable via trial-and-error. Operando spectroscopy coupled to kinetic and isotopic tracing elucidates working structures. And modulated model systems with systematic variations build intrinsic activity-composition-structure insights from the bottom-up. 

Integrating these tools while leveraging richness of zeolites, MOFs, single atom and cluster architectures opens new horizons for custom-designing optimized PDH catalysts. And intersecting with green hydrogen through renewable-powered electrocatalysis and bio-inspired enzyme mimics may unlock unforeseen productive pathways. The foundations lie set to meet growing propylene demands through imaginative, convergent catalytic innovation.

根据文件提供的详细信息,几篇文章总结了丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂研究中仍存在的挑战和知识空白[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103, 10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1, 10.1021/acscatal.1c02621, 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.01.030, 10.1021/acscatal.1c05724, 10.1021/acscatal.1c00331, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005, 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。

突出的主要挑战包括:需要替代昂贵的铂基工业催化剂的非贵金属催化剂[10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1]; 缺乏对催化剂性能的本质效应(如氧空位)的理解[10.1021/acscatal.1c02621]; 开发用于选择性丙烷氧化的有效催化剂,因为大多数工作都集中在更反应性更强的烷烃上[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.01.030];明确定性的表征高活性位点(如硼元素种类)的身份和结构 [10.1021/acscatal.1c05724];来自焦炭形成的快速催化剂失活问题[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005, 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]; 以及需要提高能效、催化剂稳定性和克服转换限制的平衡[10.1021/cr5002436]。

确定的具体未来研究方向包括:探索单原子和包埋簇催化剂[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331]; 使用计算筛选和微观动力学建模来预测活性位点[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]; 开发明确定义的模型催化剂以系统地导出催化功能[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]; 并利用工作状态光谱技术跟踪工作催化剂。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:详细分析表明,虽然取得了重大进展,但丙烷脱氢催化剂在基础科学理解和技术性能方面仍面临重大挑战。解决这些差距将需要跨多个学科的广阔视野。

在目标合成之前,计算机中先对新型活性中心进行筛选,可探索超出试错法的广阔可能空间。工作状态光谱技术与动力学和同位素示踪相结合,阐明工作结构。而系统变化的调制模型系统则可从底层建立内在的活性-组成-结构视角。  

将这些工具与斑岩石,金属有机框架,单原子和簇体体系的丰富性结合,为定制优化的 PDH 催化剂开启新视野。并与绿氢能源相交汇,通过可再生电催化和仿生酶催化,可能会解锁前所未有的高产途径。基础已经奠定,通过想象力和汇聚的催化创新,满足不断增长的丙烯需求。
