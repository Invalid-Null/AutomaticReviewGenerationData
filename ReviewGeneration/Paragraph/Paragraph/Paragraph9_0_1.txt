Based on the details extracted from the file, there is evidence showing how tuning oxidation states, oxygen mobility, and acid-base properties of metal oxides can affect propane dehydrogenation performance. 

For oxidation states, references [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033], [10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014], [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814], and [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183] reveal relationships. Creating oxygen vacancies on Al2O3 reduces aluminum coordination, generating Alcu-O acid-base pairs that tune intermediates' adsorption energies and lower reaction barriers, enhancing dehydrogenation activity [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033]. Chromium's oxidation state also matters - Cr(VI) tetrahedral sites are the active form, with Cr(III) octahedral sites being less active. Re-oxidizing Cr(III) to Cr(VI) regenerates catalyst activity [10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014]. Similarly, an oxidized Cr2O3 surface interacts more strongly with reactants, enabling exothermic dehydrogenation with lower barriers compared to an endothermic mechanism with higher barriers on reduced Cr2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]. For CrOx/SiO2 and CrOx/Al2O3 catalysts, increasing the average Cr oxidation state from 2 to 4 boosts propane conversion [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183].  

For oxygen mobility, [10.1021/acscatal.2c01374], [10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c03999], and [10.1021/acscatal.8b04701] reveal insights. Matching the rates of oxidative dehydrogenation and lattice re-oxidation by utilizing bulk lattice oxygen mobility could raise reactivity [10.1021/acscatal.2c01374]. High activity of β-Ga2O3 is linked to abundant tetrahedral Ga sites that serve as Lewis acid sites [10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c03999]. Plus, monomeric VOx with high oxygen mobility is much more active than polymeric VOx [10.1021/acscatal.8b04701].

Acid-base property tuning also plays a role. Gallium oxide's catalytic activity correlates with medium-strong acid site density [10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017]. And tetrahedral Ga3+ sites behave as Lewis acid sites that influence selectivity [10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c03999].

根据文件提取的详细信息,有证据表明调整金属氧化物的氧化态、氧迁移能力和酸碱性如何影响丙烷脱氢反应性能。

对于氧化态,参考文献 [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033]、[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014]、[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814] 和 [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183] 透露了一些关系。在 Al2O3 上创建氧空穴会降低铝的配位数,生成 Alcu-O 酸碱对,这可以调节中间体的吸附能,降低反应障碍,增强脱氢活性[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033]。铬的氧化态也很重要——Cr(VI)的四面体位是活性形式,Cr(III)的八面体位活性较低。将 Cr(III) 氧化为 Cr(VI) 可以恢复催化剂活性[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014]。类似地,氧化的 Cr2O3 表面与反应物的相互作用更强,使得脱氢反应为放热反应,障碍也较低;而在还原的 Cr2O3 上,反应为吸热,障碍较高 [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]。 对于 CrOx/SiO2 和 CrOx/Al2O3 催化剂,将平均 Cr 氧化态从 2 增加到 4 可以提高丙烷转化率 [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]。

对于氧迁移能力,[10.1021/acscatal.2c01374]、[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c03999] 和 [10.1021/acscatal.8b04701] 透露了一些见解。匹配氧化脱氢和再氧化反应的速率,利用体相氧迁移能力补充氧空位,这可能会提高反应性[10.1021/acscatal.2c01374]。β-Ga2O3 的高活性与其丰富的四面体型 Ga 位点有关,这些位点起到路易斯酸位点的作用[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c03999]。此外,单体 VOx 的氧迁移能力远高于聚合态 VOx [10.1021/acscatal.8b04701]。

调节酸碱性也起着作用。氧化镓的催化活性与中等强度的酸性位点的密度有关[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017]。四面体型 Ga3+ 位点也表现为路易斯酸位点,影响选择性[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c03999]。
