	<English>
		The file details several catalysts studied for propane dehydrogenation (PDH), spanning common industrial options to more recent explorations. Conventional PDH catalysts include chromium oxide and supported platinum, leveraged commercially in Lummus' Catofin and UOP's Oleflex processes respectively (References [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331], [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]). Economic considerations have driven investigations into alternatives like supported vanadium and iron oxides, gallium oxides, as well as composite catalysts with tunable active sites and improved stability (References [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999], [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7], [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]). Zeolites present opportunities to carefully tailor active sites and micropore environments for targeted PDH performance (References [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019], [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]).
		※※※
		Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Complementing industrial benchmarks of chromium oxide and platinum catalysts, ongoing academic studies reveal metal oxides, supported metals, composite configurations, nanocarbons, and zeolite architectures to accomplish enhanced, stable, and economical propane dehydrogenation. Future pursuits may prioritize earth-abundant options with precisely engineered active centers within scalable support matrices such as mesoporous silicas, versatile carbon structures, and zeolites, streamlining innovative compositions with sustainable commercial potential. Realizing such catalysts will rely on synergizing knowledge across inorganic chemistry, materials science, catalyst design, and chemical reaction engineering.

		文件详细介绍了用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)的几种催化剂,涵盖从常见的工业选项到最近的探索。 常规PDH催化剂包括Chromium 氧化物和支持的白金,分别用于Lummus的Catofin和UOP的Oleflex工艺中(参考文献[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331], [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7])。经济考量促使人们研究诸如支持的钒和铁氧化物、镓氧化物以及具有可调活性位点和改进稳定性的复合催化剂等替代品(参考文献[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999], [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7], [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103])。沸石提供了精心调控活性位点和微孔环境以实现目标PDH性能的机会(参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019], [10.1021/jacs.2c03941])。
		※※※
		综合理解和前景展望: 除了铬氧化物和铂催化剂的工业标准以外,正在进行的学术研究表明,金属氧化物、受支撑金属、复合配置、纳米碳和沸石体系可以实现增强的稳定经济的丙烷脱氢。未来的工作可能会优先考虑使用丰富的地球元素选项,在可扩展的支持基质(例如介孔硅、多元碳构件和沸石)中精确工程活性中心,简化创新组成具有可持续商业潜力。实现这种催化剂将依赖于协同无机化学、材料科学、催化剂设计和化学反应工程方面的知识。
