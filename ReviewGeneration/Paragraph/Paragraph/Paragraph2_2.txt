Based on the details extracted from the references, catalysts facilitate propane dehydrogenation by providing active sites that can selectively activate C-H bonds in propane over C-C bonds with lower activation barriers [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01916, 10.1021/acscatal.2c01374]. Platinum-based catalysts in particular are widely employed, leveraging Pt's ability to break C-H bonds while minimizing cracking reactions [10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019, 10.1021/acscatal.5b01423]. The key mechanisms described involve hydrogen abstraction to form metal-carbon bonds on coordinatively unsaturated sites [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01924]. 

Alternative catalysts are also being explored, including metal oxides like ZrO2 and Ga2O3 which can provide oxygen vacancies or Lewis acid sites [10.1021/acscatal.1c02621, 10.1021/acscatal.0c05454]. Zeolites exchanged with metal cations can similarly donate electron density to adsorbed propane [10.1002/advs.202207756]. Support effects are additionally important, with mesoporous silicas offering opportunities to tune selectivity and activity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The reviewed studies reveal consistent strategies in catalyst design to promote selective C-H activation for propane dehydrogenation, while avoiding deep dehydrogenation and coking issues. This understanding may guide development of optimal single-atom Pt catalysts, building on demonstrated synergy between theory and experiment [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01924]. Stabilizing coordinatively unsaturated metal sites could also be a fruitful direction, whether via confinement in zeolites/mesoporous supports or surface organometallic frameworks. More broadly, shale gas availability renders propane dehydrogenation increasingly viable, necessitating accelerated innovation in selective, stable and scalable catalysts [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.025, 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726].

<Chinese>

根据参考文献中的细节,催化剂通过提供能够选择性地活化丙烷中的C-H键而不是C-C键的活性位点来促进丙烷脱氢,降低活化能垒[10.1021/acscatal.3c00889, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01916, 10.1021/acscatal.2c01374]。尤其广泛使用的钼白催化剂能选择性断裂C-H键而最小化裂解反应[10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019, 10.1021/acscatal.5b01423]。描述的关键机制涉及在未饱和配位位点上形成金属-碳键的氢原子抽象[10.1021/acscatal.3c00939, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01924]。

也在探索替代催化剂,包括二氧化锆和氧化镓等金属氧化物,可提供氧空位或路易斯酸位点[10.1021/acscatal.1c02621, 10.1021/acscatal.0c05454]。与金属阳离子交换的沸石也可以类似地向吸附的丙烷提供电子密度[10.1002/advs.202207756]。载体效应也很重要,介孔硅氧化物提供了调节选择性和活性的机会[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 所审阅的研究揭示了催化剂设计中一致的策略,以促进丙烷选择性C-H活化,同时避免深度脱氢和结焦问题。这一理解可能会指导优化的单原子Pt催化剂的开发,在理论和实验之间的协同效应的基础上[10.1021/acscatal.1c01916, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01924]。稳定未饱和配位金属位点也可能是一个富有成效的方向,无论是通过分子筛/介孔支持物的限制还是通过表面有机金属框架。更广泛地说,页岩气的可获得性使得丙烷脱氢变得越来越可行,这需要对选择性、稳定性和可扩展性的催化剂进行加速创新[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.025, 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。
