Based on the details extracted from the file, several studies have evaluated supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most successful and widely used catalysts for commercial PDH processes [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1021/acssuschemeng.8b05206]. An alumina-supported Pt catalyst is described as a vital PDH catalyst due to its superior ability to activate paraffinic C-H bonds while minimizing C-C cleavage [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. One study shows that an ALD Al2O3 or TiO2 overcoat on a Pt/Al2O3 catalyst can increase PDH yield and selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. Adding sulfur or 1 wt% boron promoter is also found to improve Pt/alumina selectivity [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548].

Several studies have explored the effect of metal-support interactions with Pt. Pt/BN is reported to have higher stability than Pt/Al2O3, potentially due to Pt-BN interactions [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]. Confining sub-nanometric Pt clusters in Ge-UTL zeolite pores via Pt-O-Ge bonds also leads to high PDH activity, selectivity and stability [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7].

A few studies have also evaluated other supported metals. Data shows supported RhCu, Ir, and Ru have intrinsic PDH activity, but inferior selectivity compared to optimized Pt catalysts [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]. At low Co loadings, isolated Co2+ cations in dealuminated BEA zeolite act as primary active sites [DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726].

In summary, Pt-based catalysts, especially supported on Al2O3, BN, and zeolites, have been the most extensively studied and optimized mono-metallic PDH catalysts. Metal-support interactions play a key role in enhancing stability and selectivity. Other metals show activity but have not matched Pt performance. Further studies on emerging supports and bimetallics may uncover additional opportunities.

根据文件提取的详细信息,几项研究已经评估了supported单金属Pt、Pd和Ir催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

报道称,Pt基催化剂是最成功和最广泛使用的商业PDH催化剂之一 [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1021/acssuschemeng.8b05206]。一项研究表明,Al2O3支持的Pt催化剂由于其优越的活化烷基C-H键而最小化C-C键的断裂能力,是一种重要的PDH催化剂[DOI:10.1002/anie.201507119]。 一项研究表明,Pt/Al2O3催化剂上的ALD Al2O3或TiO2覆层可以提高PDH产量和选择性[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。 添加硫或1 wt%硼促进剂也被发现可以提高Pt/氧化铝的选择性[DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。

几项研究已经探索了Pt与载体之间的相互作用效应。 研究表明,与Pt/Al2O3相比,Pt/BN具有更高的稳定性,这可能是由于Pt-BN之间的相互作用[DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]。 通过Pt-O-Ge键将亚纳米Pt簇限制在Ge-UTL分子筛孔内也导致高PDH活性,选择性和稳定性[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。


还评估了其他几种supported金属。 数据表明,supported RhCu、Ir和Ru具有内在的PDH活性,但与优化的Pt催化剂相比,选择性较差[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]。 在低Co负载量时,dealumin化BEA分子筛中的孤立Co2+阳离子起主要活性中心的作用[DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。


总之,尤其是supported在Al2O3、BN和分子筛上的Pt基催化剂,已经是最广泛研究和优化的单金属PDH催化剂。 金属与载体之间的相互作用对提高稳定性和选择性起关键作用。 其他金属显示活性,但尚未达到Pt性能。 对新型载体和双金属的进一步研究可能会揭示额外的机会。
