Based on the details extracted from the files, several articles summarize remaining challenges and knowledge gaps in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysis research. 

One major challenge highlighted is the need to develop alternative catalysts to replace expensive platinum-based industrial catalysts, which is noted as a "long-term pursuit" [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1]. Related to this is the need to reduce noble metal loading and improve catalyst stability and energy efficiency more broadly [DOI: 10.1021/cr5002436].

Another key issue identified is insufficient understanding of the intrinsic mechanisms and active site structures involved in PDH catalysis over promising catalysts like chromium-substituted zeolites [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014] and metal-nitrogen-carbon single atom catalysts [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1]. Better experimental characterization and modeling is required to elucidate these catalytic mechanisms. 

Researchers also underscore the recurrent challenge of rapid catalyst deactivation from coke formation during PDH, necessitating the development of more resistant catalyst architectures [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005]. Computational screening of coking kinetics could aid these efforts [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00331].

Overall, advancing beyond the constraints imposed by equilibrium limitations, unoptimized selectivity, insufficient stability, and incomplete mechanistic pictures represents the foremost challenges in making PDH catalysis industrially viable on a large scale.

根据从文件中提取的详细信息,几篇文章总结了丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂研究中剩余的挑战和知识空白。

一个主要挑战是需要开发替代催化剂来取代昂贵的铂基工业催化剂,这被称为PDH的“长期追求” [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1]。与此相关的是,需要更广泛地降低贵金属负载,提高催化剂稳定性和能量效率 [DOI: 10.1021/cr5002436]。

另一个关键问题是,在诸如铬取代沸石 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014] 和金属-氮-碳单原子催化剂 [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1]等有前途的催化剂上参与PDH催化的本质机制和活性位点结构的理解不足。需要更好的实验表征和建模来阐明这些催化机理。

研究人员还强调PDH过程中从焦炭形成导致的快速催化剂失活这一经常性挑战,这需要开发更耐焦炭的催化剂体系[DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005]。计算焦炭形成动力学可以帮助这些工作[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00331]。

总的来说,在平衡限制、选择性不优化、稳定性不足和机理图片不完整的约束下取得进展,是使PDH催化在规模上工业可行的主要挑战。
