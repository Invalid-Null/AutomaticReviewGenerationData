 <English>

Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic platinum (Pt), palladium (Pd), and iridium (Ir) catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful catalysts for this reaction [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. For example, Pt/alumina catalysts demonstrate superior ability to activate paraffinic C-H bonds while minimizing undesired C-C bond cleavage [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. Adding promoters such as sulfur [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333] and boron [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548] are shown to enhance Pt/alumina selectivity and stability. Other studies explore the effect of Pt cluster size, showing smaller clusters tend to be more selective [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]. Doping Pt clusters with Sn generally decreases activity but can improve other aspects of performance [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639].

Besides alumina, other Pt supports like SBA-15 [DOI: 10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005] and zeolites [DOI: 10.1016/cej.2015.01.008, DOI: 10.1038/s41467-018-07502-5] have been investigated. Strong metal-support interactions are reported to play a key role in governing activity, selectivity, and stability [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008].

Data on Pd, Rh, and Ir catalysts is more limited. One study showed an RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst was considerably more active and selective than a traditional Pt/alumina catalyst [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]. Overall however, Pt-based catalysts seem to dominate exploration efforts due to the superior intrinsic activity of Pt for alkane dehydrogenation [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings reveal supported Pt catalysts, especially on acidic supports like alumina, represent the current state-of-the-art for propane dehydrogenation. However, stability issues due to strong metal-support interactions leading to sintering and coking necessitate further research. Emerging opportunities may lie in designing single atom alloy catalysts that isolate highly active metal sites while resisting deactivation. Additionally, recent work on metal-free catalysts hints at their potential to overcome limitations of supported metals. Exploring synergies between metal and metal-free catalysts could uncover new paradigms. Overall, gaining deeper mechanistic insights into catalyst design criteria can guide innovation of materials that balance activity, selectivity, and stability.

<Chinese>

根据文件提供的详细信息,已经有几项研究探索了载体支撑的单金属铂(Pt)、钯(Pd)和铱(Ir)催化剂用于丙烷脱氢。

报道Pt基催化剂是这一反应中使用最广泛和商业上最成功的催化剂之一 [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。例如,Pt/氧化铝催化剂表现出色的抗烷烃C-H键活化能力和抑制不需要的C-C键断裂反应的能力 [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。加入促进剂如硫 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333] 和硼 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548] 被证明可以增强Pt/氧化铝的选择性和稳定性。其他研究探索了Pt簇的大小效应,发现较小的Pt簇往往具有更高的选择性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。掺杂Sn到Pt簇中通常会降低活性,但可以改善性能的其他方面 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]。

除氧化铝外,也有研究探索SBA-15 [DOI: 10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005] 和分子筛[DOI: 10.1016/cej.2015.01.008, DOI: 10.1038/s41467-018-07502-5] 等其他Pt载体。证据表明金属与载体之间的强相互作用在调控活性、选择性和稳定性方面起关键作用[DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]。

关于Pd、Rh和Ir催化剂的数据更为有限。一项研究表明RhCu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂的活性和选择性远高于传统的Pt/氧化铝催化剂[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。然而总的来说,由于铂具有卓越的烷烃脱氢活性,Pt基催化剂似乎主导了研究工作[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。

※※※
综合理解和前景展望:研究结果揭示载体支撑的Pt催化剂,尤其是在氧化铝等酸性载体上的Pt催化剂,代表丙烷脱氢反应当前的最先进技术水平。但是,由于强的金属与载体相互作用导致结烬和结焦等稳定性问题,需要进一步研究。新机遇可能来自设计可以隔离高活性金属位点同时又抵抗失活的单原子合金催化剂。此外,最近关于无金属催化剂的工作暗示了它们具有的潜力,可以克服载体金属催化剂的限制。探索金属和无金属催化剂之间的协同作用可以揭示新的范式。总的来说,深入理解催化剂设计标准的机制可以指导材料创新,实现活性、选择性和稳定性之间的平衡。
