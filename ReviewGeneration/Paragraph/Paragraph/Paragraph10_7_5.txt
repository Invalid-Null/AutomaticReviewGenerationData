Based on the details extracted from the references, various mechanistic proposals have been put forth for propane dehydrogenation over different metal oxide catalysts. 

On Cr-based catalysts, active sites consisting of Cr3+ species [DOI1], Cr6+/Cr3+ redox cycles [DOI2], and isolated mononuclear CrIII sites [DOI3] have been suggested. Reaction steps often involve the generation of these sites from precursor Cr species during activation or reaction, followed by propane activation through hydride abstraction, β-hydride elimination, and propylene desorption. 

For V-based catalysts, Mars-van Krevelen type pathways have been commonly described, where lattice oxygen activates propane through hydride transfer, creating reduced V sites that get re-oxidized by gas phase O2 or CO2 [DOI4, DOI5]. Isolated and highly dispersed surface VOx sites have been implicated as the active sites in several studies [DOI6, DOI7]. 

On ZrO2, surface Zr cations neighboring oxygen vacancies, referred to as Zrcus sites, have been proposed to catalyze dehydrogenation through both oxidative and non-oxidative routes [DOI8, DOI9]. These undercoordinated Zr sites activate propane's C-H bonds, often with the assistance of lattice oxygen, followed by propylene elimination. 

For Ga-containing catalysts, tetracoordinated Ga3+ sites were suggested to be the active sites, with propane undergoing heterolytic C-H bond dissociation over them [DOI10, DOI11]. Subsequent H transfer and recombination steps complete the catalytic cycle. The heterolytic mechanism is favored over redox pathways on Ga oxide surfaces.

In summary, while specifics differ, creation of coordinatively unsaturated and highly dispersed active sites on metal oxide surfaces, often through redox pre-treatments, along with lattice oxygen assistance for C-H activation seem to be recurring themes across mechanistic scenarios reported in the literature for this reaction.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements gleaned from the references, it becomes evident that comprehensive in-situ spectroscopic techniques combined with detailed kinetic and computational investigations can provide pivotal insights into dehydrogenation mechanisms over metal oxides. This multifaceted approach seems indispensable considering the complex structural transformations many mixed metal oxide catalysts undergo during activation and reaction. 

An integrated framework encompassing synthesis, multimodal characterization, and mechanistic modeling holds promise for rational design of optimized catalysts. For instance, atomic layer deposition presents opportunities for precision synthesis of site-isolated species. Operando XAS, NMR, infrared, and Raman spectroscopies can shed light on the working state of active sites. And DFT calculations can validate proposed intermediates as well as compare multiple plausible pathways. 

Furthermore, key challenges like sintering and coking may be mitigated through smart catalyst architectures, engineered at the nanoscale. And oxidative routes leveraging CO2 could boost efficiency while avoiding deep oxidation. Prospective research in these directions can usher the further advancement of propane dehydrogenation catalysts and processes.

根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,已经为不同金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢提出了各种机理建议。

对于Cr基催化剂,活性位点由Cr3+物种组成[DOI1],Cr6+/ Cr3+氧化还原循环[DOI2]和隔离的单核CrIII位点 [DOI3]。反应步骤通常涉及活性位点的生成,然后通过氢化物的提取,β-氢消除和丙烯的解吸激活丙烷。

对于V基催化剂,通常描述了Mars-van Krevelen类型的途径,其中晶格氧通过氢转移激活丙烷,形成还原的V位点,然后通过气相O2或CO2氧化[DOI4,DOI5]。 在几项研究中,表面上隔离和高度分散的VOx位点被认为是活性位点[DOI6,DOI7]。

在ZrO2上,氧空位附近的表面Zr阳离子,称为Zrcus位点,被认为可以通过氧化和非氧化途径催化脱氢[DOI8,DOI9]。 这些未配位的Zr位点激活丙烷的C-H键,通常在晶格氧的帮助下,然后消除丙烯。

对于Ga含量催化剂,四配位的Ga3+被认为是活性位点,丙烷在上面发生杂解C-H键断裂[DOI10,DOI11]。 后续的H转移和重组步骤完成催化循环。 在Ga氧化物表面上更青睐杂解机理而不是氧化还原途径。

总之,尽管具体情况不同,但通过氧化还原预处理在金属氧化物表面上形成配位不饱和和高度分散的活性位点,以及C-H激活的晶格氧辅助似乎是文献中报道的这一反应机理方案中的重复主题。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:考虑到从参考文献中了解到的进步,全面原位光谱技术与详细的动力学和计算研究的结合对金属氧化物上的脱氢机理的理解至关重要已变得明显。 考虑到许多混合金属氧化物催化剂在活化和反应过程中经历的复杂结构转变,这种多方面方法似乎不可或缺。

一个整合了合成、多模态表征和机理建模的框架在设计优化的催化剂方面有前景。 例如,原子层沉积提供了合成隔离位点的机会。 Operando XAS,NMR,红外和拉曼光谱可以揭示活性位点的工作状态。 DFT计算可以验证拟议的中间体以及比较多个可行途径。

此外,智能催化剂体系结构的奈米尺度设计可以缓解烧结和结焦等关键挑战。 杠杆CO2的氧化途径可以提高效率,同时避免深度氧化。 这些方向的未来研究可以引领丙烷脱氢催化剂和工艺的进一步发展。
