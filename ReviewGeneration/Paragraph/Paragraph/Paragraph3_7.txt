Based on the details extracted from the references, several major catalyst classes have been explored for propane dehydrogenation, including metal oxides, supported metals, alloys, and zeolites. 

Specifically, metal oxides such as VOx/CrOx supported on mesoporous silicas like MCM-41 and SBA-15 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816], gallium oxide [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999], and cobalt-incorporated zeolites [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726] have shown promise. Supported metals like PtSn on silica [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939] and single atom catalysts with metals such as Fe, Zn, and Co on supports like silica and nitrogen-doped graphene [10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63196-1] have also been studied. Additionally, zeolites like H-Beta [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019] and intermetallic alloys of Pt, Pd and Ni with promoters [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916] have exhibited potential. 

Industrially, Pt-based catalysts are utilized in the Oleflex process and CrOx-based catalysts are used in the Catofin process for propane dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The major catalyst classes studied for propane dehydrogenation cover a diverse range of materials, from conventional options like supported metals and metal oxides to more recently explored alternatives such as single atom catalysts and zeolites. While Pt and CrOx catalysts dominate industrial applications currently, ongoing research efforts to enhance performance metrics including conversion, selectivity, stability and cost-effectiveness will shape future trajectories. Emerging catalyst formulations that optimize site isolation, metal-support interactions, and resistance to deactivation may unlock the potential for transitioning from traditional PDH catalyst regimes to innovative systems with transformative economic and sustainability prospects.

根据参考文献的详细信息,已探索了几种主要的丙烷脱氢催化剂类别,包括金属氧化物、负载金属、合金和分子筛。

具体来说,负载在MCM-41和SBA-15等介孔硅上的VOx/CrOx [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]、氧化镓[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]和掺钴分子筛[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726] 显示了前景。负载在二氧化硅上的PtSn [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939] 和负载在二氧化硅和氮掺杂石墨烯上的单金属原子催化剂,如Fe、Zn和Co [10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63196-1],也得到了研究。 此外,H-Beta [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019] 分子筛和掺杂剂的Pt、Pd和Ni的互金属合金[10.1021/acscatal.1c01916]也显示了潜力。

在工业上,Pt基催化剂用于Oleflex工艺,而CrOx基催化剂用于丙烷脱氢的Catofin工艺[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331]。


※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 已研究的丙烷脱氢催化剂主要类别涵盖了从常规选择如负载金属和金属氧化物到最近探索的替代品如单原子催化剂和沸石在内的各种材料。 尽管目前工业应用主要由Pt和CrOx催化剂主导,但正在进行的研究工作旨在提高转化率,选择性,稳定性和成本效益等性能指标,这将塑造未来的发展轨迹。 新兴的催化剂配方在优化位点隔离、金属-支撑物相互作用和抗失活性方面可能会解锁潜力,从而实现从传统PDH催化剂体系向具有革命性经济效益和可持续性前景的创新系统的转变。
