 <English>
Based on the details extracted from the references, several mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over different metal oxide catalysts. 

On chromium oxide catalysts, active Cr3+ sites generated in situ from Cr6+ species are responsible for dehydrogenation [DOI1]. Isolated Cr3+ sites with coordination number >4 are more active than oligomeric Cr species [DOI2]. Mononuclear Cr3+ sites with coordinative vacancies have also been suggested as active sites [DOI3].

On gallium oxide catalysts, propane activation occurs via heterolytic dissociation over tetrahedral Ga3+ sites to form gallium hydride and alkoxide intermediates [DOI4, DOI5]. An alkyl mechanism has been proposed involving Ga+H+ pair sites [DOI6].

On vanadium oxide catalysts, Mars-van Krevelen [DOI7, DOI8] and redox mechanisms [DOI9, DOI10] have been described. Lattice oxygen activates propane, gets reduced upon dehydrogenation, and gets replenished by O2 or CO2. Isolated V3+ [DOI11], tetrahedral VO4 [DOI12], and sub-monolayer vanadia species [DOI13] have been suggested as active sites.  

On zirconia catalysts, coordinatively unsaturated Zr4+ sites neighboring oxygen vacancies catalyze dehydrogenation steps, forming zirconium propyl and OH intermediates [DOI14, DOI15]. The proximity between Zr4+ and dopant sites like Zn2+ [DOI16] or Ga3+ [DOI17] provides a synergistic effect.

Additionally, concerted dehydrogenation pathways involving simultaneous CH cleavage have been proposed on Ga-doped zirconia [DOI18], while heterolytic H2 dissociation followed by propane dehydrogenation was described on TiO2 nanosheets [DOI19].

※※※ 

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The current mechanistic picture reveals crucial active sites like isolated or unsaturated metal cations and lattice oxygen, along with Mars-van Krevelen, redox, heterolytic, and concerted reaction coordinates. Elucidating these atomic-level structural-functional synergies aids rational design of optimized catalysts. Emerging in-operando techniques can offer further dynamic insights. There also lies scope to explore more dopant and support combinations that engender favorable energetics and tunable selectivity over target products. Advanced multi-scale simulations can potentially unravel new pathways and descriptors. Ultimately, unraveling fundamental dehydrogenation mechanisms facilitates targeted development of selective, stable, and scalable catalysts.

<Chinese>
根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,已经为不同金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢提出了几种机理场景。

在铬氧化物催化剂上,Cr6+种在原位生成的活性Cr3+位点负责脱氢[DOI1]。配位数大于4的隔离Cr3+位点比低聚体Cr种更活跃[DOI2]。单核Cr3+位点与配位空位也被认为是活性位点[DOI3]。

在镓氧化物催化剂上,丙烷通过在四面体Ga3+位点上发生杂解裂化活化,形成镓氢化物和烷氧化物中间体[DOI4,DOI5]。在Ga+H+成对位点上提出了一个烷基机理[DOI6]。

在钒氧化物催化剂上,已经描述了Mars-van Krevelen [DOI7,DOI8]和氧化还原机理[DOI9,DOI10]。晶格氧活化丙烷,在脱氢过程中被还原,并被O2或CO2再生。隔离的V3+[DOI11]、四面体VO4 [DOI12]和亚单层酸钒种[DOI13]已被认为是活性位点。

在二氧化锆催化剂上,配位不饱和的Zr4+位点与氧空位相邻,催化脱氢步骤,形成锆丙基和OH中间体[DOI14,DOI15]。Zr4+与掺杂剂位点(如Zn2+ [DOI16]或Ga3+ [DOI17])之间的亲和力提供了协同增效。

此外,在镓掺杂的二氧化锆上已经提出了同时发生CH键裂化的顺式脱氢途径[DOI18],而在TiO2纳米片上描述了杂解H2解离后随之发生的丙烷脱氢[DOI19]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:当前的机制图景揭示了关键的活性位点,如隔离或不饱和金属阳离子和晶格氧,以及Mars-van Krevelen、氧化还原、杂解和顺式反应坐标。阐明这些原子水平的结构功能协同有助于设计优化的催化剂。新兴的原位技术可以提供进一步的动态见解。探索更多掺杂剂和载体组合的空间也为获得针对目标产物的有利能量和可调节选择性提供了机会。先进的多尺度模拟有可能揭示新的途径和描述子。最终,阐明基本的脱氢机制有助于有针对性地开发选择性好、稳定和可扩展的催化剂。
