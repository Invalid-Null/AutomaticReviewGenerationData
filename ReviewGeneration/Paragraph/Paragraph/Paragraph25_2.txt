One article uses in-situ DRIFTS and temperature programmed desorption with MS detection to probe propylene adsorption modes on cobalt catalysts, revealing how silicon modification impacts propylene interaction [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.021]. Another combines pulse titration experiments with quantitative in-situ FTIR to determine the identity and density of active gallium species in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts during propane dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.1c05724]. Synchrotron-based in-situ XAS at the Zn K-edge distinguishes binuclear ZnOx species on silicalite-1 from 3D ZnOx clusters formed in-situ on deAl beta during reaction [10.1021/acscatal.2c05704]. In-situ Raman spectroscopy and online MS enables identification of a VOx-C3H5 intermediate species and determination of rate-limiting H2 formation over VOx/HMS catalysts [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.07.017].

Other examples utilize combined in-situ UV-Vis/Raman spectroscopy with XANES and EXAFS to correlate changes in chromium oxidation states with propane dehydrogenation activity and deactivation over time [10.1021/cr5002436]. In-situ FTIR reveals transformation of inactive zinc hydroxide species into active zinc hydride sites during propane dehydrogenation induction periods over ZnO/SBA-15 [10.1021/acscatal.2c00928]. And a study combining GC measurements with in-situ XRD and DRIFTS elucidates changes in product distribution, phase transitions of vanadium oxides, and surface species during time-on-stream [10.1021/acscatal.8b04701].

The diversity of in-situ techniques provides detailed insights into working catalysts spanning from bulk to surface and even identifies specific active sites in some cases. Advancements in characterization capabilities now allow tracking catalyst structural dynamics from the molecular level up. While most studies have focused on simpler lab systems, adoption of these operando methods for online monitoring in industrial reactors could facilitate better process control and mechanistic-driven optimization.

一篇文章使用原位 DRIFTS 和温程脱附联用 MS 检测来探究钴基催化剂上的丙烯吸附模式,揭示硅修饰如何影响丙烯与催化剂的相互作用 [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.021]。另一篇文章将脉冲滴定实验与定量原位 FTIR 相结合,以确定 Ga/H-ZSM-5 催化剂中活性镓种在丙烷脱氢过程中的身份和密度 [10.1021/acscatal.1c05724]。基于同步辐射的原位 Zn K 边 XAS 区分了硅分子筛上的二核 ZnOx 物种和原位在去Al β沸石上形成的三维 ZnOx 簇合物 [10.1021/acscatal.2c05704]。原位拉曼光谱与在线 MS 实现了 VOx-C3H5 中间体物种的识别以及确定 VOx/HMS 催化剂上限速的 H2 形成步骤 [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.07.017]。

其他例子使用了联用的原位 UV-Vis/Raman 光谱与 XANES 和 EXAFS 来关联铬的氧化态变化与丙烷脱氢活性及随时间的失活 [10.1021/cr5002436]。原位 FTIR 揭示了丙烷脱氢诱导期间 ZnO/SBA-15 上非活性氧化锌物种向活性氢化锌物种的转变[10.1021/acscatal.2c00928]。一项结合 GC 测量与原位 XRD 和 DRIFTS 的研究阐明了氧化钒相变、表面物种和产物分布随时间的变化 [10.1021/acscatal.8b04701]。

这些多样的原位技术为理解催化剂体积到表面乃至识别某些具体活性位点的变化提供了详细信息。表征能力的进步现在允许从分子水平追踪催化结构动力学。尽管大多数研究集中在更简单的实验室系统上,但这些共存方法用于工业反应器的在线监测中,可以实现更好的过程控制和以机理为驱动的优化。
