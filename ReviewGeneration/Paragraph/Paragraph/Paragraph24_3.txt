 <English>
Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts require optimized active phase dispersion to maximize selectivity towards propylene. Multiple studies investigate preparation methods aimed at optimizing dispersion. 

Co@Si-BEA catalysts with 0.1-0.5 wt% cobalt loadings prepared by vacuum impregnation show highest intrinsic activity due to well dispersed isolated Co2+ sites [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]. Dealuminated Co@Si-BEA produces less coke than standard Co@BEA, indicating better dispersion [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726].

Boron nitride sheets with defects introduced to increase active sites are selective for propane dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728]. Dispersion is also improved by loading boron species into 3D porous structures or frameworks [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728].  

Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts are commonly prepared by impregnating Ga salts on the support [10.1002/advs.202207756]. Tailoring framework Al distribution optimizes PDH activity by localizing isolated Ga species [10.1002/advs.202207756].

Ligand-protected hydrothermal synthesis constructs isolated {(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2} sites in Co-MFI, showing high efficiency and selectivity [10.1021/jacs.2c02636]. This prevents formation of less selective CoOx clusters/nanoparticles [10.1021/jacs.2c02636].

Increasing vanadia dispersion in silica gel improves selectivity by isolating active sites [10.1016/S0021-9517(02)00015-5]. The highest propylene rate occurs at 10 wt% V2O5, above which excess bulk V2O5 likely forms [10.1016/S0021-9517(02)00015-5].

※※※

The studies reveal tailoring support properties, controlling metal loading, and using advanced preparation techniques enable optimizing active phase dispersion. Key strategies evident from the analyses include:

- Introducing promoters or modifiers that anchor dispersed metal species 
- Tuning pore architecture to prevent mobility/aggregation
- Limiting metal precursor concentration to achieve isolated motifs
- Applying specialty methods like ligand protection and atomic layer deposition

Further work could explore extending these strategies to emerging catalyst architectures like metal-organic frameworks and covalent organic frameworks. Rational design of multi-functionalized supports may also enable programmed dispersion motifs. Operando characterization techniques would offer additional insight into dispersion dynamics.

<Chinese>
丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂需要优化活性相分散度以最大化丙烯的选择性。多项研究调查了旨在优化分散度的制备方法。

采用真空渗透法制备的Co负载量为0.1-0.5 wt%的Co@Si-BEA催化剂由于孤立Co2+位点分散良好而显示出最高的固有活性[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。除硅Co@Si-BEA产生的焦炭比标准Co@BEA少,表明分散度更好[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。

在氮化硼片中引入缺陷以增加活性位点,这些位点对丙烷脱氢选择性很高[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728]。通过将硼种加载到3D多孔结构或框架中也可以改进分散度[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728]。

Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂通常通过在支撑体上浸渍Ga盐来制备[10.1002/advs.202207756]。 调整框架Al分布可以通过定位隔离的Ga种类来优化PDH活性[10.1002/advs.202207756]。

配体保护的水热合成在Co-MFI中构建了隔离的{(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2}位点,显示出高效率和选择性[10.1021/jacs.2c02636]。 这可以防止形成选择性较低的CoOx簇/纳米粒子[10.1021/jacs.2c02636]。

在二氧化硅凝胶中增加五氧化二钒的分散度可以通过隔离活性位点来改进选择性[10.1016/S0021-9517(02)00015-5]。当V2O5含量为10 wt%时,丙烯的生成速率最高,超过该含量会形成过量的V2O5聚集[10.1016/S0021-9517(02)00015-5]。

※※※

这些研究表明,调整载体性质、控制金属负载量以及使用先进的制备技术可以实现活性相的最佳分散。从分析中可以看出的关键策略包括:

- 引入锚定分散金属种的促进剂或修饰剂
- 调节孔隙结构以防止活性中心流动/聚集  
- 限制金属前驱体浓度以获得隔离基团
- 应用配体保护和原子层沉积等专门方法

进一步的工作可以探索将这些策略扩展到新兴的催化剂体系如金属有机框架和共价有机框架。合理设计多功能化的载体也可以实现规划的分散基团。操作表征技术可以提供对分散动力学的更深入的了解。
