Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Pt-based catalysts have shown exceptional capability for activating C-H bonds in alkane dehydrogenation reactions like propane dehydrogenation (PDH) to produce propylene [Placeholder_Of_DOI1]. However, monometallic Pt catalysts suffer from drawbacks like fast deactivation and lower selectivity [Placeholder_Of_DOI2]. Alloying Pt with secondary metals has emerged as an efficient strategy to overcome these limitations and tune catalytic performance. Common alloying elements explored for PDH include post-transition metals like Sn, Ga, Zn, In etc. and early transition metals like Fe, Co, Ni [Placeholder_Of_DOI3, Placeholder_Of_DOI4]. 

Pt-Sn alloy catalysts are the most extensively studied system for PDH, and have been successfully commercialized (Oleflex process by UOP) [Placeholder_Of_DOI5]. Incorporating Sn serves critical functions - it helps break down the Pt ensemble sites, altering selectivity for propylene formation over secondary cracking/hydrogenolysis reactions [Placeholder_Of_DOI6]. Sn also minimizes coke accumulation on Pt, improving stability [Placeholder_Of_DOI7]. The optimal Sn/Pt ratio is typically around 1-1.5, beyond which excess Sn starts poisoning sites causing activity loss [Placeholder_Of_DOI8]. Characterization confirms the presence of Pt-Sn intermetallic bonding for fresh catalysts, which enhances electronic modifications on Pt sites [Placeholder_Of_DOI9, Placeholder_Of_DOI10]. On varying supports, Sn predominantly interacts with the support matrix e.g. on acidic zeolites, or reduces to form alloys e.g. on reducible oxides like silica [Placeholder_Of_DOI11].

Investigations on supported Pt-Ga nanoparticles also demonstrate improved catalyst dispersion and inhibition of dehydrogenation byproducts with Ga addition [Placeholder_Of_DOI12, Placeholder_Of_DOI4]. This suppresses coke buildup, thereby greatly enhancing stability. XPS and TEM data verifies change from Ga oxide state in fresh samples to formation of Fe3Ga1 type intermetallic phases under reaction conditions, responsible for superior performance [Placeholder_Of_DOI4].

Fewer studies explore Pt-Zn catalysts, but clear modifications induced by Zn are reported. On zeolitic supports, isolated Pt atoms can be stabilized within Zn-modified framework cages, showing impressive propene selectivity ~95% and stability over extended duration [Placeholder_Of_DOI13, Placeholder_Of_DOI14]. This strategy of precise Pt site isolation prevents agglomeration at high temperatures. Addition of Zn as a promoter on other supports also increases stability by altering ensemble requirements for C-H dissociation [Placeholder_Of_DOI3].

In summary, alloying Pt with secondary metals has proven highly effective for optimizing propane dehydrogenation efficiency by modulating catalyst geometry, improving resistance to deactivation pathways, and favorably tuning electronic properties of Pt sites. While Sn and Ga are the common choices as PDH promoters, emerging work highlights unexplored opportunities with economical metals like Zn. Rational catalyst design harnessing such metal-metal synergies has potential to push the boundaries of commercial dehydrogenation processes.

双金属和合金催化剂 - Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

基于Pt的催化剂已展现出色的活性,能有效激活丙烷脱氢(PDH)等烷烃脱氢反应中C-H键[Placeholder_Of_DOI1]。然而,单金属Pt催化剂存在快速失活和选择性较低等缺点[Placeholder_Of_DOI2]。利用与Pt形成合金的第二金属成为优化催化性能的有效策略。常见的PDH合金配体元素包括Sn、Ga、Zn、In等后转矩金属和Fe、Co、Ni等早期过渡金属[Placeholder_Of_DOI3,Placeholder_Of_DOI4]。

Pt-Sn合金催化剂是PDH中研究最广泛的体系,已成功商业化(UOP的Oleflex工艺)[Placeholder_Of_DOI5]。引入Sn发挥关键作用——它有助于打破Pt整体位点,改变丙烯生成的选择性,抑制副反应如裂化和氢解[Placeholder_Of_DOI6];Sn还可以最大限度地抑制Pt上的焦炭积累,提高稳定性[Placeholder_Of_DOI7]。 最佳的Sn/Pt比通常约为1-1.5,超过此比例,过量的Sn会中和有效位点导致活性损失[Placeholder_Of_DOI8]。表征结果确认新鲜催化剂中存在Pt-Sn金属间化合物结合,这增强了Pt位点的电子修饰效应[Placeholder_Of_DOI9,Placeholder_Of_DOI10]。在不同载体上,Sn主要与载体基质(如酸性分子筛)相互作用,或还原形成合金(如可还原氧化物如二氧化硅)[ Placeholder_Of_DOI11]。

对载体支持Pt-Ga纳米粒子的研究也证实,Ga的加入可以改善催化剂分散度,抑制脱氢副产物[Placeholder_Of_DOI12,Placeholder_Of_DOI4]。这抑制了焦炭积累,从而显著提高了稳定性。 XPS和TEM数据证实,反应条件下新鲜样品中存在的Ga氧化物会转变为Fe3Ga1等金属间化合物,这是其优异性能的根源[Placeholder_Of_DOI4]。

较少研究探讨Pt-Zn催化剂,但Zn带来的明确改变也有报道。在分子筛载体上,Pt原子可以被稳定在Zn修饰的框架笼中,显示出色的丙烯选择性约95%和长期稳定性[Placeholder_Of_DOI13,Placeholder_Of_DOI14]。这种精确隔离Pt位点的策略防止了高温下的聚集。在其他载体上将Zn作为助剂也可通过改变C-H解离所需的整体效应来提高稳定性[Placeholder_Of_DOI3]。

总之,与Pt形成合金的次要金属已被证明对优化丙烷脱氢反应效率非常有效,通过调节催化剂几何形貌、提高抗脱活途径的能力和有利地调谐Pt位点的电子性能实现。尽管Sn和Ga是常见的PDH助剂选择,新兴研究也强调了使用经济金属(如Zn)的未被开发的机会。合理利用这种金属间协同效应设计催化剂,有可能推进商业脱氢过程的界限。
