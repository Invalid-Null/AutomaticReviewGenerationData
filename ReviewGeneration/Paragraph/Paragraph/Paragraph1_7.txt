 <English>

Propylene is an essential chemical feedstock with a wide range of applications in the production of polymers, chemicals, and plastics, as noted extensively across the references [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.025], [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816], [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008], [10.1021/acscatal.0c01580], [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019], [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939], [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889], [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.01.005], [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]. Global demand for propylene currently exceeds 100 million tons per year and is projected to grow substantially in the coming decade, presenting challenges for traditional production methods like naphtha cracking and fluid catalytic cracking to keep pace [10.1021/acscatal.1c01924]. 

Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) has emerged as an increasingly important route for on-purpose propylene generation, taking advantage of abundant and low-cost propane feedstocks from shale gas and other sources [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999], [10.1021/acscatal.0c01455]. PDH is an attractive and competitive propylene production technology due to its economic and environmental advantages [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]. Multiple studies note PDH already accounts for over 10% of global propylene output, with significant potential for further expansion [10.1002/advs.202207756], [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The accumulating research underscores propylene's immense value as a foundational chemical building block, matched by rapidly rising global demand that strains traditional production routes. As such, propane dehydrogenation has clearly emerged as an essential and increasingly cost-competitive pathway to boost propylene availability. 

Looking ahead, intensifying efforts to enhance PDH efficiency, conversion, selectivity and catalyst longevity through advanced materials and process intensification could unlock greater propylene volumes to enable continued growth of invaluable polymer industries. If shale gas economics remain favorable, PDH methodologies may eventually rival traditional techniques as the foremost driver of propylene capacity expansion. Moreover, modular PDH units could allow distributed production to unlock smaller-scale propylene applications.

丙烯是一种重要的化学原料,广泛应用于聚合物、化学品和塑料的生产,这在参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.025]、[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]、[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]、[10.1021/acscatal.0c01580]、[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019]、[10.1021/acscatal.3c00939]、[10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]、[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.01.005]、[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]中有广泛的论述。全球对丙烯的需求当前超过了每年1亿吨,并且预计在未来十年里将大幅增长,这给传统的生产方法如裂化石脑油和催化裂化带来了挑战,难以跟上需求增长的速度 [10.1021/acscatal.1c01924]。

丙烷脱氢(PDH)作为一种新兴的丙烯生产途径,利用了页岩气等来源提供的丰富而低成本的丙烷原料,其重要性日益增加 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999],[10.1021/acscatal.0c01455]。PDH技术因其经济性和环境优势,成为一种有吸引力和有竞争力的丙烯生产工艺[10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]。多项研究指出,PDH当前已占全球丙烯产量的10%以上,并且有极大的扩展潜力[10.1002/advs.202207756],[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331]。  

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 积累的研究强调了丙烯作为基础化学大宗原料的巨大价值,与此匹配的是全球需求的快速增长,这给传统的生产途径带来了挑战。因此,丙烷脱氢明确地成为一个必不可少且日益具有成本竞争力的途径,以提高丙烯的可获得性。

展望未来,通过先进材料和过程集成等手段,提高PDH的效率、转换率、选择性和催化剂稳定性的不懈努力,能够释放出更大量的丙烯,支持无价值的聚合物产业继续发展。如果页岩气经济性保持优势,PDH方法最终可能会与传统技术匹敌,成为推动丙烯产能扩张的主要动力。此外,模块化的PDH单元还可以实现分布式生产,开启小规模丙烯应用。
