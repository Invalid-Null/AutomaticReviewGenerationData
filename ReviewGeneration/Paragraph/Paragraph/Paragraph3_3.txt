	<English>
		The file details several major catalyst classes studied for propane dehydrogenation (PDH), including supported metals, metal oxides, alloys, and zeolites. Commonly explored supported metals are platinum and chromium oxide, utilized in major industrial PDH processes like UOP's Oleflex and Lummus' Catofin \[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331\]. Alternative supported metal catalysts incorporate vanadium, iron, cobalt, zinc, molybdenum, and tin oxides, often on carriers like silica, alumina, and MCM-41 mesoporous silica \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.025\]. Alloy catalysts studied include intermetallic Pt, Pd, and Ni alloys with Ga, Sn, Zn, Fe, and In promoters \[10.1021/acscatal.1c01916\]. Zeolites explored encompass H-Beta, iron-silicates, and gallium-exchanged CHA, which can selectively convert propane to propylene via proposed redox cycles and CH bond activation pathways \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\], \[10.1021/jacs.2c03941\].
		※※※
		Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The major industrial PDH catalysts of supported Pt and Cr oxides provide a performance benchmark, but alternative classes like metal oxides, alloys, and zeolites present opportunities to enhance selectivity, stability and cost-efficiency. Composite catalysts integrating distinct active phases could manifest synergistic effects. Exploring single-atom catalysts anchored on unconventional supports merits attention. Elucidating reaction mechanisms through advanced spectroscopy and computing may uncover activity descriptors to expedite optimization.

	<Chinese>
		文件详细介绍了丙烷脱氢(PDH)中研究的几大催化剂类别,包括受支撑的金属、金属氧化物、合金和沸石。常见的受支撑金属是铂和铬氧化物,在UOP公司的Oleflex和Lummus公司的Catofin等主要工业PDH工艺中使用\[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331\]。替代受支撑金属催化剂包含钒、铁、钴、锌、钼和锡氧化物,通常载于二氧化硅、氧化铝和MCM-41介孔硅上\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.025\]。研究的合金催化剂包括铂、钯和镍的金属间化合物,与Ga、Sn、Zn、Fe和In的助剂\[10.1021/acscatal.1c01916\]。探索的沸石包括H-Beta、铁硅酸盐和钒交换的CHA,这些沸石可以通过提议的红氧循环和CH键活化途径选择性地将丙烷转化为丙烯\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\], \[10.1021/jacs.2c03941\]。
		※※※
		综合理解与未来展望: 工业PDH催化剂受支撑的Pt和Cr氧化物提供了一个性能基准,但是金属氧化物、合金和沸石等其他类别给改善选择性、稳定性和成本效益带来了机会。整合不同活性相的复合催化剂可以产生协同效应。探索锚定在非传统载体的单原子催化剂值得关注。高级光谱和计算公开反应机制可能会揭示活性描述符以加速优化。
