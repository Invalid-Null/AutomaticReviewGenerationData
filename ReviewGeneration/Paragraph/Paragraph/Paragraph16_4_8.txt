Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Pt-Sn alloy catalysts have been extensively studied for propane dehydrogenation and even commercialized, such as in the Oleflex process by UOP using Pt-Sn/γ-Al2O3 [Placeholder_of_10.1016/S1872-2067(23)64402-X]. Adding Sn as a promoter to Pt catalysts leads to smaller Pt particle size and improves catalytic performance in terms of activity, selectivity and stability compared to monometallic Pt catalysts [Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018, Placeholder_of_10.1002/anie.201507119]. The Sn modifies the electronic properties of Pt, leading to selective C-H bond activation over C-C bond rupture, and Sn produces a "drain-off" effect to remove coke precursors from active sites [Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018]. However, excess Sn beyond an optimal Pt:Sn ratio (e.g. 2:1) suppresses activity [Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]. 

Pt-Zn catalysts have also shown promise, with isolated Pt stabilized by SiOZn-OH groups in dealuminated zeolite Beta achieving high activity, propene selectivity and stability attributed to isolated PtZn4-6 sites [Placeholder_of_10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]. A Pt:Zn ratio around 4 was found optimal, higher than this led to similar activity but lower stability [Placeholder_of_10.1021/jacs.1c10261]. The ordered intermetallic PtZn structure reduces Pt ensemble size and improves thermal stability [Placeholder_of_10.1021/acscatal.1c01808].

Additionally, Pt-Ga catalysts demonstrated high initial activity close to equilibrium conversion at 450C along with >90% propylene selectivity and stability over Pt/Al2O3 [Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.apcatb.2021.120731]. The Ga helps disperse and stabilize Pt active sites. Overall, alloying Pt with promoters like Sn, Zn and Ga serves to modulate the geometric and electronic structure of Pt active sites, altering ensemble effects and ligand properties to enhance selectivity and stability.
	
※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements elucidated regarding supported Pt alloy catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, ample promising opportunities exist moving forward. As catalytic performance is intimately tied to the nanostructure of bimetallic sites, future efforts might delve into novel synthetic techniques to exquisitely control active site morphology. This could encompass crafting single atom alloy catalysts or core-shell structures with Pt-based shells. Expanding the library of secondary metals beyond Sn, Zn and Ga may also unveil synergies that surpass current benchmarks. Finally, the rational incorporation of promoters into hierarchically porous supports offers intriguing prospects to overcome diffusion limitations that curtail productivity.

双金属和合金催化剂 —— Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

Pt-Sn合金催化剂已经被广泛研究用于丙烷脱氢,甚至已经商业化,例如UOP公司使用Pt-Sn/γ-Al2O3的Oleflex工艺 [Placeholder_of_10.1016/S1872-2067(23)64402-X]。 在Pt催化剂中添加Sn促进剂导致Pt颗粒尺寸变小,并在活性、选择性和稳定性方面改进催化性能,与单金属Pt催化剂相比[Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018, Placeholder_of_10.1002/anie.201507119]。 Sn改变了Pt的电子性质,导致优先发生C-H键活化而不是C-C键断裂,Sn产生“排出”效应,以清除活性位点上的焦炭前体 [Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018]。然而,超过最佳的Pt:Sn比例(例如2:1)的过量Sn会抑制活性[Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]。

Pt-Zn催化剂也展现了前景,如通过SiOZn-OH基团稳定在渗透白云母Beta型分子筛上的隔离Pt原子,取得了高活性、丙烯选择性和稳定性,这与隔离的PtZn4-6位点有关[Placeholder_of_10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]。Pt:Zn= 4比例被发现是最佳的,比这更高的比例会导致相似的活性但更差的稳定性[Placeholder_of_10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。 有序的金属间化合物PtZn结构减小了Pt配体尺寸并提高了热稳定性[Placeholder_of_10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]。

此外,与Pt/Al2O3相比,Pt-Ga催化剂在450°C表现出接近平衡转化的高初始活性以及>90%的丙烯选择性和稳定性[Placeholder_of_10.1016/j.apcatb.2021.120731]。 Ga有助于分散和稳定Pt活性位点。总体而言,用促进剂如Sn、Zn和Ga合金化Pt有助于调控Pt活性位点的几何和电子结构,改变配体集团效应和配体性质以提高选择性和稳定性。
	
※※※

综合理解与前景展望:考虑到所阐明的支持Pt合金催化剂在丙烷脱氢方面的进步,在继续推进过程中存在着丰富的前景。由于催化性能与双金属位点的纳米结构息息相关,未来的工作可能会深入探索新颖的合成技术来精确控制活性位点形态。这可能包括制备单原子合金催化剂或含Pt基核壳结构。扩大探索Sn、Zn和Ga之外的其他金属也可能发现超越当前基准的协同效应。最后,在分层多孔支持体中合理掺入促进剂提供了克服扩散限制、提高生产力的有前景的契机。
