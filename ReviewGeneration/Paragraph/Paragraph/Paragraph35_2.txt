The literature reviewed does not extensively envision potentially transformational catalyst architectures for propane dehydrogenation, with most studies focused on incremental improvements to existing catalyst materials and syntheses [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.06.016, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103, 10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008, 10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1, 10.1021/acscatal.0c05711, 10.1021/acscatal.1c02621, 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.01.030, 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63369-3, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01814, 10.1016/j.cej.2017.05.058, 10.1021/acscatal.1c05724, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1016/S1872-2067(23)64402-X, 10.1021/acscatal.2c05042, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005, 10.1016/j.cej.2007.11.009, 10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7, 10.1016/j.jcat.2014.10.018, 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014, 10.1016/j.cej.2020.126656, 10.1016/j.jcat.2017.12.004]. 

One study proposed single atom and confined cluster catalysts as potentially transformative to help minimize coke formation [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331]. However, most work remains grounded in conventional supported metal catalysts rather than pioneering novel architectures.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The reviewed research currently lacks extensive focus on disruptive catalyst design innovations for propane dehydrogenation. While single atom and confined cluster catalysts show early promise [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331], future work might further explore their large-scale manufacturability and economic viability. More broadly, developments in high-throughput computational screening, machine learning-guided design, and operando spectroscopy could systematically guide discovery of groundbreaking dehydrogenation catalyst architectures. Diffraction, microscopy and spectroscopy may illuminate catalyst structure-function correlations to help engineer superior catalytic sites and geometries. Elucidating degradation pathways and coking phenomena through fundamental surface science could shape rational design principles and revamp dehydrogenation catalyst longevity. Ultimately transformational architectures might pair step-change enhancements in both activity and stability.
