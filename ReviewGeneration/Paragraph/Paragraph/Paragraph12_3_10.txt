 <English>
Based on the details extracted from the references, several studies have evaluated supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts. For example, Pt/Al2O3 is a key component of the Oleflex process from UOP [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]. One study showed Pt/Al2O3 maintained by ALD metal oxide overcoats can increase PDH yield and selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. Doping Pt/γ-Al2O3 with boron was also found to boost performance, decreasing coke formation 3-fold and improving selectivity from 90% to 98% [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. On the other hand, some studies report rapid deactivation of Pt catalysts due to coking and agglomeration [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]. Analyzing coked Pt/Al2O3 and PtSn/Al2O3 catalysts via TPO revealed different coke types that are suppressed by hydrogen [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361].

Beyond Pt, supported Pd, Rh, and Ir are intrinsically active for C-H bond activation but expensive alternatives [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. One study showed supported single atom alloy RhCu/SiO2 has higher activity and 100% propene selectivity compared to Pt/Al2O3 with 80% selectivity [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389].

Overall, Pt-based catalysts seem to be the most promising supported metal catalysts for PDH, but deactivation issues persist. Modifying Pt with promoters or overcoats shows promise to enhance stability and selectivity. More work is needed to optimize supported metal catalyst design.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements made with supported Pt catalysts for PDH, an opportunity exists to explore modifications beyond conventional oxides. Emerging supports like MXenes and zeolites may offer new ways to disperse and stabilize Pt active sites. Machine learning could also help optimize compositions and structures. Broader adoption of operando methods can provide better insights into deactivation mechanisms. While Pt dominates currently, single atom alloy catalysts with enhanced selectivity may find increasing commercial relevance. Multifunctional catalysts that integrate dehydrogenation with downstream olefin processing also warrant research. Overall, pursuing creative innovations anchored in fundamental understanding will drive development of better supported metal catalysts for PDH.

<Chinese>
根据参考文献的细节,已经对丙烷脱氢(PDH)评估了几种载体单金属Pt、Pd和Ir催化剂。

Pt基催化剂被报道是最广泛使用和商业上最成功的PDH催化剂之一。例如,Pt/Al2O3是UOP Oleflex工艺的关键组成部分[DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。一项研究表明,通过ALD金属氧化物覆膜的Pt/Al2O3可以增加PDH收率和选择性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。在Pt/γ-Al2O3上掺杂硼也被发现可以提高性能,可将焦炭形成量降低3倍,选择性从90%提高到98% [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。另一方面,一些研究报道Pt催化剂由于结焦和聚集而快速失活[DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]。通过TPO分析结焦的Pt/Al2O3和PtSn/Al2O3催化剂揭示了不同类型的焦炭,这些焦炭被氢所抑制[DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]。

除Pt外,载体Pd、Rh和Ir在C-H键激活上具有固有活性,但代价高昂[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。一项研究表明,RhCu/SiO2单原子合金催化剂比选择性为80%的Pt/Al2O3具有更高的活性和100%丙烯选择性[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。

总的来说,Pt基催化剂似乎是PDH最有希望的载体金属催化剂,但失活问题依然存在。 用助剂或覆膜改性Pt有助于增强稳定性和选择性。 需要更多工作来优化载体金属催化剂的设计。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到Pt催化剂在PDH方面所取得的进展,存在探索Pt催化剂超越常规氧化物修饰的机会。 像MXenes和分子筛这样的新型载体可能提供新的方法来扩散和稳定Pt活性位点。 机器学习也可以帮助优化组成和结构。 更广泛地采用操作方法可以提供关于失活机理的更好洞察。 虽然Pt目前占主导地位,但选择性更高的单原子合金催化剂可能会找到越来越大的商业意义。 集成脱氢与下游烯烃处理的多功能催化剂也值得研究。 总的来说,基于基础理解进行创造性创新的追求,将推动更好的PDH载体金属催化剂的发展。
