Based on the detailed analysis from the references, the article with DOI [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008] describes computational work that performed linear regressions between formation energies to identify the ethyl and methylidyne adsorption energies as optimal descriptors correlating catalyst properties to propane dehydrogenation performance. 

Additionally, the reference [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889] reveals a computational study that found a volcano-type activity trend with respect to the propylene adsorption energy Ead(C3H6) based on scaling relationships and microkinetic modeling of single-atom catalysts. This structure-activity relationship demonstrated that catalyst binding strength correlates to dehydrogenation activity.

Furthermore, experimental and computational investigations in the study [10.1021/acscatal.0c01455] were conducted to elucidate structure-activity relationships for propane dehydrogenation catalysts.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements discussed regarding establishing quantifiable descriptors and structure-activity relationships for propane dehydrogenation catalysts, future work can build on these foundations to explore high-throughput computational screening and machine learning for catalyst discovery. As more data accumulates from experiments and modeling linking catalyst characteristics to performance metrics, data-driven approaches leveraging advanced algorithms may accelerate identification of optimal catalyst formulations and geometries.

此外,参考文献[10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]透露了一项计算研究,该研究基于单原子催化剂的缩放关系和微观动力学建模,发现了与丙烯吸附能Ead(C3H6)相关的火山型活性趋势。这种结构-活性关系表明,催化剂的结合强度与脱氢活性相关。

此外,研究[10.1021/acscatal.0c01455]中的实验和计算调查是为了阐明丙烷脱氢催化剂的结构-活性关系。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:考虑到关于建立丙烷脱氢催化剂的可量化描述符和结构-活性关系的进展,未来的工作可以在这些基础上探索高通量计算筛选和机器学习用于催化剂发现。随着来自实验和模型的关联催化剂特征与性能指标的数据积累,利用先进算法的数据驱动方法可能会加速识别最佳催化剂配方和几何形状。
