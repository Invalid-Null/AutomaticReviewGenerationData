Based on the details extracted from the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. An alumina-supported Pt catalyst shows good ability to activate C-H bonds while minimizing C-C bond cleavage, which is vital for selective PDH [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. Adding promoters such as H2S [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333] and boron [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548] to supported Pt catalysts has been found to increase propylene selectivity and stability. Encapsulating Pt clusters in microporous supports like germanosilicate zeolites via strong Pt-O-support bonds also imparts exceptional stability [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7].

Comparisons of supported monometallic Pt catalysts to other compositions provide further insights. Pt/Al2O3 showed lower activity and selectivity than RhCu single atom alloy catalysts [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389] and optimized PtSn catalysts [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg7894, DOI: 10.1016/S1385-8947(02)00158-4] for PDH under similar conditions. Data also reveals inferior performance of Pt versus PtZn [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631] and Pt3Sn [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639] sites. Added promoters like Sn [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1997.1531] and Pb [DOI: 10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9] help isolate highly selective Pt sites.

Besides Pt, a few studies provide insights on other supported metals. Pd, Rh, and Ir are noted to have high intrinsic activity, but are still too expensive for widespread PDH use [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. Supported monometallic Ru nanoparticles also show very low propylene selectivity around 4.5%, far inferior to Pt and optimized Ru phosphide nanoparticles [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667].

In summary, Pt-based catalysts, especially with promoters and optimized metal-support interactions, currently appear most promising for selective and stable PDH among the supported mono-metallic catalysts studied thus far. But continued research may uncover improved compositions and architectures.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The studies reviewed underscore the promise but also limitations of existing mono-metallic catalysts for commercial PDH applications. While Pt demonstrates good intrinsic activity, deactivation via coking and agglomeration remains a key challenge needing mitigation. Promoters help enhance selectivity and stability, but optimal combinations for extended lifetime remain to be found. Emerging single atom alloy compositions also warrant further attention. Moreover, little work has focused on economic alternatives to noble metals like Pt. Expanded explorations of more affordable metals combined with bespoke supports and modulation of metal-support interfaces could uncover composites balancing activity, selectivity and stability for feasible propane dehydrogenation.

<Chinese>
根据文件提取的详细信息,已有几项研究探索了负载单金属Pt、Pd和Ir作为丙烷脱氢(PDH)的催化剂。

文献报道,Pt基催化剂是最广泛使用和商业上最成功的PDH催化剂之一 [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。负载在氧化铝上的Pt催化剂表现出良好的C-H键活化能力和最小的C-C键断裂,这对选择性PDH至关重要 [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。在负载Pt催化剂上添加促进剂,如H2S [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333] 和硼 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548],被发现可以提高丙烯选择性和稳定性。将Pt簇封装在微孔支撑材料(如硅锗分子筛)中的氧与支撑材料的强绑定也可使其在PDH反应中表现出异常的稳定性 [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。 

与其他组成的负载单金属Pt催化剂的比较可以提供进一步的信息。在相似条件下,Pt/Al2O3相对于RhCu单原子合金催化剂 [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389] 和优化的PtSn催化剂[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg7894, DOI: 10.1016/S1385-8947(02)00158-4]显示出更低的活性和选择性。数据也显示Pt相对于PtZn [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631] 和Pt3Sn [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]位点的性能较差。添加促进剂如Sn [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1997.1531] 和Pb [DOI: 10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9] 有助于隔离高选择性的Pt位点。

除Pt以外,少数几项研究提供了其他负载金属催化剂的信息。文献指出,Pd、Rh和Ir具有高的内在活性,但价格仍然过高,无法大规模应用于PDH [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。负载单金属Ru纳米粒子的丙烯选择性也非常低,仅约4.5%,远低于Pt和优化的Ru磷化物纳米粒子 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]。

总的来说,在迄今为止研究的负载单金属催化剂中,掺杂促进剂和优化金属与支撑材料的相互作用的Pt基催化剂,目前似乎是PDH选择性和稳定性最有希望的。但持续的研究可能会发现更好的组成和结构。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:所审阅的研究突出了现有单金属催化剂在商业PDH应用中的潜力和局限性。 尽管Pt表现出良好的内在活性,但焦炭和聚集导致的失活仍然是一个关键的挑战,需要得到缓解。促进剂有助于提高选择性和稳定性,但要实现扩展使用寿命还需要找到最佳的组合。新出现的单原子合金组成也值得进一步关注。此外,很少有工作关注Pt等贵金属的经济替代品。扩大研究范围至更具备成本效益的金属,并与定制的支撑材料和调控金属与支撑材料界面的相互作用的复合材料,可能会带来兼顾活性、选择性与稳定性的丙烷脱氢催化剂。
