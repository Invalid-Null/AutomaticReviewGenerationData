Based on the details extracted from the files, catalysts facilitate propane dehydrogenation by providing alternative reaction pathways and active sites that lower the activation energy barriers [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008, 10.1021/acscatal.0c01580]. Industrial PDH processes utilize supported metal catalysts like Pt/Al2O3 and CrOx/Al2O3, where the metal sites activate C-H bonds in propane [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008, 10.1021/acscatal.0c01580]. DFT calculations have provided insights into specific active site structures and mechanisms; for example, showing how two neighboring coordinatively unsaturated Zr cations in ZrO2 catalysts facilitate dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.0c01580]. Additionally, the incorporation of metals like Sn into zeolite frameworks creates Lewis acid sites that are able to catalyze propane dehydrogenation [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019]. The shale gas boom has provided inexpensive propane feedstocks, enabling commercialization of catalytic PDH processes [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008].

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The reviewed studies highlight how rational catalyst design and characterization techniques like DFT calculations can provide fundamental insights into active site structures and mechanisms enabling propane dehydrogenation. As PDH processes are commercialized to meet propylene demand, further research should focus on designing optimized multifunctional catalysts and processes. Key priorities are enhancing selectivity, stability and regenerability while minimizing environmental impacts. Emerging areas include bi-functional catalysts, additive engineering approaches, and integrated reaction-regeneration cycling. Broader adoption of PDH also relies on optimization across the value chain from shale gas production to propylene end use.

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:回顾的研究突出了如何通过DFT计算等理性催化剂设计和表征技术来深入了解促进丙烷脱氢所需的活性位点结构和机理。随着PDH工艺的商业化满足丙烯需求,进一步的研究应专注于设计优化的多功能催化剂和工艺。关键优先事项是提高选择性、稳定性和再生能力,同时最大限度地减少环境影响。新兴领域包括双功能催化剂、添加剂工程方法和集成反应-再生循环。 PDH的更广泛采用也依赖于从页岩气生产到丙烯最终使用的整个价值链的优化。
