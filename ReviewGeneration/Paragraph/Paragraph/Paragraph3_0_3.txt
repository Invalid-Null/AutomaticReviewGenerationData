Three major catalyst classes explored for propane dehydrogenation are introduced in the file: metal oxides, supported metals, and zeolites. 

Specific metal oxide catalysts include VOx/CrOx supported on MCM-41 and SBA-15 mesoporous silica [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816], Sn-incorporated hexagonal mesoporous silica [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816], and Co-incorporated mesoporous silica [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. Commercial scale metal oxide catalysts based on chromium oxide are also used [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00189].

Supported metal catalysts referenced are silica-supported PtSn [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939] and single atom Pt catalysts on nitrogen- or boron-doped graphene [10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63196-1]. Commercially used supported metal catalysts are platinum-based [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008].  

Zeolite catalysts include H-Beta [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019] and iron-silicate zeolites [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019], which facilitate propane dehydrogenation through isolated iron sites.

※※※

The file reveals several promising catalyst classes and formulations for propane dehydrogenation, poised to overcome limitations of conventional options. Continued innovation in mesoporous supports, single atom catalysts, and zeolite hosts offers newfound selectivity, stability and cost-effectiveness. An integrated theoretical and experimental approach, systematically screening novel compositions like bimetallics and trimetallics across various substrates, promises to unravel optimal synergies for this crucial process. Persisting bottlenecks around commercial implementation and scalability invite techno-economic assessments alongside intensified flow chemistry configurations.

介绍了三大用于丙烷脱氢的催化剂类:金属氧化物、负载金属和分子筛。

特定的金属氧化物催化剂包括:负载在MCM-41和SBA-15介孔硅上的VOx/CrOx [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]、六方介孔硅嵌入的Sn [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]和介孔硅嵌入的Co [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。商业化的金属氧化物催化剂也在使用,以铬氧化物为基础[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00189]。


提到的负载金属催化剂有:负载在硅上的PtSn [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939]和负载在氮掺杂或硼掺杂石墨烯上的单原子Pt [10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63196-1]。商业上使用的负载金属催化剂以铂为基础 [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]。

分子筛催化剂包括:H-Beta [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019] 和铁硅酸盐分子筛 [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019],这些催化剂通过离子铁位点促进丙烷脱氢。

※※※

文件揭示了几种有前景的丙烷脱氢催化剂类别和配方,有望克服常规选择的局限。继续在介孔支撑体、单原子催化剂和分子筛载体方面的创新为选择性、稳定性和经济性带来了新的机遇。综合的理论和实验方法,有系统地在各种底物上筛选新的成分如双金属和三金属,有望揭示这个关键过程的最佳协同效应。围绕商业实现和可扩展性的持续瓶颈邀请了技术经济评估以及强化流化学配置。
