<English>
Thermally induced deactivation remains a major issue with propane dehydrogenation catalysts despite decades of commercialization, as noted by [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]. Exploring catalysts with improved stability and propylene selectivity is an urgent need to meet industrial demand. Replacing expensive platinum-based catalysts with lower cost, higher stability alternatives is also still a long-term goal, as described in [10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1]. 

Additionally, [10.1021/acscatal.1c05724] highlights that definitive characterization of the most active gallium species in terms of composition and structure at real catalyst loadings remains a gap needing further research. Controversy exists regarding assignments of proposed IR bands and interpretation of X-ray absorption spectroscopy data for key intermediate Ga species.

Other persistent challenges include gaining a more complete understanding of coke formation kinetics [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331], overcoming equilibrium limitations and undesired side reactions at higher temperatures [10.1021/cr5002436], and elucidating the nature of active chromium sites as noted in [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and findings discussed, promising opportunities exist to explore novel single atom and confined cluster architectures, leverage computational modeling to prescreen materials, and design efficient non-precious metal catalysts. An interdisciplinary approach coupling spectroscopy, microscopy, and theory may prove fruitful. Operando methods could offer additional insights into working catalysts. Broader adoption of unconventional synthesis techniques and high-throughput experimentation could accelerate discovery. Ultimately, continued efforts to enhance catalytic performance while reducing costs and environmental impacts will be vital.

尽管商业化使用已有几十年历史,但热致失活仍然是丙烷脱氢催化剂的一个大问题,如[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]所述。 探索具有高稳定性和丙烯选择性的催化剂仍然迫切需要满足工业需求。 如[10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1]中所述,用低成本、高稳定性的替代品替换昂贵的铂基催化剂仍然是一个长期目标。

 此外,[10.1021/acscatal.1c05724] 强调,在实际的催化剂负载下,最活性的镓种的确切表征在组成和结构上仍有差距有待进一步研究。 对提出的红外光谱带的归属和对关键中间镓种的X射线吸收光谱数据的解释存在争议。

 其他持续存在的挑战还包括更全面地理解焦炭形成动力学[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331], 克服平衡限制和高温下不 desired副反应[10.1021/cr5002436], 以及阐明[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]中提出的主动铬位点的性质。
