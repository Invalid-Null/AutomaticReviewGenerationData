These studies calculated reaction and activation energies using methods like CI-NEB transition state searches. The computations quantitatively relate catalytic activity/selectivity to electronic structure descriptors like the d-band center.

Overall, these examples showcase how DFT offers quantitative insights into structure-function relationships in propane dehydrogenation that can guide rational catalyst design. The electronic effects, adsorption modes, active site types, and kinetic barriers calculated provide a molecular-level picture of the reaction mechanisms.
