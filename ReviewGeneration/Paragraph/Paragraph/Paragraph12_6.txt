Based on the details extracted from the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic platinum (Pt), palladium (Pd), and iridium (Ir) catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts have received significant attention, as Pt shows superior capability for C-H bond activation while minimizing undesired C-C bond scission [Reference [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]]. Supported Pt catalysts, including Pt/Al2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03381, 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X], Pt/BN [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008], and Pt/zeolites [10.1038/s41467-018-07502-5, 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7], have demonstrated promising PDH performance. Key findings include:

- Atomic layer deposition (ALD) Al2O3 or TiO2 overcoats on Pt/Al2O3 extrudates increased PDH yield and selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391].  

- Adding 1 wt% boron promoter to Pt/γ-Al2O3 reduced coking 3-fold and boosted propylene selectivity from 90% to 98% over 12 hours [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548].

- Confining sub-nanometric Pt clusters in Ge-UTL zeolite micropores via Pt-O-Ge bonds gave a catalyst (Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL) with exceptional stability - no deactivation after 100 hours on stream [10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7].

Data also shows Pt/Al2O3 outperformed NiMo/Al2O3 for PDH under similar conditions, but had poorer selectivity versus optimized RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalysts [10.1021/acscatal.0c05711, 10.1126/science.abg8389].

Besides Pt, a few studies explored Pd- and Ir-based catalysts. Pd, Rh, Ir are noted to have high intrinsic PDH activity, but remain costly options [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. No thorough evaluations of supported Pd or Ir catalysts are provided.

Overall, supported mono-metallic Pt catalysts, especially on Al2O3 and zeolite supports, have demonstrated promising capability for selective and stable PDH operation. Further tailoring metal-support interfaces and electronic properties via promoters or overcoats presents opportunities to enhance Pt's function. Evaluations of supported Pd and Ir are lacking and warrant future attention.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The collected insights spotlight supported mono-metallic Pt catalysts as frontrunners for commercial PDH processes, corroborating their widespread use. While Pt dominates current efforts, emerging single atom alloy systems with tuned metal-support couplings illustrate untapped potential beyond conventional Pt nanoparticles. Capitalizing on these early advances, future work might explore:

- Novel supports like metal-organic frameworks to manipulate Pt dispersion and metal-support interfaces
- Combining computational screening with high-throughput experiments to rapidly identify optimized mono- and multi-metallic formulations
- In-depth analysis of electronic and geometric effects governing catalyst restructuring, coking, and stability under aggressive PDH environments

Such undertakings can expand the palette of selective and robust PDH catalysts, moving beyond conventional Pt benchmarks.

根据文件提取的详细信息,已经有几项研究探索了载体支撑的单金属铂(Pt)、钯(Pd)和铱(Ir)催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

Pt基催化剂受到了广泛关注,因为Pt显示出卓越的C-H键活化能力,同时将不希望发生的C-C键断裂最小化 [参考文献[10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]]。 已经证明载体支撑的Pt催化剂,包括Pt/Al2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03381, 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X], Pt/BN [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]和Pt/分子筛[10.1038/s41467-018-07502-5,10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]在PDH中显示出有前途的性能。 主要发现包括:

 - 原子层沉积(ALD) Al2O3或TiO2外壳在Pt/Al2O3挤出催化剂上增加了PDH产率和选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。

 - 在Pt/γ-Al2O3上加入1 wt%硼促进剂,12小时内可将结焦降低3倍,丙烯选择性从90%提高到98% [10.1021/ acscatal.9b05548]。

 - 通过Pt-O-Ge键将亚纳米Pt簇限域在Ge-UTL分子筛微孔内,得到的催化剂(Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL)具有异常稳定性 - 100小时内无活性衰减[10.1038/s41929-023- 00968-7]。

数据也表明,在相似条件下,Pt/Al2O3的PDH性能优于NiMo/Al2O3,但选择性较低于优化的RhCu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂[10.1021/acscatal.0c05711, 10.1126/science.abg8389]。

除Pt之外,少数几项研究探索了Pd和Ir基催化剂。 文献指出Pd、Rh和Ir具有高固有的PDH活性,但仍然是昂贵的选择[10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。 没有提供详细评价载体支撑的Pd或Ir催化剂。

总的来说,尤其是载于Al2O3 和分子筛载体上的单金属Pt催化剂,已经展示出选择性和稳定的PDH运行的有前途的能力。通过促进剂或外层进一步调控金属与载体的界面和电子学性质为提高Pt的功能提供了机会。 目前很少对载体支撑的Pd和Ir催化剂进行评价,这值得未来关注。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 收集的见解突出了载体单金属Pt催化剂作为商业PDH工艺的先锋,这也证实了它们的广泛使用。 尽管Pt主导着当前的研究工作,但调控过的金属与载体耦合的新兴单原子合金系统展示了超越常规Pt纳米粒子的未开发潜力。 在这些早期进展的基础上,未来的工作可能会探索:

- 新型载体,如金属有机框架,以操纵Pt分散和金属与载体的界面

- 结合计算筛选与高通量实验来快速确定优化的单金属和多金属配方

- 深入分析电子和几何效应如何制约催化剂重构、结焦和在严苛PDH环境下的稳定性

这种工作可以扩大选择性和稳定PDH催化剂的范围,突破常规的Pt基准。
