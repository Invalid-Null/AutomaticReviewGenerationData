Pt-Sn Catalysts

Adding Sn as a promoter increases propylene selectivity and stability while decreasing coke formation and catalyst deactivation compared to monometallic Pt catalysts [Placeholder_of_DOI3], [Placeholder_of_DOI4]. This is attributed to electronic and ensemble effects where Sn modifies the electron density of Pt active sites, improving selectivity towards propylene over cracking reactions [Placeholder_of_DOI5]. Sn also blocks certain ensemble sites responsible for coke formation [Placeholder_of_DOI4]. However, some studies note initial activity can be lowered with high Sn content as it increases the barrier for propane dissociative adsorption [Placeholder_of_DOI6]. An optimal Pt:Sn ratio around 2:1 to 3:1 balances activity and stability [Placeholder_of_DOI7]. The industrial Oleflex process uses PtSn/Al2O3 catalysts [Placeholder_of_DOI8].

Pt-Ga Catalysts 
Pt-Ga catalysts have also shown excellent potential for propane dehydrogenation. Ga improves dispersion and stabilization of Pt active sites, achieving close to equilibrium conversion levels [Placeholder_of_DOI9]. Selectivity above 99% has been reported for Pt-Ga catalysts with various supports like Al2O3 and zeolites [Placeholder_of_DOI10]. The electronic and structural changes induced by Ga are suggested to suppress side reactions including coke formation [Placeholder_of_DOI11]. However, studies directly comparing Pt-Ga and Pt-Sn performance are lacking.

Pt-Zn Catalysts
Several recent studies have focused on Pt-Zn catalysts. Isolating Pt atoms within zeolite-confined Zn nests gave exceptionally high stability [Placeholder_of_DOI12]. PtZn intermetallic nanoparticles can also achieve remarkable durability along with high activity and selectivity [Placeholder_of_DOI13]. The ordered intermetallic structure helps minimize the ensemble size [Placeholder_of_DOI14]. A moderate Pt:Zn ratio balances performance, similar to Pt:Sn [Placeholder_of_DOI15]. PtZn catalysts remain relatively underexplored and may hold great promise.

In summary, alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, or Zn has been clearly demonstrated to improve propane dehydrogenation performance through electronic, ensemble and structural effects. Each alloy uniquely impacts selectivity, activity and stability. Continued research to elucidate the fundamental origins of these synergies can further advance these promising catalysts.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: 

The detailed analysis of Pt alloy catalysts reveals remarkable strides in propane dehydrogenation efficiency enabled by modulating Pt's electronic and geometric structure through secondary metals. However, open quests persist regarding deciphering intricate structure-performance correlations across length scales. Advanced in-situ spectroscopy techniques coupled with theoretical modeling can offer pivotal insights into discerning active sites and delineating deactivation pathways. An emerging horizon encompasses extending beyond typical supports using zeolitic architectures with germane pore environments that can stabilize single sites. Furthermore, while Pt-based materials currently dominate, expanding the arsenal of selective and regenerable non-precious metal catalysts can spur more sustainable and economical processes. Overall, cross-pollinating knowledge from adjacent disciplines and adopting cross-functional research methodologies harbors vast untapped potential.

双金属和合金催化剂 - Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

Pt-Sn催化剂
几项研究评估了Pt-Sn双金属催化剂用于丙烷脱氢,发现Pt与Sn合金化可以显著改善性能[Placeholder_of_DOI1]、[Placeholder_of_DOI2]。将Sn作为掺杂剂增加了丙烯的选择性和稳定性,同时减少了焦炭形成和催化剂失活,与单金属Pt催化剂相比[Placeholder_of_DOI3]、[Placeholder_of_DOI4]。这归因于电子和团簇效应,Sn改变了Pt活性位点的电子密度,提高了丙烯而不是裂化反应的选择性[Placeholder_of_DOI5]。Sn还会屏蔽某些负责焦炭生成的团簇位点[Placeholder_of_DOI4]。然而,一些研究表明,随着Sn含量的增加,初始活性会降低,因为它会增加丙烷解离吸附的屏障[Placeholder_of_DOI6]。Pt:Sn比率约为2:1至3:1可以平衡活性和稳定性[Placeholder_of_DOI7]。工业Oleflex工艺使用PtSn/Al2O3催化剂[Placeholder_of_DOI8]。

Pt-Ga催化剂
Pt-Ga催化剂在丙烷脱氢方面也显示出了极大的潜力。 Ga改善了Pt活性位点的分散和稳定,实现接近平衡转化水平[Placeholder_of_DOI9]。在Al2O3和沸石等不同载体上的Pt-Ga催化剂报道了99%以上的选择性[Placeholder_of_DOI10]。 Ga诱导的电子和结构变化可能会抑制副反应,包括焦炭形成[Placeholder_of_DOI11]。然而,直接比较Pt-Ga和Pt-Sn性能的研究还不足。

Pt-Zn催化剂
最近的几项研究关注Pt-Zn催化剂。 将Pt原子隔离在分子筛内的Zn巢中,实现了异常高的稳定性[Placeholder_of_DOI12]。 PtZn金属间化合物纳米粒子也可以实现卓越的稳定性和高活性及选择性[Placeholder_of_DOI13]。 有序的金属间化合物结构有助于最小化组合场[Placeholder_of_DOI14]。 适度的Pt:Zn比例可以平衡性能,类似于Pt:Sn[Placeholder_of_DOI15]。 PtZn催化剂仍然相对未被充分开发,可能具有巨大的前景。

总之,使用Sn、Ga或Zn合金化Pt已经明确地证明可以通过电子、组合和结构效应改善丙烷脱氢性能。 每种合金对选择性、活性和稳定性的影响都是独特的。 持续的研究可以进一步推动这些有前途的催化剂,阐明这些协同效应的基本起源。
