 Here is a draft review section on propane dehydrogenation catalysts with a focus on using high-throughput computations to accelerate catalyst screening:

<English>
A recent study utilizes a descriptor-based microkinetic analysis combined with DFT calculations to systematically screen potential intermetallic compound catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]. By considering both terraces and steps in modeling the catalytic behavior, over 2000 catalyst candidates are assessed. This high-throughput computational approach enables accelerated screening to identify inexpensive and environmentally friendly Fe-Ga intermetallics as promising PDH catalysts. 

Additional works highlight the promise of leveraging computational techniques like machine learning and descriptor-based strategies to expedite PDH catalyst design [10.1021/acscatal.0c05711]. With frameworks correlating catalytic activity to key surface properties, vast materials spaces can be scanned to pinpoint optimal catalyst formulations. Further advancement of these methods and expansion to screen catalyst libraries like trimetallic alloys is expected to uncover novel active and selective PDH catalysts.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The discussed studies reveal the potential for high-throughput computations to enormously accelerate the identification and optimization of propane dehydrogenation catalysts. By screening thousands to millions of candidates, novel catalyst compositions and geometries inexpensively and rapidly, optimal catalysts can be discovered orders of magnitude faster. Future undertakings may focus on expanding machine learning algorithms for autonomous catalyst screening and on-the-fly active learning. Researchers could also leverage simulations in tandem with robotic high-throughput experimentation to close-the-loop on catalyst design.
</English>

<Chinese>
一项最近的研究利用基于描述符的微动力学分析结合DFT计算,以系统地筛选丙烷脱氢(PDH)的潜在中间合金化合物催化剂[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]。通过在建模催化行为时同时考虑台阶和阶梯,评估了2000多种催化剂候选材料。这种高通量计算方法可以加速筛选以识别廉价和环保的Fe-Ga中间金属化合物,作为有前途的PDH催化剂。

另外一些研究也强调了利用计算技术如机器学习和基于描述符的策略来加速PDH催化剂设计的前景[10.1021/acscatal.0c05711]。通过将催化活性与关键表面属性相关联的框架,可以扫描巨大的材料空间以定位最佳的催化剂配方。这些方法的进一步发展和扩展到三元合金类催化剂库的筛选,有望发现新的活性好和选择性高的 PDH催化剂。  

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:讨论的研究揭示了高通量计算有潜力大大加速丙烷脱氢催化剂的识别和优化。通过筛选成千上万种候选材料,可以低成本地快速发现新的催化剂组成和结构,从而使最佳催化剂的发现速度提高几个数量级。未来的工作可能会关注扩展机器学习算法以实现催化剂的自动筛选和实时主动学习。研究人员还可以利用模拟与高通量机器人实验平台的结合,实现催化剂设计的闭环优化。
</Chinese>
