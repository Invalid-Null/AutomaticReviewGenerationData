	<English>
		Isolated metal sites anchored to zeolitic frameworks have attracted interest as single-site catalysts for alkane dehydrogenation reactions. As discussed by Schreiber et al. [10.1002/anie.200702463], zeolites like H-ZSM-5 can be used to stabilize isolated Ga+ active sites by quantitatively replacing Brønsted protons with gallium cations. Dealumination techniques are also widely employed to generate vacant lattice sites in zeolites like Beta [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103], which act as anchors for isolated metal species. Sun et al. infiltrated cobalt compounds at the resulting hydroxyl nests in dealuminated Si-Beta. Isolated Co2+ sites stabilized by zeolite confinement were the active phase in subsequent propane dehydrogenation. Besides dealumination, direct hydrothermal synthesis allows trapping isolated metal ions like Zn2+ at cation exchange positions in the micropores of S-1 [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034]. Ligand additives like ethylenediamine help prevent aggregation. Chemical vapor deposition (CVD) using precursors like dimethylzinc is an alternative [10.1021/cs200441e], depositing isolated zinc methyl species that transform into Zn2+ sites upon high-temperature hydrogen treatment. In all cases, advanced spectroscopy and microscopy confirm the single-atom dispersion and isolated nature of the active sites.
		※※※
		The described methods exemplify the versatility in synthesizing isolated metal sites in zeolites for dehydrogenation catalysis. Dealumination, ligand protection, and CVD allow precise control over the coordination environment, optimizing activity and stability. Further work should focus on expanding the portfolio of zeolite topologies and transition metals amenable to single-site isolation. Spectroscopic tracking during catalyst synthesis and testing would reveal the nature of the active sites under working conditions. Operando methods could also correlate single-site speciation with dehydrogenation performance.
	</English>
	<Chinese>
		锚定在沸石框架上的隔离金属位点已引起人们作为单位点催化剂用于烷烃脱氢反应的兴趣。正如 Schreiber 等人 [10.1002/anie.200702463] 所讨论的,像 H-ZSM-5 这样的沸石可以用定量地用镓离子替换 Brønsted 质子来稳定隔离的 Ga+ 活性位点。提炼技术也被广泛使用生成沸石如 Beta [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103] 中的空位点,这些位点作为隔离金属物种的锚点。Sun等人渗透了氧化钴化合物到去氧硅铝Beta产生的羟基窝中。经沸石限域稳定的 Co2+ 隔离位点是后续丙烷脱氢中活性相。除了提炼之外,直接的热合成允许在限域多孔 S-1 [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034] 中捕获像 Zn2+ 这样的隔离金属离子到交换位点上。如乙二胺等配体添加剂有助于防止聚集。化学气相沉积(CVD)使用前驱物质如二甲基锌 [10.1021/cs200441e] 是另一种选择,它沉积隔离的锌甲基,并在高温氢气处理下转化为 Zn2+。 在所有情况下,先进的光谱与显微镜确认金属的单原子分散与隔离性。
		※※※
		这些方法证明了合成沸石中用于脱氢催化的隔离金属位点的通用性。提炼、配体保护以及CVD允许精确地控制配位环境,优化活性与稳定性。进一步的工作应该集中在扩大适合单位隔离的沸石拓扑结构与过渡金属的组合。在催化剂的合成与测试期间跟踪光谱会显示工作条件下活性位点的本质。根据位点组分动态变化建立操作规律也将有助于关联单个位点与脱氢性能。
