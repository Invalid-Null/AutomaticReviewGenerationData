 <English>

Propane dehydrogenation is an important industrial process for production of propylene, a major building block in the petrochemical and polymer industries. Metal oxides are a promising class of catalysts for propane dehydrogenation due to their high activity, selectivity, and stability. This review summarizes recent progress on metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, with a focus on Cr2O3, Pt-Sn, and Ga2O3 catalysts based on the provided file.  

Chromium oxide (Cr2O3) is one of the most extensively studied metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. Cr2O3 shows high catalytic activity, especially when modified by other metal oxides like ZnO [DOI]. Doping Cr2O3 with Pt is also effective for improving catalytic performance [DOI]. Studies using kinetic Monte Carlo simulations and microkinetic modeling provide mechanistic insights into propane and butane dehydrogenation pathways over Cr2O3 surfaces [DOI]. While deactivation due to coking remains an issue, Cr2O3 is considered among the most promising options for oxidative dehydrogenation of propane.

Bimetallic Pt-Sn catalysts are the basis for many commercial propane dehydrogenation processes. Supported Pt-Sn nanoparticles on various oxide supports like silica and alumina demonstrate excellent activity and propylene selectivity [DOI]. Formation of intermetallic Pt3Sn sites is found to be critical for achieving high performance by enhancing propylene desorption and suppressing coke formation [DOI]. Stabilizing Pt-Sn sites in frameworks like zeolite Beta gives exceptional stability with near complete propylene selectivity [DOI]. The combination of activity, selectivity and regenerability makes Pt-Sn catalysts very promising for propane dehydrogenation.

Among gallium oxide polymorphs, β-Ga2O3 shows the highest activity for propane dehydrogenation, attributed to its surface acidity [DOI]. Supported β-Ga2O3 nanoparticles on silica are also selective dehydrogenation catalysts. The catalytic performance depends strongly on the Ga2O3 crystal structure and metal-support interactions. While less studied than Cr2O3 and Pt-Sn, Ga2O3 is a metal oxide with considerable potential for continued development as a propane dehydrogenation catalyst.

Overall, metal oxides including Cr2O3, Pt-Sn and Ga2O3 demonstrate encouraging properties as propane dehydrogenation catalysts. Advances in characterization techniques and theoretical modeling are enabling greater understanding of active sites and reaction mechanisms on these materials. With further progress in controlling metal-support interfaces and nanostructures, as well as mitigating deactivation pathways, metal oxides may find expanded roles in commercial propane-to-propylene processes.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Taking into consideration the advancements and findings discussed in the file, there lies an opportunity to explore emerging fields and innovative methodologies. Future research endeavors might focus on combining computational screening with high-throughput experimentation to rapidly identify and optimize novel metal oxide compositions, structures and synthetic methods. Operando characterization techniques can also elucidate metal-support interfacial effects and dynamic structural changes occurring under working conditions, allowing catalyst design principles to be established. Additionally, surface modification strategies like seed mediation could tailor active site structures to selectively activate C-H bonds in propane. Advances in these areas may enable rational design of stable and selective metal oxide catalysts customized for propane dehydrogenation. Broader adoption of earth-abundant metal oxides could make this a more sustainable and economical process as global propylene demand continues rising.

丙烷脱氢是用于生产丙烯的一个重要的工业过程,丙烯是石化和聚合物工业中的一个主要基础化学品。由于其高活性、选择性和稳定性,金属氧化物催化剂是丙烷脱氢非常有前景的一类催化剂。本综述根据所提供的文件,重点总结了最新关于丙烷脱氢金属氧化物催化剂的研究进展,特别是Cr2O3、Pt-Sn和Ga2O3催化剂。

铬氧化物(Cr2O3)是丙烷脱氢研究最广泛的金属氧化物催化剂之一。Cr2O3表现出高的催化活性,特别是在与其他金属氧化物如ZnO修饰时[DOI]。用Pt掺杂Cr2O3也能有效提高催化性能[DOI]。动力学Monte Carlo模拟和微动力学模型研究提供了Cr2O3表面上丙烷和丁烷脱氢的机理信息[DOI]。虽然deactivate仍然是一个问题,但Cr2O3被认为是丙烷氧化脱氢最有前景的选择之一。

双金属Pt-Sn催化剂是许多商业丙烷脱氢工艺的基础。受支持的Pt-Sn纳米粒子在各种氧化物支撑如二氧化硅和氧化铝上表现出优异的活性和丙烯选择性[DOI]。形成Pt3Sn金属间化合物被发现对实现高性能至关重要,因为它提高了丙烯脱附并抑制了焦炭生成[DOI]。在分子筛Beta等框架中稳定Pt-Sn活性中心使得催化剂具有异常的稳定性和近乎完全的丙烯选择性[DOI]。Pt-Sn催化剂卓越的活性,选择性和再生能力使其非常适合丙烷脱氢反应。

在低价锗氧化物多形体中,β-Ga2O3对丙烷脱氢活性最高,这归因于它的表面酸性[DOI]。受支持的β-Ga2O3纳米粒子在硅胶上的选择性脱氢催化剂。催化性能高度取决于Ga2O3的晶体结构和金属与载体的相互作用。虽然研究不如Cr2O3和Pt-Sn广泛,但Ga2O3作为丙烷脱氢催化剂仍有很大的继续发展的潜力。

总体而言,Cr2O3、Pt-Sn和Ga2O3等金属氧化物催化剂表现出令人鼓舞的丙烷脱氢催化性能。先进的表征技术和理论计算使得对这些材料的活性位点和反应机制有了更深入的理解。随着进一步控制金属与载体界面的结构和纳米结构,以及减轻失活途径,金属氧化物可能会在商业丙烷制丙烯工艺中找到更广泛的应用。

综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到文件中讨论的先进成果和发现,我们有机会探索新兴领域和创新方法。未来的研究努力可能会集中在利用计算筛选与高通量实验相结合,快速识别和优化新型金属氧化物组成、结构和合成方法。同时,原位表征技术也可以阐明金属与载体界面效应以及工作条件下发生的动态结构变化,从而建立催化剂设计原则。此外,表面修饰策略如种子介导法也可以调控活性位点的结构,选择性激活丙烷中的C-H键。这些领域的进步可能实现对稳定高选择性的金属氧化物催化剂的合理设计,定制丙烷脱氢反应。使用更多可获得的金属氧化物可能使这一过程在丙烯需求不断增长的情况下变得更加可持续和经济。
