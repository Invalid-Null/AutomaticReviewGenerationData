		Based on the details extracted from the file, some metal oxide catalysts like chromium oxide (Cr2O3) and gallium oxide (Ga2O3) have shown promising propane dehydrogenation activity. Specifically, Cr-based catalysts are reported to be among the most active catalysts for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation reactions, with CrOx being a key component of the commercial CATOFIN process [DOI1, DOI2]. Mesoporous silica-supported Cr also exhibits higher activity compared to other metal oxides like Ga, Ni, V, Fe, Mn, and Co [DOI2]. However, poor stability remains an issue for Cr-based catalysts. 
		Of the Ga2O3 polymorphs, β-Ga2O3 demonstrated the highest initial activity for propane dehydrogenation, correlating with its higher surface acidity [DOI3]. Supported γ-Ga2O3 also achieved comparable or higher propylene yield compared to common PtSn and CrOx/Al2O3 catalysts [DOI4]. The Ga2O3-ZrO2 mixed oxide system also revealed enhanced Lewis acidity and thus activity with increasing Ga content [DOI3]. These findings showcase the promise of Ga2O3-based catalysts through systematic tuning of composition, polymorphism, acid-base properties, and metal-support interactions.
		The bimetallic PtSn catalyst, where Pt likely serves as the active site, has been commercialized and exhibits high propane conversion (~40%) along with exceptional propylene selectivity (>90%) [DOI5, DOI6]. Stabilizing Pt on isolated Sn sites in zeolite frameworks further boosts stability and regenerability [DOI6]. Optimization of metal-support interactions also aids PtSn performance, evidenced by the superior productivity of PtSn supported on mesoporous SBA-15 silica compared to Pt/SBA-15 [DOI5].

		在Ga2O3多形体中,β-Ga2O3表现出最高的丙烷脱氢初始活性,这与其更高的表面酸度有关[DOI3]。 支持的γ-Ga2O3也实现了与常见的PtSn和CrOx/Al2O3催化剂相当或更高的丙烯收率[DOI4]。 当Ga含量增加时,Ga2O3-ZrO2混合氧化物系统也显示Lewis酸性增强,因此活性增强[DOI3]。 这些发现展示了通过Ga2O3基催化剂组成,多形性,酸碱性质和金属载体相互作用的系统调谐的前景。
		双金属PtSn催化剂在工业上已经商业化,并表现出高丙烷转化率(~40%)和异常优异的丙烯选择性(>90%)[DOI5,DOI6]。 稳定Pt位于分子筛框架中的隔离Sn位点上还可以进一步提高稳定性和再生能力[DOI6]。 优化金属载体相互作用也有助于PtSn性能的改善,这可以从PtSnSupported on SBA-15介孔硅上的优异生产力优于Pt/SBA-15 [DOI5]。
