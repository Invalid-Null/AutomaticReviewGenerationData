Based on the details extracted from the file, the article investigates catalyst preparation methods aimed at optimizing active phase dispersion for propane dehydrogenation catalysts. Specifically, some key methods discussed include:

Vacuum impregnation method to prepare Co@Si-BEA catalysts with varying cobalt loadings from 0.1-5 wt%. The 0.1-0.5 wt% samples showed the highest intrinsic activity due to well dispersed isolated Co2+ sites [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726].

Direct hydrothermal synthesis and template-ion exchange methods to incorporate metal ions into the framework or on the mesopore walls of MCM-41. Direct hydrothermal synthesis showed a tendency to incorporate metal ions inside the framework with good dispersion [10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014].  

Ligand-protected hydrothermal synthesis to construct isolated {(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2} motifs in Co-MFI, avoiding the formation of CoOx clusters or nanoparticles. The isolated Co-motifs showed much higher PDH activity [10.1021/jacs.2c02636].

Wetness impregnation with controlled metal precursor concentration and support interactions to achieve monolayer dispersion capacities of active phases like vanadium oxide [10.1021/acscatal.2c00878, 10.1002/anie.202005968] and molybdenum oxide [10.1006/jcat.2000.3125].

Surface organometallic chemistry using molecular precursors to install isolated metal sites [10.1021/acs.accounts.9b00138, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103].

Modifying supports to enhance metal-support interactions and active phase dispersion, through tuning surface functional groups or incorporating promoters like Ce, La, Zn and Ga [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.10.012, 10.1021/acscatal.8b04578, 10.1038/s41467-018-06967-8].

Utilizing nanosized, mesoporous, and high surface area supports like silica, MCM-41, SBA-15, MFI zeolites, and alumina to provide sites for uniform dispersion [10.1016/j.cej.2019.03.017, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01924].

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The literature reveals extensive efforts toward developing specialized catalyst preparation techniques for dispersing isolated active sites, tuned nanoclusters, and ultrasmall nanoparticles in selective catalytic frameworks. focus areas include ligand engineering, support functionalization, controlled impregnation, and in-situ hydrothermal routes. While much progress has been made, additional opportunities exist to explore preparation methods tailored for emerging supports like metal-organic frameworks and 2D heterogenous catalysts which offer high tunability. Novel in-situ characterization techniques can also shed light on active phase dynamics during preparation and under working conditions. Overall, the next horizon will likely witness the emergence of cutting-edge catalyst preparation technologies centered on atomic/molecular level customization for optimized active phase dispersion.

根据文件中提取的详细信息,该文献研究了旨在优化丙烷脱氢催化剂活性相分散的催化剂制备方法。 具体来说,讨论了一些关键方法,包括:

使用真空浸渍法制备 Co@Si-BEA 催化剂,Co 负载量从 0.1-5 wt% 不等。由于 Co2+ 位点分散良好,0.1-0.5 wt% 的样品显示出最高的固有活性 [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。

直接水热合成和模板离子交换法将金属离子引入 MCM-41 的骨架或微孔壁中。直接水合成方法倾向于将金属离子融入结构中,使其分散良好 [10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014]。

配体保护的水热合成方法构建 Co-MFI 中的 {(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2} 隔离基团,避免形成 CoOx 簇或纳米粒子。隔离的 Co 基团显示出比 CoOx 簇/纳米粒子更高的 PDH 活性 [10.1021/jacs.2c02636]。

控制金属前体浓度和载体交互作用的湿度浸渍法,实现如钒氧化物 [10.1021/acscatal.2c00878, 10.1002/anie.202005968] 和钼氧化物 [10.1006/jcat.2000.3125] 等活性相的单层分散量。

使用分子前体安装隔离的金属位点的表面有机金属化学方法 [10.1021/acs.accounts.9b00138, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]。

通过调谐表面官能团或掺入 Ce、La、Zn 和 Ga 等助剂来改性载体,以提高金属与载体之间的相互作用和活性相的分散 [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.10.012, 10.1021/acscatal.8b04578, 10.1038/s41467-018-06967-8]。

利用二氧化硅、MCM-41、SBA-15、MFI 分子筛和氧化铝等纳米级、介孔和高比表面的载体,提供均匀分散的位点 [10.1016/j.cej.2019.03.017, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01924]。

综合理解与前瞻展望:文献揭示了广泛的努力,以开发专门的催化剂制备技术,将隔离的活性位点、调谐的纳米簇和超小纳米粒子分散在选择性催化剂框架中。 主要关注领域包括配体工程、载体官能化、受控浸渍和原位水热路线。 尽管取得了很大进展,但仍有机会探索适用于新型载体(如金属有机框架和二维异质催化剂)的制备方法,这些载体具有高度的可调节性。 新颖的原位表征技术也可以深入了解活性相在制备和工作条件下的动态行为。 总体而言,下一步发展很可能见证出现围绕原子/分子层面定制的催化剂制备尖端技术,以实现活性相最佳分散。
