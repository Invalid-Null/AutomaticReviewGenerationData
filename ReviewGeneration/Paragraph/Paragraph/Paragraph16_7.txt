Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Pt-based catalysts have been extensively studied for propane dehydrogenation due to their high activity arising from excellent activation of C-H bonds [10.1021/acscatal.3c00548]. However, monometallic Pt catalysts suffer from issues like fast deactivation and lower selectivity. Alloying Pt with promotors like Sn, Ga, Zn has emerged as an effective strategy to enhance the catalytic performance.

Multiple studies have demonstrated that adding Sn as a promoter increases the propene selectivity and enhances the stability of Pt catalysts for propane dehydrogenation [[10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084]], [[10.1016/j.apcatb.2021.120731]]. The improved selectivity stems from Sn altering the electronic properties of Pt that promote selective C-H bond activation over undesired C-C bond rupture [10.1002/anie.201507119]. Additionally, Sn helps minimize coke formation, attributed to a "drain-off" effect where Sn sites capture coke precursors and prevent deposition on Pt sites causing deactivation [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018]. Investigations on supported PtSn nanoparticles reveal intermetallic Pt-Sn phases are critical to achieve high propylene selectivity along with stability [10.1126/science.abg7894].

Likewise, studies on Pt-Ga bimetallic catalysts demonstrate increased stability and propylene selectivity versus monometallic Pt. The Pt-Ga structure is proposed to suppress side reactions like coke formation and secondary hydrogenolysis that lower selectivity [10.1002/anie.202107210]. Highly active and selective Pt-Ga catalysts with PtGa intermetallic nanoparticles have been synthesized using strategies like encapsulating PtGa sites in zeolites [10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129].

Alloying Pt with Zn has also shown promise, where isolated Pt stabilized by neighbouring Zn atoms in the lattice of zeolite Beta achieved remarkable durability along with near equilibrium conversion for propane dehydrogenation [10.1021/jacs.1c10261]. Computational modelling indicates Zn selectively modifies the valence orbitals of Pt to promote selective dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103].

In summary, alloying Pt with secondary metals like Sn, Ga, Zn helps minimize the size of Pt ensembles while modifying geometric and electronic properties. This leads to enhanced selectivity and stability compared to monometallic Pt catalysts for propane dehydrogenation.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements made in developing highly selective and stable Pt alloy catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, further optimization can be targeted in aspects like economical usage of noble metals and operational simplicity. As greater mechanistic clarity is attained on the role of various promoters, future endeavors may focus on designing simplified single-site catalysts with isolated Pt sites coordinated by promoters. Machine learning techniques coupled with combinatorial high-throughput experiments also offer opportunities to rapidly screen and discover novel bimetallic formulations. Translation of fundamental advances to pilot-scale reactors can pave the path for wider commercial adoption. Broader implications lie in adapting these design strategies to produce value-added chemicals like light olefins from other alkanes.

双金属和合金催化剂 - Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

由于其优异的C-H键激活能力[10.1021/acscatal.3c00548],Pt基催化剂已广泛用于丙烷脱氢反应。然而,单金属Pt催化剂存在快速失活和选择性较低等问题。 使Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn等促进剂形成合金被证明是提高催化性能的有效策略。

多项研究表明,添加Sn作为促进剂可以提高Pt催化剂丙烯的选择性并增强其在丙烷脱氢反应中的稳定性 [[10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084]], [[10.1016/j.apcatb.2021.120731]]。 提高选择性源于Sn改变Pt的电子性质,促进选择性C-H键断裂而不是不需要的C-C键断裂 [10.1002/anie.201507119]。 此外,人们认为Sn有助于最小化焦炭生成,这被归因于“引流”效应,Sn位点捕获焦炭前体并防止沉积在Pt位点上导致失活 [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018]。 对负载PtSn纳米粒子的研究表明,Pt-Sn间金属间化合物阶段对于实现高丙烯选择性和稳定性至关重要 [10.1126/science.abg7894]。

类似地,Pt-Ga双金属催化剂的研究表明,与单金属Pt相比,其稳定性和丙烯选择性有所提高。 Pt-Ga结构被认为可以抑制副反应如焦炭形成和次生氢化裂解,从而提高选择性 [10.1002/anie.202107210]。 已成功合成高活性和高选择性的Pt-Ga催化剂,采用将PtGa金属间化合物纳米粒子封装在分子筛中的策略 [10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129]。

 Pt与Zn合金也具有前景,其中β分子筛中被相邻Zn原子稳定的隔离Pt原子实现了显着的稳定性和近平衡的丙烷转化率 [10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。 计算模型表明Zn可以选择性地修饰Pt的价层轨道以促进选择性脱氢 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]。

总之,Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn等副金属形成合金有助于最小化Pt组分的大小,同时改变其几何和电子性质。 这导致比单金属Pt催化剂更高的选择性和稳定性用于丙烷脱氢。

综合理解与前景展望:考虑到在开发高选择性和高��定性Pt合金丙烷脱氢催化剂方面所取得的进展,未来优化可关注诸如贵金属的经济使用和操作简单性等方面。随着对各促进剂作用机理的更深入理解,未来研究工作可能会关注设计简化的单中心催化剂,其中Pt位点由促进剂配位隔离。 机器学习技术结合组合高通量实验也提供了快速筛选和发现新型双金属配方的机会。 从基础到中试放大反应器的转化可以为更广泛的商业采用铺平道路。 更广泛的启示在于将这些设计策略应用于从其他烷烃生产增值化学品如轻烯烃。
