	Metal Oxide Catalysts - What metal oxides like Cr2O3, Ga2O3 have shown promising propane dehydrogenation activity?
	
	Among metal oxide catalysts studied for propane dehydrogenation (PDH), chromium oxide (Cr2O3) and gallium oxide (Ga2O3) have demonstrated promising catalytic activity. Cr-based catalysts are reported to exhibit high activity for the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane in the presence of CO2 [Placeholder_Of_DOI1]. The reduced α-Cr2O3(0001) surface has been computationally investigated for non-oxidative PDH using kinetic Monte Carlo simulations [Placeholder_Of_DOI2]. Experimentally, supported CrOx catalysts on SiO2 and Al2O3 show appreciable propene yields up to 30% [Placeholder_Of_DOI3]. Turning to gallium oxide, polymorphs of Ga2O3 including α-, β-, and γ-Ga2O3 were evaluated for PDH, with β-Ga2O3 demonstrating the best performance owing to optimal surface acid-base properties [Placeholder_Of_DOI4]. The yields of propene over Ga2O3 polymorphs exceed that over Cr2O3. Furthermore, partially substituting Zr4+ into Ga3+ sites in Ga2O3-ZrO2 mixed oxides increases Lewis acidity and consequently raises initial catalytic activity [Placeholder_Of_DOI5].
	
	In summary, both Cr and Ga oxide-based solid acids have exhibited promising capability for cleaving propane C-H bonds. Future research into tuning the oxidation state, coordinative unsaturation, polymorphism, and incorporation of secondary metal dopants in these metal oxides may uncover compositions boasting enhanced propene selectivity and long-term stability. Gaining deeper mechanistic clarity into the active sites and reaction intermediates through operando spectroscopic methods is also key.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The promising propane conversion activity demonstrated by Cr2O3 and Ga2O3 catalysts highlights the potential of metal oxides as alternatives to traditional supported noble metal PDH catalysts. Several research directions emerge to further advance metal oxide catalysts. Elucidating the impact of synthetic techniques on surface properties may enable rational design of highly active sites. Compositional modifications through secondary metal doping also warrant investigation. More broadly, expanding the materials search space beyond known oxides by leveraging high-throughput DFT screening and machine learning could unveil novel non-oxide or mixed metal/non-metal compositions with superior functionality. Operando characterization techniques to probe dynamic surface changes under working conditions will lend critical mechanistic insights. Overall, continued research in fundamentally understanding and purposefully tailoring metal oxide or related catalysts harbors possibilities for cost-effective and selective PDH processes.

	在评估用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)的金属氧化物催化剂中,氧化铬(Cr2O3)和氧化镓(Ga2O3)表现出有前途的催化活性。 在CO2存在下,报道Cr基催化剂表现出高的氧化脱氢活性[Placeholder_Of_DOI1]。 使用动力学蒙特卡罗模拟研究了还原α-Cr2O3(0001)表面用于非氧化PDH[Placeholder_Of_DOI2]。 实验上,受SiO2 和Al2O3 支撑的CrOx催化剂可实现高达30%的丙烯收率[Placeholder_Of_DOI3]。 对于氧化镓,对α-、β- 和γ-Ga2O3 多形进行了评估,其中β-Ga2O3 由于优良的表面酸碱性能表现最佳[Placeholder_Of_DOI4]。 Ga2O3多形体的丙烯收率超过Cr2O3。 此外,在 Ga2O3-ZrO2混合氧化物中部分替换Ga3+位点的Zr4+会增加路易斯酸性,从而提高初始催化活性[Placeholder_Of_DOI5]。

	总之,Cr和Ga氧化物固体酸都展现出有前景的丙烷C-H键裂解能力。 未来对这些金属氧化物氧化态、配位不饱和度、多形性和掺入二次金属掺杂剂的调控,可能会揭示组成更高的丙烯选择性和长期稳定性。 通过操作谱学方法深入了解活性中心和反应中间体的机理也很关键。  

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: Cr2O3和Ga2O3催化剂所展现的有前景的丙烷转化活性,凸显金属氧化物作为传统负载贵金属PDH催化剂的替代品的潜力。 出现几个研究方向以进一步推进金属氧化物催化剂。 澄清合成技术对表面性质的影响,可以实现对高活性位点的合理设计。 通过二次金属掺杂的成分调控也值得研究。 更广泛地,通过运用高通量DFT筛选和机器学习,扩大材料搜索空间,超出已知氧化物,可以揭示具有优越功能的新型非氧化物或混合金属/非金属成分。 操作表征技术来检测工作条件下的动态表面变化,将提供关键的机制信息。 总的来说,继续研究从根本上了解和有目的地调控金属氧化物或相关催化剂,孕育实现成本效益高和高选择性PDH工艺的可能性。
