Based on the details extracted from the references, mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over various metal oxide catalysts, with some key aspects summarized below:

On Cr-based catalysts, active sites have been proposed to include Cr3+ species [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1183], coordinatively unsaturated Cr3+ [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014], and CrOx sites going through Cr6+/Cr3+ or Cr3+/Cr2+ redox cycles [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Key reaction steps often involve hydrogen abstraction by lattice oxygen.

For V-based catalysts, V3+ sites [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.6b00893, 10.1021/jacs.9b09235] as well as isolated V5+ sites going through a Mars-van Krevelen cycle [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023, 10.1006/jcat.1999.2510] have been proposed. Reaction steps involve oxidative dehydrogenation followed by catalyst reoxidation.

Over ZrO2, coordinatively unsaturated Zr cations neighboring oxygen vacancies [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012, 10.1002/anie.201508731] catalyze propane dehydrogenation through homolytic or heterolytic C-H bond cleavage. 

For Ga-based catalysts, tetrahedral Ga3+ sites are often cited, with a heterolytic dissociative pathway proposed [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017, 10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]. Ga+ sites can also activate propane via oxidative addition [DOI: 10.1021/jacs.2c03941].

Additionally, synergistic active sites like Zn-Zr [DOI: 10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074] and MnOx-CeO2 interfaces [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c02782] have been proposed to facilitate key reaction steps.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Significant advancements have been made in unraveling mechanistic intricacies over various metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, providing vital clues into the nature of active sites and Construction of multifunctional single sites through judicious engineering of metals and ligands can open up new horizons. Furthermore, operando spectroscopic techniques combined with theoretical simulations can offer unprecedented glimpses into transient intermediates and dynamic structure-functionality interconnections guiding rational design.

根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,已经为多种金属氧化物催化剂的丙烷脱氢反应提出了机理场景,其中一些关键方面总结如下:

在Cr基催化剂上,活性位点被提出包括 Cr3+ 物种 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]、配位不饱和的Cr3+ [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]以及经历 Cr6+/Cr3+ 或 Cr3+/Cr2+ 氧化还原循环的 CrOx 位点 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。关键反应步骤通常涉及晶格氧抽提氢原子。

对于V基催化剂,V3+位点 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.6b00893, 10.1021/jacs.9b09235] 以及经历Mars-van Krevelen循环的隔离的V5+位点 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023, 10.1006/jcat.1999.2510] 已经被提出。反应步骤涉及氧化脱氢,然后是催化剂氧化。

在ZrO2上,配位不饱和的锆阳离子邻近氧空位 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012, 10.1002/anie.201508731],通过均裂或异裂C-H键的断裂催化丙烷脱氢。

对于Ga基催化剂,四面体的Ga3+位点经常被引用,并提出了一种异裂解的解离途径 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017, 10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]。 Ga+位点也可以通过氧化加成激活丙烷[DOI: 10.1021/jacs.2c03941]。

此外,协同活性位点如Zn-Zr [DOI: 10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074] 和 MnOx-CeO2 界面的 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c02782]也已被证明可以促进关键的反应步骤。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 在揭示各种金属氧化物催化剂上丙烷脱氢反应的机理复杂性方面已经取得了重大进展,这为活性位点的性质以及反应途径提供了至关重要的线索。通过策略性地设计金属和配体,可以开辟构建多功能单一位点的新领域。此外,操作谱技术与理论模拟的结合可以提供前所未有的瞬态中间体和动态结构-功能关系的示踪,以指导合理的设计。
