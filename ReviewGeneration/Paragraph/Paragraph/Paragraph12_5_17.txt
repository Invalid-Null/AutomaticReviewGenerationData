Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts. For example, Pt/Al2O3 is a vital PDH catalyst component due to Pt's ability to activate C-H bonds while minimizing C-C cleavage [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. One study showed an ALD Al2O3 or TiO2 overcoat on a Pt/Al2O3 catalyst increased PDH yield and selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. Other work found supported Pt on boron nitride (Pt/BN) gave higher stability and near 98-99% propylene selectivity, potentially due to metal-support interactions [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]. Optimizing the dispersion of Pt as isolated atoms in the pores of zeolite supports has also proven effective, with a Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL catalyst showing excellent stability [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. Additionally, some studies have explored the impact of promoters like B and Sn on supported Pt catalysts [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548; DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639].

Less research has focused on Pd and Ir catalysts. One study notes Pd, Rh, and Ir can activate C-H bonds but are expensive alternatives [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. No specific data or insights on supported Pd or Ir catalysts are provided in the file details.

Overall, supported Pt catalysts are identified as the most widely used and successful option commercially. Key areas of focus have included stabilizing isolated Pt sites, metal-support interactions, and promoters to enhance Pt's intrinsic PDH capability. Further work is still needed to optimize activity, selectivity and stability.

根据文件提供的细节,已经有几项研究探索了负载单金属铂(Pt)、钯(Pd)和铱(Ir)的催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

Pt基催化剂被报道是最广泛使用和商业上最成功的PDH催化剂之一。例如,Pt/Al2O3是一种重要的PDH催化剂组分,因为Pt能激活C-H键同时最小化C-C键的断裂 [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。一项研究表明,在Pt/Al2O3催化剂上沉积ALD Al2O3或TiO2涂层可以提高PDH的收率和选择性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。其他工作发现,负载在氮化硼(BN)上Pt (Pt/BN)提供了更高的稳定性和接近98-99%的丙烯选择性,可能是由于金属与载体的相互作用[DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]。优化Pt分散在分子筛载体孔内作为隔离原子也被证明是有效的,Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL催化剂表现出优异的稳定性[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。此外,一些研究还探索了B和Sn等助剂对负载Pt催化剂的影响[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548; DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]。

较少的研究关注Pd和Ir催化剂。一项研究指出,Pd、Rh和Ir可以激活C-H键,但作为替代方案昂贵[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。文件细节中没有提供关于负载Pd或Ir催化剂的具体数据或见解。

总的来说,负载Pt催化剂被认为是商业上使用最广泛和最成功的选择。重点领域包括稳定隔离Pt位点,金属与载体的相互作用以及促进剂以提高Pt固有的PDH能力。仍然需要进一步的工作来优化活性,选择性和稳定性。
