	<English>
		The article introduces several major catalyst classes explored for propane dehydrogenation, including metal oxides like VOx/CrOx supported on mesoporous silica MCM-41 and SBA-15 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816], Sn-incorporated hexagonal mesoporous silica (HMS) [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816], and Co-incorporated mesoporous silica [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. It also discusses Pt- and Cr-based catalysts used industrially [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008], as well as intermetallic compounds [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]. Additionally, H-Beta zeolite [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019] and iron-silicate zeolites [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019] are introduced as catalysts that can activate propane and convert it to propylene through proposed redox cycles. Supported metals like silica-supported PtSn [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939] and single-atom catalysts [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889] are also mentioned as promising options for propane dehydrogenation.
		※※※
		Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The major catalyst classes studied for propane dehydrogenation encompass a diverse range of materials like metal oxides, zeolites, supported metals and single atom catalysts. As research continues to uncover improved catalytic performance through optimizations in composition, structure and support interactions, there exists potential to integrate hybrid systems that synergistically combine the advantages of different components. Advanced in-situ characterization techniques and computational modeling can further elucidate catalyst evolution and unlock superior designs. Ultimately, by bridging fundamental understanding and targeted development, next-generation catalysts may reach new horizons of conversion, selectivity and stability.

	<Chinese>
		文章介绍了几种用于丙烷脱氢的主要催化剂,包括载有介孔硅MCM-41和SBA-15 的 VOx/CrOx [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]金属氧化物,掺杂六方介孔硅(HMS) [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]的Sn,掺杂介孔硅[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816] 的Co。它还讨论了工业上使用的Pt- 和Cr-基催化剂[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008],以及金属间化合物[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]。 此外,还引入了H-Beta分子筛[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019]和铁硅酸盐分子筛[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019]作为能通过提议的氧化还原循环激活丙烷并将其转化为丙烯的催化剂。 支撑金属如硅载PtSn [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939]和单原子催化剂[10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]也被提及为有前景的丙烷脱氢选择。

		※※※
		综合理解与未来展望: 已研究的丙烷脱氢的主要催化剂类别包括各种材料,如金属氧化物、分子筛、受体金属和单原子催化剂。随着研究继续通过优化组成、结构和载体相互作用来发现改进的催化性能,存在将不同组分的优点协同整合的杂化系统的潜力。先进的原位表征技术和计算建模可以进一步阐明催化剂的演变,并解锁卓越的设计。最终,通过桥接基础理解和有针对性的开发,新一代催化剂可能会达到新的高度,实现更高的转化率、选择性和稳定性。
