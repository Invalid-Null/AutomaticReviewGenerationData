Cr-based catalysts like Cr2O3/Al2O3 have been explored as promising metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. CrOx/Al2O3 supplied by Nikki Chemical was evaluated and achieved propene yields up to 30%, comparable to conventional reactors [10.1016/j.cej.2004.11.005]. Reduced α-Cr2O3(0001) was investigated using kinetic Monte Carlo and microkinetic modeling for propane and butane dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]. However, poor stability remains an issue with Cr-based catalysts. 

Pt-based catalysts, especially Pt-Sn alloys, are effective metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. Pt-Sn/SBA-15 synthesized by coimpregnation showed 40% propane conversion and 92% propylene selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]. Pt-Sn catalysts supported on zeolite Beta achieved 50% conversion and 99% selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]. The Pt3Sn phase in Pt-Sn catalysts improves activity and selectivity by geometric and electronic effects [10.1002/advs.202207756].

Among gallium oxide polymorphs, β-Ga2O3 exhibits the best propane dehydrogenation activity, correlated with its higher surface acidity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Silica-supported β-Ga2O3/SiO2 and γ-Ga2O3/SiO2 were also evaluated, along with an amorphous Ga/SiO2 benchmark [10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]. While promising, the catalytic performance of Ga2O3 still falls short of state-of-the-art Pt-Sn and Cr2O3 catalysts.

		※※※
		Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Recent advances in propane dehydrogenation metal oxide catalysts have highlighted Cr2O3, PtSn, and Ga2O3 systems. However, instability from coking and phase changes remains an obstacle. Further research could explore novel synthetic methods or alloying additions to enhance stability. Operando spectroscopy and advanced theoretical studies can provide mechanistic insights to guide optimization. Expanding the search space beyond traditional metal oxides may uncover new active phases with tunable properties.

Cr基催化剂如Cr2O3/Al2O3已被探索为丙烷脱氢的有前景的金属氧化物催化剂。 Nikki化工供应的CrOx/Al2O3被评价并达到高达30%的丙烯产率,可与传统反应器媲美[10.1016/j.cej.2004.11.005]。 使用动力学Monte Carlo方法和微观动力学建模研究了还原α-Cr2O3(0001)的丙烷和丁烷脱氢反应[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]。 然而,Cr基催化剂的稳定性差仍是一个问题。

尤其是Pt-Sn合金的Pt基催化剂是有效的丙烷脱氢金属氧化物催化剂。 共沉淀法合成的Pt-Sn/SBA-15展示了40%的丙烷转化率和92%的丙烯选择性[10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]。 支持在分子筛Beta上的Pt-Sn催化剂达到50%的转化率和99%的选择性[10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]。 Pt-Sn催化剂中的Pt3Sn相通过几何和电子效应改善了活性和选择性[10.1002/advs.202207756]。

在钒氧化物多型中,β-Ga2O3表现出最佳的丙烷脱氢活性,这与其较高的表面酸度相关[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。 支持在二氧化硅上的β-Ga2O3/SiO2和 γ-Ga2O3/SiO2也得到了评价,以及无定形Ga/SiO2基准[10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]。 尽管有前景,但Ga2O3的催化性能仍落后于最先进的Pt-Sn和Cr2O3催化剂。
		※※※
		综合理解与未来展望: 丙烷脱氢金属氧化物催化剂的最近进展突出了Cr2O3、PtSn和Ga2O3体系。 然而,焦炭和相变导致的不稳定性仍然是障碍。 未来的研究可以探索新颖的合成方法或添加掺杂来增强稳定性。 同步光谱和先进的理论研究可以提供机理信息来指导优化。 超越传统金属氧化物可能会发现具有可调节性能的新活性相。
