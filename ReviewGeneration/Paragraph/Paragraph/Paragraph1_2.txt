	<English>
		Propylene is an essential organic feedstock for producing a wide variety of valuable chemicals such as polypropylene, propylene oxide, acrylonitrile, acetone, etc., with global demand rapidly rising [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103, 10.1002/advs.202207756]. However, conventional production routes like steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking struggle to meet this increasing demand due to crude oil supply constraints [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. In this context, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) has emerged as an promising technology by utilizing abundant and inexpensive propane feedstocks from shale gas [10.1021/acscatal.0c01455, 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.01.055]. PDH is also praised as an economically competitive and eco-friendly process [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889].
		※※※
		Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the increasing propylene demand and issues with conventional production, PDH stands to play a pivotal role in ensuring adequate and sustainable supplies. Further research could focus on improving catalyst design and reactor engineering to enhance productivity and selectivity. Techno-economic assessments comparing various process configurations may also illuminate optimal pathways forward. Broader adoption of renewable propane feeds could make PDH even more environmentally friendly.

		丙烯是生产各种有价值化学品(如聚丙烯、丙烯醇、丙烯腈、丙酮等)的重要有机原料,其全球需求正在快速增长 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103, 10.1002/advs.202207756]。然而,常规生产路线(如蒸汽裂解和流化催化裂解)由于原油供应的约束,难以满足不断增长的需求[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。在这种背景下,丙烷脱氢(PDH)作为一种有前景的技术,利用了从页岩气中获得的丰富廉价的丙烷原料 [10.1021/acscatal.0c01455, 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.01.055]。PDH 也被誉为经济有竞争力且环保的工艺[10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]。
		※※※
		综合理解与未来展望:考虑到丙烯需求的增加和常规生产的问题,PDH 将发挥关键作用,确保足够和可持续的供应。进一步的研究可以集中在提高催化剂设计和反应器工程方面,以增强生产力和选择性。不同工艺配置的技术经济评估也可以指明最佳的发展道路。更广泛地采用可再生丙烷原料,可以使PDH变得更环保。
