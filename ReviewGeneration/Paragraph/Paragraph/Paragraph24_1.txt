 <English>
Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts require optimized dispersion and stabilization of the active metal phase to achieve high activity, selectivity, and stability. The reviewed study [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726] investigates catalyst preparation methods for optimizing active phase dispersion of cobalt species in BEA zeolites. Co@Si-BEA catalysts with 0.1-5 wt% Co are prepared by vacuum impregnation, with 0.1-0.5 wt% Co showing the highest intrinsic activity attributed to isolated Co2+ sites. Dealumination also improves dispersion and coke resistance. 

Further works utilize various techniques for optimizing dispersion:

- Controlling metal loading, support interactions, and calcination conditions during impregnation, ion exchange, CVD, etc. to tune metal speciation and prevent nanoparticle formation [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7].

- Employing mesoporous, high surface area supports like SBA-15 and modifying supports to increase metal anchoring [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, 10.1021/acscatal.8b04859].  

- Utilizing surface organometallic chemistry to graft isolated metal sites [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, 10.1021/acs.accounts.9b00138].

- Tuning solvents, surfactants, and pH during impregnation and sol-gel routes to control nanoparticle nucleation, growth and support interactions [10.1021/acscatal.3c00818, 10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008].

- Atomic layer deposition for uniform, highly dispersed metal oxide layers [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.01.005].

The reviewed insights on optimizing active phase dispersion pave the way for developing superior PDH catalysts via rational design principles. Future directions include extending these strategies to emerging supports and metastable multimetallic assemblies unattainable by conventional means. Harnessing in-situ probes during catalyst activation is also vital to unlocking coveted nanoscale motifs through transient precursors.

<Chinese>
丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂需要优化活性金属相的分散和稳定化,以实现高活性、选择性和稳定性。所审阅的研究[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]调查了优化BEA沸石中钴种类分散的催化剂制备方法。采用真空渗透法制备了0.1-5 wt% Co的Co@Si-BEA催化剂,0.1-0.5 wt% Co显示出最高的固有活性,归因于隔离的Co2+位点。除铝也改善了分散性和抗焦炭性。

进一步的工作利用各种技术来优化分散:

- 控制渗透、离子交换、CVD等过程中的金属负载量、载体相互作用和钙化条件,以调谐金属化学规范,防止纳米粒子的形成[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。

- 采用如SBA-15等介孔高比表面积载体,并改性载体以增加金属锚定点[10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7,10.1021/acscatal.8b04859]。

- 利用表面有机金属化学嫁接隔离的金属位点[10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, 10.1021/acs.accounts.9b00138]。 

- 调节渗透和溶胶凝胶法中的溶剂、表面活性剂和pH值,以控制纳米粒子的核化、生长和载体相互作用[10.1021/acscatal.3c00818, 10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008]。

- 原子层沉积制备均匀的高度分散的金属氧化物层[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.01.005]。

关于优化活性相分散的审阅见解为通过合理的设计原则开发优越的PDH催化剂铺平了道路。未来的方向包括将这些策略扩展到新兴的载体和通过常规方法无法获得的多金属瞬态组装体。 在催化剂活化期间利用原位探针也对解锁通过瞬态前体获得的稀有纳米级模体至关重要。
