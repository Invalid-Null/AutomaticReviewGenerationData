Specifically, it uses a descriptor-based microkinetic analysis with DFT calculations to screen over 2000 intermetallic compound catalysts and identify promising Fe-Ga candidates. 

The article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.0c05711] also discusses utilizing descriptor-based strategies and machine learning for high-throughput catalyst screening and design. It suggests further screening of trimetallic alloys to identify more propane dehydrogenation catalysts.

The remaining articles cited do not propose leveraging high-throughput computational techniques. Instead they rely on experimental synthesis and characterization methods.

其余引用的文章没有提出利用高通量计算技术。相反,它们依靠实验合成和表征方法。
