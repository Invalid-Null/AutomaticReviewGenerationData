Computational studies utilizing density functional theory (DFT) calculations have offered valuable insights into propane dehydrogenation mechanisms, kinetics, and pathways over various catalysts. \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816\] DFT results coupled with microkinetic modeling have elucidated structure-activity relationships, identifying descriptors like d-band centers that correlate with selectivity on metals \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816\], and revealed kinetically relevant steps like propylene CH activation. \[10.1021/acscatal.1c01916\] DFT has also explored single atom catalysts, showing dopant and support effects tune performance. \[10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63196-1\] 

On solid acid catalysts, DFT clarified Zn-O-Zn sites have lower apparent activation barriers than Zn^2+^ for dehydrogenation over H-ZSM-5. \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816\] For Sn-BEA, DFT found the direct pathway more kinetically favorable than the indirect route in closed sites, with open sites more reactive pending hydroxylation. \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\] DFT also provided mechanistic details over zeolites like showing dissociative propane adsorption prevails over radical formation on Zn-exchanged mordenite. \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.10.018\]

Overall DFT offers fundamental atomic-level insights connecting structure, descriptors, and mechanisms to observed catalyst performance trends. Continued development of functionals, incorporation of entropy and solvent effects, and integration with microkinetic modeling will enable enhanced computational characterization and screening for optimized PDH catalysts.  

※※※

The valuable mechanistic perspectives into propane dehydrogenation catalysis furnished by DFT calculations harbor prospects for rationally navigating an enhanced exploratory phase. By pinpointing influential attributes like coordination motifs and electronic properties governable through compositional and architectural modifications, computational efforts lay the foundation for deliberately maneuvering selectivity constraints. 

Capitalizing on these actionable handles, future undertakings might examine hitherto unexplored catalytic mediums through high-throughput virtual experimentation calibrated to empirical observations. Such exertions could stimulate the conception of unconventional catalyst frameworks with tailored coordination surroundings engineered to promote desired mechanistic routes. Moreover, incorporating dynamic transformations in catalyst speciation occurring under practical reactive atmospheres into computational methodologies is pivotal for predictive prowess. 

Overall while extant DFT contributions have afforded invaluable functional insights, the integration of heightened levels of configurational and environmental complexity in simulation protocols promises more expansive and nuanced perspectives into tailored catalyst design.

利用密度泛函理论(DFT)计算的计算研究为丙烷脱氢机制、动力学和各种催化剂上的反应途径提供了有价值的见解。 \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816\] DFT结果与微观动力学建模相结合,阐明了结构-活性关系,识别出与选择性相关的描述符,如金属的d带中心 \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816\],并揭示了丙烯CH 激活等动力学相关步骤。\[10.1021/acscatal.1c01916\] DFT还探索了单原子催化剂,显示掺杂剂和载体效应调节性能。 \[10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63196-1\]

在固体酸催化剂上,DFT 明确了Zn-O-Zn 位点的表观活化能障碍较Zn^2+在 H-ZSM-5 上的脱氢反应更低。 \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816\] 对于 Sn-BEA,DFT 发现封闭位点上的直接途径在动力学上比间接途径更有利,开放位点的反应活性取决于羟基化程度。\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\] DFT还提供了沸石上诸如显示解离丙烷吸附优先于丙烷在锌交换沸石上的自由基形成等机制细节。 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.10.018\]

总体而言,DFT为连接结构、描述符和机制与观察到的催化剂性能趋势的原子水平的基本见解。 泛函继续发展,考虑熵和溶剂效应,以及与微观动力学建模的结合,将有助于增强计算表征和筛选优化PDH催化剂。

※※※

DFT计算提供的丙烷脱氢催化剂机理视角蕴含着有前景的理性导航增强的探索阶段。通过明确协调基团和电子特性等具有影响力的属性可以通过组成和建筑修改进行控制,计算工作为有意识地操纵选择限制奠定了基础。 

利用这些可操作的手柄,未来的努力可能会通过针对经验观察进行校准的高通量虚拟实验来检查迄今为止未被探索的催化介质。这种努力可能会激发构思非传统催化剂框架的概念,这些框架具有通过工程设计来促进所需机制途径的定制协调环境。此外,将实际反应大气下催化剂规范的动态转变纳入计算方法学中对预测能力至关重要。

总的来说,虽然现有的DFT贡献提供了宝贵的功能性洞察,但在模拟协议中整合更高水平的配置和环境复杂性,这将承诺获得更广泛和细致的定制催化剂设计的视角。
