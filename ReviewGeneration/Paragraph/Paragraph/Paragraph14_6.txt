Based on the details extracted from the references, most of the literature does not propose specific mechanistic pathways for propane dehydrogenation over supported metal catalysts. 

A few studies provide some insights into potential steps and intermediates over specific supported metal catalysts. For example, reference [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391] shows different pathways for Pt(111) versus Pt(211), with Pt(111) following a reverse Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism. Reference [10.1021/acscatal.2c01631] describes a 6-step cycle for supported Pt catalysts involving C-H bond activation, hydrogen removal, and propene desorption. Reference [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671] calculates free energy barriers for dehydrogenation, cracking, and hydrogenation pathways over Pt and PtSn nanoclusters.

However, most of the cited works do not elucidate detailed mechanistic steps, intermediates formed, or active sites involved during propane dehydrogenation over general supported metal catalysts. More in-depth computational and experimental studies probing the catalyst-reactant interactions at a molecular level will be needed to advance mechanistic understandings beyond empirical observations.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: While mechanistic intricacies still need to be resolved, the collected insights highlight opportunities in tuning catalytic properties like metal nanoparticle size, composition, interactions with supports, and isolation of active sites to promote selective dehydrogenation while mitigating coke formation and over-oxidation. For instance, isolated Pt sites show promise in controlling the number of dehydrogenation steps. Future work can build on these structure-function insights and explore novel catalyst designs through synergies with supports and promoters. Operando spectroscopic techniques combined with kinetic and theoretical modeling will likely play pivotal roles in unraveling mechanistic complexities. Broader horizons might involve extensions to oxidative dehydrogenation pathways and comparisons to biomimetic catalysts.

 然而,大多数引用的作品没有阐明一般Supported metal催化剂上丙烷脱氢期间形成的详细机理步骤、中间体或活性位点。 需要更深入的计算和实验研究在分子水平上检验催化剂与反应物的相互作用,以超越经验观察推进机理理解。

※※※

Integrative understanding 和前景展望: 虽然机理复杂性仍然需要解决,但收集的见解强调了调谐催化剂性能的机会,如金属纳米颗粒的大小、组成、与载体的相互作用以及活性位点的隔离,以促进选择性脱氢,同时减少焦炭形成和过度氧化。 例如,独立的Pt位点有望控制脱氢步骤的数量。 未来的工作可以依据这些结构-功能见解,通过与载体和助剂的协同作用探索催化剂设计的新概念。 同位素谱技术与动力学和理论建模相结合,可能会发挥关键作用,揭示机理复杂性。 更广阔的视野可能涉及氧化脱氢途径的扩展以及与生物模拟催化剂的比较。
