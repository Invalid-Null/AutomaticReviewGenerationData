Adding these metals as promoters can modulate the geometric and electronic properties of Pt active sites.

Pt-Sn bimetallic catalysts are widely studied for PDH, with PtSn/Al2O3 being used commercially in the Oleflex process [Placeholder_Of_DOI4]. The addition of Sn increases propene selectivity and stability by minimizing side reactions like hydrogenolysis and coke formation [Placeholder_Of_DOI5]. This is attributed to an ensemble effect where Sn blocks certain Pt active sites as well as an electronic effect where Sn increases the electron density on neighboring Pt sites, altering adsorption properties [Placeholder_Of_DOI6]. DFT studies predict Pt-Sn intermetallic phases like Pt3Sn and Pt2Sn can reduce dehydrogenation barriers [Placeholder_Of_DOI7]. However, excessive Sn content reduces activity by completely blocking active sites, thus an optimal Pt:Sn ratio around 3:1 is necessary [Placeholder_Of_DOI8]. The preparation method of Pt-Sn catalysts impacts resulting phase and morphology. Impregnating Sn on supported Pt versus co-impregnation changes Sn dispersion. Zeolite confinement and interaction with Sn sites in the support lattice can also tune Pt-Sn structure [Placeholder_Of_DOI9].

Pt-Ga bimetallic catalysts have demonstrated remarkable PDH performance exceeding equilibrium conversion at mild temperatures (≤500°C) [Placeholder_Of_DOI10]. The GaPt structure suppresses side reactions including coke formation. Ga enables high metal dispersion, though typical synthetic procedures yield ill-defined sites [Placeholder_Of_DOI11]. Control over GaPt morphology through core-shell structures or ordered intermetallic compounds may further enhance properties by optimizing ensemble effects [Placeholder_Of_DOI12].

Pt-Zn catalysts synthesized by anchoring Pt on isolated SiOZn-OH sites in dealuminated zeolite Beta achieved exceptional stability with near equilibrium conversion [Placeholder_Of_DOI13]. The zeolite environment facilitated uniform single PtZn4-6 sites. Ordered intermetallic PtZn nanoparticles and nanoclusters also displayed promising performance [Placeholder_Of_DOI14]. Zn helps maintain small, stabilized Pt entities to promote selective C-H activation over C-C cleavage. Further mechanistic and synthetic studies should aim to distinguish isolated, intermetallic, and disordered Pt-Zn configurations.

In summary, alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, or Zn has demonstrated great potential to enhance PDH performance through electronic, ensemble, and geometric effects. These secondary metals help disperse Pt, providing isolated active sites resistant to deactivation. Promoter identity and loading must be optimized to balance activity, selectivity and stability. Support and preparation variation to control size, morphology and metal-metal interactions represent ongoing challenges.

Pt-Sn双金属催化剂广泛用于PDH反应,其中PtSn/Al2O3已在Oleflex工艺中商业化[[Placeholder_Of_DOI4]]。Sn的添加增加丙烯选择性和稳定性,通过最小化氢解和焦炭形成等副反应[[Placeholder_Of_DOI5]]。这归因为Sn堵塞了某些Pt活性位点的集体效应,以及Sn增加相邻Pt位点电子密度的电子效应,从而改变了吸附特性[[Placeholder_Of_DOI6]]。DFT研究预测Pt-Sn金属间化合物(如Pt3Sn和Pt2Sn)可以降低脱氢壁垒[[Placeholder_Of_DOI7]]。但是,过量的Sn含量会通过完全堵塞活性位点来降低活性,因此最佳的Pt:Sn比约为3:1[[Placeholder_Of_DOI8]]。Pt-Sn催化剂的制备方法会影响最终生成的相和形态。相比共浸渍,先支撑Pt再浸渍Sn会改变Sn的分散度。分子筛限域和与支撑体晶格中的Sn位点的相互作用也可以调控Pt-Sn的结构[[Placeholder_Of_DOI9]]。

Pt-Ga双金属催化剂在温和温度(≤500°C)下显示出超过平衡转化率的显著PDH性能[[Placeholder_Of_DOI10]]。其GaPt结构抑制了副反应,包括焦炭形成。Ga促进高度金属分散,尽管典型的合成程序会产生定义不明确的位点[[Placeholder_Of_DOI11]]。通过核壳结构或有序金属间化合物来控制GaPt形态,可以通过优化集体效应来进一步增强其性能[[Placeholder_Of_DOI12]]。

双金属和合金催化剂 - 合金化Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn如何影响丙烷脱氢反应性能?

几项研究调查了合金化铂与锡(Sn)、镓(Ga)和锌(Zn)等辅助金属对改善丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂性能的影响[10.1021/acscatal.3c00548]、[10.1016/j.jcat.2014.02.084]、[10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。这些金属的添加能够调控Pt活性位点的几何和电子特性。

Pt-Sn双金属催化剂广泛用于PDH反应,其中PtSn/Al2O3已在Oleflex工艺中商业化[10.1016/S1872-2067(23)64402-X]。Sn的添加增加丙烯选择性和稳定性,通过最小化氢解和焦炭形成等副反应[10.1002/anie.201507119]。这归因为Sn堵塞了某些Pt活性位点的集体效应,以及Sn增加相邻Pt位点电子密度的电子效应,从而改变了吸附特性[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.09.006]。DFT研究预测Pt-Sn金属间化合物(如Pt3Sn和Pt2Sn)可以降低脱氢壁垒[10.1016/j.cej.2014.09.107]。但是,过量的Sn含量会通过完全堵塞活性位点来降低活性,因此最佳的Pt:Sn比约为3:1 [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]。Pt-Sn催化剂的制备方法会影响最终生成的相和形态。相比共浸渍,先支撑Pt再浸渍Sn会改变Sn的分散度。分子筛限域和与支撑体晶格中的Sn位点的相互作用也可以调控Pt-Sn的结构[10.1038/s41467-022-30522-1]。
