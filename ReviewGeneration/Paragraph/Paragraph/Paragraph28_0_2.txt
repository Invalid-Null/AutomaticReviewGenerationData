Based on the details provided in the file, the articles generally do not evaluate different reactor types for propane dehydrogenation. Most focus on catalyst synthesis and characterization using fixed bed reactors. 

However, one article [DOI 10.1016/j.cej.2009.06.037] does compare performance of a two-zone fluidized bed reactor (TZFBR) integrated with a Pd membrane to a fixed bed reactor for propane dehydrogenation over PtSn/Al2O3. The TZFBR provides separate zones for reaction and catalyst regeneration to mitigate deactivation. Results showed the membrane reactor increased conversion but also coking rate compared to fixed bed, indicating need for improved catalyst.

Another article [DOI 10.1016/S1383-5866(01)00085-5] examined silica membranes for integration with fixed beds to improve propene yields by selective hydrogen removal. Membrane permeability and propane fluxes limited performance, but yields still exceeded fixed bed reactors.

Overall the literature is limited in evaluating reactors beyond fixed beds. Opportunities remain to explore combining emerging membrane materials or novel fluidized bed designs with optimized catalysts to improve propane dehydrogenation efficiency.

根据文件提供的详细信息,文章通常并没有评估不同的反应器类型用于丙烷脱氢。大多数文章集中在固定床反应器上合成和表征催化剂。

然而,一篇文章 [DOI 10.1016/j.cej.2009.06.037] 比较了两区态流化床反应器(TZFBR)与钯膜反应器的性能,用于PtSn/Al2O3催化剂上的丙烷脱氢反应。TZFBR提供了独立的反应区和催化剂再生区以缓解失活。结果显示膜反应器提高了转换率,但也增加了结焦速率,与固定床反应相比,这表明需要改进催化剂。

另一篇文章[DOI 10.1016/S1383-5866(01)00085-5] 检查了二氧化硅膜与固定床反应器的整合,通过选择性氢移除提高丙烯收率。膜的渗透性和丙烷通量的限制影响了性能,但收率仍高于固定床反应器。

总的来说,文献在评估固定床反应器之外的反应器方面是有限的。探索将新兴膜材料或新颖的流化床设计与优化的催化剂组合,以提高丙烷脱氢效率的机会仍然存在。
