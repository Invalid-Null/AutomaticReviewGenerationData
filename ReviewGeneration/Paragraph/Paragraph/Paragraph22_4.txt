For example, [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103] discusses dealuminating Beta zeolite with nitric acid to create defects, followed by grafting tributyltin hydride complexes onto these sites to form isolated Sn sites.

Ion exchange is also used to introduce isolated metal ions into zeolites by replacing protons.
