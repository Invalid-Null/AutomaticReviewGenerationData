As this field advances further, developing rigorous protocols to preclude runaway reactions and contain explosions will be imperative. Future undertakings could explore inheriting perspectives from analogous processes in the oil refining and petrochemical industries to enact preemptive measures against accidents. On the catalyst development front, crafting stable and selective single-atom site catalysts with optimized heat management could mitigate safety issues. Overall, consolidating process safety knowledge from adjacent domains alongside innovating upon catalyst and reactor fundamentals appears to be a prudent pathway.
