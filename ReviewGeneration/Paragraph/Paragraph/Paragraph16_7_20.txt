		Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?
		
		Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) is an endothermic reaction that converts propane to propylene, an important building block chemical in the petrochemical industry. While Pt-based catalysts exhibit high activity for C-H bond activation in PDH, there are opportunities to improve propylene selectivity, catalyst stability, and resistance to deactivation from coking [Placeholder_Of_DOI1]. Strategies like alloying have shown promise to address these challenges.
		
		Pt-Sn catalysts supported on various metal oxides have been extensively explored for PDH, and successfully commercialized by companies like UOP [Placeholder_Of_DOI2]. Adding Sn serves to decrease Pt particle size, reduce hydrogenolysis/cracking activity, and mitigate coke formation - all leading to enhanced propylene selectivity [Placeholder_Of_DOI3, Placeholder_Of_DOI4]. DFT calculations reveal Sn weakens the adsorption strength of dehydrogenation intermediates while limiting binding configurations that enable secondary reactions [Placeholder_Of_DOI5]. However, excess Sn beyond optimal ratios (Pt:Sn ~ 3) causes a drop in activity.
		
		Incorporating promoters like Ga and Zn have analogous effects of tuning the geometric and electronic properties of Pt to curb overbinding. Studies on atomically dispersed Pt in zeolite-confined Zn ensembles (PtZn4-6 sites) [Placeholder_Of_DOI6] and intermetallic Pt-Ga nanoparticles [Placeholder_Of_DOI7] found up to >95% propylene selectivity could be attained over 500-600°C. Although initial rates were lower than conventional PtSn catalysts, exceptional stability was demonstrated - highlighting the stability-activity tradeoffs with alloying.

		丙烷脱氢(PDH)是一种需要吸热的反应,它将丙烷转化为丙烯,丙烯是石化工业中的重要基础化学品。 尽管Pt基催化剂在PDH中C-H键断裂活性很高, 但提高丙烯选择性、催化剂稳定性和抗焦炭失活性仍有改进空间 [Placeholder_Of_DOI1]。 合金化等策略显示了解决这些挑战的前景。
		
		不同金属氧化物支撑的Pt-Sn催化剂已经被广泛研究用于PDH,并且已经被UOP等公司商业化 [Placeholder_Of_DOI2]。 Sn的添加有助于降低Pt颗粒大小,减少氢化裂解/裂解活性,并减轻焦炭形成 - 所有这些都导致丙烯选择性的提高 [Placeholder_Of_DOI3, Placeholder_Of_DOI4]。 DFT计算揭示Sn削弱了脱氢中间体的吸附强度,同时限制了促进次生反应的吸附配置 [Placeholder_Of_DOI5]。 然而,过量的Sn (Pt:Sn ~ 3)会导致活性下降。
		
		掺入Ga和Zn等助剂也具有调节Pt的几何和电子性质来抑制过度吸附的类似效果。 对硅酸锌嵌段分离的Pt原子 [Placeholder_Of_DOI6]和Pt-Ga金属间化合物纳米颗粒 [Placeholder_Of_DOI7]的研究发现,500-600°C时可以达到95%以上的丙烯选择性。 尽管初始反应速率低于常规PtSn催化剂,但显示出异常稳定性 - 强调了合金化所带来的稳定性-活性权衡。
