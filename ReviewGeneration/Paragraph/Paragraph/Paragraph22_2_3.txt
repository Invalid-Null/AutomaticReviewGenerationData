Based on the details extracted from the file, the article with DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636 describes techniques for synthesizing isolated single Co sites in zeolitic MFI supports. Specifically, it uses a ligand-protected hydrothermal synthesis approach to construct isolated Co-motifs with a distorted tetrahedral structure of {(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2} that are homogeneously embedded into the T1(7) and T3(9) sites of the MFI framework. This is evidenced by advanced characterization techniques like iDPC-STEM, XAS, and FTIR [DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636].

Additionally, the article with DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103 discusses dealumination of Beta zeolite to generate vacant Al sites and SiOH nests that can anchor and isolate active metal sites. Examples include incorporating Ga species into the dealuminated SiBeta framework to achieve single Ga sites stabilized by the zeolite support, and confining Co compounds in hydroxyl nests of dealuminated SiBeta [DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]. 

The work by Zhou et al. on synthesizing boron-doped silicate zeolites with isolated boron sites is also mentioned in the article with DOI 10.1126/science.abe7935. This uses a solvent-free crystallization method with TPA+ hydroxide at 180°C followed by calcination to obtain a zeolite with an Si/B ratio of 62 and isolated boron sites [DOI 10.1126/science.abe7935].

根据文件中提取的细节,DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636的文章描述了在分子筛MFI载体中合成隔离的单个Co位点的技术。 具体而言,它使用配体保护的水热合成方法来构建被扭曲的四面体结构{(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2}的隔离Co基元,这些基元均匀嵌入到MFI框架的T1(7)和T3(9)位点中。 这是先进的表征技术(如iDPC-STEM,XAS和FTIR)证实的[DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636]。

此外,DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103的文章讨论了对Beta沸石的脱铝以生成空置的Al位点和SiOH巢,这些位点可以锚定和隔离活性金属位点。 例子包括将Ga种类掺入脱铝SiBeta框架以获得沸石支持稳定的单个Ga位点,以及将Co化合物限制在脱铝SiBeta的羟基巢内[DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]。

Zhou等人关于合成含有隔离硼位点的硅硼沸石的工作也在DOI 10.1126/science.abe7935的文章中提到。 这使用了以180°C和TPA+氢氧化物的无溶剂结晶方法,然后通过煅烧获得Si/B比为62且含有隔离硼位点的沸石[DOI 10.1126/science.abe7935]。
