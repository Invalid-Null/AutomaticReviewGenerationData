Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Several studies have evaluated the effect of alloying platinum with post-transition metals like tin (Sn), gallium (Ga), and zinc (Zn) for improving the performance of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts. 

Pt-Sn alloy catalysts are widely investigated for PDH, with commercial implementations using PtSn/Al2O3 such as the Oleflex process developed by UOP [10.1016/j.jechem.2020.08.058]. Adding Sn as a promoter is found to increase propene selectivity and stability of Pt catalysts by modifying ensemble and electronic effects. One study on PtSn clusters in MFI zeolite channels showed greatly improved initial activity and stability along with over 95% propylene selectivity [10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7]. The optimal ratio of Pt:Sn is suggested to be around 2-3:1, while equal amounts lead to inactivity [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]. 

Investigations on supported PtGa nanoparticles also demonstrate increased dispersion and inhibition of coking resulting in higher propylene selectivity versus Pt catalysts [10.1002/anie.202107210, 10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129]. Isolated PtZn sites stabilized by grafting Zn into the framework of dealuminated zeolite Beta achieved exceptional activity and stability, attributed to single PtZn4-6 sites [10.1021/jacs.1c10261].

In summary, strategic alloying of Pt with promoters like Sn, Ga, and Zn has proven effective for improving catalytic performance for propane dehydrogenation via geometric and/or electronic tuning of the active sites. Further targeted explorations may focus on optimizing bimetallic compositions and local environments to unlock the full potential of these multifunctional catalysts.

双金属和合金催化剂 - Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn形成合金如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

几项研究评估了用锡(Sn)、镓(Ga)和锌(Zn)等后过渡金属与铂形成合金,以改进丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂的性能。

Pt-Sn合金催化剂在PDH反应中被广泛研究,商业实施中使用PtSn/Al2O3,如UOP公司开发的Oleflex工艺[10.1016/j.jechem.2020.08.058]。添加Sn作为助剂可以通过更改配合效应和电子效应来增加Pt催化剂的丙烯选择性和稳定性。一项在MFI沸石通道中PtSn簇的研究表明,初始活性和稳定性大大提高,丙烯选择性超过95% [10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7]。 Pt:Sn的最佳比例建议为2-3:1,而等量则导致无活性[10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。

对受支撑的PtGa纳米粒子的研究也表明,Ga的增加使Pt分散度提高并抑制结焦,从而比Pt催化剂具有更高的丙烯选择性[10.1002/anie.202107210, 10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129]。通过在去铝Beta沸石的框架中掺入Zn稳定的隔离PtZn位点达到了异常的活性和稳定性,这被归因于单一的PtZn4-6位点[10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。

总之,Pt与Sn、Ga和Zn等助剂的战略合金化已经被证明通过几何和/或电子调节活性位点而有效地改进丙烷脱氢的催化性能。进一步的目标性探索可能会关注优化双金属成分和局部环境,以释放这些多功能催化剂的全部潜力。

综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到文件中讨论的先进成果和发现,我们有机会探索新兴领域和创新方法。未来的研究努力可能会集中在:

- 设计具有协同助剂效应的三元Pt合金催化剂
- 通过操作光谱研究反应机理和失活途径
- 利用单原子合金隔离和稳定化策略在定制的支撑矩阵中
- 扩大地球丰富的过渡金属助剂种类,超出Sn、Ga、Zn
- 开发新的模型体系,以桥接表面科学研究和实际催化剂之间的材料差距

这些开拓性方向潜在地可以克服目前在活性-选择性权衡、催化剂寿命和经济可行性方面困扰丙烷脱氢的局限性。扎根于分子水平的理解的创造性冒险可能催化下一次催化剂创新浪潮,这些创新准备转变这一重要过程。
