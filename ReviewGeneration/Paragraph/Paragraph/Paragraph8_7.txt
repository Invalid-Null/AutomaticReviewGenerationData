	Metal Oxide Catalysts - What metal oxides like Cr2O3, Ga2O3 have shown promising propane dehydrogenation activity?

	Among the metal oxide catalysts investigated, gallium oxide (Ga2O3) and chromium oxide (Cr2O3) have demonstrated promising catalytic performance for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Gallium oxide exhibited higher propylene yield compared to chromium oxide and other metal oxides like Fe2O3 and Al2O3. Specifically, the catalytic performance of different Ga2O3 polymorphs correlates strongly with surface acidity, where β-Ga2O3 with the highest surface acid site density showed maximum initial PDH activity [10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]. Weaker metal-support interactions, such as Ga2O3/SiO2, also led to higher propylene selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. For Cr2O3 catalysts, the partially oxidized surface showed improved activity, especially in conjunction with promoters like Pt [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814] or coupled with supports like SiO2 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Detailed kinetic and mechanistic studies using DFT and microkinetic modeling have also offered insights into PDH pathways over Cr2O3 surfaces [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]. While deactivation via coke formation remains an issue, tuning the oxidation state and structural promotion of Cr2O3 and Ga2O3 catalysts presents opportunities to further enhance the activity, selectivity and stability.

	Among supported Pt catalysts, metallic Pt is clearly established as the active site. However, alloying Pt with Sn as well as supports like silica and zeolite Beta significantly improves catalytic performance by modulating geometric and electronic properties [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527, 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]. Other bimetallic combinations like PtIn have also shown promise when stabilized on supports like Ca/Al2O3 [10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044]. And while Rh, Ir and Pd exhibit high intrinsic activity, their cost remains prohibitive versus Pt group metals [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002].

	※※※
	
	Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Propane dehydrogenation over both metal and metal oxide nanoparticle catalysts is an intensely active area of research. Key advances have established Cr2O3, Ga2O3 and supported Pt-group metals as promising catalyst candidates in terms of activity and selectivity. However, stability issues due to coking as well as cost considerations for precious metals underscore the need for further progress. Two potential pathways forward are:
	
	1) Heteroatom doping of metal oxide catalysts to modulate surface acid-base properties and active site speciation while suppressing coke formation.

	2) Stabilization of Pt nanoclusters on open framework supports using isolation or encapsulation strategies to improve efficiency by fully utilizing precious metal sites.

	Additionally, operando spectroscopic techniques combined with theoretical modeling will likely play an increasingly vital role in unraveling reaction mechanisms and sites to further guide next-generation catalyst formulation and process intensification.

	金属氧化物催化剂——Cr2O3、Ga2O3等金属氧化物表现出有前景的丙烷脱氢活性吗?
	
	在所调查的金属氧化物催化剂中,氧化镓(Ga2O3)和氧化铬(Cr2O3)已展示出丙烷脱氢(PDH)的有前景的催化性能[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。与氧化铬和其他金属氧化物(如Fe2O3和Al2O3)相比,氧化镓的丙烯收率更高。具体来说,不同Ga2O3多晶体的催化性能与表面酸度高度相关,其中表面酸位密度最高的β-Ga2O3显示出最大的初始PDH活性[10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]。较弱的金属载体相互作用,例如Ga2O3/SiO2,也导致更高的丙烯选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。 对于Cr2O3催化剂,部分氧化表面活性更高,尤其是与促进剂(如Pt [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814])或载体(如SiO2 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999])相结合时。 DFT和微观动力学建模也为Cr2O3表面上的PDH途径提供了详细的动力学和机理研究[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]。 虽然焦炭形成导致的失活仍是一个问题, 但调节Cr2O3和Ga2O3催化剂的氧化态和结构促进提供了进一步提高活性,选择性和稳定性的机会。
	
	在受支持的Pt催化剂中,金属Pt明确被确立为活性位点。 然而,将Pt与Sn合金以及硅和沸石Beta等载体,通过调控其几何和电子特性,显著提高了催化性能[10.1021/acscatal.9b03527,10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]。 PtIn等其他双金属组合在Ca/Al2O3等载体的稳定下也显示出有前景[10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044]。 尽管Rh,Ir和Pd具有高内在活性,但与Pt族金属相比,其成本仍然过高[10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。
	
	※※※
	综合理解与未来展望: 金属和金属氧化物纳米粒子催化剂上的丙烷脱氢反应是一个研究非常活跃的领域。 关键的进展确立了Cr2O3、Ga2O3和受支持的铂族金属是在活性和选择性方面具有前景的催化剂候选物。 然而,因焦炭形成导致的失活问题以及贵金属的成本考虑凸显了进一步进步的必要性。 两种潜在的前进路径是:

	1)通过调节表面酸碱性质和活性位点种类抑制焦炭形成来掺杂金属氧化物催化剂。

	2) 通过隔离或封装策略来稳定受支持的Pt纳米簇,以提高效率,充分利用贵金属活性位点。

	此外,联合理论建模的原位光谱技术可能会在揭示反应机理和活性位点以进一步指导新一代催化剂配制和工艺强化方面发挥越来越重要的作用。
