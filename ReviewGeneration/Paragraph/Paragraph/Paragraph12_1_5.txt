Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic platinum (Pt), palladium (Pd), and iridium (Ir) catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH).

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts. For example, Pt/Al2O3 is a key component of industrial PDH processes due to Pt's superior ability to activate C-H bonds while minimizing C-C bond breaking [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. Adding promoters such as boron [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548] and metal oxide overcoats [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391] to supported Pt catalysts can further enhance yield and selectivity. Ultra-high stability has also been achieved by confining isolated Pt atoms in zeolite pores via strong Pt-O-support bonding [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. 

Besides Pt/Al2O3, other evaluated supports include spinel [DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X], SBA-15 [DOI: 10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005], and boron nitride (BN) [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008], which surprisingly showed higher stability than Pt/Al2O3. Adding Sn generally enhances propane conversion but can decrease selectivity over Pt catalysts [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631, 10.1006/jcat.1997.1531]. Supported Pd, Rh, and Ir are noted as being intrinsically active for PDH but impractical due to cost considerations [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002].

Several studies also provide insights into deactivation mechanisms of supported Pt catalysts during PDH using characterization techniques like temperature programmed oxidation (TPO) [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]. Coke formation and metal agglomeration are identified major causes of Pt catalyst deactivation.

根据文件提供的详细信息,已经有几项研究探索了负载型单金属铂(Pt)、钯(Pd)和铱(Ir)催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

报道称,Pt基催化剂是最广泛使用和商业上最成功的PDH催化剂。例如,Pt/Al2O3是工业PDH工艺的关键组成部分,因为Pt有出色的能力激活C-H键而最小化C-C键的断裂[DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。在负载型Pt催化剂上添加硼[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]和金属氧化物涂层[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]可以进一步提高收率和选择性。通过强Pt-O-支持体键将隔离Pt原子固定在分子筛孔内,也实现了超高稳定性[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。

除了Pt/Al2O3之外,其他评估过的支持体还包括MgAl2O4 自旋磁体[DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]、SBA-15[DOI: 10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005]和氮化硼(BN)[DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008],后者的稳定性令人惊讶地高于Pt/Al2O3。在Pt催化剂上通常增强丙烷转化率但会降低选择性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631, 10.1006/jcat.1997.1531]。支持型钯、铑和铱天生就具有PDH活性,但由于成本考虑而难以应用 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。

几项研究还利用程序升温氧化(TPO)等表征技术,提供了负载Pt催化剂在PDH过程中的失活机理的见解[DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]。焦炭生成和金属团聚被确定为Pt催化剂失活的主要原因。

<Integrative understanding and prospective outlook>
The studies covered in the file showcase promising progress in developing highly efficient supported metal catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, with Pt-based materials being most thoroughly explored and well-understood. However, there remain opportunities to further optimize these catalysts and deepen mechanistic comprehension. For instance, the strong metal-support interaction exhibited between Pt and boron nitride merits more dedicated investigations to elucidate the nature of this synergy and guide design of next-generation supports [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]. Stability also continues to be an issue, so strategies like single atom stabilization could be extended beyond Pt to other metals like Pd and Ir [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. Expanding beyond empirical trial-and-error approaches, the mechanistic insights and dataset compiled here could facilitate the use of computational screening and machine learning models to accelerate identification of optimal catalyst compositions and structures for commercial propane dehydrogenation processes [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c05711].
