Based on the details provided, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic platinum (Pt), palladium (Pd), and iridium (Ir) catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH).

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. For example, Pt/alumina catalysts demonstrate superior ability to activate propane C-H bonds while minimizing undesired C-C cleavage [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. Adding promoters such as sulfur [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333] and boron [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548] are shown to enhance Pt/alumina stability and propylene selectivity. Other studies have explored the effects of Pt particle size [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671], with Pt2 clusters exhibiting optimal reactivity resembling a volcano-curve relationship. Tin addition generally suppresses Pt activity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, DOI: 10.1126/science.abg7894].

Besides Pt/alumina, other Pt-based catalysts have been evaluated including Pt/SBA-15 [DOI: 10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005], Pt/boron nitride [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008], and Pt confined in zeolite micropores [DOI: 10.1038/s41467-018-07502-5, DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. These often aim to modulate metal-support interactions. Under optimized conditions, Pt catalysts can achieve 58-60% propane conversion for over 25 hours time-on-stream [DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044].

In comparison, fewer studies evaluate Pd or Ir. One report shows supported RhCu single atom alloy catalyst has much higher intrinsic activity and 100% propene selectivity compared to Pt/alumina which gave only 80% selectivity [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The reviewed studies provide significant insights into Pt-based catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, which are established as the predominant commercial PDH technology. Nonetheless, opportunities remain to further advance alternative Pd, Ir, and other metal catalysts. An expansive perspective should also evaluate more diversified supports and comprehensive spectroscopic characterization of metal-support interfaces which likely modulate selectivity. Future research might focus on metastable isolated metal sites, single atom alloys, and cluster-support composites to push PDH performance boundaries.

<Chinese>
根据所提供的详细信息,已经有几项研究探索了负载单金属铂(Pt)、钯(Pd)和铱(Ir)催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

报道称,Pt基催化剂是最广泛使用和商业上最成功的PDH催化剂之一 [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。例如,Pt/氧化铝催化剂表现出优异的丙烷C-H键活化能力,同时最小化不期望的C-C键裂解[DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。加入促进剂如硫[DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333] 和硼[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548] 被证明可以增强Pt/氧化铝的稳定性和丙烯选择性。其他研究探索了Pt颗粒大小的影响[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671],Pt2簇展现出最佳反应活性,呈现出火山曲线关系。添加锡通常会抑制Pt活性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, DOI: 10.1126/science.abg7894]。

除了Pt/氧化铝,也评价了其他Pt基催化剂,包括Pt/SBA-15 [DOI: 10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005]、Pt/氮化硼[DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008] 和限制在分子筛微孔内的Pt [DOI: 10.1038/s41467-018-07502-5, DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。这些通常旨在调控金属-载体相互作用。在优化条件下,Pt催化剂可以达到58-60%的丙烷转化率,保持25小时以上的时间[DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044]。

相比之下,评价Pd或Ir的研究较少。一份报告显示,负载的RhCu单原子合金催化剂比Pt/氧化铝具有更高的固有活性和100%的丙烯选择性,而Pt/氧化铝只给出80%的选择性[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:这些综述研究对Pt基丙烷脱氢催化剂提供了重要的认识,它们已被确立为占主导地位的商业PDH技术。尽管如此,提高替代Pd、Ir和其他金属催化剂的性能仍有机会。采取更加宽广的视角,也应该评估更多样化的载体和对金属-载体界面进行全面的光谱表征,因为这可能会调节选择性。未来的研究可能会关注转瞬即逝的隔离金属活性中心、单原子合金和簇-载体复合材料,以推动PDH性能的极限。
