For example, one study investigated VOx/Al2O3 catalysts modified with PH3 surface treatment, which inhibited coking and enhanced stability during propane dehydrogenation. The inserted phosphorus separated polymerized vanadium species into more isolated species, weakened the vanadium-support interactions, expanded the distance between coke precursors, and facilitated desorption of products [10.1016/j.apcatb.2020.119089]. These changes in oxidation states and acid-base properties led to significant improvements in performance.

Another report studied GaNixOy mixed oxide catalysts and established strong correlations between the concentration of oxygen vacancies/Ni2+ sites and propane dehydrogenation activity [10.1021/acscatal.3c00818]. Dynamic hydrogen adsorbates on the Ni2+ sites were shown to effectively promote C-H bond activation and H2 desorption. Here, controlling oxidation states and oxygen mobility of the metal oxide lattice directly impacted activity and selectivity.

Furthermore, an investigation on ZnZrxOy catalysts revealed that increasing Zr content simultaneously enhanced acid strength while reducing basic site density [10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074]. The higher acidity coupled with properties like oxygen mobility and formation of ZnCUS-ZrCUS pairs improved propane conversion. This demonstrates the complex interplay between oxidation states, oxygen mobility, and acid-base sites in determining performance. 

Thus, strategies like doping/alloying, varying metal-support interactions, controlling oxygen nonstoichiometry, and managing redox cycles could potentially be utilized to optimize metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation and related C3-C4 alkane conversion processes. Advanced in situ spectroscopy techniques combined with theoretical modeling will likely play pivotal roles in unraveling the intricate structure-activity relationships.

例如,一项研究调查了用PH3表面处理改性的VOx/Al2O3催化剂,抑制焦炭生成并在丙烷脱氢过程中增强了稳定性。插入的磷将聚合的钒种分离成更加隔离的种类,削弱了钒与支撑体的相互作用,扩大了焦炭前驱体之间的距离,并促进了产物的解吸[10.1016/j.apcatb.2020.119089]。这些氧化态和酸碱性质的变化导致性能显着提高。

另一份报告研究了GaNixOy混合氧化物催化剂,并建立了氧空位/Ni2+位点浓度与丙烷脱氢活性之间的强相关关系[10.1021/acscatal.3c00818]。Ni2+位点上的动态氢吸附物被证明可以有效促进C-H键活化和H2解吸。在这里,控制金属氧化物晶格的氧化态和氧迁移度直接影响活性和选择性。

此外,对ZnZrxOy催化剂的一项调查显示,随着Zr含量的增加,酸强度增加而碱性位点密度降低[10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074]。更高的酸度再加上氧迁移度等性质以及ZnCUS-ZrCUS对的形成,改善了丙烷转化率。这证明了氧化态、氧迁移和酸碱位点之间的复杂相互作用在确定性能方面起决定性作用。

因此,掺杂/合金化、改变金属与支撑体之间的相互作用、控制氧的非化学计量比和管理氧化-还原循环等策略可能被用来优化C3-C4烷烃转化过程中丙烷脱氢用的金属氧化物催化剂。先进的原位光谱技术与理论模型的结合可能在揭示错综复杂的结构-活性关系方面发挥关键作用。
