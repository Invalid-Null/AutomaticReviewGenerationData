 <English>
The article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816] investigates tuning zeolite Si/Al ratio by dealumination of Beta zeolites to create silanol nests, which enhances metal dispersion and stabilization. Meanwhile, the study with DOI [10.1021/jacs.2c03941] evaluates propane dehydrogenation over CHA zeolites with varying Si/Al ratios. It demonstrates that optimizing the Ga/Al ratio is critical, as extra-framework Ga+ sites are the active centers regardless of Si/Al ratio. While rates decrease over time, propylene selectivity remains stable at 96%. 

Additionally, mesoporosity effects are explored in the article [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024], where smaller TS-1 crystals introduce mesopores that improve diffusion and lifetime. Lastly, the work in [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808] incorporates mesoporosity into MFI zeolites using nanosheets, finding that the mesopores and silanol groups facilitate intermetallic nanoparticle formation.

※※※
The investigations reveal complex structure-function relationships in propane dehydrogenation over zeolites, with performance depending on coupled effects between composition, porosity, and metal speciation. While initial studies have probed these phenomena, future work might explore high-throughput computational screening of multidimensional zeolite descriptors to construct quantitative structure-activity relationships. Data-driven models could then guide rational design of optimized materials. Broader horizons may also shift focus toward sustainable biorefinery applications, where bio-derived propane serves as a building block for value-added chemicals.

<Chinese>
DOI为[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]的文章通过脱铝Beta分子筛来调节Si/Al比,形成硅醇巢,这增强了金属的分散和稳定。同时,DOI为[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]的研究评估了具有不同Si/Al比的CHA分子筛上的丙烷脱氢反应。它表明优化Ga/Al比是关键的,因为无论Si/Al比如何,额外的Ga+位都是活性中心。虽然反应率随时间的推移而降低,但丙烯选择性保持在96%稳定。

此外,文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024]中探讨了介孔效应,其中较小的TS-1晶体引入介孔,改善了扩散和使用寿命。最后,文献[10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]利用纳米片在MFI分子筛中引入介孔,发现介孔和硅醇基团有利于形成互化金属纳米粒子。

※※※
调查结果揭示了丙烷脱氢反应中分子筛的复杂结构-功能关系,性能取决于组成、孔隙度和金属配位化学之间的耦合效应。尽管初步研究已经探明了这些现象,但未来的工作可能会聚焦于高通量计算筛选多维分子筛描述符,以构建定量的结构-活性关系。然后,数据驱动的模型可以指导优化材料的合理设计。更广阔的研究视野也可能将重点转向可持续生物炼制应用,其中生物衍生丙烷作为构建块用于高附加值化学品。
