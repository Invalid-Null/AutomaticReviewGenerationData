Based on the details extracted from the references, catalysts facilitate propane dehydrogenation by providing alternative reaction pathways and active sites that lower the activation energy barriers [DOI1, DOI2]. Commonly used industrial catalysts like Pt, CrOx, and Ga2O3 enable propane activation and dehydrogenation through mechanisms such as the Horiuti-Polanyi process involving alkane dissociative adsorption, hydrogen elimination to form a C=C double bond, and product desorption [DOI3]. DFT calculations have elucidated the catalyst structure-activity relationships, showing how coordinatively unsaturated metal atoms and smaller metal clusters enhance reactivity [DOI4, DOI5]. The shale gas boom has enabled cost-effective propane feedstocks, opening up opportunities to develop improved PDH catalysts and processes [DOI6, DOI7]. 

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: With increasing propylene demand and abundant propane supplies, direct catalytic dehydrogenation provides an attractive route for on-purpose propylene production. As research continues to unravel catalyst design principles and structure-activity correlations through advanced characterization and computational techniques, we may see the emergence of novel single-atom and multimetallic catalysts with optimized selectivity. The integration of catalyst and process design also presents opportunities to push conversion limits through intensified reaction-regeneration cycling. Broader adoption of PDH technology will be facilitated by responsible development pathways that minimize environmental impacts.

根据参考文献的详细信息,催化剂通过提供替代反应途径和活性位点来降低丙烷脱氢的活化能垒 [DOI1, DOI2]。常用的工业催化剂如Pt、CrOx和Ga2O3可以通过诸如Horiuti-Polanyi机理的机理激活丙烷和促进脱氢,这涉及到烷烃解离吸附、氢原子消除以形成C=C双键以及产物脱附 [DOI3]。 DFT计算已经阐明了催化剂的结构-活性关系,展示了如何协调不饱和的金属原子和较小的金属簇增强反应性[DOI4,DOI5]。 页岩气革命使得丙烷原料成本效益更高,为开发改进的PDH催化剂和工艺创造了机会 [DOI6, DOI7]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:随着丙烯需求的增加和丙烷供应的充裕,直接催化脱氢提供了一条生产丙烯的有吸引力的途径。随着研究继续通过先进的表征和计算技术揭示催化剂设计原理和结构-活性关系,我们可能会看到新型单原子和多金属催化剂的出现,这些催化剂具有优化的选择性。 催化剂和工艺设计的整合也为通过强化反应-再生循环推动转化限制提出了机会。 PDH技术的更广泛采用将通过负责任的发展途径来促进,以最大限度地减少环境影响。
