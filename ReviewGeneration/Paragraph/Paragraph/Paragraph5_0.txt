Here is a draft review section on propane dehydrogenation catalysts, with a focus on how DFT calculations have been applied to explore reaction mechanisms and pathways:

Multiple studies have utilized density functional theory (DFT) calculations to elucidate propane dehydrogenation mechanisms and kinetics over various catalysts. For instance, calculations revealed that Zn-O-Zn sites in specific catalysts possess lower activation barriers (1.81 eV) compared to single Zn2+ sites (2.14 eV), correlating well with experimental observations [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. Additionally, DFT energies showed that electron-enriched Pt catalysts have more negative d-band centers, with the d-band center value inversely related to propylene selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. More broadly, DFT-derived descriptors enabled quantitative structure-activity relationships, identifying key variables that control selectivity [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]. 

Microkinetic models constructed from computed barriers and energetics provided critical mappings between microscopic mechanistic steps and macroscopic catalytic performance [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]. For example, DFT elucidated that the direct propane dehydrogenation route is kinetically favored over the indirect pathway on certain catalysts [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019]. Notably, spin-polarized DFT calculations elucidated the stability and reactivity of 3N-coordinated single-atom catalysts for propane dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889].

Computations also revealed co-adsorbates like carbon can drastically impact barriers [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331], while characterizations of model PtSn surfaces provided explanations for favorable selectivity and coking resistance experimentally observed in propane dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331]. More recently, DFT-calculated descriptors enabled quantitative comparisons of propylene binding against barriers for unselective dehydrogenation routes across surfaces, pinpointing the kinetic relevance of specific mechanistic steps [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916].

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The detailed DFT explorations summarized above underscore the utility of modern computational techniques in unraveling propane dehydrogenation mechanisms, descriptors, and structure-activity insights across an array of catalysts. While existing efforts have greatly enhanced fundamental comprehension, there remain opportunities to leverage cutting-edge methodologies. 

For instance, researchers could apply machine learning models in tandem with DFT databases to rapidly predict selectivity trends. Dynamic simulations of defective surfaces over long time spans may also elicit valuable discoveries related to deactivation and coking. Lastly, high-throughput DFT screening of novel single-atom and Mo-based formulations could unlock additional practical catalyst candidates. Overall, by interfacing computational developments with existing knowledge, future undertakings can potentially overcome present-day limitations in propane dehydrogenation systems.

这里是关于丙烷脱氢催化剂的综述部分草稿,重点关注 DFT 计算如何被应用于探索反应机理和途径:

多项研究运用了密度泛函理论(DFT)计算来阐明各种催化剂上的丙烷脱氢机理和动力学。例如,计算显示某些催化剂中的 Zn-O-Zn 位点相比单 Zn2+ 位点(2.14 eV)具有更低的活化屏障(1.81 eV),与实验观察高度相关[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。 此外,DFT 能量表明,电子富集的 Pt 催化剂具有更负的 d 带中心,d 带中心的值与丙烯选择性呈反比[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。 更广泛地,基于 DFT 的描述符使定量的结构-活性关系成为可能,确定控制选择性的关键变量[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]。

从计算出的屏障和能量构建的微动力学模型在微观机理步骤与宏观催化性能之间提供了关键的映射[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]。 例如,DFT 阐明了某些催化剂上直接丙烷脱氢途径在动力学上优于间接途径[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019]。 值得注意的是,自旋极化 DFT 计算阐明了 3N 配位的单原子催化剂在丙烷脱氢反应中的稳定性和反应活性[10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]。

计算也揭示了类似碳的共吸附物可以极大地影响屏障[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331],而 PtSn 模型表面的表征为实验上观察到的丙烷脱氢反应中的良好选择性和抗焦炭性提供了解释[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331]。 更近期,DFT 计算出的描述符使丙烯吸附与非选择性脱氢途径的屏障进行定量比较成为可能,确定了跨表面的某些机理步骤的动力学相关性[10.1021/acscatal.1c01916]。

综合理解与未来展望:上述详细的 DFT 探索概括突显了现代计算技术在阐明各类催化剂上的丙烷脱氢机理、描述符和结构-活性关系方面的效用。虽然现有的努力极大地增进了基本理解,但仍有机会利用尖端方法展开新的研究。  

例如,研究人员可以结合 DFT 数据库应用机器学习模型来快速预测选择性趋势。长时间跨度下缺陷表面动态模拟也可能引发与失活和焦炭相关的有价值发现。最后,高通量 DFT 筛选新的单原子和 Mo 基材料可能解锁额外的实用催化剂候选者。总体而言,通过计算发展与现有知识的界面,未来的研究可以潜在地克服当前丙烷脱氢系统中的局限性。
