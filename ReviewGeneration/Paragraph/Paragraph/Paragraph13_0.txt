Based on the details extracted from the references, there is evidence showing how properties like metal particle size, shape, support interactions, and pretreatment conditions influence the catalytic performance of supported metal catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH).

Specifically, reference [10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005] reveals that a Pt/SBA-15 catalyst prepared by deposition-precipitation has much smaller Pt particles compared to one prepared by wet impregnation. The smaller Pt particles lead to higher initial conversion and propylene selectivity, although deactivation occurs due to coke formation. Reference [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391] demonstrates that depositing uniform, nanoscale Al2O3 overcoat layers on Pt nanoparticles by ALD enhances stability and selectivity by suppressing sintering and limiting formation of less selective sites during steam regeneration. An optimal coating thickness of 6-8 Å after 5-7 ALD cycles is identified. 

In terms of shape effects, reference [10.1021/acscatal.0c03381] calculates that under-coordinated Pt(211) sites dominate the reactivity over close-packed Pt(111) terrace sites, despite having lower surface area. This exemplifies the significant influence of surface structure on catalytic behavior. Additionally, reference [10.1021/jacs.6b08705] shows the reaction pathway and key intermediates involved during PDH on Pt(111), providing mechanistic insights.

Regarding metal-support interactions, reference [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008] demonstrates strong interactions between Pt and boron nitride after high temperature pretreatments. This causes partial encapsulation of Pt particles by boron oxide overlayers, which constrains particle size, blocks unselective sites, and enhances stability. Similarly, reference [10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7] stabilizes ultra-small Pt clusters in 14-membered ring zeolite pores via Pt-O-Ge linkages. This tailored metal-support interaction provides outstanding PDH performance.

In summary, strategic modulation of properties like particle size, shape, metal-support interfaces and pretreatment conditions allows control over the abundance and accessibility of selective sites, enabling optimization of supported metal catalysts for targeted PDH conversion and propylene selectivity.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings reveal promising opportunities to advance PDH catalysis by engineering multifunctional supported metal systems. For instance, synergy between shaped metal nanoparticles and hierarchically porous supports could combine stability from overcoat layers with accessibility to tunable active sites. Stabilizing isolated metal atoms or ultra-small clusters in ordered frameworks is also a pioneering direction. Operando spectroscopy techniques will likely play an integral role in unraveling dynamic structure-performance interrelationships under working conditions. Furthermore, machine learning models trained on extensive datasets could rapidly predict optimal compositions and pretreatments for targeted applications. In summary, by elucidating fundamental principles governing activity and selectivity, then implementing those guidelines through precisely tailored synthesis methods, significant enhancements in productivity and sustainability are attainable.

基于参考文献中的详细信息,有证据表明,金属颗粒大小、形状、载体相互作用和预处理条件等属性会影响负载金属催化剂在丙烷脱氢反应(PDH)中的催化性能。 

具体而言,参考文献[10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005]显示,通过沉淀沉积法制备的Pt/SBA-15催化剂具有比湿法浸渍制备的催化剂小得多的Pt颗粒。较小的Pt颗粒导致了更高的初始转化率和丙烯选择性,尽管由于积炭的形成而发生了失活。参考文献[10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]证明,通过ALD在Pt纳米颗粒上沉积均匀的纳米Al2O3覆层,可以通过抑制烧结和限制选择性较低位点的形成,从而提高稳定性和选择性。确定了最佳的覆层厚度为6-8Å,ALD循环5-7次。

在形状效应方面,参考文献[10.1021/acscatal.0c03381]计算出,尽管表面积更低,但未配位饱和的Pt(211)位点主导了近PACKED的Pt(111)台阶位点的反应活性。这证明了表面结构对催化性能的重大影响。 此外,参考文献[10.1021/jacs.6b08705]显示了PDH在Pt(111)上的反应机理和关键中间体,提供了机制见解。

关于金属与载体的相互作用,参考文献[10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]证明,高温预处理后Pt与氮化硼之间形成了强相互作用。这导致Pt颗粒被硼氧化物覆层部分包裹,从而约束颗粒大小,阻隔非选择性位点,提高了稳定性。 类似地,参考文献[10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]通过Pt-O-Ge连接将超小Pt簇稳定在14元环分子筛孔内。这种定制的金属与载体间相互作用提供了出色的PDH性能。

总的来说,选择性位点的丰度和可及性可以通过故意调控诸如颗粒大小、形状、金属与载体界面以及预处理条件等属性来精确控制,这使得负载金属催化剂的优化以达到目标的PDH转化率和丙烯选择性成为可能。

 ※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 这些发现揭示了通过设计多功能负载金属体系来推进PDH催化的前景。例如,形状金属纳米颗粒与分层多孔载体的协同效应,可以结合来自覆层的稳定性和可调活性位点的可及性。 在有序框架中稳定隔离金属原子或超小簇也是一个开拓性方向。 Operando光谱技术在揭示工作条件下的动态结构-性能关系方面可能发挥关键作用。 此外,经过大规模数据集训练的机器学习模型可以快速预测特定应用的优化成分和预处理方法。 总之,通过阐明控制活性和选择性的基本原理,然后通过精确定制的合成方法实施这些指导方针,实现生产力和可持续性的重大提高是可以实现的。
