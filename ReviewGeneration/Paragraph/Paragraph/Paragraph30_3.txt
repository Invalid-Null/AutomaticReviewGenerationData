The literature reviewed does not provide a detailed analysis of the techno-economic factors affecting the viability of propane dehydrogenation processes. The focus is primarily on catalyst design, characterization, and performance evaluation, with no quantitative analysis of process economics or considerations of scale-up provided. 

Specifically, references [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.06.016], [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103], [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008], [10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1], [10.1021/acscatal.0c05711], [10.1021/acscatal.1c02621], [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.01.030], [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63369-3], [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814], [10.1016/j.cej.2017.05.058], [10.1021/acscatal.1c05724], [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331], [10.1021/cr5002436], [10.1016/S1872-2067(23)64402-X], [10.1021/acscatal.2c05042], [10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005], [10.1016/j.cej.2007.11.009], [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016], [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816], [10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7], [10.1016/j.jcat.2014.10.018], [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014], [10.1016/j.cej.2020.126656], and [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.12.004] focus exclusively on scientific and engineering aspects of catalysts, with no analysis of larger scale process economics.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: While current research provides important foundations in catalyst development, future work should also consider techno-economic analyses and process simulations to understand commercial viability. As propane dehydrogenation moves further towards commercialization, quantifying operating and capital costs at scale will be essential to determine economic competitiveness. Techno-economic factors like equipment and raw material costs, plant capacity, operating conditions, and integration schemes should be explored using process models. This quantitative analysis can guide research and identify technical targets for catalysts and processes. Overall there is significant opportunity to advance propane dehydrogenation through an integrated approach considering both fundamental catalysis and applied process engineering perspectives.
