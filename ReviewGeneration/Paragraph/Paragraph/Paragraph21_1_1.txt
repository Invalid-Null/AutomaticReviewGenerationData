 <English>

The article with DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816 investigates tuning zeolite Si/Al ratio by dealumination of Beta zeolites. Dealumination creates abundant silanol nests and vacant T-sites, which serve as efficient anchor sites to improve metal dispersion and stabilization. 

Additionally, the study with DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c03941 examines tuning the Si/Al ratio of CHA zeolites for propane dehydrogenation. By evaluating CHA with Si/Al ratios of 5, 12 and 25 and properly tuning the Ga/Al ratio, high propylene selectivity of 96% is maintained regardless of Si/Al ratio, although rates decrease over time. This demonstrates that extra-framework Ga+ sites are the active sites for propane dehydrogenation.

The article with DOI 10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024 investigates the effects of tuning TS-1 crystal size from 100 nm to 5.7 μm. Smaller crystal size introduces mesoporosity, enhancing diffusion and avoiding coking. This significantly improves catalyst conversion, selectivity and lifetime.

Lastly, the study with DOI 10.1021/acscatal.1c01808 incorporates mesoporosity into MFI zeolite nanosheets. The mesoporous structure and abundant silanol groups promote intermetallic alloy nanoparticle formation for enhanced propane dehydrogenation performance.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The files reveal promising approaches of tuning zeolite properties for optimized propane dehydrogenation performance. Critical areas for future focus may encompass:

1) Systematically exploring Si/Al ratio effects across broader zeolite families beyond Beta and CHA. Rational Si/Al ratio tuning guidelines grounded in reaction mechanisms could inform optimized composition.  

2) Further developing mesoporosity introduction techniques to precisely control pore architecture. Ordered hierarchical designs may offer enhanced active site accessibility and product diffusion.

3) Coupling Si/Al ratio tuning with metal doping strategies to potentially synergize Brønsted acid sites with metal active centers. New multifunctional single sites may emerge with targeted co-optimization.

4) Applying operando characterization to gain atomic-level insight on active site evolution under working conditions over time. This foundational understanding of deactivation phenomena may enable enhanced catalyst longevity through rational design.

By integrating these forward-looking perspectives rooted in the insights uncovered, transformative propane dehydrogenation systems with breakthrough efficiency and sustainability may be unlocked.

<Chinese>

DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816的文章通过脱铝Beta沸石来调节分子筛的Si/Al比。脱铝作用形成大量硅醇巢穴和空出的T位点,这些位点可用作有效的锚定位点,以改进金属的分散和稳定。

此外,DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c03941的研究调节了CHA分子筛在丙烷脱氢反应中的Si/Al比。通过评估Si/Al比为5、12和25的CHA样品,并适当调节Ga/Al比,无论Si/Al比如何,丙烯选择性可保持在96%左右,尽管速率随时间减小。这表明架桥Ga+位点是丙烷脱氢反应的活性位点。  

DOI 10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024的文章研究了调节TS-1晶体大小对催化剂性能的影响,从100 nm调节到5.7 μm。更小的晶体大小可引入介孔,增强扩散,避免焦炭,显著提高了催化剂的转化率、选择性和使用寿命。

最后,DOI 10.1021/acscatal.1c01808的研究在MFI分子筛纳米片中导入了介孔。介孔结构和大量硅醇基团有助于形成金属间化合物纳米颗粒,以提高丙烷脱氢性能。

※※※

综合理解和前瞻展望:这些文件揭示了调节分子筛性质以优化丙烷脱氢性能的有前景的方法。未来关键关注的领域可能包括:

1)在Beta和CHA之外的更广泛的分子筛家族中有系统地探索Si/Al比的效应。基于反应机理的合理Si/Al比调节方针可以指导优化组成。
 
2)进一步开发介孔导入技术以精确控制孔结构。 有序的分层设计可以提供增强的活性位点可及性和产物扩散。

3)将Si/Al比调节与金属掺杂策略相结合,以潜在协同Brønsted酸位点与金属活性中心。 针对性的共同优化可能会出现新的多功能单一位点。

4)应用工作条件下的原位表征技术,以原子水平了解活性位点随时间的演变。 对反应失活现象的这种基础理解可以通过合理的设计来提高催化剂的使用寿命。

通过将这些植根于所发现洞见的前瞻性视角整合起来,可能会解锁突破性的丙烷脱氢系统,使其效率和可持续性达到前所未有的高度。
