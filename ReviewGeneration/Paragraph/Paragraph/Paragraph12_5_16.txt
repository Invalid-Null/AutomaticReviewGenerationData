 <English>
Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have evaluated supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most successful and widely used commercially for PDH, employed in the Oleflex process by UOP [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]. One study showed an alumina-supported Pt catalyst maintained high initial activity, before deactivating over multiple cycles due to Pt agglomeration accelerated by sulfur [DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]. Adding sulfur species during preparation or during reaction was found to improve propene selectivity on Pt/alumina, with predosing at 873 K giving the best selectivity [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333]. Atomic layer deposition of metal oxide overcoats like Al2O3 and TiO2 on supported Pt catalysts can enhance PDH yield and selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391].

For Ir-based catalysts, one study compared a supported RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst to a standard Pt/Al2O3 catalyst for PDH. Under kinetic flow conditions, RhCu/SiO2 SAA showed higher activity and 100% propene selectivity versus 80% on Pt/Al2O3 [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]. No studies were found evaluating supported Pd catalysts.

Several investigations focused on the effect of promoters or supports in modulating supported Pt catalyst performance. Addition of 1 wt% boron to Pt/γ-Al2O3 was found to reduce coking 3-fold and increase selectivity to 98% [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. Incorporating sub-nanometric Pt clusters into Ge-UTL zeolites gave an ultrastable and selective catalyst [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. Pt/MgAl2O4 deactivated over time, but had high initial activity [DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]. On dealuminated BEA zeolites, isolated Co2+ cations acted as primary sites with intrinsic PDH activity [DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726].

 Several works also explored deactivation mechanisms. Temperature-programmed oxidation profiles revealed three coke types on spent Pt/Al2O3 PDH catalysts, modulated by H2 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]. And aberration-corrected STEM showed 0.9-1.6 nm Pt clusters on Al2O3 nanosheets [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The studies demonstrate supported mono- and bi-metallic Pt, Ir, and Rh catalysts can achieve high propane conversion, propylene selectivity and stability. But deactivation via coking and metal agglomeration remains a key challenge. The findings highlight opportunities to tune metal-support interfaces and isolate active sites to further enhance performance. Future work might explore novel supports like zeolites and MOFs to stabilize single sites. And operando spectroscopy could offer insights into deactivation pathways during PDH for rational design. Machine learning could also accelerate screening of optimized compositions and structures. There is still much unknown about these catalysts that future efforts might elucidate.

<Chinese>
根据文件提供的详细信息,几项研究评估了用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)的受支持的单金属Pt、Pd和Ir催化剂。

报道称,Pt基催化剂是PDH中最成功和广泛使用的商业催化剂之一,在UOP的Oleflex工艺中被采用[DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。一项研究表明,Al2O3支持的Pt催化剂在多循环后由于硫促进的Pt团聚而失活,但保持了较高的初始活性[DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]。在Pt/Al2O3 催化剂的制备或反应过程中添加硫种可以提高丙烯的选择性,在873 K下预处理获得最佳选择性[DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333]。原子层沉积Al2O3和TiO2覆层可提高受支持Pt催化剂的PDH产率和选择性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。

对于Ir基催化剂,一项研究将RhCu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂与标准Pt/Al2O3催化剂进行了比较。在动力学流动条件下,RhCu/SiO2 SAA催化剂展示出比Pt/Al2O3更高的活性和100%的丙烯选择性(Pt/Al2O3为80%)[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。未找到评估受支持Pd催化剂的研究。

几项研究关注了促进剂或载体对受支持Pt催化剂性能的调节作用。向Pt/γ-Al2O3中添加1 wt%硼可使焦炭形成减少3倍,选择性提高到98% [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。将过渡态Pt簇嵌入Ge-UTL分子筛中可以获得超稳定和选择性高的催化剂[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。Pt/MgAl2O4最初活性很高,但随后会逐渐失活[DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]。在去除铝的BEA分子筛上,孤立的 Co2+阳离子是PDH的主要活性中心[DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。

几项工作也探索了失活机理。温程氧化分析揭示了3种不同焦炭在失活的Pt/Al2O3 PDH催化剂上的存在,这些焦炭受H2调控[DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]。球差校正STEM显示Al2O3纳米片上存在0.9-1.6 nm的Pt簇[DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 这些研究表明,受支持的单金属和双金属Pt、Ir和Rh催化剂可以实现高的丙烷转化率、丙烯选择性和稳定性。但是焦化和金属团聚仍然是关键的失活方式。这些发现强调了优化金属-载体界面和隔离活性中心以进一步提高性能的机会。未来的工作可能会探索像分子筛和金属有机框架等新型载体来稳定单一活性中心。同时,反应过程中的原位光谱技术可以提供有关PDH过程中失活路径的信息,用于合理设计。机器学习也可以加速优化成分和结构的筛选。这些催化剂还有许多未知之处有待未来的研究阐明。
