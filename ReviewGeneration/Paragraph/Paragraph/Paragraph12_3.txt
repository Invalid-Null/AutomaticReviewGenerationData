Several of the articles provided evaluate supported mono-metallic Pt catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). One study shows adding 1 wt% boron promoter to Pt/γ-Al2O3 reduces coke formation threefold and boosts propylene selectivity from 90% to 98% over 12 hours [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. Another investigates the effect of atomic layer deposition (ALD) Al2O3 and TiO2 overcoats on supported Pt catalysts, finding increased PDH yield and selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. 

Data is also presented on other metals like Ru, Rh, and Co for PDH. Supported monometallic Ru nanoparticles are found to give only 4.5% propylene selectivity at 25% conversion, much lower than Pt (46%) [10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]. A single atom alloy RhCu/SiO2 catalyst shows higher activity, 100% selectivity, and coking resistance compared to a Pt/Al2O3 standard [10.1126/science.abg8389]. Isolated Co2+ cations in dealuminated BEA zeolites are identified as the primary active sites [10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726].

Overall, Pt-based catalysts seem most promising for commercial PDH processes. The articles emphasize stabilizing isolated Pt sites and tuning metal-support interactions to optimize stability and minimize deactivation. However, emerging catalysts like SAAs may offer opportunities to surpass Pt. Further research is still needed to develop optimal PDH catalysts.

※※※ 
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings reveal Pt is the predominant mono-metallic catalyst studied for commercial PDH applications thus far. However, challenges remain regarding stability and selectivity. This presents opportunities to explore burgeoning concepts like single atom alloys and isolated metal sites with enhanced interfaces. Adopting advanced characterization techniques can also better elucidate catalyst structure-activity relationships to guide optimization. Bringing such innovative approaches to the fore may unlock superior PDH catalysts beyond conventional Pt benchmarks.

<Chinese>
几篇文章评估了载体支撑的单金属Pt催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。一项研究表明,向Pt/γ-Al2O3添加1 wt%的硼促进剂可以将焦炭形成量减少三倍,并将丙烯选择性从90%提高到98%,反应12小时[10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。另一项研究调查了ALD Al2O3和TiO2覆层对载体支撑Pt催化剂的影响,发现PDH产率和选择性得以提高[10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。

数据也呈现了Ru,Rh和Co等其他金属用于PDH的性能。结果表明,载体支撑的单金属Ru纳米粒子在25%转化率下仅给出4.5%的丙烯选择性,远低于Pt的46% [10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]。一种单原子合金RhCu/SiO2催化剂相比Pt/Al2O3标准展现了更高的活性、100%选择性和抗焦炭性[10.1126/science.abg8389]。硅-沸石分子筛中离散的Co2+正离子被确认为主要活性位点[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。

总体而言,Pt基催化剂在商业PDH工艺中最有前景。文章强调稳定Pt隔离活性位点并调节金属与载体间的相互作用,以优化稳定性并最大限度减少失活。但是,新兴的SAA催化剂或许能超越Pt。开发理想PDH催化剂仍需要进一步研究。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:研究结果显示,到目前为止Pt是研究最多的用于商业PDH应用的单金属催化剂。但是,Pt催化剂在稳定性和选择性方面仍存在挑战。这为探索新兴的单原子合金和隔离的金属活性位点概念以及增强的界面提供了机会。采用先进的表征技术也可以更好地阐明催化剂的结构-活性关系,以指导优化。将这些创新方法推向前沿或许能解锁优于常规Pt基准的卓越PDH催化剂。
