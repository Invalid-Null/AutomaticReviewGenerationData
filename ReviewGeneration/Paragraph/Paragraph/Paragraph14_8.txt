Based on the detailed analysis from the references, the articles do not propose specific mechanistic pathways for propane dehydrogenation over supported metal catalysts in most cases. 

A few exceptions are noted where mechanistic insights are provided for specific supported metal systems:

- On supported vanadium oxide (VOx/ZrO2) catalysts, a Mars-van Krevelen redox mechanism is proposed involving lattice oxygen activation, alkyl species formation, and OH recombination steps [10.1006/jcat.1999.2510].

- On supported Pt catalysts, isolated Pt sites are identified as crucial for selectively cleaving the first and second C-H bonds while avoiding the third dehydrogenation that leads to coking [10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9]. A 6-step cycle is described involving propane adsorption, dehydrogenation, hydrogen removal and propene desorption over single Pt sites [10.1021/acscatal.2c01631].

- Detailed free energy profiles are calculated for propane dehydrogenation pathways over supported Pt and PtSn nanoclusters, considering dehydrogenation, cracking and hydrogenation steps [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671].

Overall, further mechanistic studies focused specifically on propane dehydrogenation pathways over practical supported metal catalysts could provide useful insights to guide the development of more selective and stable catalysts.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the sporadic mechanistic pictures revealed in the references, an opportunity exists to undertake more dedicated kinetic and spectroscopic studies to elucidate the underlying pathways over supported metals under realistic reaction environments. Uncovering these fundamental steps and intermediates can pave the way for rational catalyst optimization. Future undertakings may emphasize bridging the material and mechanistic gaps between surface science models and practical catalysts.

注意到一些例外,其中为特定的受支持金属体系提供了机理见解:

- 在受支持的钒氧化物(VOx/ZrO2)催化剂上,提出了Mars-van Krevelen氧化还原机理,包括晶格氧激活、烷基物种形成和OH重组步骤 [10.1006/jcat.1999.2510]。

- 通过计算受支持的Pt和PtSn纳米簇的自由能曲线,考虑了脱氢、裂解和氢化步骤的丙烷脱氢途径的详细情况 [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。

总的来说,关注实际受支持金属催化剂上的丙烷脱氢途径的进一步机理研究可以提供有用的见解,以指导更选择性和更稳定催化剂的开发。
