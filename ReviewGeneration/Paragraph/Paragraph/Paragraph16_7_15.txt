Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Pt-Sn Catalysts
Several studies have evaluated Pt-Sn bimetallic catalysts for propane dehydrogenation and found improved performance compared to monometallic Pt catalysts. Adding Sn as a promoter helps decrease Pt particle size, improves dispersion, increases stability, and enhances propylene selectivity while inhibiting coke formation [Placeholder_of_DOI1], [Placeholder_of_DOI2]. This can be attributed to electronic and ensemble effects where Sn modifies the electron density of Pt through ligand effects and reduces the number of Pt multiple adsorption sites through partially blocking the surface (ensemble effect) [Placeholder_of_DOI3]. 

Computational studies suggest Pt-Sn intermetallic alloys increase dehydrogenation stability but can also decrease activity by increasing the propane dissociation barrier [Placeholder_of_DOI4]. This trade-off between stability and activity is supported experimentally, with increasing Sn content in PtSn/SiO2 lowering activity but improving stability [Placeholder_of_DOI5]. An optimal Pt/Sn ratio around 2:1 to 3:1 maintains moderate activity while gaining stability benefits [Placeholder_of_DOI6], [Placeholder_of_DOI7]. Characterization indicates Pt3Sn1 and Pt2Sn1 are highly active phases [Placeholder_of_DOI8].

Pt-Ga and Pt-Zn Catalysts
Beyond Pt-Sn, other Pt bimetallic catalysts like Pt-Ga and Pt-Zn have shown promise for propane dehydrogenation. Pt-Ga intermetallic nanoparticles can achieve >95% propylene selectivity over a broad temperature window with excellent stability, significantly outperforming reference PtSn/Al2O3 catalysts [Placeholder_of_DOI9]. Isolated Pt atoms stabilized by grafting Zn into zeolite frameworks also display remarkable catalytic performance approaching equilibrium conversion and selectivity [Placeholder_of_DOI10]. PtZn intermetallic alloy clusters in MFI zeolite channels boost initial activity 10 times higher than Pt nanoparticles while greatly suppressing deactivation [Placeholder_of_DOI11].

In summary, alloying platinum with promoters like Sn, Ga and Zn in propane dehydrogenation catalysts serves to modulate the geometric and electronic properties of Pt active sites. This tuning of adsorption properties and modification of active site ensembles generates a balanced enhancement between activity, selectivity and stability. Careful compositional and structural control is necessary to optimize the synergistic effects arising from the Pt-promoter interactions.

Pt-Sn 催化剂
几项研究评估了Pt-Sn双金属催化剂用于丙烷脱氢反应,发现与单金属Pt催化剂相比具有改进的性能。添加Sn作为助剂有助于降低Pt颗粒大小,改善分散性,增加稳定性,增强丙烯选择性,同时抑制焦炭生成 [Placeholder_of_DOI1]、[Placeholder_of_DOI2]。这可以归因于电子效应和配位数效应,其中Sn通过配体效应改变Pt的电子密度,并通过部分阻挡表面减少Pt的多重吸附位点数量(配位数效应)[Placeholder_of_DOI3]。

Pt-Ga 和 Pt-Zn 催化剂
除了Pt-Sn之外,其他Pt双金属催化剂如Pt-Ga和Pt-Zn在丙烷脱氢反应中也展现出前景。Pt-Ga中间体纳米粒子可以在宽温度范围内实现>95%的丙烯选择性与卓越的稳定性,明显优于参比的PtSn/Al2O3催化剂[Placeholder_of_DOI9]。通过在分子筛框架中掺入Zn固定的隔离Pt原子也显示出接近平衡转换率和选择性的显着催化性能[Placeholder_of_DOI10]。MFI分子筛孔道中PtZn中间金属合金簇可使初始活性提高10倍,同时大大抑制失活[Placeholder_of_DOI11]。

总结起来,在丙烷脱氢反应催化剂中采用Sn、Ga和Zn等助剂与铂形成合金,可以调节Pt活性位点的几何和电子性质。这种对Pt活性位点的吸附性质调控和配位环境改造,实现了活性、选择性与稳定性之间的平衡增强。必需精密控制成分和结构以优化Pt-助剂相互作用所产生的协同效应。
