Based on the details extracted from the file, a number of studies employ advanced microscopy techniques to characterize metal-support interfaces in propane dehydrogenation catalysts. 

One study utilizes integrated differential phase contrast scanning transmission electron microscopy (iDPC-STEM) to reveal isolated cobalt motifs with distorted tetrahedral {(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2} structure embedded at T1(7) and T3(9) sites within the MFI framework [DOI:10.1021/jacs.2c02636]. This directly images the metal-support interface.

Another study uses aberration-corrected HAADF-STEM and EDS mapping to show the formation of atomically ordered intermetallic PtZn nanoparticles with alternating columns on the SiO2 support [DOI:10.1016/j.chempr.2020.10.008]. The images reveal periodic [PtZn4] ensembles that expose isolated Pt single sites. 

One article employs TEM coupled with electron diffraction analysis to provide insights into Pt particle agglomeration and sintering on alumina supports during propane dehydrogenation [DOI:10.1016/j.cej.2011.11.055]. This sheds light on changes occurring at the metal-support interface over time.

A number of other advanced techniques are also utilized, including aberration-corrected STEM [DOI:10.1038/s41467-021-23426-z, DOI:10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7], HRTEM [DOI:10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63202-4, DOI:10.1002/anie.201805150], and STEM-EDS mapping [DOI:10.1016/j.jcat.2020.02.019, DOI:10.1016/j.chempr.2023.07.002]. These provide valuable nanoscale insights into the dispersion, location, structure, and stability of metal sites on various supports.

一项研究利用集成差分相差扫描透射电镜(iDPC-STEM)揭示了扭曲的四面体 {(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2} 结构的隔离钴基团嵌入 MFI 框架中的 T1(7) 和 T3(9) 位点[DOI:10.1021/jacs.2c02636]。这直接成像了金属载体界面。

另一项研究采用消 spherical 校正 HAADF-STEM 和 EDS 映射表明 SiO2 支撑上形成了原子序阶的 PtZn 纳米粒子,具有交替的列[DOI:10.1016/j.chempr.2020.10.008]。该图像揭示了周期性的 [PtZn4] 组合体,暴露出孤立的 Pt 单体位点。

一篇文章采用 TEM 耦合电子衍射分析,提供了丙烷脱氢过程中铝载体上 Pt 颗粒的团聚和烧结的见解[DOI:10.1016/j.cej.2011.11.055]。这揭示了随时间金属载体界面的变化。

还采用了许多其他先进技术,包括消差修正 STEM [DOI:10.1038/s41467-021-23426-z, DOI:10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]、HRTEM [DOI:10.1016/S1872-2067(18)63202-4, DOI:10.1002/anie.201 805150]和 STEM-EDS 映射[DOI:10.1016/j.jcat.2020.02.019,DOI:10.1016/j.chempr.2023.07.002]。这些提供了各种载体上金属位点的分散、位置、结构和稳定性的宝贵纳米尺度的见解。
