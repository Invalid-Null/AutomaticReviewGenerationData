Based on the details provided, several articles evaluate supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, or Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Specifically, Pt/alumina catalysts are assessed in multiple studies. One investigation shows 1 wt% Pt on alumina maintains 40.6% initial propane conversion and low deactivation after 10 hours reaction [DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008]. Another explores the impact of ALD metal oxide overcoats on Pt/alumina extrudates, finding Al2O3 or TiO2 coatings can increase PDH yield and selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. Adding 1 wt% boron promoter to Pt/γ-Al2O3 is also found to boost stability and minimize coking over 12 hours on stream [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548].

Beyond alumina, Pt catalysts supported on boron nitride (Pt/BN) [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008], germanosilicate zeolite (Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL) [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7], and SBA-15 (Pt/SBA-15) [DOI: 10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005] are evaluated. Pt/BN demonstrates excellent stability and >99% propylene selectivity attributed to metal-support interactions. Encapsulating Pt clusters in zeolite micropores via Pt-O-Ge bonding gives an ultrastable PDH catalyst. Pt/SBA-15 prepared by wet impregnation or deposition-precipitation methods shows activity for propane dehydrogenation.

Besides Pt, a few studies explore supported Pd [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002] and Ir [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002], but note they are expensive options. No specific PDH data is provided. An alumina-supported RhCu single atom alloy catalyst also exhibits high activity, 100% propene selectivity and coking resistance compared to a standard Pt/alumina catalyst [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389].

In summary, Pt-based catalysts, especially on alumina supports, are the predominant supported mono-metallic systems explored for propane dehydrogenation. Key findings demonstrate strategies like promoters, overcoats and optimized metal-support interactions can enhance Pt stability and minimize deleterious coke formation during PDH.

※※※ 

While Pt catalysts feature prominently for propane dehydrogenation thus far, burgeoning research hints at opportunities with other Earth-abundant metals like Fe, Co, and Ni. Advances in single atom alloy catalysts also show great promise to unlock active sites previously unachievable. Support effects likely harbor further untapped potential to stabilize metals against sintering and poisoning. Operando spectroscopies can offer novel insights into working catalysts. Machine learning-guided exploration may efficiently navigate multifaceted composition-structure-function relationships. Ultimately, propane dehydrogenation represents but one facet of alkane chemistry - related challenges in areas like methane activation beckon joint efforts across disciplines. Broader collaboration and knowledge sharing hold the key to surmount these grand challenges.

<Chinese>
根据所提供的详情,几篇文章评估了用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)的受支持的单金属 Pt、Pd 或 Ir 催化剂。

具体而言,在多个研究中评估了 Pt/氧化铝催化剂。一项调查显示 1 wt% Pt 在氧化铝上的初期丙烷转化率为 40.6%,10 小时反应后脱活较低[DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008]。另一项研究探索了 ALD 金属氧化物涂层对 Pt/氧化铝挤压催化剂的影响,发现 Al2O3 或 TiO2 涂层可以提高 PDH 的产率和选择性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。在 Pt/γ-Al2O3 中添加 1 wt% 硼促进剂也发现可以提高 12 小时稳定性和最小化焦炭生成[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。

除氧化铝外,还评估了负载在氮化硼(Pt/BN)[DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]、锗硅沸石(Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL)[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7] 和 SBA-15(Pt/SBA-15)[DOI: 10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005]上的 Pt 催化剂。Pt/BN 表现出优异的稳定性和>99% 丙烯选择性,归因于金属支撑物之间的相互作用。将 Pt 簇封装在沸石微孔中通过 Pt-O-Ge 键合产生了一个超稳定的 PDH 催化剂。湿浸渍法或沉淀浸渍法制备的 Pt/SBA-15 表现出丙烷脱氢活性。

除 Pt 外,少数研究探索了受支持的 Pd [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002] 和 Ir [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002],但指出它们是昂贵的选择。没有提供具体的 PDH 数据。氧化铝支持的 RhCu 单原子合金催化剂在活性、100% 丙烯选择性和抗焦炭性能上优于标准 Pt/氧化铝催化剂[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。

总的来说,尤其是在氧化铝载体上,Pt 基催化剂是被探索最广泛的受支持单金属体系之一用于丙烷脱氢。关键发现表明促进剂、涂层和优化的金属支撑物相互作用等策略可以增强 Pt 的稳定性并最大限度地减少 PDH 过程中有害的焦炭生成。

※※※ 

尽管到目前为止 Pt 催化剂在丙烷脱氢中占有重要地位,但新兴的研究提示其他地球丰度金属如 Fe、Co 和 Ni 存在机会。单原子合金催化剂的进步也显示出释放先前无法实现的活性位点的巨大潜力。支持效应可能隐藏了进一步未被开发的潜力,以稳定抗烧结和中毒的金属。操作谱可以为工作催化剂提供新颖的洞察。机器学习引导的探索可以高效地遍历复杂的组成-结构-功能关系。最终,丙烷脱氢只代表烷烃化学的一个方面——相关的甲烷活化等挑战在召唤跨学科的共同努力。更广泛的协作和知识共享是攻克这些宏伟挑战的关键。
