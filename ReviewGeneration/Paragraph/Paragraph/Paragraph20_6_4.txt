Zeolites and Mesoporous Supports - What zeolite topologies like MFI, FER, BEA have shown promise as supports?

Several studies have explored zeolite topologies like MFI, FER, and BEA as promising supports for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts due to properties like large surface area, ordered micropore channels, thermal stability, and tunable acidity and pore dimensions [Placeholder_Of_DOI1, Placeholder_Of_DOI2, Placeholder_Of_DOI3].

MFI topology zeolites such as silicalite-1 and ZSM-5 have been frequently investigated. For example, one study showed that PtSn nanoclusters confined within the 10R channels of purely siliceous MFI zeolite demonstrated high activity and selectivity for PDH by optimizing the Pt:Sn ratio and interaction within the 0.5-0.6 nm clusters [Placeholder_Of_DOI4]. Another report found that isolated Pt atoms anchored to SiOZn-OH groups in the framework of hierarchical MFI nanosheets gave superior performance to dealuminated BEA, achieving 30% propane conversion and 90% propylene selectivity approaching equilibrium levels [Placeholder_Of_DOI5].

Dealuminated zeolite BEA has also been explored as a promising support. Studies have managed to introduce isolated Pt atoms bonded with Zn sites inside BEA pores, forming durable catalysts with high conversion and selectivity [Placeholder_Of_DOI6]. The hydroxyl nests on dealuminated BEA were also able to anchor PtM alloys [Placeholder_Of_DOI7]. 

Some comparisons have been made between MFI and BEA supports. One study found Pt-Zn/MFI suppressed propylene desorption leading to coking while Pt-Ga/BEA achieved close to equilibrium conversion for over 30 days [Placeholder_Of_DOI8]. Overall however, few direct comparisons of different zeolite topologies have been undertaken.

In summary, MFI and BEA zeolite supports have shown particular promise for stabilizing isolated Pt active sites and bimetallic clusters, enabling design of highly durable and selective PDH catalysts. Further research could systematically compare different zeolitic supports and porous structures.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The insights uncovered in the numerous reports highlight the potential of zeolites like MFI and BEA as specialized supports to create extremely durable and active PDH catalysts when combined with suitable Pt-based species. Key knowledge gaps remain however in achieving a truly comprehensive perspective regarding optimal support properties. Future undertakings could adopt high-throughput methodologies to rapidly screen various zeolite compositions paired with data-driven models to predict ideal combinations of support pores, acid sites and metal dispersions tailored for targeted PDH performance. Such efforts promise to expand the horizons and offer hitherto uncharted directions toward designing exceptionally robust PDH catalyst systems.

分子筛和介孔支持——MFI、FER、BEA 等结构的分子筛作为支撑剂显示什么前景?

几项研究探索了 MFI、FER 和 BEA 等拓扑结构的分子筛,作为丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂的有前景的支撑剂,这是由于它们具有大的比表面积、有序的微孔通道、热稳定性以及可调节的酸度和孔径尺寸等特性[Placeholder_Of_DOI1,Placeholder_Of_DOI2,Placeholder_Of_DOI3]。

如硅沸石-1 和 ZSM-5 等 MFI 结构的分子筛已经被广泛研究。例如,一项研究表明,限制在纯硅 MFI 分子筛 10R 通道中的 PtSn 纳米簇,通过优化 Pt/Sn 的比例和 0.5-0.6 nm 簇中的相互作用,表现出高的活性和选择性[Placeholder_Of_DOI4]。另一报告发现,锚定在分层 MFI 纳米片上硅氧锌羟基团构架中的离子态 Pt 原子给出优于去铝 BEA 分子筛的性能,丙烷转化率达 30%,丙烯选择性接近平衡水平[Placeholder_Of_DOI5]。


去铝化 BEA 分子筛也被证明是一种有前景的支撑剂。一些研究成功将孤立的 Pt 原子与 BEA 微孔内的 Zn 位点结合,形成具有高转化率和选择性的稳定催化剂[Placeholder_Of_DOI6]。去铝 BEA 分子筛上的羟基巢也能锚定 PtM 合金颗粒[Placeholder_Of_DOI7]。


已进行一些 MFI 和 BEA 支撑剂的比较。一项研究发现 Pt-Zn/MFI 抑制丙烯解吸导致结焦,而 Pt-Ga/BEA 30 天内保持接近平衡转化率[Placeholder_Of_DOI8]。但是,目前少有系统比较不同分子筛拓扑结构的研究。


总之,MFI 和 BEA 分子筛支撑剂在稳定孤立的 Pt 活性中心和双金属簇方面显示出特别的前景,这使得设计高稳定和高选择性 PDH 催化剂成为可能。进一步的研究可以系统地比较不同的分子筛支撑和多孔结构。


※※※

综合理解与未来展望:众多研究报告的发现表明,当与合适的 Pt 基础构筛相结合时,MFI 和 BEA 等分子筛可以作为专门的支撑剂来制备极其稳定和活跃的 PDH 催化剂。但是,对于实现真正全面视角以确定最佳的支撑剂性质,关键的知识空白仍然存在。未来的工作可以采用高通量方法快速筛选各种分子筛组合,并与数据驱动模型相结合来预测理想的支撑剂孔结构、酸位和金属dispersion组合,这些都是为达到目标的 PDH 性能而量身定制的。这种工作有望拓宽研究视野,提供迄今未有过的方向,以设计异常稳定的 PDH 催化剂体系。
