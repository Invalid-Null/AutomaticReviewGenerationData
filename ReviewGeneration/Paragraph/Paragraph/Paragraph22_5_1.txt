Based on the details provided in the file, some of the articles describe techniques for synthesizing isolated single sites in zeolitic supports for propane dehydrogenation catalysts. 

The article with DOI 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726 discusses using dealumination to generate isolated silanol groups in the zeolite Beta support, and then immobilizing isolated Co2+ cations at these sites via vacuum impregnation [DOI 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726].

Another article employs a ligand-protected hydrothermal synthesis approach to construct isolated Co-motifs with a distorted tetrahedral structure that are homogeneously embedded into the T1(7) and T3(9) sites of MFI zeolites [DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636]. Advanced microscopy and spectroscopy techniques confirm the location, uniformity and structure of these isolated Co sites.

Isolated boron sites in zeolitic supports are achieved in the DOI 10.1126/science.abe7935 article via a solvent-free crystallization strategy with TPA+ hydroxide. This yields the BS-1 catalyst with an Si/B ratio of 62 and uniform dispersion of isolated boron in the framework.

The DOI 10.1021/acscatal.2c05042 work utilizes Fe-EDTA complexation during hydrothermal synthesis to dope Fe species into silicalite-1 while preventing precipitates. This anchors isolated Pt atoms, yielding stable single Pt sites interacting with the Fe-doped framework.

In contrast, several other articles focus on non-zeolitic supports like silica and alumina, or supported nanoparticles, without describing single site zeolite synthesis techniques [DOIs 10.1021/acscatal.3c00939, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00728, 10.1016/S0021-9517(02)00015-5, 10.1021/acscatal.0c05711 etc.]

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The collected details reveal some promising techniques like dealumination, hydrothermal synthesis with structure-directing agents, and framework doping that enable stabilizing isolated single sites in zeolitic materials. However, most of the techniques are demonstrated only for a few specific zeolite types and active metals. Further research could explore extending these methods to incorporate different transition metals and metastable sites into various zeolite topologies beyond conventional supports like ZSM-5 and Beta. Emerging characterization tools with higher sensitivity and resolution would also aid such efforts. Ultimately, progress in this area may spur development of next-generation single-site zeolite catalysts with finely tuned properties and performance for targeted reactions. Broader adoption of these materials could impact processes like propane dehydrogenation at commercial scales.

根据文件提供的详细信息,一些文章描述了用于丙烷脱氢催化剂的分离式单位点沸石载体的合成技术。

DOI 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726的文章讨论了使用去铝技术在分子筛Beta载体中生成分离的硅醇基团,然后通过真空浸渍技术将分离的Co2+阳离子固定在这些位点上。

另一篇文章采用配体保护的水热合成方法,在MFI分子筛的T1(7)和T3(9)位点构建均匀嵌入的扭曲四面体结构的分离Co基团[DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636]。先进的显微技术和光谱技术确认了这些分离的Co位点的位置、均匀性和结构。

DOI 10.1126/science.abe7935的文章中,采用TPA+氢氧化物的无溶剂结晶策略实现了沸石载体中分离的硼位点。这产生了Si/B比为62且骨架中均匀分散有分离硼的BS-1催化剂。

DOI 10.1021/acscatal.2c05042的工作采用水热合成过程中的Fe-EDTA络合将Fe种植入硅沸石-1,同时防止沉淀。这使得分离的Pt原子稳定地锚定,产生与掺Fe框架相互作用的稳定单 Pt 位点。

相比之下,其他几篇文章侧重于非沸石载体如二氧化硅和氧化铝,或者载有纳米粒子的催化剂,没有描述单位点沸石合成技术[DOIs 10.1021/acscatal.3c00939,10.1021/acscatal.3c00728,10.1016/S0021-9517(02)00015-5,10.1021/acscatal.0c05711等]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:收集的详细信息揭示了一些有希望的技术,如去铝、带有结构引导剂的水热合成和框架掺杂,这些技术可以实现沸石材料中单独位点的稳定。然而,大多数技术只针对少数特定的沸石类型和金属活性位点进行了演示。进一步的研究可以探索将这些方法拓展到不同过渡金属和各种沸石拓扑结构中的非稳定位点,超出常规的ZSM-5和Beta载体。新兴的表征工具具有更高的灵敏度和分辨率,也将有助于这些工作。最终,这一领域的进展可能会促进新一代单站点沸石催化剂的开发,这些催化剂具有针对目标反应进行精细调节的性能。这些材料的更广泛采用可能会影响丙烷脱氢等商业规模的工艺。
