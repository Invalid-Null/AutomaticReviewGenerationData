 <English>

Based on the details extracted from the references, catalysts can facilitate propane dehydrogenation by lowering the activation energy barriers through various mechanisms. 

Industrial PDH processes utilize supported metal catalysts like Pt/Al2O3 and CrOx/Al2O3 that provide active sites to selectively break C-H bonds in propane [DOI1, DOI2, DOI7, DOI8, DOI9]. DFT calculations have elucidated the challenge of tuning catalyst reactivity to activate propane C-H bonds while avoiding over-reaction [DOI3]. Alternative metal oxides like ZrO2, Ga2O3, and VOx have shown promise, where defects and coordination unsaturation create active sites [DOI4, DOI5]. The incorporation of metals like Sn into zeolites introduces Lewis acid sites that catalyze dehydrogenation [DOI6]. Mesoporous supports and metal alloying also facilitate dehydrogenation through electronic and geometric effects [DOI10, DOI11].

※※※

The insights into propane dehydrogenation mechanisms and active site requirements open up new directions for catalyst design. Further computational screening and microkinetic modeling can identify optimum sites balancing activity and selectivity [DOI12]. Operando spectroscopy and imaging can provide atomic-level understanding of working catalysts to guide improvements [DOI13]. Machine learning applied to PDH data may reveal additional useful descriptors [DOI14]. Ultimately, progress in these and other areas can enable the rational design of optimized PDH catalysts.

根据参考文献的详细信息,催化剂可以通过各种机制降低活化能垒促进丙烷脱氢。

工业PDH过程使用Pt/Al2O3和CrOx/Al2O3等支持金属催化剂,提供活性位点选择性地断裂丙烷中的C-H键[DOI1,DOI2,DOI7,DOI8,DOI9]。 DFT计算已经阐明了调谐催化剂反应性以激活丙烷C-H键而避免过度反应的挑战[DOI3]。如ZrO2,Ga2O3和VOx等替代金属氧化物显示了前景,其中的缺陷和配位不饱和化创建了活性位点[DOI4,DOI5]。金属如Sn掺入沸石中引入了路易斯酸位点,催化脱氢[DOI6]。介孔支持和金属合金化也通过电子和几何效应促进脱氢[DOI10,DOI11]。

※※※

对丙烷脱氢机制和活性位点要求的洞察为催化剂设计开辟了新方向。进一步的计算筛选和微动力学建模可以识别在活性和选择性之间达到最佳平衡的位点[DOI12]。原位光谱和成像可以提供工作催化剂的原子水平理解以指导改进[DOI13]。应用于PDH数据的机器学习可以揭示额外的有用描述符[DOI14]。最终,在这些和其他领域的进步可以实现优化的PDH催化剂的合理设计。
