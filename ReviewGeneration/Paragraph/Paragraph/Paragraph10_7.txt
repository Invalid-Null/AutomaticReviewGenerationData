 <English>
Based on the details extracted from the literature, two neighboring coordinatively unsaturated Zr cations (Zrcus) located at an oxygen vacancy are proposed as the active sites for propane dehydrogenation over ZrO2 catalysts [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012]. These sites break the C-H bonds in propane homolytically to form an isopropyl intermediate, followed by further dehydrogenation to propene. For CrZrOx mixed oxide catalysts, the presence of Cr helps reduce the energy barrier for methyl C-H bond activation compared to pure ZrO2, facilitating propene formation [10.1021/acscatal.9b05063]. 

Mars-van Krevelen type redox mechanisms are suggested for propane oxidative dehydrogenation over VOx [10.1006/jcat.1999.2510, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023] and MoVO [10.1021/jacs.9b09235] catalysts, involving lattice oxygen participation. As lattice oxygen is depleted, V3+ sites catalyze subsequent non-oxidative pathways in these catalysts [10.1021/jacs.9b09235]. For Ga2O3 catalysts, a heterolytic dissociation mechanism is proposed, with propane dissociatively adsorbing to form gallium hydride and alkoxide intermediates [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001, 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The current literature provides important mechanistic insights into propane dehydrogenation over various metal oxide catalysts. Key themes include the significance of coordinatively unsaturated sites at oxygen vacancies in ZrO2, the role of Cr in promoting activity in mixed oxides like CrZrOx, and the involvement of Mars-van Krevelen vs heterolytic dissociation pathways over redox-active (VOx, MoVO) versus non-redox (Ga2O3) metal oxides respectively. 

However, many aspects remain unclear regarding the full reaction networks and how to optimize selectivity. Future research could explore operando spectroscopy techniques paired with isotopic labeling to track reaction intermediates over different sites. Data science approaches may uncover improved descriptors and provide atomic-level understanding connecting structure with selectivity trends. Utilizing these advancing tools to obtain a more wholistic mechanistic picture could unlock new catalyst design principles targeting optimized productivity and propene selectivity.

<Chinese>
根据文献中提取的详细信息,两种位于氧位的无配位锆金属阳离子(Zrcus)被提出为丙烷脱氢反应上的ZrO2催化剂的活性中心[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012]。这些位点同源裂解丙烷中的C-H键,形成异丙基中间体,后续发生进一步的脱氢生成丙烯。 对于CrZrOx混合氧化物催化剂,Cr的存在有助于降低甲基C-H键激活的能垒,促进丙烯的形成[10.1021/acscatal.9b05063]。

Mars-van Krevelen氧化-还原机理被提出用于VOx [10.1006/jcat.1999.2510, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023] 和MoVO [10.1021/jacs.9b09235]催化剂上的丙烷氧化脱氢反应,涉及晶格氧的参与。随着晶格氧的耗尽,V3+位点催化这些催化剂中的后续非氧化路径[10.1021/jacs.9b09235]。 对于Ga2O3催化剂,提出了一种异裂解机理,丙烷发生解离吸附形成镓氢化物和碱化物中间体[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001, 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:当前文献对于各种金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢反应提供了重要的机理视角。主要主题包括氧位无配位位点在ZrO2中的重要性,Cr在像CrZrOx这样的混合氧化物中提高活性的作用,以及Mars-van Krevelen与异裂解途径在VOx,MoVO等氧化-还原型和Ga2O3等非氧化-还原型金属氧化物上的参与。

然而,关于完整的反应网络和如何优化选择性,许多方面仍不清楚。未来的研究可以利用操作技术与同位素标记相结合,跟踪不同位点上的反应中间体。数据科学方法可以发现改进的描述符,并在原子水平上理解结构与选择性趋势之间的联系。利用这些先进的工具获得更加立体的机理画面,可以解锁新的催化剂设计原则,以实现生产率和丙烯选择性的优化。
