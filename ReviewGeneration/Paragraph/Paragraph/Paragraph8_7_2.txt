	<English>
		Based on the details extracted from the literature, a few metal oxide catalysts have demonstrated promising propane dehydrogenation activity. Chromia (Cr2O3) based catalysts, in particular, are among the most active options for the CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation pathway. As noted in reference [10.1021/acscatal.2c05286], Cr-based catalysts supported on MCM-41 exhibit superior performance than other metal oxides like Ga, Ni, V, Fe, Mn, and Co. Furthermore, CrOx serves as the vital component in the commercial CATOFIN process leveraged for direct propane dehydrogenation to propylene. However, poor thermal stability and rapid deactivation remain pressing issues that need addressing.
		
		Gallium oxide (Ga2O3) has also garnered attention, especially the β-Ga2O3 polymorph with its abundance of medium-strength acid sites [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]. Experiments suggest the catalytic activity trends with surface acidity, implicating the influential role of Brønsted acid sites. Incorporating Ga2O3 within mixed oxides like ZrGaO presents another opportunity to potentially modulate selectivity via metal-support interactions [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. 
		
		Besides the individual metal oxide catalysts, composites and mixed oxides hold promise to manifest synergistic effects. For instance, coupling Cr2O3 with SiO2 modestly bolsters propane conversion rates [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. The interfacial contact possibly mitigates over-reduction. Furthermore, references [10.1016/j.jcat.1994.1183, 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333] have inspected supported CrOx catalysts on varied carriers like SiO2 and Al2O3. Although not extensively characterized,initial activity data at high temperatures hints at decent propylene yields.
		
		In summary, Cr2O3 and Ga2O3 centered catalysts have exhibited propane dehydrogenation feasibility. Nonetheless, scalable synthesis procedures and stability assessments remain lacking. Multifaceted strategies blending optimized metal oxide compositions, tailored metal-support interplays and considered reactor configurations could pave the way. ※※※
		
		Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Capitalizing on the chromium and gallium oxide catalyst platforms, future work might concentrate on unlocking favorable surface structural motifs, potentially through selective doping and lost-cost mesoporous carriers to boost propylene selectivity. Operando spectroscopic and microscopic interrogations could elucidate transformation mechanisms and deactivation phenomena. Translating the fundamental knowledge to flow reactor testing would generate representative data for technoeconomic analysis, guiding scale-up directions.

		根据从文献中提取的详细信息,少数几种金属氧化物催化剂已经展示了很有前景的丙烷脱氢活性。尤其是以铬氧化物(Cr2O3)为基础的催化剂,是 CO2 辅助氧化脱氢途径中最活跃的选择之一。如参考文献[10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]所指出的,与其他金属氧化物如 Ga、Ni、V、Fe、Mn 和 Co 相比,载于 MCM-41 的铬基催化剂表现更好。此外,CrOx 作为商业 CATOFIN 工艺中将丙烷直接脱氢制丙烯所用的关键组分。然而,其较差的热稳定性和快速失活仍是亟待解决的紧迫问题。
		
		氧化镓(Ga2O3)也引起了人们的重视,特别是拥有大量中等强度酸点的 β-Ga2O3 多晶体[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]。实验表明,其催化活性与表面酸度有关,这意味着 Brønsted 酸位的关键作用。在混合氧化物基体如 ZrGaO 中掺入 Ga2O3,这为通过金属支撑相互作用来调控选择性开辟了另一种机会 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。
		
		除了单一金属氧化物催化剂之外,复合材料和混合氧化物也有前景通过协同效应来实现更高的催化性能。例如,铬氧化物与二氧化硅的复合可使丙烷转化率有所提高[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。界面接触有可能抑制过度还原。此外,参考文献 [10.1016/j.jcat.1994.1183, 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333] 检查了在 SiO2 和 Al2O3 等不同载体上的 CrOx 支持催化剂。尽管没有广泛表征,但在高温下的初步活性数据显示了相当可观的丙烯收率。
		
		总之,以 Cr2O3 和 Ga2O3为基础的催化剂已经显示了一定的丙烷脱氢可行性。尽管如此,可扩展的合成工艺和稳定性评估仍然缺乏。把握优化的金属氧化物成分、定制的金属支撑相互作用和考量后的反应器配置等多面手段,可能会为获得突破性进展铺平道路。※※※
		
		综合理解与未来展望: 在铬氧化物和镓氧化物催化剂平台的基础上,未来的工作可能会集中在揭示有利的表面结构模式,例如通过选择性掺杂和廉价的介孔载体来提高丙烯选择性。操作波谱学和显微学方面可以阐明反应机理和失活现象。将这些基础知识转化为流式反应器测试会产生具有代表性的数据以供技术经济分析,指导规模化方向。
