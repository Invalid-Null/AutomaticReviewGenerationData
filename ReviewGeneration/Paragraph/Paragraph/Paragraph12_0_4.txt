Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic platinum (Pt), palladium (Pd), and iridium (Ir) catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most successful and widely used propane dehydrogenation catalysts commercially [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]. An alumina-supported Pt catalyst shows good ability to activate paraffinic C-H bonds while minimizing C-C bond cleavage, making it an important component of propane dehydrogenation processes [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. Adding promoters such as 1 wt% boron to supported Pt/γ-Al2O3 catalysts can decrease coke formation 3-fold and increase propylene selectivity from 90% to 98% over 12 hours [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. Atomic layer deposition (ALD) Al2O3 or TiO2 overcoats on supported Pt/Al2O3 catalysts are also shown to boost propane dehydrogenation yield and selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391].

In terms of characterization, aberration-corrected HAADF-STEM imaging reveals small Pt clusters around 1.3 nm in size evenly dispersed on Al2O3 supports [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. Under propane dehydrogenation conditions, various types of coke are observed to form on Pt/Al2O3 and PtSn/Al2O3 catalysts, with hydrogen able to suppress coke formation [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361].

For performance evaluation, an alumina-supported monometallic Pt catalyst demonstrates relatively low 15% propane conversion and poorer selectivity compared to PtSn catalysts [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg7894]. Flow reactor data also shows a RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst supported on SiO2 outpaces a traditional Pt/Al2O3 catalyst standard, with higher activity, 100% propene selectivity, lower light-off temperature and coking resistance [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389].

Overall, supported mono-metallic Pt catalysts have been extensively studied for propane dehydrogenation, providing significant insights into design improvements via promoters and overcoats. But bimetallic Pt catalysts and single atom alloys may offer superior performance.

Pt基催化剂被报道是商业上最成功和最广泛使用的丙烷脱氢催化剂之一 [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。负载在Al2O3上的Pt催化剂表现出良好的平行碳氢键激活和最小化CC键断裂的能力,使其成为丙烷脱氢工艺的重要组成部分 [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。向负载Pt/γ-Al2O3 催化剂中添加1 wt%硼促进剂,可以使炭形物的生成降低3倍,并在12小时内将丙烯选择性从90%提高到98% [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。原子层沉积(ALD) Al2O3或TiO2涂层也被证明可以提高负载Pt/Al2O3催化剂的丙烷脱氢产率和选择性 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。

在特征化方面,artifact校正的HAADF-STEM成像揭示了均匀分散在Al2O3载体上的 Pt簇均为1.3 nm大小 [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。在丙烷脱氢条件下,各种类型的炭形物被观察到会在Pt/Al2O3 和 PtSn/Al2O3 催化剂上形成,而氢气可以抑制炭形物的生成 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]。

在性能评价方面,铝矾土负载单金属Pt催化剂表现出相对较低的15%丙烷转化率和较差的选择性,与PtSn催化剂相比[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg7894]。流化反应器数据也表明,与传统的Pt/Al2O3催化剂标准相比,负载在SiO2上的RhCu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂具有更高的活性、100%丙烯选择性、更低的点火温度和抗炭黑能力[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。

总体而言,人们已经广泛研究了负载单金属Pt催化剂用于丙烷脱氢,通过添加助剂和涂层为设计性能的改善提供了重要信息。但是,双金属Pt催化剂和单原子合金可能会提供更好的性能。
