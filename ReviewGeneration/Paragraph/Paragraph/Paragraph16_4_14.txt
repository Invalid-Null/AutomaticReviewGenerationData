    Based on the detailed analyses from the references furnished [10.1021/acscatal.3c00548, 10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, 10.1021/acscatal.1c04092, 10.1021/jacs.1c10261, 10.1016/j.jechem.2017.09.035, 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527, 10.1016/j.jcat.1996.0361], bimetallic and alloy Pt catalysts with promoters like Sn, Ga, Zn demonstrate modulated performance for propane dehydrogenation compared to monometallic Pt. Key enhancements enabled by Pt alloying include increased stability via suppressed coking and sintering tendencies [10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]; heightened activity through electronic structure changes and optimized active site configuration [10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.1c04092, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]; and improved propylene selectivity by constraining side reactions [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]. These advances are underpinned by factors like tuning the Pt ensemble effect and ligand influence to engender isolated sites [10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]; electronic donation to Pt improving propane activation while suppressing hydrogenolysis and cracking [10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]; compositional effects on coke characteristics [10.1016/j.jechem.2017.09.035]; and structure sensitivity with ordered intermetallic phases demonstrating superior performance [10.1021/acscatal.1c04092, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. 

    The bifunctional role of zeolites as promoters and stabilizers has also come to fore, with strategies like confining ultra-small alloy clusters in micropores and grafting transition metals onto silanol nests engendering remarkable single-site catalysts [10.1021/acscatal.1c04092, 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]. However, excessive Sn and Zn loading lead to propane activation barriers that offset initial activity increments [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, 10.1016/j.jechem.2017.09.035]. Consequently, Pt:M ratios around 3:1 to 2:1 seem optimal for balancing stability and activity enhancements [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, 10.1016/j.jechem.2017.09.035]. The compilation hence attests alloying with Sn, Ga, Zn as viable means for functionally optimizing Pt catalysts.
※※※
    With remarkable strides evidenced in alloy and multifunctional catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, upcoming research could target hybrid systems that couple multiple modulation strategies to attain augmented performance. For instance, Sn grafted zeolites could provide isolated Pt ensembles, while mesoporous capsules stabilize the alloy configuration. Machine learning approaches leveraging existing data could also predict optimal compositions and geometries to minimize trial-and-error experimental efforts. Expanding the catalyst palette to Earth-abundant alternatives like Fe, Co, and Cu alloys merits exploration from sustainability and economic standpoints. Mechanistic elucidations of multifunctional catalysts could uncover design principles to rationally couple promoters for realizing emergent functionality.

    根据所提供参考文献的详细分析[10.1021/acscatal.3c00548, 10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, 10.1021/acscatal.1c04092, 10.1021/jacs.1c10261, 10.1016/j.jechem.2017.09.035, 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527, 10.1016/j.jcat.1996.0361],与单金属Pt相比,掺杂有Sn、Ga、Zn等助剂的双金属和合金Pt催化剂的丙烷脱氢性能有所改善。关键的改进包括:稳定性的提高,通过抑制结焦和烧结倾向[10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]; 活性的提高,通过电子结构的改变和优化活性中心的配置[10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.1c04092, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]; 丙烯选择性的提高,通过限制副反应[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]。这些改进的基础包括:调谐Pt的配体效应,产生隔离活性中心[10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]; 向Pt供电子,改善丙烷激活而抑制氢解和裂解[10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527];  焦炭特性的组成效应[10.1016/j.jechem.2017.09.035]; 有序间金属相显示优异性能的结构敏感性[10.1021/acscatal.1c04092, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。

    沸石作为助剂和稳定剂的双功能作用也变得突出,比如将超小合金簇限制在微孔内和在硅醇巢穴上接枝过渡金属,可以产生杰出的单一活性中心[10.1021/acscatal.1c04092, 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]。然而,过量的Sn和Zn负载会导致丙烷激活能垒抵消最初的活性增加[10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, 10.1016/j.jechem.2017.09.035]。因此,3:1到2:1的Pt:M比例似乎是平衡稳定性和活性增强的最佳选择[10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, 10.1016/j.jechem.2017.09.035]。该合集证明,与Sn、Ga、Zn形成合金是功能化优化Pt催化剂的可行手段。  
※※※
    鉴于合金和多功能丙烷脱氢催化剂有了显著的进步,未来的研究可以目标是开发结合多种调控策略的杂化系统,以达到增强的性能。例如,接枝Sn的沸石可以提供隔离的Pt配合位,而介孔胶囊则稳定合金结构。机器学习方法也可以利用现有的数据预测最佳组成和几何结构,最小化试误的实验工作。从可持续性和经济性的角度考虑,扩大催化剂种类至丰富且廉价的Fe、Co和Cu合金也值得探索。多功能催化剂的机理阐明可以揭示设计原理,以理性地耦合助剂实现新功能的涌现。
