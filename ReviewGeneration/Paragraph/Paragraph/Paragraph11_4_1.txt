It shows that varying the calcination temperature of alumina supports modulates their acidity, which affects catalyst stability and coking resistance [Placeholder_Of_DOI1]. It finds that silica triggers undesired 3D nanoparticle formation compared to other supports due to less reactive surface silanol groups. Adding 0.4 wt% Na+ promotes more reactive SiONa+ sites, enhancing 2D vanadia dispersion to levels of other supports. This enhances activity while maintaining selectivity [Placeholder_Of_DOI2].

More in-depth characterization of interfacial sites using advanced spectroscopies combined with computational modeling can offer further insights. 		另一项研究DOI 10.1021/acscatal.5b01679考察了以二氧化硅为载体的钒氧化物催化剂的金属支持效应。结果发现,与其他载体相比,二氧化硅会导致不需要的三维纳米粒子的形成,这是由于其表面硅醇基团活性较低。添加0.4 wt% Na+ 促进了更具活性的 SiONa+ 位点的形成,从而提高了钒氧化物的二维分散度,达到与其他载体相当的水平。这提高了活性,同时保持选择性 [Placeholder_Of_DOI2]。
		
		对于 VOx/Al2O3 催化剂,膦改性削弱了钒种与Al2O3 支持体之间的相互作用,如 DOI 10.1016/j.apcatb.2020.119089 中所示。这有助于中间体/产物丙烯从催化剂表面脱附,从而提高选择性和稳定性 [Placeholder_Of_DOI3]。
