Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most successful and widely used commercially for PDH, employed in the Oleflex process by UOP [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]. One study showed an alumina-supported Pt catalyst maintained high activity over multiple cycles before deactivating due to Pt agglomeration accelerated by sulfur [DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]. Adding atomically dispersed promoters such as B and Al2O3 or TiO2 overcoats on supported Pt catalysts has been found to increase PDH yield and selectivity by reducing coking [DOIs: 10.1021/acs.chemrev.9b00238, 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. There is evidence that strong metal-support interactions in Pt/BN catalysts lead to enhanced stability and propylene selectivity compared to Pt/Al2O3 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008].

Isolated Pt2+ sites created by substituting Al atoms in zeolite frameworks were identified as the active sites for PDH, with Pt2+ content directly correlating with activity [DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]. Stabilizing isolated Pt1 sites while blocking multilayer Pt ensembles on silica increased propylene selectivity [DOI: 10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9]. DFT studies predict Pt2 clusters exhibit a volcano-type trend between stability and reactivity, being the most active size [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671].

While less studied than Pt, some work has explored Pd and Ir. Pd, Rh, and Ir are noted to have high intrinsic activity for C-H bond activation relevant for PDH but are expensive alternatives [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. One study showed an RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst benchmarked against Pt/Al2O3 exhibiting higher activity and 100% propylene selectivity [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389].

Overall, Pt-based catalysts have received the most attention for PDH, with key strategies being stabilization of isolated Pt sites and engineering strong metal-support interactions. Further mechanistic studies are needed to truly optimize these catalysts by Clarifying the identity and lability of dehydrogenation sites.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The insights uncovered in these studies spotlight supported metal catalysts as prime candidates for propane dehydrogenation. However, instability due to factors like metal agglomeration and coking motivates development of novel synthetic techniques and in-situ characterization methods. Future research might explore synergistic multi-metallic combinations to tune activity while forestalling deactivation pathways. Operando spectroscopy could elucidate degradation mechanisms occurring under reaction environments. Translating atomic-level discoveries to pilot demonstrations would validate promising options on a realistic development timeline towards commercialization. Broader adoption necessitates techno-economic analysis balancing capital and operating costs against sustainability metrics in different global contexts.

<Chinese>
根据文件提供的详细信息,已有几项研究探索了载体型单金属Pt、Pd和Ir催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

Pt基催化剂被报道是商业上最成功和最广泛使用的PDH催化剂,被UOP公司Oleflex工艺所采用[DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。一项研究表明,氧化铝载体型Pt催化剂在多次反应周期内保持高活性,直到因硫的存在加速Pt聚集而失活[DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]。在载体型Pt催化剂中加入B等原子分散的助剂或者TiO2、Al2O3外壳,可以降低焦炭生成,提高PDH产率和选择性[DOIs: 10.1021/acs.chemrev.9b00238, 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。有证据表明,Pt/BN催化剂中的强载体-金属相互作用可以提高催化剂的稳定性和丙烯选择性,优于Pt/Al2O3[DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]。 

通过用Al原子取代分子筛框架中的Si原子,可以得�高分散的Pt2+孤立活性中心,Pt2+含量与活性呈直接相关[DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726]。在二氧化硅载体上稳定Pt1单原子位,同时封锁多层Pt结构,可以提高丙烯选择性[DOI: 10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9]。DFT研究预言Pt2 簇状物在稳定性和反应性之间表现出火山型关系,是最活性的大小[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。

相比Pt研究较少的是Pd和Ir。Pd、Rh和Ir具有与PDH相关的高C-H活化活性,但作为替代材料昂贵[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。一项研究表明,与Pt/Al2O3基准催化剂相比,RhCu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂表现出更高的活性和100%的丙烯选择性[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。

总体而言,Pt基催化剂在PDH方面受到最多关注,关键策略是稳定Pt孤立活性中心,优化载体与金属间的相互作用。需要进一步的机理研究来优化这些催化剂,厘清脱氢活性位点的身份和反应性。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:这些研究的发现突显了载体金属催化剂作为丙烷脱氢的主要候选材料。但是,由于金属聚集和焦炭等原因导致的不稳定性激发了开发新合成技术和原位表征方法。未来的研究可能探索协同多金属组合以调控活性的同时抑制失活途径。原位光谱可以阐明反应条件下的降解机制。将原子水平的发现转化为中试演示,将有助于实际开发周期内对有前景的选择方案进行验证。更广泛的采用需要考虑不同全球情况下的投资成本、运行成本与可持续性指标的权衡。
