	<English>
Metal oxide catalysts such as Cr2O3, Pt-Sn, and Ga2O3 have been explored for propane dehydrogenation due to their potential for high activity, selectivity, and stability. Recent studies have provided insights into their performance and properties as PDH catalysts.

Chromium oxide (Cr2O3) catalysts demonstrate promising propane dehydrogenation activity, which can be further improved by doping with Pt [Reference [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]]. Cr2O3 is a key component of industrial PDH catalysts used in the CATOFIN process [Reference [10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]]. Reduced Cr2O3 surfaces have been computationally studied for propane dehydrogenation using DFT calculations combined with kinetic Monte Carlo simulations and microkinetic modeling [Reference [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]]. Weak metal-support interactions with SiO2 help enhance propylene selectivity over Cr2O3 [Reference [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]]. Among various metal oxides, Cr2O3/SiO2 delivers high propane conversion and propylene yield [Reference [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]].

Gallium oxide (Ga2O3) polymorphs including α-Ga2O3, β-Ga2O3 and γ-Ga2O3 exhibit appreciable activity for dehydrogenating propane, especially in the presence of CO2. Their performance correlates with surface acidity, with β-Ga2O3 displaying the highest initial dehydrogenation rates [Reference [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]]. Unsupported nanocrystalline β-Ga2O3 and γ-Ga2O3 as well as silica-supported β-Ga2O3/SiO2 and γ-Ga2O3/SiO2 have been tested for propane dehydrogenation, showing medium activity [Reference [10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]]. Incorporating gallium into mixed Ga2O3-ZrO2 oxides boosts their Lewis acidity and propane conversion [Reference [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]].

Supported Pt-Sn catalysts synthesized via coimpregnation methods produce intermetallic Pt-Sn alloy nanoparticles that demonstrate greatly enhanced catalytic performance for propane dehydrogenation compared to Pt only catalysts. The formation of Pt3Sn sites significantly improves propane conversion and propylene selectivity while suppressing side reactions like hydrogenolysis and coke formation [References [10.1021/acscatal.1c00639], [10.1002/anie.202005968], [10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]]. Pt3Sn/SBA-15 achieved an initial propane conversion of 40% and propylene selectivity of 92% [Reference [10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]]. However, deactivation via Sn leaching remains an issue.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The explored metal oxide catalysts of Cr2O3, Ga2O3 and Pt-Sn show encouraging potential for propane dehydrogenation, with Cr and Pt-Sn being closest to industrial utilization. However, fundamental mechanistic understanding is still lacking, and stability, especially for Ga and Pt-Sn catalysts, needs improvement. An opportunity exists to further tune these catalysts via compositional modifications, unconventional supports, and smart reactor design. Advanced characterization and computational techniques can offer insights to guide optimization. Broader families of complex multicomponent oxides may also be worth investigating. Overall, continuous research efforts focusing on key bottlenecks can help realize more active, selective and robust catalytic systems for this industrially important reaction.

	<Chinese>
诸如Cr2O3、Pt-Sn和Ga2O3之类的金属氧化物催化剂由于其潜在的高活性、选择性和稳定性已被广泛研究以用于丙烷脱氢反应。 最近的研究提供了有关它们作为丙烷脱氢催化剂性能和特性的见解。 

铬氧化物(Cr2O3)催化剂表现出很有前途的丙烷脱氢活性,其活性可通过掺杂Pt而得到进一步改善 [引用[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]]。 Cr2O3是CATOFIN工艺中使用的工业丙烷脱氢催化剂的关键组成部分 [引用[10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]]。 已经利用DFT计算方法结合动力学Monte Carlo模拟和微观动力学模型计算研究了还原Cr2O3表面催化丙烷脱氢反应 [引用[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]]。 SiO2的弱金属载体相互作用有助于提高Cr2O3催化剂上的丙烯选择性 [引用[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]]。 在各种金属氧化物中,Cr2O3/SiO2催化剂实现了较高的丙烷转化率和丙烯产率[引用[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]]。

鎵氧化物(Ga2O3)的多形如α-Ga2O3、β-Ga2O3和γ-Ga2O3都表现出可观的丙烷脱氢活性,尤其是在CO2存在下。 它们的性能与表面酸度有关,其中β-Ga2O3显示出最高的初始脱氢速率[引用[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]]。 无载体纳米结晶β-Ga2O3和γ-Ga2O3以及硅支持的β-Ga2O3/SiO2和γ-Ga2O3/SiO2均已被研究用于丙烷脱氢反应,表现出中等活性[引用[10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]]。 在Ga2O3-ZrO2混合氧化物中掺入鎵可提高其路易斯酸性和丙烷转化率[引用[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]]。

采用共浸渍法合成的载体Pt-Sn催化剂产生了金属间化合物Pt-Sn合金纳米粒子,与仅含Pt的催化剂相比,这类催化剂表现出极大地提高的丙烷脱氢催化性能。 Pt3Sn位点的形成可显著提高丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性,同时抑制如氢化裂解和焦炭生成等副反应[引用[10.1021/acscatal.1c00639], [10.1002/anie.202005968], [10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]]。 Pt3Sn/SBA-15催化剂实现了40%的初始丙烷转化率和92%的丙烯选择性[引用[10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]]。 然而,Sn溶出导致的失活仍是一个问题。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 已探索的Cr2O3、Ga2O3和Pt-Sn金属氧化物催化剂在丙烷脱氢方面显示了很有前景的潜力,其中Cr和Pt-Sn最接近工业应用。 但是,仍缺乏基本的机理理解,特别是Ga和Pt-Sn催化剂的稳定性需要改进。 有机会通过组分调控、非常规载体和智能反应器设计进一步调优这些催化剂。 先进的表征和计算技术可以提供指导优化的见解。 更广泛的复合多组分氧化物家族也值得研究。 总体而言,持续关注关键瓶颈的研究工作有助于实现更加活跃、选择性和稳定的催化系统用于这个工业上非常重要的反应。
