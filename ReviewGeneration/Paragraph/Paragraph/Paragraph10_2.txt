Based on the details extracted from the references, several mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over different metal oxide catalysts. 

On Cr/ZrOx catalysts, two neighboring coordinatively unsaturated Zr (Zrcus) sites located at an oxygen vacancy are concluded to be responsible for breaking C-H bonds in propane homolytically [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012]. Isolated Cr3+ species with coordination unsaturation are also suggested as active sites [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]. 

On Zn/ZrOx catalysts, isolated tricoordinated Zn2+ sites anchored on ZrO2 catalyze the first dehydrogenation step with neighboring Zrcus sites catalyzing the second step in a synergistic manner [10.1021/acscatal.0c01580, 10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074].
 
On Ga/zeolite catalysts, Ga+ cations activate propane via an alkyl mechanism, either on isolated Ga+ sites or Ga+ paired with Brønsted acid sites. Key steps involve formation of a Ga-propyl intermediate, hydride abstraction to form propylene, and hydrogen recombination [10.1021/acscatal.1c01497, 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.004].

On VOx catalysts, a Mars-Van Krevelen mechanism is widely proposed, involving lattice oxygen mediating dehydrogenation to reduced V species followed by reoxidation [10.1006/jcat.1999.2510, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023, 10.1021/acscatal.8b04701]. Isolated and oligomeric V species are identified as active sites.

Alternative pathways are also described like concerted dehydrogenation on Ga/ZrOx [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002], heterolytic dissociation on Ga/zeolites [10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017]  and Ga oxides [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001], and bifunctional synergy between Pt and Ga oxides [10.1021/acscatal.1c02553].

※※※ 
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The current mechanistic picture reveals that isolated transition metal cations (Cr, V, Ga) in partially reduced states, paired with oxide supports that can provide labile lattice oxygen, constitute prevalent active site motifs for propane dehydrogenation. However, atomic-level understanding of these sites and systematic trends across different metals remain lacking. Future research could focus on achieving tunable control over the oxidation state, coordination environment, and surrounding lattice of isolated sites, guided by computational modeling. Operando spectroscopy techniques combined with isotopic labeling and DFT calculations also have immense potential to elucidate reaction intermediates and pathways. An integrated approach spanning materials synthesis, characterization, and reaction studies is imperative to unlock superior propane dehydrogenation catalysts.

<Chinese>
根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,已经为不同金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢提出了几种机理场景。

在Cr/ZrOx催化剂上,确认位于氧空位的两个相邻的无配位Zr (Zrcus)位点负责同裂丙烷中的C-H键[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012]。还提示隔离的Cr3+物种与无配位是活跃位点 [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]。

在Zn/ZrOx催化剂上,锚定在ZrO2上的三配位隔离Zn2+位点催化第一步脱氢,而相邻的Zrcus位点催化第二步,两者之间存在协同效应[10.1021/acscatal.0c01580, 10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074]。

在分子筛上的Ga催化剂上,Ga+阳离子通过烷基机理激活丙烷,无论是在隔离的Ga+位点还是Ga+与Brønsted酸位点结合的位点上。关键步骤包括Ga-丙基中间体的形成,通过提取氢来形成丙烯,以及氢的重组[10.1021/acscatal.1c01497, 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.004]。

在VOx催化剂上,广泛提出了Mars-Van Krevelen机理,其中涉及晶格氧介导脱氢生成还原态V物种,然后被氧化物再氧化[10.1006/jcat.1999.2510, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023, 10.1021/acscatal.8b04701]。识别出隔离和低聚V物种是活跃位点。

还描述了其他途径,如Ga/ZrOx上的共轭脱氢[10.1021/acscatal.3c01002],Ga/分子筛[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017] 和 Ga氧化物[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]上的异裂解,以及Pt和Ga氧化物之间的双功能协同效应[10.1021/acscatal.1c02553]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望: 当前的机理图景显示,部分还原状态下的过渡金属阳离子(Cr、V、Ga)与可以提供活泼晶格氧的氧化物载体的配对构成了丙烷脱氢的流行活性位点基元。 然而, 对这些位点的原子水平的理解和跨不同金属的系统趋势仍然缺乏。 未来的研究可以集中在实现对隔离位点氧化状态、配位环境和周围晶格的可调控制,在计算建模的指导下。 原位光谱技术与同位素标记相结合以及DFT计算也有巨大的潜力来阐明反应中间体和途径。 一个综合的方法跨越材料合成、表征和反应研究对于解锁优越的丙烷脱氢催化剂至关重要。
