	<English>
		Based on the in-depth analysis of the references provided [10.1021/acscatal.2c05286, 10.1021/acscatal.1c02621, 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999], chromium oxide, gallium oxide, and platinum-tin alloy catalysts have demonstrated promising activity for propane dehydrogenation. Chromia (Cr2O3) catalysts are reported to be among the most active for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation, with CrOx being a key component in industrial propane dehydrogenation reactors [10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]. Gallium oxide polymorphs, especially β-Ga2O3 with abundant surface acid sites, achieve high initial reaction rates that surpass benchmark catalysts [10.1021/acscatal.1c02621]. Weak metal-support interactions are found to boost propylene yields for Cr2O3/SiO2 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Moreover, increasing Ga incorporation in Ga2O3-ZrO2 generates more Lewis acid sites, improving catalytic behavior [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Apart from metal oxides, supported PtSn intermetallic nanoparticles demonstrate elevated propane conversion and propylene selectivity attributed to electronic and geometric effects [10.1021/acscatal.1c00639].
		※※※
		Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The encouraging propane dehydrogenation activity observed for Cr2O3, Ga2O3 and PtSn catalysts spotlights the potential of tailoring acid-base properties and metal-support interplay to further advance selectivity and stability. An interdisciplinary approach coupling experimentation with computational modeling could systematically unravel reaction mechanisms and active site requirements. Discovering additional synergistic metal, oxide and support combinations by high-throughput experimentation and machine learning holds exciting possibilities. Eventually, optimized multifunctional metal oxide nanocomposites may supplant conventional catalytic systems.

	<Chinese>
		根据提供的参考文献[10.1021/acscatal.2c05286, 10.1021/acscatal.1c02621, 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]的深入分析,铬氧化物、镓氧化物和铂锡合金催化剂在丙烷脱氢反应中表现出很好的活性。铬氧化物催化剂是CO2辅助氧化脱氢反应中最活跃的催化剂之一,CrOx是工业丙烷脱氢反应器中的关键成分[10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]。镓氧化物多形体,特别是β-Ga2O3,具有丰富的表面酸性位,比基准催化剂达到更高的初始反应速率[10.1021/acscatal.1c02621]。在Cr2O3/SiO2上,弱的金属支撑体相互作用被发现可以提高丙烯的收率[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。此外,Ga2O3-ZrO2混合氧化物中Ga含量的增加会产生更多的刘易斯酸性位,从而改善催化性能[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。除了金属氧化物,受支撑的PtSn金属间化合物纳米颗粒表现出更高的丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性,这是电子效应和几何效应的结果[10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]。
		※※※
		综合理解与未来展望: Cr2O3、Ga2O3和PtSn催化剂所观察到的令人鼓舞的丙烷脱氢活性,凸显了通过调节酸碱性质和金属支撑体相互作用进一步提高选择性和稳定性的潜力。运用实验和计算建模的跨学科方法,可以系统地揭示反应机理和活性位点的要求。通过高通量实验和机器学习发现更多协同作用的金属、氧化物和载体组合,这具有令人振奋的可能性。最终,优化的多功能金属氧化物纳米复合材料可以取代传统的催化体系。
