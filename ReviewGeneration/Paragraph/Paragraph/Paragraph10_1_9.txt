Based on the details extracted from the references, mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over certain metal oxide catalysts, while several studies did not delve into mechanistic investigations. 

On chromium-based oxides, active Cr3+ sites were generated in situ from Cr6+ precursors during propane dehydrogenation [DOI1]. Isolated Cr3+ sites with coordination number >4 were deemed as active in mesoporous silicas, attributing to their good activity and resistance to deactivation [DOI2]. For Cr/Al2O3, oligomeric Cr species exhibited higher intrinsic activity than isolated sites [DOI2]. Over ZrO2, DFT calculations predicted propane undergoing homolytic C-H bond scission on two Zr cations with unsaturated coordination located at an oxygen vacancy [DOI3]. These Zrcus sites were also proposed as active for Ga-doped ZrO2, along with a change in mechanism from stepwise to concerted [DOI4]. 

Investigations on vanadium oxides revealed a Mars-van Krevelen type pathway, with lattice oxygen abstracting hydrogen and reduced V centers re-oxidizing by O2 or CO2 [DOI5, DOI6]. Sub-monolayer VOx nanostructures on TiO2 could suppress overoxidation by preventing bulk O2 diffusion and evolution into electrophilic oxygen species [DOI7]. For molybdenum vanadium mixed oxides, depletion of lattice oxygen led to V3+ sites catalyzing non-oxidative dehydrogenation after initial oxidative dehydrogenation by lattice oxygen [DOI8].

Over gallium oxides, heterolytic dissociation mechanistic routes were preferred, entailing propane adsorption and dissociation into gallium hydride and alkoxide intermediates [DOI9, DOI10]. Propane activation occurred on Ga3+ sites, with subsequent hydride extraction by proximal Brønsted acid sites [DOI11]. Changing the coordination environment of Ga was noted to tune energies of key transition states and intermediates [DOI12].

Several studies focused solely on Pt or bimetallic catalysts. A Horiuti-Polanyi type mechanism was described for Pt involving C-H bond cleavage, hydrogen formation and propylene desorption steps [DOI13]. No mechanistic particulars were extracted for other metal or mixed oxide systems. Further fundamental investigations could enrich comprehension on structure-activity correlations over promising catalyst formulations.

※※※ 
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Significant advancements have been attained in deciphering mechanistic intricacies and active site requirements over selective metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. While Cr, V, Ga and Zr-based oxides have been extensively explored, opportunities abound in expanding mechanistic inquiries towards emerging nanostructured mixed metal oxides like spinels, perovskites amongst others. 

Besides conventional experimental approaches, high-throughput computations could accelerate screening of multifaceted compositions and archetypes. Coupled in situ characterization techniques with isotopic tracing provide another prospect for tracking transformation of surface intermediates. Concomitantly, engineering reaction environments like chemical looping may impede overoxidation side-reactions. 

As research endeavors make further headway, the field inches closer to rational catalyst blueprinting for direct propane dehydrogenation. Knowledge accrued thus far serves as the cornerstone for purposeful design of multifunctional sites in cost-effective and stable metal oxide catalysts.

根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,目前已经为某些金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢提出了机理场景,而一些研究并未深入机理方面的调查。

在以铬为基础的氧化物上,Cr6+前体在丙烷脱氢过程中原位生成了活性Cr3+位点 [DOI1]。在介孔硅酸盐中,配位数大于4的隔离Cr3+位点被认为是活性位点,这解释了它们的良好活性和抗失活性[DOI2]。 对于Cr/Al2O3,寡聚Cr物种表现出比隔离位点更高的固有活性[DOI2]。 DFT计算预测丙烷在位于氧空位的两个铽饱和配位阳离子上发生均裂C-H键断裂[DOI3]。这些Zrcus位点也被预测为掺Ga ZrO2的活性位点,还伴随着机理从逐步转变为协同[DOI4]。

对于钒酸盐的研究揭示了Mars-van Krevelen类型的途径,晶格氧摘除氢原子,还原的V中心被O2或CO2氧化[DOI5,DOI6]。载于TiO2上的亚单分子层VOx纳米结构可以通过防止O2的体相扩散和演变成电子吸引性氧化物而抑制过氧化[DOI7]。 对于钼钒混合氧化物,晶格氧耗尽导致V3+位点在最初的氧化脱氢之后催化非氧化脱氢[DOI8]。

在氧化镓上,优先考虑了杂裂离解的机理途径,包括丙烷吸附和裂解成镓化氢和烷氧基中间产物[DOI9, DOI10]。丙烷在Ga3+位点激活,随后由邻近的Brønsted酸位点提取氢化物[DOI11]。 改变镓的配位环境被注意到可调节关键过渡态和中间体的能量[DOI12]。

几项研究仅专注于Pt或双金属催化剂。 描述了一种Horiuti-Polanyi类型的机制,包括C-H键断裂,氢形成和丙烯脱sorption步骤[DOI13]。没有提取其他金属或混合氧化物系统的机理细节。进一步的基础研究可以丰富对有前景的催化剂配方上结构-活性相关性的理解。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:在揭示选择性金属氧化物催化剂上丙烷脱氢的机理复杂性和活性位点需求方面已经取得了重大进展。虽然以Cr、V、Ga和Zr为基础的氧化物已被广泛研究,但是在新兴的纳米结构混合金属氧化物(如尖晶石、钙钛矿等)的机理研究中机遇无处不在。

除了传统的实验方法之外,高通量计算可以加速复杂成分和原型的筛选。 同位素标定的原位表征技术提供了跟踪表面中间体转化的另一种前景。 同时,如化学回流等反应环境工程可以抑制过氧化副反应。

随着研究工作的进一步推进,该领域距离丙烷直接脱氢的合理催化剂设计更近一步。 到目前为止积累的知识作为基石,用于有目的地在具有成本效益和稳定性的金属氧化物催化剂中设计多功能位点。
