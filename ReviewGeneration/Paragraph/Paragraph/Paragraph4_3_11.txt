 <English>

Based on the details extracted from the references, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) faces significant thermodynamic constraints due to being a highly endothermic reaction (ΔH ̊> 100 kJ/mol). To drive the equilibrium conversion, high temperatures of 500-650°C are required [References DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.01.055, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c01916, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01455]. Lower pressures also help shift the equilibrium towards products [References DOI: 10.1002/advs.202207756]. 

However, high temperatures lead to accelerated catalyst deactivation via coke formation, as well as undesired cracking and deep dehydrogenation reactions [References DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c01916, DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019]. An apparent activation energy of 180-200 kJ/mol has been calculated for the dehydrogenation step [References DOI: 10.1021/jacs.2c03941]. Selectivities depend on the nature of active sites, with isolated metal centers suggested to promote dehydrogenation over cracking [References DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019].

Oxidative routes utilizing O2 or CO2 co-feeds can improve thermodynamic limitations, providing exothermic oxidation reactions [References DOI: 10.1002/advs.202207756]. However, kinetic factors including side reactions, catalyst stability, and mechanistic details remain insufficiently understood [References DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.5b01423].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Taking into consideration the advancements and findings discussed in the references, emerging computational techniques offer promise in elucidating mechanistic intricacies and guiding design of optimized catalysts. Operando spectroscopic probes could unveil kinetics over relevant temperature and pressure regimes. Exploring redox-mediated pathways may provide alternative, milder routes to shift equilibria. Future inquiries might examine such innovative directions to overcome current constraints.

<Chinese>

根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,丙烷脱氢(PDH)由于是一种高度放散反应(ΔH ̊> 100 kJ/mol),面临重大的热力学限制。为驱动平衡转化,需要500-650°C的高温[引用DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.01.055, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00103, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c01916, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01455]。较低的压力也有助于向产物方向转移平衡[引用DOI: 10.1002/advs.202207756]。

然而,高温会导致通过焦炭形成加速的催化剂失效,以及不希望的裂解和深度脱氢反应[引用DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c01916, DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019]。计算出脱氢步骤的表观活化能为180-200 kJ/mol [引用DOI: 10.1021/jacs.2c03941]。选择性取决于活性位点的性质,发现孤立的金属中心有利于脱氢而不是裂解[引用DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019]。

使用O2或CO2共料的氧化途径可以改善热力学限制,提供放热氧化反应[引用DOI: 10.1002/advs.202207756]。然而,副反应、催化剂稳定性和机理细节的动力学因素理解仍然不足[引用DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.5b01423]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:考虑到文件中讨论的先进成果和发现,新兴的计算技术在阐明机理复杂性和指导优化催化剂设计方面提供了前景。Operando光谱探针可以揭示相关温度和压力范围内的动力学。探索氧化还原介导的途径可能提供替代性的、温和的途径来转移平衡。未来的调查可能检查这些创新方向以克服当前的限制。
