	<English>
		Among metal oxide catalysts, chromium oxide (Cr2O3), platinum-tin (Pt-Sn), and gallium oxide (Ga2O3) have exhibited promising activity for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). Cr-based catalysts are recognized as highly active for alkane dehydrogenation reactions, and CrOx is a key component of the industrial CATOFIN PDH process [10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]. Doping Cr2O3 with promoters like Pt can further improve performance by modifying the Cr2O3 surface [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]. Weak metal-support interactions with SiO2 are also found to raise propylene selectivity for Cr2O3 catalysts [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Among different polymorphs, α-Cr2O3 exhibits the highest intrinsic activity, as revealed by kinetic Monte Carlo simulations [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814].
		PtSn catalysts have been extensively explored, with the formed Pt3Sn alloy acting to suppress coke formation and enhance propylene selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.11c00639, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]. The PtSn system has been successfully commercialized, reaching 30-40% propane conversion and 85.5-88% propylene selectivity [10.1002/advs.202207756]. Supporting PtSn on zeolites demonstrates excellent thermal stability and regenerability [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]. 
		For Ga2O3, the catalytic performance for PDH follows β-Ga2O3 > γ-Ga2O3 > δ-Ga2O3 > α-Ga2O3, correlating with surface acid site density [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Increasing Ga content in mixed Ga2O3-ZrO2 oxides raises the concentration of Lewis acid sites, optimizing activity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Compared to Fe2O3 and Cr2O3, Ga2O3 delivers the highest propylene yield [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999].
		※※※
		Taking into account the advancements around Cr2O3, PtSn, and Ga2O3 catalysts, continued research into optimal co-catalysts, promoters, and supports may unlock even greater improvements in conversion, yield, and stability. Leveraging in-situ characterization techniques can shed light on degradation pathways. Exploring unconventional synthetic methods could enhance metal-support interactions and active phase dispersion. Lastly, machine learning models trained on accumulated catalytic data may accelerate discovery of next generation PDH metal oxide catalysts.

	<Chinese>
		在金属氧化物催化剂中,铬氧化物(Cr2O3)、铂锡(Pt-Sn)和钙氧化物(Ga2O3)表现出很有前景的丙烷脱氢活性。基于铬的催化剂被认为在烷烃脱氢反应中高活性,而CrOx是工业CATOFIN PDH工艺的关键组成部分[10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]。用Pt等助剂掺杂Cr2O3能够进一步改善性能,通过修饰Cr2O3表面[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]。与SiO2的弱金属载体相互作用也能提高Cr2O3催化剂的丙烯选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。在不同的多晶型中,α-Cr2O3表现出最高的固有活性,正如动力学蒙特卡罗模拟所揭示的[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]。
		PtSn催化剂进行了广泛的研究,形成的Pt3Sn合金起抑制焦炭生成和增强丙烯选择性的作用[10.1021/acscatal.11c00639, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]。PtSn体系已经成功商业化,丙烷转化率达到30-40%,丙烯选择性达到85.5-88%[10.1002/advs.202207756]。在分子筛上的PtSn支持示出了优异的热稳定性和再生能力[10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]。
		对于Ga2O3,PDH的催化性能遵循β-Ga2O3 > γ-Ga2O3 > δ-Ga2O3 > α-Ga2O3的顺序,与表面酸位密度相关[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。在Ga2O3-ZrO2混合氧化物中,Ga含量的增加提高了Lewis酸位的浓度,优化了活性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。与Fe2O3和Cr2O3相比,Ga2O3提供了最高的丙烯产率[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。
		※※※
		考虑到围绕Cr2O3,PtSn和Ga2O3催化剂的进展,继续研究优化的共催化剂、助剂和载体可能会带来更大的转化率、产率和稳定性的改进。利用原位表征技术可以阐明降解途径。探索非常规的合成方法可能会增强金属载体间的相互作用和活性相的分散。最后,在积累的催化数据上训练的机器学习模型可能会加速下一代金属氧化物PDH催化剂的发现。
