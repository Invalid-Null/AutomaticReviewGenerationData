Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have evaluated supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts. For example, Pt/Al2O3 is a vital PDH catalyst component due to Pt's ability to activate paraffinic C-H bonds while minimizing C-C cleavage [10.1002/anie.201507119]. One study showed an alumina-supported Pt catalyst maintained high initial activity, although it deactivated over multiple cycles due to Pt agglomeration accelerated by sulfur [10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]. Adding promoters such as 1 wt% boron to Pt/γ-Al2O3 was found to reduce coking by 3 times and increase propylene selectivity from 90% to 98% [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. Atomic layer deposition (ALD) of Al2O3 or TiO2 overcoats on Pt/Al2O3 extrudates also improved PDH yield and selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391].

Besides Pt/Al2O3, other supports have been studied. Pt/SBA-15 catalysts demonstrated activity for both hydrogenation and PDH reactions [10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005]. Confining sub-nanometric Pt clusters in Ge-UTL zeolites via Pt-O-Ge bonds gave a catalyst with excellent stability and 96% propylene selectivity [10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. A Pt/Zn-zeolite catalyst maintained 40% initial conversion over 10 hours, showing high activity and low deactivation [10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008].

Some studies have also explored Pd and Ir. A supported RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst showed higher activity, 100% propene selectivity and coking resistance compared to a standard Pt/Al2O3 catalyst [10.1126/science.abg8389]. However, many articles focused solely on non-noble metal catalysts.

Overall, Pt-based catalysts are identified as the most promising supported metal catalysts for commercial PDH processes, with tuning of supports, promoters, and overcoats as strategies to further enhance stability and selectivity.

※※※

Integrative Understanding & Prospective Outlook:
The examined studies provide significant insights into supported metal catalysts for PDH. Pt-based materials are clearly established as the foremost commercial catalysts. However, stability and selectivity remain challenges. The dependency on critical noble metals also motivates exploration of non-precious alternatives. 

Advancements in characterization techniques enabling atomic-level views of working catalysts could unravel degradation mechanisms. This knowledge can guide engineering of more robust catalysts and supports. Operando spectroscopy under industrially-relevant conditions may reveal additional promoters or overcoats to curtail coking. 

Machine learning models applied to PDH could accelerate screening and optimization. High-throughput DFT calculations have shown promise for predicting single atom alloy catalysts. Similar data-driven approaches may uncover reduced or non-precious compositions with favorable kinetics. Ultimately, progress lies at the intersection of computational experiments and insights from operating catalysts.

根据文件提供的细节,已经有几项研究评价了铂系单金属催化剂在丙烷脱氢反应中的应用。

报道显示,铂基催化剂是最常用和商业上最成功的丙烷脱氢催化剂之一。例如,Pt/Al2O3是重要的丙烷脱氢催化剂组分,因为Pt能激活链烷基C-H键而最小化C-C键的断裂[10.1002/anie.201507119]。一项研究表明,Al2O3支持的Pt催化剂保持了高的初始活性,尽管它在多次循环中因硫的加速Pt聚集而失活[10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]。在Pt/γ-Al2O3中添加1 wt% 的硼促进剂被发现可以降低焦炭形成3倍,并将丙烯选择性从90%提高到98% [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。在Pt/Al2O3挤出物上沉积的Al2O3或TiO2外涂层也改进了丙烷脱氢反应的产率和选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。

除了Pt/Al2O3之外,也研究了其他载体。 Pt/SBA-15催化剂表现出氢化和丙烷脱氢反应的活性[10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005]。通过Pt-O-Ge键将亚纳米Pt簇限制在Ge-UTL沸石中,得到了一个具有优异稳定性和96% 丙烯选择性的催化剂[10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。 Pt/Zn-沸石催化剂在10小时内保持40%的初始转化率,显示出高活性和低衰减[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008]。

一些研究还探索了钯和铱。与标准Pt/Al2O3催化剂相比,支持的RhCu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂显示出更高的活性、100%丙烯选择性和抗焦炭性[10.1126/science.abg8389]。 然而,许多文章仅关注非贵金属催化剂。

总的来说,铂基催化剂被确定为最有希望的商业丙烷脱氢过程的支持金属催化剂,通过调节载体、促进剂和外层涂膜来进一步提高稳定性和选择性。

综合理解与展望:
调查的研究对丙烷脱氢反应的金属催化剂提供了重要信息。铂基材料显然建立为首要的商业催化剂。然而,稳定性和选择性仍然是挑战。对关键贵金属的依赖也促进了对非贵金属替代品的探索。  

先进的表征技术使得以原子水平查看工作催化剂成为可能,这可能揭示降解机制。这些知识可以指导设计更坚固的催化剂和载体。工艺条件下的原位光谱可能揭示额外的促进剂或外涂层来抑制焦炭形成。  

机器学习模型应用于丙烷脱氢反应中可以加速筛选和优化。高通量DFT计算已经显示出预测单原子合金催化剂的前景。类似的数据驱动方法可能发现具有良好动力学的减少贵金属或非贵金属成分。 最终,进步存在于计算实验和工作催化剂信息的交汇处。
