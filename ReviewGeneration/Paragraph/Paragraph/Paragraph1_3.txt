 <English>

Propylene is a crucial chemical feedstock utilized for producing a diverse range of products including polypropylene, acrylonitrile, propylene oxide, acetone, etc [1]. Traditional production routes via steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking of naphtha and heavy oils are unable to satisfy the rapidly rising global demand [2] \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816\]. Hence, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) has emerged as an promising technology by leveraging abundant and cost-effective propane feedstocks [3] \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008\]. The high temperatures necessitated by the endothermic equilibrium-limited PDH reactions can be moderated through steady advancements in catalysis and process intensification [4] \[10.1021/acscatal.1c01924\].

※※※

With burgeoning propylene demands [1] and substantial propane reserves [2], PDH technologies hold vast potential for continued growth and innovation. Future research could explore combining PDH with emerging methods like membrane separations, 3D printing, and machine learning optimization to develop highly efficient and compact plants [5]. The modular and transportable nature of PDH also creates opportunities to deploy small-scale units directly at remote propane production sites. Overall, by building on recent advancements, PDH is poised to solidify its position as a sustaining pillar of the global propylene supply chain.

<Chinese>

丙烯是一种重要的化工原料,用于生产诸如丙烯醛、丙烯腈、环氧丙烷、丙酮等多种产品[1]。由于全球需求快速增长,传统的蒸汽裂解和重油催化裂解等生产途径已无法满足需求[2]\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816\]。因此,丙烷脱氢(PDH)技术作为一种有前景的新兴技术,利用丰富且低成本的丙烷原料引起了广泛关注[3]\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008\]。内吸热的PDH反应平衡限制条件可以通过催化剂和工艺强化的稳步进步而得到缓解[4]\[10.1021/acscatal.1c01924\]。

※※※

随着丙烯需求量的激增[1]和丰富的丙烷储量[2],PDH技术在持续发展和创新方面蕴藏巨大潜力。未来的研究可以探索将PDH与新兴的膜分离、3D打印、机器学习优化等技术相结合,开发高效紧凑的装置[5]。PDH装置规模灵活,便于运输的特性也为直接在丙烷生产现场部署小型单元提供了机会。总体而言,在最近取得的进展基础上,PDH技术有望巩固其在全球丙烯供应链中的关键地位。
