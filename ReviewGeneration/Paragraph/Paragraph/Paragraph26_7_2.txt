Based on the details extracted from the file, the article with DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636 employs integrated differential phase contrast scanning transmission electron microscopy (iDPC-STEM) to characterize the Co-MFI interface. The images reveal that isolated Co atoms with a distorted tetrahedral coordination are embedded at the T1(7) and T3(9) sites within the MFI framework [DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636]. 

Additionally, the article with DOI 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7 uses aberration corrected STEM paired with integrated differential phase contrast imaging (iDPC) to precisely localize subnanometric Pt and PtSn clusters preferentially within the sinusoidal channels of the MFI zeolite structure [DOI 10.1038/s41563-019-0430-4].

Furthermore, the article with DOI 10.1038/s41467-018-06967-8 employs aberration-corrected HAADF-STEM to visually confirm isolated Pt atoms dispersed in Cu nanoparticles supported on alumina [DOI 10.1038/41467-018-06967-8]. This reveals strong metal-support interactions preserving the single atom dispersion.

In summary, advanced microscopy techniques have enabled the direct visualization of isolated atoms and subnanometric clusters at specific sites within zeolite frameworks and on oxide supports. The location and coordination environment of active sites derived from these images provides critical insights into metal-support interfaces. However, bulk spectroscopic methods still play an important complementary role. Future research might explore correlative microscopy workflows integrating spectroscopic and theoretical data with high resolution imaging. Machine learning assisted image analysis could also help automate the quantification of site distributions. Overall, continued microscopy advances offer exciting opportunities to elucidate catalyst structure-activity relationships.

根据文件中提取的详细信息,DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636的文章使用集成差分相对比扫描传输电子显微镜(iDPC-STEM)来表征Co-MFI界面。该图像显示,在MFI框架的T1(7)和T3(9)位点内嵌入了配位环境扭曲的隔离Co原子[DOI 10.1021/jacs.2c02636]。


此外,DOI 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7的文章使用球差校正的STEM与集成差分相对比成像(iDPC)技术来精确定位MFI分子筛结构的正弦波通道中的亚纳米Pt和PtSn簇[DOI 10.1038/s41563-019-0430-4]。


而且,DOI 10.1038/s41467-018-06967-8的文章采用球差校正HAADF-STEM观察到alumina支撑的Cu纳米颗粒中分散的隔离Pt原子[DOI 10.1038/s41467-018-06967-8]。这揭示了强大的金属支撑相互作用保持了单原子分散度。

总而言之,先进的显微技术使人们能够直接观察到分子筛框架内特定位点上的隔离原子和亚纳米团簇以及氧化物支撑体上的这些活性位点。这些图像中获得的活性位点的位置和配位环境提供了有关金属支撑体界面明确信息。但是,大体谱学方法在互补方面仍起重要作用。未来的研究可能会探索结合显微成像与光谱学、理论数据的相关显微流程。机器学习辅助的图像分析也可以帮助自动定量站点分布。总的来说,显微技术的持续进步为阐明催化剂的结构-活性关系提供了激动人心的机遇。
