 <English>

The article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816] investigates tuning zeolite Si/Al ratio by dealumination to create silanol nests in Beta zeolites, which enhances metal dispersion and stabilization.

The study with DOI [10.1021/jacs.2c03941] evaluates propane dehydrogenation over CHA zeolites with varying Si/Al ratios of 5, 12 and 25. It shows that optimal Ga+ active sites are formed across the range of Si/Al ratios examined. While reaction rates decrease over time, propylene selectivity remains stably high at 96%. This reveals complexity in interpreting single Si/Al ratio studies.  

An investigation utilizing mesoporous MFI zeolite nanosheets as the support (DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]) demonstrates enhanced formation of PtZn alloy nanoparticles. The mesoporous structure and abundant silanol groups play an integral role.

In another example (DOI [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024]), decreasing TS-1 zeolite crystal size down to 100 nm introduces mesoporosity. This significantly improves catalyst lifetime, conversion and selectivity by enhancing diffusion and avoiding coking.

※※※

The examined studies reveal zeolite Si/Al ratio and mesoporosity tuning can substantially impact metal dispersion, diffusion, coking and ultimately catalytic performance. However, interpretations remain complex; activity depends intricately on coupled effects between metal loadings and zeolite properties. An expansive, multidimensional exploration assessing couplings between properties and across wide compositional ranges appears necessary to extract true structure-function relationships and optimize systems. Efforts could also focus on extending tunability of zeolites to even more extreme Si/Al ratios.

<Chinese>

DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]的文章通过除铝来调节Beta分子筛的Si/Al比,形成硅醇巢,这增强了金属的分散和稳定。

DOI [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]的研究评估了Si/Al比为5、12和25的CHA分子筛上的丙烷脱氢反应。它显示在所研究的Si/Al比范围内都形成了优化的Ga+活性位点。尽管反应速率随时间下降,丙烯选择性保持在96%的高水平。这揭示了解释单一Si/Al比研究的复杂性。

一项利用介孔MFI分子筛纳米片作为载体的研究(DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808])表明PtZn合金纳米粒子的形成得到增强。介孔结构和大量硅醇基团发挥着关键作用。

在另一个例子(DOI [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024])中,将TS-1分子筛晶体尺寸降低到100 nm引入了介孔。这通过增强扩散和避免结焦显著改善了催化剂的使用寿命、转化率和选择性。

※※※

所研究的文献揭示了分子筛Si/Al比和介孔的调节可以显著影响金属dispersion、扩散、结焦和最终的催化性能。然而,解释仍然复杂;活性取决于金属负载量和分子筛性质之间复杂的相互作用。为了提取真正的结构-功能关系并优化系统,似乎有必要进行广泛的、多维的探索,以评估性质之间的耦合效应并跨越广泛的组成范围。努力还可以集中在将分子筛的可调范围扩展到更极端的Si/Al比。
