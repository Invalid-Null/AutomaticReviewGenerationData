Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Pt-Sn Alloys
    The addition of Sn has been widely shown to improve propene selectivity and catalyst stability during PDH by inhibiting secondary hydrogenolysis and coking reactions. This leads to lower deactivation rates. [10.1002/anie.201507119]※ ※ [10.1016/j.jcat.2018.09.006] Computational and experimental studies suggest Sn incorporation produces a trade-off, improving stability but decreasing initial activity by increasing propane dissociation barriers on Pt sites. An optimal Sn/Pt ratio around 1-1.5 is reported. [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]
    At the atomic level, Sn modifies the electron density of Pt through ligand effects. The formation of intermetallic Pt-Sn bonding character is noted from XRD, XPS, IR, and theoretical studies. [10.1002/anie.201507119] [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018]※ ※

Pt-Ga Alloys 
    The addition of Ga as a promoter also effectively enhances PDH performance in terms of selectivity, initial activity close to equilibrium levels, and stability. [10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129] Ga incorporation helps anchor and disperse Pt active sites during reaction conditions, leading to the observed improvements.

Pt-Zn Alloys
    Studies report that embedding isolated Pt atoms within Zn-modified zeolite frameworks leads to remarkable PDH performance in terms of activity, selectivity and stability. This is attributed to site-isolation and uniform single-atom dispersion enabled by the zeolite structure. A Zn/Pt ratio >4 is noted as optimal. [10.1021/jacs.1c10261] Pt-Zn intermetallic alloy nanoparticles also demonstrate high thermal stability and activity.

双金属和合金催化剂 - 合金化Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

Pt-Sn合金
    添加Sn已被广泛证明可通过抑制次生氢解和焦化反应来改善丙烯选择性和催化剂在PDH中的稳定性。这导致较低的失活率。 [10.1002/anie.201507119]※ ※ [10.1016/j.jcat.2018.09.006] 计算机和实验研究表明Sn掺杂产生权衡,提高了稳定性但降低了初始活性,通过增加Pt位点上的丙烷解离屏障来增加。 报道Sn/Pt比率约为1-1.5是最佳的。 [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]  
    在原子水平上,Sn通过配体效应改变了Pt的电子密度。 XRD、XPS、IR和理论研究注意到了金属间Pt-Sn键的形成。 [10.1002/anie.201507119] [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018]※ ※  

Pt-Ga合金
    添加Ga作为助剂也有效地提高了PDH性能,提高选择性、接近平衡的初始活性和稳定性。 [10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129] Ga掺杂有助于锚定和分散Pt活性位点。  

Pt-Zn合金
    研究报告将隔离的Pt原子嵌入经Zn修饰的分子筛框架中,在活性、选择性和稳定性方面取得了显着的PDH性能。这归因于分子筛结构所启用的位点隔离和均匀的单原子分散。 Zn/Pt比率> 4被报道为最佳的。 [10.1021/jacs.1c10261] Pt-Zn金属间化合物纳米颗粒也表现出高热稳定性和活性。
