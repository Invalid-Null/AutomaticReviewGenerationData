Based on the details extracted from the file, several articles evaluate supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, or Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation and provide relevant data and insights. 

Specifically, a Pt/alumina catalyst was found to show improved propylene selectivity from 90% to 98% when promoted with 1 wt% boron, due to reduced coke formation over 12 hours [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. Sub-nanometric Pt clusters confined in Ge-UTL zeolite pores via Pt-O-Ge bonds (Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL) displayed high catalytic activity, propylene selectivity and stability for propane dehydrogenation [10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. Adding 0.6-1.5 wt% Ca to a PtSnIn/CaAl catalyst led to excellent stability for over 25 hours, maintaining >58% propane conversion [10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044]. HAADF-STEM images of a 0.4Pt2.4Zn-SPP catalyst showed isolated Pt atoms and potentially small clusters that delivered high activity and selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]. A Pt/BN catalyst was found to have higher stability than Pt/Al2O3, attributed to metal-support interactions [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]. 

Comparisons of Pt with other metals were also reported. A RhCu single atom alloy catalyst supported on silica exhibited higher activity, 100% propene selectivity, and coking resistance compared to a standard Pt/Al2O3 catalyst [10.1126/science.abg8389]. At the same conditions, a NiMo/Al2O3 catalyst showed twice the selectivity of Pt/Al2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c05711].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings showcase promising advancements with supported Pt group metal catalysts for propane dehydrogenation in terms of activity, selectivity and stability. Strategies like adding promoters, optimizing metal-support interactions, and isolating single sites prevent agglomeration and coking issues. Expanding these concepts to cheaper non-precious metals and continuing structure-performance studies could unlock more active, selective and stable catalysts. Operando spectroscopy and theoretical modeling will lend mechanistic insights to guide rational design. Overall there is significant potential to develop enhanced solid catalysts to meet industry needs.

根据文件提取的详细信息,几篇文章评估了受支持的单金属 Pt、Pd 或 Ir 催化剂用于丙烷脱氢,并提供了相关的数据和见解。

具体而言,在12小时内,1 wt%硼的促进剂可将Pt/氧化铝催化剂的丙烯选择性从90%提高到98%,这是由于可消除结焦形成减少了[10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。通过 Pt-O-Ge 键将亚纳米 Pt 簇(Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL)限制在 Ge-UTL 分子筛的孔道中,显示出高的催化活性、丙烯选择性和丙烷脱氢反应的稳定性[10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。向 PtSnIn/CaAl 催化剂中添加 0.6-1.5 wt% 的 Ca 可获得超过 25 小时的极佳稳定性,丙烷转化率可维持在 58% 以上[10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044]。HAADF-STEM 图像显示 0.4Pt2.4Zn-SPP 催化剂中存在被隔离的 Pt 原子和潜在的小簇,这些都体现出高的活性和选择性[10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]。一种 Pt/BN 催化剂比 Pt/Al2O3 表现出更高的稳定性,这被归因于金属与载体间的相互作用[10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]。


也有文章报道了 Pt 与其他金属的比较。硅胶负载的 RhCu 单原子合金催化剂相比标准的 Pt/Al2O3 催化剂表现出更高的活性、100%的丙烯选择性和抗结焦性[10.1126/science.abg8389]。在相同条件下,NiMo/ Al2O3 催化剂的选择性是 Pt/Al2O3 的两倍[10.1021/acscatal.0c05711]。


※※※
综合理解与未来展望:这些发现展示了受支持的铂族金属催化剂在提高丙烷脱氢反应的活性、选择性和稳定性方面具有前景。添加促进剂、优化金属与载体的相互作用以及隔离单一活性位点可以防止团聚和积炭问题。将这些概念扩展到更便宜的非贵金属,并继续结构-性能研究,可能会解锁出更加活跃、选择性和稳定的催化剂。原位光谱和理论建模将提供机理方面的洞见来指导合理的设计。总的来说,发展增强型固体催化剂以满足行业需求有巨大的潜力。
