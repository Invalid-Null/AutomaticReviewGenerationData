Based on the details extracted from the references, the articles do not propose specific mechanistic pathways for propane dehydrogenation over supported metal catalysts in most cases. 

A few exceptions where mechanistic insights are provided:

- Over Pt and PtSn clusters, detailed reaction networks considering dehydrogenation, cracking and hydrogenation steps are mapped out via DFT calculations [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671].

- Isolated Pt sites are proposed to selectively promote the first and second C-H activations while preventing the third one leading to coke [DOI: 10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9].

- A Mars-Van Krevelen redox pathway is suggested over VOx/ZrO2 involving lattice oxygen activation, alkyl species formation, and re-oxidation [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1999.2510].

- Over Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL, the first dehydrogenation step is identified as rate-limiting with a lower barrier of 21.2 kJ/mol [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7].

In most supported metal catalysts though, specific intermediates, active sites, and elementary steps are not elaborated. The articles focus more on compositional effects rather than mechanistic details. Opportunities remain to develop an improved mechanistic understanding of these catalysts through advanced spectroscopic and computational studies.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the lack of definitive mechanistic details for supported metal catalysts in propane dehydrogenation, future research could concentrate efforts on elucidating reaction mechanisms using operando spectroscopy and multi-scale modeling. Capturing transient intermediates and mapping out potential energy surfaces associated with key steps over different metal and support combinations can reveal structure-activity relationships to guide rational design. Furthermore, while metals dominate, emerging alternatives like metal-free catalysts could proffer new mechanistic scenarios worth investigating. Exploring unfamiliar territory may uncover unexpected activity and selectivity trends.

根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,在大多数情况下,这些文章没有为 Supported metal 催化剂提出特定的丙烷脱氢机理途径。

少数几个有机理见解的例外:

- 通过 DFT 计算,对 Pt 和 PtSn 簇详细描绘了脱氢、裂化和氢化步骤的反应网络[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。

- 被隔离的 Pt 位点被建议选择性地促进第一和第二个 C-H 活化,同时阻止导致焦炭形成的第三个[DOI: 10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9]。

- 在 VOx/ZrO2 上提出了 Mars-Van Krevelen 氧化还原途径,涉及晶格氧活化、烷基物种形成和再氧化[DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1999.2510]。

- 在 Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL 上,第一个脱氢步骤被确定为速率限制步骤,屏障较低,为 21.2 kJ/mol [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。

然而,在大多数 Supported metal 催化剂中,没有详细阐述特定的中间体、活性位点和基元步骤。这些文章更关注组成效应,而不是机制细节。仍有机会通过先进的光谱和计算研究来开发这些催化剂的改进机制理解。


※※※

综合理解与未来展望:鉴于 Supported metal 催化剂在丙烷脱氢反应中机制细节的明确缺乏,未来的研究可以集中精力利用 operando 光谱学和多尺度建模来阐明反应机制。捕捉短暂的中间体并绘制与关键步骤相关的潜在能量曲面,可以揭示不同金属和载体组合的结构-活性关系,以指导合理的设计。 此外,虽然金属占主导地位,但新兴的无机催化剂可提供值得研究的新的机制场景。 探索陌生的领域可能会发现意想不到的活性和选择性趋势。
