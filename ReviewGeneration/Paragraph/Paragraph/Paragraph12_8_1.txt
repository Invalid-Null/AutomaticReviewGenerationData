Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic platinum (Pt), palladium (Pd), and iridium (Ir) catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts. For example, one study showed Pt/alumina catalysts are key components in the Oleflex process due to Pt's ability to activate C-H bonds while minimizing C-C cleavage [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]. Other works found supported Pt on boron nitride (Pt/BN) [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008], zeolites (Pt/Zn-ZSM-5) [DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008], and germanosilicate zeolites (Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL) [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7] display enhanced stability and propylene selectivity compared to Pt/Al2O3. Doping Pt/γ-Al2O3 with 1% boron markedly reduced coking and improved selectivity over 12 hours [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. Optimizing dispersion by confining sub-nanometric Pt clusters in zeolite pores is also beneficial [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7].

Studies on Pd, Rh, and Ir are more limited. One work noted they exhibit high intrinsic activity, but did not evaluate any supported catalysts [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. Another directly compared a silica-supported RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst to Pt/Al2O3, finding the RhCu SAA has superior activity and 100% propylene selectivity [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389].

Several studies provide insights into deactivation mechanisms. Sulfur poisoning was shown to accelerate Pt agglomeration and deactivate Pt/MgAl2O4 over multiple PDH cycles [DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]. Three types of coke were identified on spent Pt/Al2O3 and PtSn/Al2O3 using TPO, attributed to different locations [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]. Hydrogen suppresses coking on Pt.

In summary, while Pt-based catalysts have been the most extensively explored, recent works indicate opportunities to tune Pt dispersion, metal-support interactions, and stability through promoters and optimized supports. More research is warranted into extended Pd, Rh, and Ir catalysts. Elucidating deactivation pathways also enables targeted improvements. Integrated computational-experimental approaches can further advance the frontier.

根据文件提供的细节,已有若干研究探索了 supported 单金属铂(Pt)、钯(Pd)和铱(Ir)催化剂用于丙烷脱氢。

Pt基催化剂被报道是应用最广泛且商业上最成功的 PDH 催化剂。例如,一项研究表明 Pt/Al2O3 催化剂是 Oleflex 工艺的关键组成部分,这是由于 Pt 可以激活 C-H 键而最小化 C-C 键的断裂[DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。其他研究发现 supported Pt 在氮硼烷(Pt/BN)[DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]、沸石(Pt/Zn-ZSM-5)[DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008]和锗硅酸盐沸石(Pt4-Ge2-d4r@UTL)[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]上显示出比 Pt/Al2O3 更高的稳定性和丙烯选择性。用 1%的硼掺杂 Pt/γ-Al2O3 可以明显减少焦炭生成并在 12 小时内提高选择性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。优化分散度通过将亚纳米 Pt 簇捕获在沸石孔内也是有益的 [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。


关于钯、铑和铱的研究更少。一项工作指出它们表现出高的固有活性,但没有评估任何 supported 催化剂 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。另一项工作直接比较了硅支持 RhCu 单原子合金(SAA)催化剂和 Pt/Al2O3,发现 RhCu SAA 具有优异的活性和 100% 的丙烯选择性 [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。

几项研究提供了失活机制的见解。硫中毒被证明会加速 Pt 凝聚和多次 PDH 循环后使 Pt/MgAl2O4 失活 [DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]。 使用 TPO 在使用过的 Pt/Al2O3 和 PtSn/Al2O3 上识别出 3 种焦炭,归因于不同部位[DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]。氢气抑制 Pt 上的焦炭生成。

总之,虽然 Pt基催化剂已被广泛研究,但最近的研究表明,通过添加剂和优化支持来调谐 Pt 的分散度、金属支撑相互作用和稳定性存在机会。拓展钯、铑和铱催化剂的研究也值得开展。阐明失活途径也可以实现有针对性的改进。 结合计算和实验方法可以进一步推进这个前沿领域。
