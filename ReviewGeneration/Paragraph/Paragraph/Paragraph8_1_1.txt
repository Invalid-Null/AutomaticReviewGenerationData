<English>
Chromium oxide (Cr2O3) is one of the most extensively studied metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). Supported Cr2O3 catalysts like CrOx/SiO2 and CrOx/Al2O3 have demonstrated catalytic activity for PDH at 723-823 K [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]. Doping Cr2O3 surfaces like α-Cr2O3(0001) with promoters such as Pt can improve PDH performance [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]. Computational studies using DFT calculations and kinetic Monte Carlo simulations have provided mechanistic insights into propane and butane dehydrogenation pathways on Cr2O3 surfaces [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]. However, issues like poor stability due to coke formation remain.

Gallium oxide (Ga2O3), especially the β-Ga2O3 polymorph, is another promising metal oxide catalyst for PDH. Studies show Ga2O3 exhibits higher C3H6 yield compared to Fe2O3 and Cr2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. The catalytic performance correlates with surface acidity, with β-Ga2O3 displaying the highest initial activity among the polymorphs [10.1021/jcat.2005.03.001]. Supported β-Ga2O3/SiO2 and γ-Ga2O3/SiO2 catalysts are also active for PDH [10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]. The tuning of Ga2O3 with secondary oxides like ZrO2 to increase Lewis acid sites is an ongoing research direction [10.1021/acscatal.1c02621].

While Cr2O3 and Ga2O3 have shown promise, metal oxide catalysts generally display lower selectivity and require harsher conditions than metals like Pt. Supported Pt catalysts on Al2O3 and SiO2 achieve PDH activity even below 300°C, attributed to exposed Pt sites that activate C-H bonds [10.1021/acscatal.8b04578]. Modifying Pt with promoters like Sn improves performance further by forming intermetallic PtSn sites [10.1002/anie.202005968]. PtSn/Al2O3 is among the most selective and stable PDH catalysts known, forming the basis for commercial processes by UOP [10.1002/advs.202207756].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The field of PDH catalysis has made tremendous strides, with longstanding metal oxides like Cr2O3 and emerging Ga2O3 polymorphs demonstrating appreciable activity. While questions around optimizing acid-base properties and mitigating coking persist, metal oxides constitute a tunable and economic catalyst class. Recent strategic improvements with promoters hold promise, as evidenced by select Cr2O3 cases. However, scalability challenges remain compared to the more selective Pt and PtSn metallic catalysts deployed industrially. Going forward, molecular-level customization of multi-metallic oxides using advanced synthetic techniques could unravel optimum compositions and architectures for improved PDH activity and stability. Prospected emerge from modulating morphology, crystallinity, porosity and interfaces in novel oxide catalysts.

铬氧化物(Cr2O3)是丙烷脱氢(PDH)最广泛研究的金属氧化物催化剂之一。CrOx/SiO2和CrOx/Al2O3等负载型Cr2O3催化剂已表现出723-823 K时的PDH催化活性[10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]。用Pt等助剂掺杂α-Cr2O3(0001)等Cr2O3表面可提高PDH性能[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]。DFT计算和动力学Monte Carlo模拟研究为Cr2O3表面上的丙烷和丁烷脱氢反应路径提供了机理见解[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]。然而,共烧导致的稳定性差等问题仍然存在。

氧化镓(Ga2O3),特别是β-Ga2O3多形,是另一种具有前景的PDH金属氧化物催化剂。研究表明,Ga2O3的C3H6产率高于Fe2O3和Cr2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。催化性能与表面酸度有关,其中β-Ga2O3的初始活性在多形体中最高[10.1021/jcat.2005.03.001]。负载的β-Ga2O3/SiO2和γ-Ga2O3/SiO2催化剂也对PDH活性[10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]。用ZrO2等次生氧化物调节Ga2O3以增加Lewis酸位也是正在进行的研究方向[10.1021/acscatal.1c02621]。

虽然Cr2O3和Ga2O3显示出前景,但与Pt等金属相比,金属氧化物催化剂的选择性普遍较低,需要更严酷的条件。负载在Al2O3和SiO2上的Pt催化剂甚至在300°C以下就能实现PDH活性,这是由暴露的Pt位点激活C-H键导致的[10.1021/acscatal.8b04578]。用Sn等助剂改性Pt可以进一步提高性能,形成PtSn间金属位点[10.1002/anie.202005968]。PtSn/Al2O3是已知选择性和稳定性最高的PDH催化剂之一,构成了UOP等商业工艺的基础[10.1002/advs.202207756]。

综合理解与未来展望: PDH催化领域取得了巨大进步,长期以来的金属氧化物Cr2O3和新兴的Ga2O3多形都表现出可观的活性。 尽管调节酸碱性和缓解共烧的问题仍存,金属氧化物构成了一个可调节且经济的催化剂类。某些Cr2O3案例表明,最近的战略性助剂改进大有前景。然而,与业已在工业上部署的更选择性的Pt和PtSn金属催化剂相比,其可扩展性仍存在挑战。展望未来,使用先进的合成技术在分子水平上定制多金属氧化物可能会揭示PDH活性和稳定性的最佳组成和结构。新奇氧化物催化剂形态、结晶度、孔隙度和界面的调控都可能带来新的机遇。
