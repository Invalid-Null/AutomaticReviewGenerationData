Based on the details extracted from the literature, various mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over different metal oxide catalysts. 

On chromium oxide catalysts, active sites consisting of coordinatively unsaturated Cr3+ species [DOI1], Cr3+/Cr2+ redox pairs [DOI2], and mononuclear Cr(III) sites [DOI3] have been suggested. The reaction mechanisms involve redox cycles between Cr6+/Cr3+/Cr2+ [DOI2], heterolytic C-H bond dissociation to form chromium alkyl species [DOI4], and re-oxidation of reduced sites by CO2 or O2. 

Over gallium oxides, tetracoordinated Ga3+ sites [DOI5] catalyzing dehydrogenation via a heterolytic dissociation path have been proposed [DOI6, DOI7]. This involves dissociative adsorption forming gallium hydride and alkoxide intermediates. Subsequent β-H elimination releases propene. Ga+H+ pair sites in zeolites also demonstrate high activity [DOI8].

On vanadium oxides, isolated V3+ sites generated upon support interaction or reduction of V5+ [DOI9, DOI10] as well as Mars-van Krevelen pathways utilizing redox cycles between V5+/V4+/V3+ and lattice oxygen [DOI11, DOI12] have been suggested. VOx nanoclusters suppress overoxidation by limiting bulk oxygen diffusion compared to bulk V2O5 [DOI13].

Zirconia catalysts have shown good activity with dual Zr4+ sites neighboring an oxygen vacancy proposed as active sites [DOI14, DOI15]. They facilitate concerted/stepwise dehydrogenation pathways via CH activation and OH recombination steps [DOI16]. Introducing dopants like Ga can further tune the stability and energies of intermediates/transition states [DOI17].

Thus, mechanistic intricacies have been deciphered across metal oxide catalysts spanning Cr, Ga, V, and Zr oxides. However, the field is still nascent and further research into definitive structure-activity correlations anchored in advanced in-situ spectroscopy and theoretical simulations might illuminate unambiguous identification of active sites and intermediates governing selectivity, activity, and stability.     

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Advancements in elucidating mechanistic pathways have paved inroads into targeted development of selective and stable metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. Looking ahead, some interesting avenues to potentially explore include:

1) Systematic computational screening of multicomponent mixed metal oxides to identify novel compositions and crystal facets optimal for activating propane while hindering side reactions.

2) Operando spectroscopy coupled with isotope labeling and advanced microscopy to directly validate proposed intermediates/active sites under working conditions.  

3) Machine learning models correlating catalyst descriptors with macroscopic performance indicators pinpointing ideal active site characteristics.

4) Modulation of metal oxidation states and oxygen mobility via smart catalyst synthesis methods and dynamic operating protocols for optimal redox functionality.

Thus, an interdisciplinary ecosystem leveraging computational predictions, advanced characterization, and clever catalyst/reactor design may fast-track development of optimal metal oxide catalysts hitherto unexplored for propane dehydrogenation.

根据从文献中提取的详细信息,已经为不同金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢反应提出了各种机理场景。

在铬氧化物催化剂上,由无配体的Cr3+物种[DOI1]、Cr3+/Cr2+氧化还原对[DOI2]和单核Cr(III)位点[DOI3]组成的活性位点已被提出。反应机理涉及Cr6+/Cr3+/Cr2+之间的氧化还原循环[DOI2]、杂化C-H键解离以形成铬烷基物种[DOI4],以及CO2或O2氧化还原位点。

在氧化镓上,四配位的Ga3+位点[DOI5]通过杂化解离途径催化脱氢[DOI6,DOI7]。这涉及解离吸附形成镓化氢和烷氧化物中间体。随后的β-H消除释放丙烯。 ZSM-5沸石中的Ga+H+成对位点也表现出高活性[DOI8]。

在V氧化物上,支持相互作用或V5+还原生成的隔离态V3+位点[DOI9,DOI10]以及利用V5+/V4+/V3+和晶格氧之间的Mars-van Krevelen途径的氧化还原循环都已被证实[ DOI11,DOI12]。 VOx纳米簇抑制过度氧化,方法是限制与V2O5块体相比的氧的大量扩散[DOI13]。


稀土金属ZrO2催化剂因其隔邻氧空位的双Zr4+位点被提出为活性位点而表现出良好活性[DOI14,DOI15]。它们通过CH活化和OH重组步骤促进协同/分步脱氢途径[DOI16]。引入Ga等掺杂剂可以进一步调节中间体/过渡态的稳定性和能量[DOI17]。

因此,在Cr、Ga、V和Zr氧化物等金属氧化物催化剂上已经解析了机理复杂性。但是,这个领域仍然很新生,进一步的研究可能会通过先进的原位光谱和理论模拟来明确说明控制选择性、活性和稳定性的活性位点和中间体的明确的结构-活性关系。

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Advancements in elucidating mechanistic pathways have paved inroads into targeted development of selective and stable metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. Looking ahead, some interesting avenues to potentially explore include:

1) 系统的计算筛选多组分混合金属氧化物,以识别理想的组成和晶面,以激活丙烷同时抑制副反应。

2) 使用同位素标记和先进显微技术在工作条件下直接验证所提出的中间体/活性位点的原位光谱。 

3) 把催化剂描述符与宏观性能指标相关的机器学习模型,确定理想的活性位点特征。

4) 通过智能催化剂合成方法和动态操作方案调制金属氧化状态和氧的迁移性,实现最佳的氧化还原功能。

因此,利用计算预测、先进表征和智能催化剂/反应器设计的跨学科生态系统,可能会加速开发一些未经开发的用于丙烷脱氢的最佳金属氧化物催化剂。
