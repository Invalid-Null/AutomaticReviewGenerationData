Based on the details extracted from the references, some articles describe techniques for synthesizing isolated single sites in zeolitic supports for propane dehydrogenation catalysts. 

One method is dealumination to generate silanol groups and vacant sites in the zeolite framework that can stabilize isolated metal ions. For example, Co2+ ions are confined in hydroxyl nests of dealuminated SiBeta zeolite [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]. Isolated Ga+ sites can also be introduced by exchanging Brønsted protons in the zeolite [10.1002/anie.200702463].

Additives like ethylenediamine during hydrothermal synthesis help coordinate and disperse metal ions like Zn2+ to prevent aggregation, resulting in isolated zinc cations trapped in the S-1 zeolite pores [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034]. Chemical vapor deposition (CVD) using precursors like dimethylzinc can deposit isolated Zn-methyl species that convert to Zn2+ sites upon high temperature reduction [10.1021/cs200441e]. 

Advanced microscopy techniques directly confirm the incorporation of isolated metal ions and clusters at targeted locations in the zeolite framework. For example, subnanometric platinum species preferentially inhabit the larger sinusoidal channels of zeolite MFI, directed by the template molecules occupying intersecting voids [10.1038/s41563-019-0412-6].

Overall, strategic introduction of heteroatoms, custom-synthesized ligands, regulated hydrothermal and post-synthesis treatments permit meticulous engineering of isolated active sites ensconced within tailored microporous zeolite matrices.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The elucidated molecular-level techniques enable precise spatial deposition of isolated single sites in customized zeolitic scaffolds. Building upon these modular toolkits, future explorations might attempt multi-step cascades merging isolated sites with proximal acid-base functionalities inside hierarchical pores, potentially unlocking superior catalytic landscapes. Orchestrating symbiotic active site-zeolite interactions through computational modeling and directing bottom-up assembly with machine learning could reveal unprecedented structural and functional possibilities. Beyond propane dehydrogenation, these emerging preparative strategies to choreograph isolated sites in zeolites may find widespread applications from biomass upgrading to nitrogen reduction.

根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,一些文章描述了在分子筛载体上合成隔离的单一活性中心以制备丙烷脱氢催化剂的技术。

一种方法是去铝以在分子筛框架中产生硅醇基团和空位点,这可以稳定隔离的金属离子。例如,Co2+离子被限制在去铝SiBeta分子筛的羟基巢穴中[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]。通过交换分子筛中的Brønsted质子也可以引入隔离的Ga+位点[10.1002/anie.200702463]。

像乙二胺这样的添加剂在水热合成过程中有助于配位和分散金属离子如Zn2+以防止聚集,从而导致被困在S-1分子筛孔中的隔离锌阳离子[10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034]。使用像二甲基锌等前体的化学气相沉积(CVD)可以沉积隔离的Zn-甲基物种,这些物种在高温还原后转换为Zn2+位点[10.1021/cs200441e]。

先进的显微技术可以直接确认隔离的金属离子和簇合物在分子筛框架内的定位。例如,亚纳米级的铂物种优先栖息在分子筛MFI较大的正弦波通道中,这是由占据交叉口的模板分子引导的[10.1038/s41563-019-0412-6]。

总的来说,异质原子的策略引入、定制合成的配体、规范的水热和合成后处理允许在定制的微孔分子筛基质中精确工程隔离的活性位点。

综合理解与展望:阐明的分子水平技术实现了隔离单一活性中心在定制分子筛支架中的精确空间沉积。在这些模块化工具包的基础上,未来的探索可能会尝试将隔离的位点与分级孔中的近端酸碱官能团融合,这可能会释放出卓越的催化景观。 通过计算建模指导单向组装和利用机器学习揭示前所未有的结构和功能可能性,可以协调隔离位点与分子筛之间的共生相互作用。除丙烷脱氢之外,这些新兴的制备策略为在分子筛中编排隔离位点可能在从生物质升级到氮还原等广泛应用中发挥重要作用。
