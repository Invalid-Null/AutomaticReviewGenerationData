Based on the details extracted from the file, some articles describe techniques for synthesizing isolated single sites in zeolitic supports for propane dehydrogenation catalysts. 

One method is dealumination to generate vacant framework sites like silanol nests that can stabilize isolated metal species. An example is dealuminating Beta zeolite, then using impregnation to confine Co compounds in the silanol nests [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]. Other articles discuss grafting organotin complexes onto defects in dealuminated zeolites to produce isolated Sn sites [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527].

Ion exchange is also used to introduce isolated metal ions into zeolites. For instance, H-ZSM-5 is ion exchanged with GaCl3 to replace Brønsted protons with isolated Ga+ sites [10.1002/anie.200702463]. Similar procedures synthesize Zn/H-ZSM-5 with isolated Zn2+ [10.1021/acscatal.2c05898].

Some articles use hydrothermal synthesis with additives like ethylenediamine to stabilize isolated transition metals against aggregation in the zeolite framework. This enables trapping isolated Zn+ in the pores of S-1 zeolite [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034].

Additionally, chemical vapor deposition (CVD) introduces organometallic precursors like dimethylzinc that get incorporated as isolated metal-methyl species. Subsequent high temperature reduction converts these to isolated metal cations [10.1021/cs200441e].

Overall, defect engineering of zeolites combined with stabilization approaches like ligand addition enables constructing isolated single sites for catalysis.

一种方法是除铝以生成类似硅醇巢的空位框架位点,这可以稳定隔离的金属族。一个例子是除铝β沸石,然后使用浸渍法将Co化合物限制在硅醇巢中[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]。其他文章讨论了在除铝沸石的缺陷上接枝有机锡复合物以产生隔离的Sn位点[10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]。

另一种方法是离子交换,将隔离的金属离子引入分子筛。例如,H-ZSM-5经GaCl3 离子交换以用隔离的Ga+位点替换Brønsted质子[10.1002/anie.200702463]。类似的方法合成了具有隔离Zn2+的Zn/H-ZSM-5 [10.1021/acscatal.2c05898]。

一些文章使用添加剂如乙二胺的水热合成方法来稳定过渡金属免受分子筛框架中的聚集。这使得在S-1沸石孔内捕获隔离的Zn+成为可能[10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034]。

此外,化学气相沉积(CVD)引入如二甲基锌的有机金属前体,这些前体以隔离的金属甲基物种的形式被并入。后续的高温还原将这些还原为隔离的金属阳离子[10.1021/cs200441e]。

总的来说,分子筛的缺陷工程与稳定方法(如配体添加)的结合可以构建出用于催化的隔离单一位点。
