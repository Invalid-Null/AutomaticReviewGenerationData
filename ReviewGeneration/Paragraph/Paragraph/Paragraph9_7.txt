Based on the detailed analysis from the references provided [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033], [10.1021/acscatal.2c00878], [10.1021/acscatal.0c02782], tuning oxidation states, oxygen mobility, and acid-base properties of metal oxides has been shown to significantly impact propane dehydrogenation performance. 

For alumina-supported vanadium oxide catalysts, modifying the material with phosphine treatment generates more isolated vanadium species, tunes surface acidity, and weakens vanadium-support interactions [10.1016/j.apcatb.2020.119089]. These changes inhibit coking and improve stability, although initial activity decreases. For supported VOx catalysts in general, partly reduced V3+/V4+ sites activate propane, while the chemical states of bridge oxygen species play a key role in C-H bond activation [10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]. Weaker VOx-support interactions lower bridge oxygen electron density, enhancing its capability to rupture C-H bonds. Introducing CO2 facilitates propane conversion by re-oxidizing VOx to create additional active lattice oxygen.

With chromium oxide catalysts, Cr3+ species generated in situ during reaction are responsible for activity [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]. On γ-Al2O3 supports, consumption of lattice oxygen by propane forms coordinatively unsaturated Alcu-O acid-base pairs that tune intermediate adsorption energies and lower activation barriers, improving propane dehydrogenation activity [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033].

Computational and experimental results also reveal the favorable thermodynamics and lowered energy requirements of oxidative vs non-oxidative propane dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.0c02782]. However, oxidative routes suffer from overoxidation of formed propene into COx due to the high reactivity of lattice oxygen. Controlling oxidation state, oxygen mobility, and acid-base properties provides opportunities to overcome this challenge.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings discussed in the references highlight the critical roles that metal oxidation states, oxygen mobility, and acid-base properties play in determining propane dehydrogenation performance over metal oxide catalysts. Key active site descriptors like the concentration of oxygen vacancies, abundance of unsaturated metal sites, and acid/base site density correlate strongly with activity, selectivity, and stability metrics.

While much progress has been made in elucidating these structure-function relationships, opportunities remain to explore emerging materials like single atom catalysts and 2D metal oxides that provide unprecedented control over oxidation states and coordination environments. Operando spectroscopic techniques combined with kinetic isotope tracing could uncover additional insights into the nature of active sites and rate-determining steps. And machine learning models trained on high-throughput DFT or experimental data may accelerate identification of optimized compositions and processing pathways. Expanding beyond traditional Mars-van Krevelen paradigms by incorporating oxidative promoters or designing bifunctional sites also represent intriguing research directions with untapped potential.

<Chinese>

根据所提供参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033]、[10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]和[10.1021/acscatal.0c02782]的详细分析,调控金属氧化物的氧化态、氧迁移性和酸碱性对丙烷脱氢性能有显着影响。

对于氧化铝载体上的钒氧化物催化剂,用磷化氢处理可以生成更多的隔离型钒物种,调节表面酸性,削弱钒与载体的相互作用[10.1016/j.apcatb.2020.119089]。这些变化抑制结焦,提高了稳定性,尽管初始活性有所下降。 对于载体上的VOx催化剂,V3+/V4+部分还原物种激活丙烷,而桥氧化合物的化学状态对C-H键的活化起关键作用[10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]。 更弱的VOx与载体的相互作用降低桥氧的电子密度,增强其断裂C-H键的能力。引入CO2有助于通过重新氧化VOx来促进丙烷转化,生成更多活性晶格氧。

对于铬氧化物催化剂,原位生成的Cr3+物种负责催化活性[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]。 在γ-Al2O3载体上,丙烷消耗晶格氧形成配位不饱和的Alcu-O酸碱对,这调节中间体吸附能,降低激活能垒,改善丙烷脱氢活性[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033]。

计算和实验结果还揭示氧化脱氢与非氧化脱氢相比,具有良好的热力学和更低的能量需求[10.1021/acscatal.0c02782]。 然而,氧化路线由于晶格氧的高反应性而发生形成的丙烯进一步氧化为COx的问题。 控制氧化态、氧迁移性和酸碱性为克服这一挑战提供了机会。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:参考文献中提到的结果强调金属氧化物催化剂丙烷脱氢性能的决定性作用。 活性中心描述符的关键,如氧空位浓度、未饱和金属位点丰度和酸碱位点密度,与活性、选择性和稳定性指标高度相关。

尽管在阐明这些结构-功能关系方面已经取得了长足的进步,但探索新兴材料(如单原子催化剂和二维金属氧化物)以前所未有的控制氧化态和配位环境的机会仍然存在。 operando光谱技术结合动力同位素示踪可以深入了解活性位点的性质和速率控制步骤。 并且,训练有素的机器学习模型可以加速优化组成和处理途径的识别。 超越传统Mars-van Krevelen范式,加入氧化促进剂或设计双功能位点,也代表了具有未开发潜力的有趣研究方向。
