 <English>
Based on the details extracted from the literature, propane dehydrogenation reaction mechanisms have been proposed over various metal oxide catalysts. 

Over CrZrOx catalysts, two neighboring coordinatively unsaturated Zr (Zrcus) sites located at an oxygen vacancy are proposed as the active sites responsible for dehydrogenation of propane to propene [10.1021/acscatal.9b05063]. The creation of these sites involves reductive treatment of the catalyst. DFT calculations show the presence of Cr helps facilitate propylene formation by reducing the energy barrier for breaking the methyl C-H bond.

Isolated tricoordinated Zn2+ sites anchored on ZrO2 are suggested to be active for propane dehydrogenation over ZnZrxOy catalysts, possibly with synergistic effects between Zn2+, ZrO2 and TiO2 [10.1021/acscatal.0c01580]. Computational studies propose Zn2+ sites catalyze the first dehydrogenation step and neighboring Zrcus sites catalyze the second step [10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074].

Over VOx/ZrO2, isolated V5+ sites are proposed to catalyze propane dehydrogenation via a Mars-van Krevelen type mechanism, with lattice oxygen participating in alkane activation steps [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.02.028]. V5+ sites reduce to V4+ and V3+ during reaction and reoxidize using O2.

γ-Al2O3 supported VOx catalysts are described to go through an initial ODH dominant period with VO as active site, followed by a PDH period with AlOV and unsaturated V sites active after VO is removed [10.1021/acscatal.8b04701].

Various other mechanistic proposals are described over Ga, Cr, Mn, Mo oxides involving heterolytic dissociation routes, redox cycles with specific active oxidation states, and CO2-assisted pathways [10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.004, 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999, 10.1021/jacs.9b09235, 10.1021/acscatal.2c02099].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The studies analyzed showcase propane dehydrogenation proceeds through diverse mechanistic routes over assorted metal oxide catalyst families. Key aspects determining reactivity and selectivity include creation of coordinatively unsaturated sites, synergism between different metal species, participation of lattice oxygen, and targeted tuning of oxidation states. 

An expansive research opportunity exists in systematically tuning these critical attributes over mixed and multicomponent oxide systems using advanced synthetic techniques. Innovative in-situ/operando methodologies can offer finer details into transient intermediates and dynamic active site transformations during catalytic turnovers. Computational screening approaches may also predict hitherto unknown compositions and crystal motifs that demonstrate superior intrinsic activity or selectivity control. Such endeavors can usher the rational design of next-generation catalysts tailored for targeted conversion of propane and other alkanes.

<Chinese>
根据从文献中提取的详细信息,己经在各种金属氧化物催化剂上提出了丙烷脱氢反应机理。

在CrZrOx催化剂上,位于氧空位的两个相邻的非配位Zr (Zrcus)位点被提出是负责将丙烷脱氢为丙烯的活性位点[10.1021/acscatal.9b05063]。这些位点的创建涉及到催化剂的还原处理。DFT计算表明,Cr的存在有助于通过降低断裂甲基C-H键的能垒来促进丙烯的形成。

锚定在ZrO2上的三配位Zn2+ 隔离位点被认为是ZnZrxOy催化剂上丙烷脱氢反应的活性位点,可能存在Zn2+、ZrO2 和TiO2之间的协同效应[10.1021/acscatal.0c01580]。计算研究提出Zn2+位点催化第一步脱氢反应,相邻的Zrcus位点催化第二步反应[10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074]。

 在VOx/ZrO2上,隔离的V5+位点通过Mars-van Krevelen机理被提出催化丙烷的脱氢,其中晶格氧参与烷烃活化步骤[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.02.028]。 V5+位点在反应过程中还原为V4+和V3+,并使用O2氧化。

γ-Al2O3支持的VOx催化剂描述为经历一个最初的ODH主导期,VO作为活性位点,随后是一个PDH期,在VO被移除后,AlOV和不饱和的V位点变得活跃[10.1021/acscatal.8b04701]。

在Ga、Cr、Mn、Mo氧化物上描述了各种其他的机理提议,包括杂裂解路径、红氧循环与特定的活性氧化态、和CO2辅助途径[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.004, 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999, 10.1021/jacs.9b09235, 10.1021/acscatal.2c02099]。

 ※※※

综合理解与未来展望:分析的研究表明,丙烷脱氢反应可以通过各种机理途径进行,遍及不同金属氧化物催化剂体系。决定反应活性和选择性的关键方面包括配位不饱和位点的产生、不同金属物种之间的协同作用、晶格氧的参与和定向调控氧化态。

系统调控这些关键属性的广阔研究机会存在于混合和多组分氧化物系统中,采用先进的合成技术。创新性的原位/操作条件下的方法论可以提供更多关于瞬态中间体和催化转化过程中动态活性位点转化的细节。计算筛选方法也可以预测迄今未知的组成和晶体基序,这些组成和基序展示出色的固有活性或选择性控制。这些努力可以引领下一代催化剂的设计,这些催化剂是为丙烷和其他烷烃的定向转换量身定制的。
