Based on the details extracted from the file, the article with DOI 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726 describes using dealumination to generate isolated silanol groups in the zeolite support, and then immobilizing isolated Co2+ cations at these sites via vacuum impregnation [1].

The work in [10.1021/jacs.2c02636] utilizes a ligand-protected hydrothermal synthesis approach to construct isolated Co-motifs with a distorted tetrahedral structure that are homogeneously embedded into the T1(7) and T3(9) sites of MFI zeolites, as confirmed by advanced characterization techniques [2].

Isolated boron sites stabilized against hydrolysis were achieved in the BS-1 catalyst synthesized using a solvent-free crystallization strategy with TPA+ hydroxide, resulting in a zeolite with an Si/B ratio of 62 after calcination [10.1126/science.abe7935] [3].

Dealumination of Beta zeolite generates vacant Al sites and SiOH nests that can anchor and isolate active metal sites, as described in [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]. Examples include incorporating Ga species into the SiBeta framework and confining Co compounds in hydroxyl nests [4]. 

Isolated Sn sites were produced by grafting tributyltin hydride complexes onto defects generated via dealumination of zeolite Beta, enabling stabilization of supported Pt clusters [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527] [5].

Utilizing a chemical vapor deposition method with dimethylzinc precursor introduced isolated Zn-methyl species into the H-ZSM-5 zeolite framework, which were then converted to isolated Zn2+ sites by high temperature hydrogen reduction [10.1021/cs200441e] [6].

The addition of Cu helped promote the exclusive formation of isolated [ZnOH]+ sites in the silicalite-1 framework by limiting Zn aggregation, as per [10.1021/acscatal.2c00928], which served as precursors to highly reactive [ZnH]+ sites during propane dehydrogenation [7].

[10.1002/anie.200702463] describes replacing Brønsted protons in ZSM-5 quantitatively with Ga+ ions to achieve isolated Ga Lewis acid sites [8].

根据文件中提取的详细信息,DOI为10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726的文章描述了使用除铝来产生分离的硅醇基团在分子筛支持体中,然后通过真空浸渍固定住分离的Co2+阳离子[1]。  

[10.1021/jacs.2c02636]的工作利用配体保护的水热合成方法来构建被扭曲的四面体结构的分离Co-基团,这些基团被均匀嵌入到MFI分子筛的T1(7)和T3(9)位点中,正如先进表征技术所证实的那样[2]。

在使用TPA+氢氧化物的无溶剂结晶策略合成的BS-1催化剂中实现了抗水解的稳定分离硼位点,煅烧后得到Si/B比为62的分子筛[10.1126/science.abe7935][3]。  

Beta分子筛的除铝生成空氧铝位点和SiOH巢,可以锚定和隔离活性金属位点,如[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]中所述。 例子包括掺入SiBeta框架中的Ga种类和限制在除铝SiBeta的羟基巢中的Co化合物[4]。

通过将三丁基锡氢化物复合物嫁接到除铝Beta分子筛产生的缺陷上,产生了分离的锡位点,这使得支持的Pt簇的稳定化成为可能 [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527][5]。

使用二甲基锌前体的化学气相沉积法向H-ZSM-5分子筛框架中引入了分离的Zn-甲基物种,然后通过高氢化还原转化为分离的Zn2+位点[10.1021/cs200441e][6]。

根据[10.1021/acscatal.2c00928],Cu的添加帮助促进硅酸盐-1框架中排他性[ZnOH]+位点的形成,限制了Zn的聚集,这些位点在丙烷脱氢反应中作为高活性[ZnH]+位点的前体。[7]。

[10.1002/anie.200702463]描述了通过定量替换ZSM-5中的Brønsted质子以获得分离的Ga Lewis酸位点的方法[8]。

综合理解与未来展望:参考文献阐明的技术强调定制催化剂合成策略,通常涉及定制的分子筛除铝、受配体或框架杂原子稳定的隔离金属离子的受控引入,以及战略性的合成后处理。先进的原位光谱学和显微技术使隔离活性位点的明确映射成为可能。 

在这些基本方法的基础上,出现了广阔的可能性。例如,高通量计算筛选可以加速识别优化的分子筛拓扑结构和活性位点位置。基于数据的合成优化可能会发现前所未有的修饰剂、配体和后处理的组合,以产生目标隔离基团。整体规模反应器的原位表征可以揭示降解途径,并激发重新设计的体系结构。与新兴领域(如单原子合金簇或二维分子筛)的交叉可能会催生进一步代的超稳定单一位点。最终,继续植根于基本催化剂设计原理的创新将使这些创新 Prime 准备满足可持续性挑战。
