Specific examples from the analysis reveal how XANES and EXAFS determine the coordination environment and oxidation states of active species like Cr, V, Ga, Pt etc. under varying conditions [DOI1, DOI2]. In situ and operando capabilities allow tracking species evolution in real-time, like changing Cr6+/Cr3+ ratios during PDH [DOI3]. XPS quantifies surface compositions, indicating formation of alloys or partial reduction [DOI4, DOI5]. 

These advances uncover pathways occurring during catalyst activation or deactivation. However, most studies detect static snapshots before/after catalysis rather than continuous evolution. Opportunities exist to implement improved time-resolved, multi-technique operando spectroscopy for simultaneously capturing bulk and surface transformations during catalysis [DOI6, DOI7]. Machine-learning algorithms can extract hidden correlations in complex data. Such mechanistic insights will facilitate rational design of tailored catalysts.

根据文件中提取的详细信息,丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂采用X射线吸收光谱(XAS)、X射线近边缘结构(XANES)、扩展X射线细结构(EXAFS)和X射线光电子能谱(XPS)等X射线技术来探测催化剂合成或反应步骤中的氧化态变化。

具体例子揭示了XANES和EXAFS如何在不同条件下确定Cr、V、Ga、Pt等活性种的配位环境和氧化态[DOI1,DOI2]。原位和在线监测功能可以实时跟踪物种的演变,如PDH过程中Cr6+/ Cr3+比率的变化[DOI3]。 XPS可以定量表面组成,表明合金的形成或部分还原[DOI4,DOI5]。

这些进展揭示了催化剂激活或失活过程中的途径。但是,大多数研究仅检测反应前后静态快照,而不是连续演变。 实现改进的时间分辨、多技术联用光谱技术以同时捕获体相和表面转化的机会依然存在[DOI6, DOI7]。机器学习算法可以从复杂数据中提取隐藏的相关性。这种机理见解将有助于理性设计定制催化剂。
