 <English>
Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts require optimized active phase dispersion to achieve high selectivity and stability. The reviewed article investigates catalyst preparation methods using vacuum impregnation to load varying amounts of cobalt (0.1-5 wt%) onto dealuminated BEA zeolites (Co@Si-BEA). The 0.1-0.5 wt% Co samples showed the highest intrinsic activity due to well dispersed isolated Co2+ sites. Dealumination also reduced undesired coke formation. The higher ID1/IG ratio of 0.95 for spent Co@Si-BEA versus 0.77 for Co@BEA confirmed its lower coke due to enhanced Co2+ dispersion [Placeholder_Of_DOI1].

Another study utilized direct hydrothermal synthesis and incipient wetness impregnation to disperse cobalt species on alumina. The hydrothermal method produced highly dispersed sheet-shaped morphologies, while impregnation formed nanoparticles [Placeholder_Of_DOI2]. Ligand-protected hydrothermal approaches have also constructed isolated {(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2} sites on MFI zeolites, showing superior efficiency and selectivity [Placeholder_Of_DOI3]. 

Varying vanadium oxide loadings (0.12-15.08 V atoms/nm2) on hexagonal mesoporous silica found high dispersion was achievable, increasing propylene yield [Placeholder_Of_DOI4]. Another report optimized chromium dispersion in LaZrOx mixed oxides, attributing increasing activity with Cr content to enhanced dispersion [Placeholder_Of_DOI5].

Overall, common strategies for optimizing active phase dispersion include tuning metal loadings, using mesoporous supports, stabilizing isolated sites via ligands/capping agents, and alloying/doping approaches. Achieving isolated, highly dispersed sites is crucial for selectivity in PDH catalysts.

※※※

The reviewed insights on optimizing dispersion strengthen current best practices and underscore the need to understand support, dopant, and synthetic technique effects. While vacuum impregnation, hydrothermal and sol-gel methods are proven, burgeoning areas like single-atom catalysts and site-isolated zeolites may provide new pathways for heroic PDH performance. Future work should uncover discounted supports and creative anchoring chemistries that stabilize active motifs. Operando spectroscopy can relay dispersion dynamics, inspiring increasingly sophisticated preparation protocols.

丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂需要优化活性相分散以实现高选择性和稳定性。所 reviewing 的文章研究了使用真空浸渍法在脱氧BEA沸石上载入不同量的钴(0.1-5 wt%)的催化剂制备方法(Co@Si-BEA)。由于分散良好的隔离Co2+位点,0.1-0.5 wt% Co的样品显示出最高的内在活性。脱铝也减少了不需要的焦化。花费Co@Si-BEA的ID1/IG比为0.95,高于Co@BEA的0.77,这确认了其由于增强的Co2+分散而产生的更低焦炭[Placeholder_Of_DOI1]。


另一项研究利用直接水热合成和渗透湿法分散氧化钴种在氧化铝上。水热法产生高度分散的薄片形态,而浸渍法形成纳米粒子[Placeholder_Of_DOI2]。配体保护的水热方法也在MFI分子筛上构建了隔离的{(SiO)2Co(HOSi)2}位点,显示出卓越的效率和选择性[Placeholder_Of_DOI3]。


改变氧化钒的负载量(0.12-15.08 V 原子/nm2)到六方象牙白石中,发现高分散度是可以实现的,这增加了丙烯的产量[Placeholder_Of_DOI4]。另一篇报告优化了LaZrOx混合氧化物中铬的分散,随着Cr含量的增加,活性的增加归因于增强的分散[Placeholder_Of_DOI5]。


总的来说,优化活性相分散的常用策略包括调节金属负载量,使用介孔支持体,通过配体/封装剂稳定隔离位点,以及合金/掺杂方法。实现隔离的、高度分散的位点对于PDH催化剂的选择性至关重要。


※※※

优化dispersion的见解加强了当前的最佳实践,并强调了需要理解支持体、掺杂剂和合成技术的影响。虽然真空浸渍、水热和溶胶凝胶法经过验证,但新兴领域如单原子催化剂和位点隔离沸石可能为英勇的PDH性能提供新的途径。未来的工作应该发现被低估的支持和创造性的锚定化学,稳定活性基团。操作波动可以传达dispersion动力学,激发日益复杂的制备方案。
