Based on the details extracted from the texts, there is evidence showing how tuning oxidation states, oxygen mobility, and acid-base properties of metal oxides can affect propane dehydrogenation performance. 

For example, one study on VOx/Al2O3 catalysts modified with phosphine treatment revealed that inserting phosphorus helped separate polymeric vanadium species into more isolated species. This resulted in weaker vanadium-support interactions, easier desorption of products, and inhibition of coke precursors - ultimately improving catalytic stability [10.1016/j.apcatb.2020.119089].

Investigations on ZnZrxOy catalysts demonstrated increased oxygen mobility and formation of active sites like Zncus and Zrcus with higher Zr content. This positively influenced propane conversion rates [10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074]. Experiments on FeNi/CeO2 unveiled how tuning acid-base properties directed selectivity, as DFT calculations showed CH dissociation was preferred over CC cleavage [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999].  

Studies also stressed on the pivotal role of oxidation states in modulating performance. For example, generation and dispersion of partially reduced Cr3+/Cr2+ species in CrOx determined CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation activity [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]. In the case of Ga2O3 polymorphs, the higher acid site density of β-Ga2O3 crystal form provided superior conversion levels [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001].

例如,在一项对VOx/Al2O3催化剂进行磷化氢处理的研究中,插入磷有助于将聚合型钒种分离成更加隔离的种类。这导致了较弱的钒支撑相互作用、产品更容易脱附以及抑制焦炭前体-最终提高了催化稳定性 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2020.119089]。

对ZnZrxOy催化剂的研究表明,随着铼含量的增加,活性氧迁移性得到了提高,并形成了如Zncus和Zrcus等活性中心。这对丙烷转换率产生了正面影响[10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074]。 FeNi/CeO2的实验揭示了如何通过调节酸碱性来指导选择性,因为DFT计算表明CH解离优先于CC断裂[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。

研究还强调了氧化态在调节性能中的关键作用。 例如,CrOx中部分还原的Cr3+/Cr2+物种的生成和分散决定了CO2辅助氧化脱氢活性[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]。 在Ga2O3多形体的情况下,β-Ga2O3晶体形式的较高酸性提供了更高的转化水平[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]。

一个开阔的系统方法可能会揭示额外的协同效应——例如,通过将金属氧化物与新型载体或助剂耦合。高通量筛选也可以加速识别最佳位点体系结构。此外,原位光谱技术可能会提供动态的见解,以推进机制的理解和精确的性能-功能映射。最终,这些面向未来的进展可以引领稳定和选择性丙烷脱氢催化剂的开发。
