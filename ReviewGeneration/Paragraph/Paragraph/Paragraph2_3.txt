 <English>

Propane dehydrogenation catalysts lower the activation energy barriers for breaking C-H bonds in propane to produce propylene. Industrial PDH processes utilize supported metal catalysts like Pt/Al2O3 and CrOx/Al2O3 that provide active sites for selectively activating C-H bonds over C-C bonds [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00939]. Theoretical calculations have elucidated reaction mechanisms showing propane adsorption and hydrogen abstraction steps on metal cation sites [10.1021/acscatal.0c01455, 10.1021/acscatal.2c01374]. Alternative catalysts like Sn-Beta zeolites also facilitate dehydrogenation through incorporated framework tin atoms that act as Lewis acid sites [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019].

※※※ 

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The reviewed research highlights progress in synthesizing and characterizing selective metal-based catalysts to lower activation barriers in propane dehydrogenation reactions. Further work could explore replacing scarce platinum group metals with earth abundant alternatives, as well as rational catalyst design guided by computational modeling to optimize active site geometries for C-H activation while suppressing undesired cracking reactions. Broader adoption of propane dehydrogenation also depends on process intensification and reactor engineering advances to address thermodynamic limitations. Ultimately, sustainability considerations around resource utilization and life cycle impacts should inform next generation PDH catalyst and process development.

丙烷脱氢催化剂通过降低断裂丙烷C-H键的活化能垒来产生丙烯。工业PDH工艺利用Pt/Al2O3和CrOx / Al2O3等负载金属催化剂,这些催化剂可选择性地激活C-H键而不是C-C键[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008,10.1021 / acscatal.3c00939]。理论计算已经阐明了反应机理,显示了金属阳离子位点上的丙烷吸附和氢抽提步骤[10.1021 / acscatal.0c01455,10.1021 / acscatal.2c01374]。另一种催化剂Sn-Beta沸石也可通过结合在框架中的锡原子起到路易斯酸位点促进脱氢反应[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019]。


※※※

综合理解与未来展望:综述研究重点介绍了合成和表征选择性金属基催化剂以降低丙烷脱氢反应中的活化能垒方面的进展。进一步的工作可能会研究以丰富的地壳金属取代稀有的铂族金属,以及利用计算建模指导的催化剂理性设计来优化C-H激活的活性位点几何形状,同时抑制不desired cracking反应。丙烷脱氢反应的更广泛采用也依赖于工艺强化和反应工程方面的进步,以解决热力学限制。 最终,资源利用和生命周期影响的可持续性考虑因素应该指导下一代PDH催化剂和工艺的开发。
