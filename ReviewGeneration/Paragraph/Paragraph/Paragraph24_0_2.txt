Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts require optimized active phase dispersion to maximize activity and selectivity. The reviewed article investigates catalyst preparation methods aimed at achieving high dispersion of the active Co species in Co@Si-BEA catalysts. Two key methods are discussed:

1. Varying cobalt loading (0.1-5 wt%) during vacuum impregnation of the BEA support. Lower Co loadings of 0.1-0.5 wt% resulted in well-dispersed isolated Co2+ sites, leading to the highest intrinsic catalytic activity [Placeholder_Of_DOI1].

2. Dealumination treatment of the BEA support prior to Co impregnation, producing Co@Si-BEA. This led to less graphitic coke deposition compared to standard Co@BEA, likely due to the improved Co dispersion [Placeholder_Of_DOI2]. 

The Co@Si-BEA synthesis optimizations demonstrate how controlling metal loading and support modifications enable optimizing active phase dispersion in these single-site solid acid PDH catalysts. Further work could explore stabilizing the isolated Co2+ sites against aggregation under reaction conditions to improve stability. Generalization of these effects across other metal/zeolite combinations may also provide insight into designing optimized solid acid catalysts through precise control of acid site speciation and distribution.

丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂需要优化活性相分散来最大化活性和选择性。综述文章研究了旨在实现Co@Si-BEA催化剂中活性Co物种高分散的催化剂制备方法。讨论了两种关键方法:

1. 在真空浸渍BEA载体过程中变化钴负载量(0.1-5 wt%)。较低的Co负载量0.1-0.5 wt%导致分散很好的隔离Co2+位点,导致最高的固有催化活性[Placeholder_Of_DOI1]。

2. 在Co浸渍前对BEA载体进行除铝处理,生产Co@Si-BEA。这导致比标准Co@BEA少的石墨化焦炭沉积,可能是由于改进的Co分散[Placeholder_Of_DOI2]。

Co@Si-BEA合成优化证明了如何通过控制金属负载量和支撑修饰来优化这些单位点固体酸PDH催化剂中的活性相分散。进一步的工作可以探索稳定隔离的Co2+位点,以防止聚集反应条件下的稳定性改善。这些效应在其他金属/分子筛组合中的普遍性也可以提供洞察力,通过精确控制酸位规格和分布来设计优化的固体酸催化剂。
