Based on the details extracted from the references, several articles propose mechanistic scenarios for propane dehydrogenation over different metal oxide catalysts. 

Over CrZrOx catalysts, two neighboring coordinatively unsaturated Zr (Zrcus) sites located at an oxygen vacancy are mainly responsible for catalyzing the dehydrogenation of propane to propene [10.1021/acscatal.9b05063]. The Zrcus sites are formed upon reductive treatment of the catalyst. DFT calculations show the presence of Cr helps reduce the energy barrier for breaking the methyl C-H bond compared to bare ZrO2.

For Zn/MFI zeolites, the mechanism involves propane dissociative adsorption over zinc oxide species to form propyl species. Then propene evolves via dehydrogenation along with recombinative desorption of hydrogen [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.037]. Propene can further oligomerize or participate in other side reactions.  

Over alumina catalysts with oxygen vacancies, propane dehydrogenation can occur through concerted or stepwise pathways involving Alcu-O defect pairs that tune energies of key steps like CH activation and H2 formation [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033].

Several articles describe Mars-van Krevelen type mechanisms where lattice oxygen participates in hydrogen abstraction from propane, leaving reduced metal sites that get reoxidized by gas phase O2. This mechanism has been proposed for supported VOx [10.1006/jcat.1999.2510, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023], MoVO mixed oxides [10.1021/jacs.9b09235], and for oxidative dehydrogenation pathways in general [10.1021/acscatal.2c01374].

Other proposed pathways include heterolytic dissociation over gallium oxides involving dissociative adsorption and decomposition steps [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001, 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017], redox cycles for Cr oxides [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999], and propane activation over isolated metal cation sites like Ga+ [10.1021/jacs.2c03941] and Zr cus [10.1002/anie.201508731].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The current mechanistic understanding reveals some common themes but also distinct pathways over different metal oxide catalyst families. While lattice oxygen and redox properties are often involved, the specific nature and proximity of sites matters significantly. More atomic-level studies can reveal detailed conceptual models to guide design of tailored sites for selective propane activation. Operando methodology holds promise to track dynamic structural changes during reactions. Knowledge gained may stimulate explorations into novel multifunctional materials with tunable redox centres and acid-base pairs for efficient dehydrogenation.

根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,几篇文章提出了丙烷在不同金属氧化物催化剂上的脱氢机理。

在CrZrOx催化剂上,两个位于氧空位的相邻配位不饱和锆(Zrcus)位点主要负责催化丙烷到丙烯的脱氢[10.1021/acscatal.9b05063]。Zrcus位点是在催化剂的还原处理过程中形成的。DFT计算表明,Cr的存在有助于降低断裂甲基C-H键的能垒,相比无掺杂ZrO2。

对于Zn/MFI分子筛,该机理涉及丙烷在氧化锌物种上的解离吸附形成丙基物种。然后丙烯通过脱氢进化以及重组脱附的氢气[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.037]。丙烯可以进一步寡聚或参与其他副反应。

在含氧空位的氧化铝催化剂上,丙烷可以通过协同或步移途径的Alcu-O缺陷对发生脱氢反应,这调谐了诸如C-H活化和H2形成等关键步骤的能量[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033]。

几篇文章描述了Mars-Van Krevelen类机理,其中晶格氧参与从丙烷中提取氢,留下还原的金属位点,然后这些位点在气相O2的氧化下被再氧化。这种机制已被提出用于supported VOx [10.1006/jcat.1999.2510, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023], MoVO混合氧化物[10.1021/jacs.9b09235],以及用于一般氧化脱氢途径[10.1021/acscatal.2c01374]。

其他提出的途径包括镓氧化物上的杂解裂解,涉及解离吸附和分解步骤[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001, 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017],Cr氧化物的氧化还原循环[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999],以及丙烷在Ga+ [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]和Zr cus [10.1002/anie.201508731]等隔离金属阳离子位点上的活化。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:目前的机理理解揭示了一些共性,但在不同金属氧化物催化剂族之间也有明显不同的途径。虽然晶格氧和氧化还原性质通常都涉及在内,但位点的特定性质和紧密程度则非常重要。更多原子水平的研究可以揭示详细的概念模型,以指导定制位点的设计选择性丙烷活化。本位研究方法有望跟踪反应过程中的动态结构变化。获得的知识可能会激发对新型多功能材料的探索,这些材料具有可调节的氧化还原中心和酸碱成对体,实现高效脱氢。
