Based on the details extracted from the references, different mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over various metal oxide catalysts. 

Over CrZrOx catalysts, two neighboring coordinatively unsaturated Zr (Zrcus) sites located at an oxygen vacancy are proposed as the active sites responsible for breaking C-H bonds in propane homolytically [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012]. The presence of Cr facilitates the subsequent methyl C-H bond activation in the resulting isopropyl intermediate compared to bare ZrO2. 

ZnZrxOy catalysts are suggested to catalyze propane dehydrogenation in a synergistic manner, with isolated tricoordinated Zn2+ sites responsible for the first dehydrogenation step and neighboring Zrcus sites catalyzing the second step [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]. This conclusion is based on DFT calculations of optimized reaction intermediates.

Several articles describe a Mars-van Krevelen type mechanism for oxidative dehydrogenation over VOx [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023, DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1999.2510] and MoVO [DOI: 10.1021/jacs.9b09235] catalysts. This involves lattice oxygen abstracting hydrogen from propane, leaving oxygen vacancies that are replenished by gas phase O2. As lattice oxygen is depleted, V3+ sites can catalyze subsequent non-oxidative dehydrogenation steps.

Over Ga/zeolite catalysts, monovalent Ga+ sites as well as Ga+ paired with Brønsted acid sites are identified as active for propane dehydrogenation through the formation of alkyl intermediates. Key steps involve hydride abstraction and elimination of propylene [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c01497].

A heterolytic dissociation mechanism is suggested for dehydrogenation over supported Ga2O3 catalysts [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017], pure polymorphic Ga2O3 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001], and Cr2O3 under oxidative conditions [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]. This pathway is preferred over a redox route for Ga oxides.

The specific nature of proposed active sites and mechanistic details vary across different metal oxide catalysts and reaction conditions. But key aspects include the involvement of coordinatively unsaturated metal sites, lattice oxygen, and Mars-van Krevelen or heterolytic dissociation pathways.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The current mechanistic understanding of propane dehydrogenation over metal oxide catalysts provides crucial insights that can guide optimization of these materials. For instance, creating coordinatively unsaturated cationic sites and engineering lattice oxygen reactivity seem to be recurrent themes. However, most of the proposed mechanistic scenarios rely heavily on model studies, simulations and indirect evidence. A persistent knowledge gap exists in directly observing and quantifying the nature of active sites and confirmation of specific reaction intermediates under working conditions. Operando spectroscopic techniques combined with isotope labeling have shown some promises in this direction. But further advancements in such characterization tools as well as synergies with computational modeling will be essential to attain a multifaceted perspective. Expanding the classes of metal oxides investigated and incorporating emerging concepts like single atom catalysis and confinement effects also provide untapped opportunities to develop original mechanistic hypotheses. Constructing a hierarchical understanding spanning multiple length and time scales - from electronic structure factors to crystallite interfaces and reactor gradients - remains an outstanding challenge.

根据提取的参考文献的详细信息,已经为各种金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢提出了不同的机理场景。

在CrZrOx催化剂上,位于氧空位的两个相邻的无配位Zr (Zrcus)位点被提议作为负责均裂丙烷中的C-H键的活性位点[DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012]。Cr的存在有助于相比于ZrO2促进结果异丙基中间体中随后的甲基C-H键的活化。

根据DFT计算的优化反应中间体,ZnZrxOy催化剂被认为以协同方式催化丙烷脱氢反应,孤立的三配位Zn2+位点负责第一次脱氢步骤,相邻的Zrcus位点催化第二步[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]。 

几篇文章描述了氧化性脱氢反应在VOx [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.01.023, DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1999.2510]和MoVO [DOI: 10.1021/jacs.9b09235]催化剂上的Mars-van Krevelen类型机理。这涉及晶格氧从丙烷中提取氢原子,留下氧空位,氧空位由气相O2补充。随着晶格氧的耗尽,V3+位点可以催化随后的非氧化脱氢步骤。

在分子筛supported Ga催化剂上,单价Ga+位点以及Ga+与Brønsted酸位点配对的位点被确定为通过烷基中间体的形成对丙烷脱氢反应是活跃的。关键步骤涉及氢化物的提取和丙烯的脱除[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c01497]。

不均裂的解离机理被认为是supported Ga2O3催化剂 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017]、纯多晶Ga2O3 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]和Cr2O3氧化条件下 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]的优先脱氢途径。这种途径优于Ga氧化物的氧化-还原途径。

针对不同的金属氧化物催化剂和反应条件,被提出的活性位点和机理细节的具体性质有所不同。 但关键方面包括无配位饱和金属位点、晶格氧以及Mars-van Krevelen或不均裂解离途径的参与。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 当前对金属氧化物催化剂上丙烷脱氢反应机理的理解为优化这些材料提供了关键的启示。 例如,创建无配位饱和的阳离子位点和工程化晶格氧的反应活性似乎是反复出现的主题。 然而,大多数拟议的机理场景严重依赖于模型研究、模拟和间接证据。 直接观察和定量活性位点的性质以及在工作条件下确认特定反应中间体的知识差距仍然存在。 同位素标记下的原位光谱技术显示了一些前景。 但先进的表征工具以及与计算建模的协同作用对获得多层面的视角至关重要。 扩大调查的金属氧化物类别和结合新兴概念如单原子催化和局限效应也提供了未开发的机会来发展原始的机理假说。 构建跨越多个长度和时间尺度的层次理解 - 从电子结构因素到晶体界面的反应器渐变 - 仍然是一个杰出的挑战。
