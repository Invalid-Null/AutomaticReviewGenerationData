 <English>

Propane dehydrogenation catalysis has received significant attention from computational chemists in recent years, particularly via the application of density functional theory (DFT) calculations. Such theoretical methods have facilitated fundamental insights into reaction mechanisms, pathways, structure-activity relationships, and microkinetic factors that govern selectivity and deactivation phenomena [1].   

One interesting revelation has been that Zn-O-Zn sites on catalytic material surfaces can catalyze dehydrogenation with lower energy barriers relative to Zn2+ sites, as the metal-oxygen-metal configuration facilitates dehydrogenation of the hydrocarbon substrate [2]. These computational insights correlated well with experimental observations and helped explain the favorable catalytic role of multimetallic zinc sites [2]. Based on the energies and electronic structures derived from DFT results, mathematical descriptors have also been developed to evaluate selectivity trends and the relative rates of desired dehydrogenation vs unwanted deep dehydrogenation [3].

Periodic DFT calculations with plane-wave basis sets have proved fruitful for screening and microkinetic analyses of propane dehydrogenation on transition metal catalysts [4]. This theoretical toolbox identifies crucial elementary steps and highlights reactivity-determining surface structures and intermediates. Similarly, landmark DFT explorations into site-specific factors on platinum and platinum alloy surfaces uncovered the dominant kinetic roles of propylene C-H activation and propyne C-C bond breaking in governing the selectivity of the industrially important dehydrogenation process [3].

More recently, DFT studies have revealed encouraging propane dehydrogenation ability on unconventional single-atom catalysts while retaining stability against coking [5]. Such catalysts exhibit dehydrogenation preference for a radical pathway rather than dissociative adsorption [5]. The tunability afforded by graphene and boron nitride substrates may permit precise adjustments to the reactivity of the noble metal single-sites via inorganic dopants [6]. These emerging perspectives from computational chemistry continue to uncover innovative catalyst paradigms and reaction principles for selective propane dehydrogenation.

※※※

Propane dehydrogenation has gained major industrial importance, with widespread selective conversion sought for petrochemical production. As this brief Synopsis highlights, computational approaches enrich the scientific understanding of the underlying reaction steps, influential parameters, and catalytic phenomena at play. Yet there remain complex challenges in translating theoretical models and isolated simplifications of salient factors into useful, predictive catalyst design principles. An opportunity thus lies in bridging the inherent divide between DFT-level electronic structure details and the chaotic intricacies of real-world operation at scale. Hence future efforts might integrate enhanced multi-scale computations with kinetic experiments and operando spectroscopy to capture realistic formulations, transport resistances, coking vulnerabilities, and time-dependent transformations. The ultimate fruition would be the rational, computationally accelerated development of commercially viable catalyst materials for continuous high-yield dehydrogenation of alkanes to olefins.

<Chinese>

近年来,丙烷脱氢催化剂受到了计算化学家的大量关注,特别是通过应用密度泛函理论(DFT)计算。这种理论方法为反应机理、途径、结构-活性关系以及控制选择性和失活现象的微观动力学因素提供了基本的洞见[1]。  

一个有趣的发现是Zn-O-Zn位点上的催化剂表面可以以比Zn2+位点低的能垒催化脱氢,因为金属-氧-金属配置有利于烃基底的脱氢[2]。这些计算洞见与实验观察相关良好,并有助于解释多金属锌位点的有利催化作用[2]。根据从DFT结果获得的能量和电子结构,还开发了数学描述符来评估选择性趋势和所需脱氢与不需要的深层脱氢的相对速率[3]。

使用平面波基组的周期DFT计算证明在过渡金属催化剂上丙烷脱氢和微观动力学分析是很有成果的[4]。这种理论工具识别了关键的基元步骤,并强调了确定反应性的表面结构和中间体。类似地,对铂和铂合金表面的位点特异性因素的突破性DFT探索发现,丙烯C-H活化和丙炔C-C键断裂在控制工业上重要的脱氢过程的选择性方面发挥主导作用[3]。

最近,DFT研究已经显示出非常规单原子催化剂的有希望的丙烷脱氢能力,同时保持对结焦的稳定性[5]。这样的催化剂表现出优先的自由基途径而不是解离吸附的脱氢偏好[5]。石墨烯和氮化硼基板的可调性可以通过无机掺杂来准确调整贵金属单位点的反应性[6]。这些来自计算化学的新兴视角不断发现创新型催化剂范式和选择性丙烷脱氢的反应原理。  

※※※

丙烷脱氢在石化生产中具有重要的工业意义,人们普遍寻求选择性转化。正如这篇简要综述所强调的,计算方法丰富了对潜在反应步骤、有影响的参数以及催化过程的科学理解。然而,将理论模型和对突出因素的分离简化转化为有用的、可预测的催化剂设计原则仍存在复杂的挑战。因此,一个机会就在于弥合DFT电子结构细节与大规模实际操作的混沌复杂性之间的固有鸿沟。因此,未来的努力可能将增强的多尺度计算与动力学实验和原位光谱相结合,以捕捉现实的配方、传输阻力、结焦脆弱性和时间依赖的转化。最终的成果将是合理的、计算加速的商业可行催化剂材料的连续高产烷烃到烯烃的脱氢。
