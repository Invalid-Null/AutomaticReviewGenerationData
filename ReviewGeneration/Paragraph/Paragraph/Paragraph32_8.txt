Based on the details extracted from the files, several articles highlight remaining challenges and knowledge gaps in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysis research. 

One major challenge emphasized is the need to develop alternative catalysts to replace expensive platinum-based industrial catalysts, which is noted as a long-term goal for improving PDH processes [10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1]. Related to this, exploring highly stable and selective non-precious metal catalysts, potentially leveraging single atom and confined cluster architectures, is identified as an area needing further work [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331].

Additionally, there is limited definitive understanding of the identity and structure of highly active sites in promising catalysts like gallium-exchanged zeolites [10.1021/acscatal.1c05724]. Improving characterization techniques to probe proposed active gallium species at real-world low loadings remains an open challenge.

Other persisting issues include unsatisfactory catalyst stability due to coke formation and thermal deactivation [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005], side reactions at higher temperatures [10.1021/cr5002436], and the inability to overcome equilibrium limitations on conversion [10.1021/cr5002436]. Tackling these limitations to enhance performance is an ongoing research need.

From a methods perspective, leveraging computational modeling and screening to explore catalytic mechanisms involving proposed active sites is put forward as a promising approach [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. Overall, despite decades of PDH catalysis research, developing improved catalysts that are highly efficient, stable over long periods, energy efficient, and based on abundant metals remains an elusive goal necessitating sustained efforts.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and persisting challenges delineated in the literature, we discern several frontiers holding promise for catalyzing innovations in PDH catalysis - 

(1) Inventive nanoscale designs capacitating site isolation and confinement of selective active centers could circumvent limitations of thermal stability and coking. Architectures worth probing include single atom catalysts with optimized coordination environments, sub-nanometer clusters stabilized in porous frameworks, and strain-modified zeolites and MOFs tailored to engender active configurations.

(2) Operando spectroscopy and in situ microscopy methods that enable characterization under actual reaction environments, combined with selective isotopic labeling and other emerging techniques, may conclusively unravel ambiguous active site identities still confounding mechanistic comprehension. 

(3) Cross-pollinative adoption of quantum machine learning, metaheuristics optimization, and statistical mechanics simulations can systematically predict highly selective sites and superior catalytic motifs inexpensively, guiding experimental validations more astutely. 

Purposeful research aligned with these horizons can usher efficacious and bold catalyst designs previously inconceivable, actualizing the vision of economically viable and sustainable PDH processes through transformative chemical innovations.

根据从文件中提取的详细信息,几篇文章强调丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂研究中存在的挑战和知识空白。

一个主要挑战是需要开发替代催化剂来取代昂贵的铂基工业催化剂,这被视为改进PDH工艺的长期目标[10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1]。相关的是,探索高度稳定和选择性的非贵金属催化剂,可能利用单原子和限域簇体系结构,被确定为需要进一步工作的领域[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331]。

此外,对于一些有前途的催化剂如加仑交换沸石,高活性位点的身份和结构的确定性理解十分有限[10.1021/acscatal.1c05724]。提高表征技术以在实际低负载条件下探测拟议的活性锗种类仍是一个未解决的挑战。


其他持续存在的问题还包括由焦炭形成和热失活引起的催化剂稳定性不满意[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103, 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005],在更高温度下发生的副反应[10.1021/cr5002436],以及无法克服转化平衡限制 [10.1021/cr5002436]。解决这些限制以提高性能是一个正在进行的研究需求。


从方法的角度来看,利用计算建模和筛选来探索涉及拟议活性位点的催化机理被提出为一种有前途的方法[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。总体而言,尽管开展了几十年的PDH 催化剂研究,但开发高效、长期高度稳定、节能和基于丰富金属的改进催化剂仍是一个难以捉摸的目标,需要持续的努力。


※※※

综合理解与未来展望:考虑文献中概述的进展和持续挑战,我们认定几个前沿领域有望催生 PDH 催化剂方面的创新——

(1)创造性的纳米尺度设计可以使选择性活性中心实现隔离和限域,可能规避热稳定性和结焦的限制。值得探索的体系结构包括配位环境优化的单原子催化剂、稳定在多孔框架中的亚纳米簇、应变修饰的沸石和金属有机框架等。

(2)工作条件下的原位光谱和显微表征技术,结合选择性同位素标记和其他新兴技术,可以确定性地阐明暧昧的活性位点身份,这些位点目前仍然困扰着机理理解。

(3)从量子机器学习、元启发式优化和统计力学模拟等交叉采用,可以低成本地系统预测高选择性位点和卓越的催化剂图案,更明智地指导实验验证。

围绕这些前沿领域开展目的明确的研究可以催生前所未有的、高效的催化剂设计,通过催化剂转型实现经济可行和可持续的 PDH 工艺的愿景。
