Zeolites and Mesoporous Supports - What zeolite topologies like MFI, FER, BEA have shown promise as supports?

The file reveals that MFI, BEA, and CHA zeolite frameworks have demonstrated potential as promising supports for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts. 

Specifically, the purely siliceous MFI zeolite silicalite-1 provides an ideal support to stabilize subnanometric PtSn clusters within its 10R sinusoidal channels, engendering a PDH catalyst with superb performance [10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7]. The microporous confinement offered by the MFI structure likely contributes to this enhanced stability. Dealuminated ferrosilicate MFI zeolites similarly anchor isolated Pt atoms, augmented by Fe incorporation that strengthens the Pt-O bond [10.1021/acscatal.2c05042]. Mesoporous MFI nanosheets likewise stabilize PtZn nanoparticles, eliciting exceptional durability [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808].

For the BEA topology, multifarious cases utilize BEA, either purely siliceous or dealuminated, to localize single Pt sites. Encapsulating Pt atoms within Zn cages inside dealuminated BEA pores engenders a uniquely stable and selective catalyst [10.1021/jacs.1c10261]. The hydroxyl nests on dealuminated BEA zeolites can also be leveraged to pin down bimetallic PtM alloys [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]. Incorporating Pt clusters onto isolated Sn sites in the BEA framework too imparts enhanced thermal stability [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]. The channel structure and intrusive void spaces afforded by BEA clearly assist in anchoring isolated metal sites. 

Additionally, Ga-exchanged CHA zeolites demonstrate encouraging capability for PDH as well, with extra-framework Ga+ constituting the predominant active center [10.1021/jacs.2c03941].

Thus zeolites with MFI, BEA, and CHA topologies have exhibited promising credentials as supports to localize ultrafine metal species for advanced PDH catalysts. Further research into additional frameworks and compositional modifications may uncover supplementary viable supports to push PDH catalyst advancement.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Taking into consideration the advancements and findings discussed in the file, there lies an opportunity to explore emerging zeolite topologies like FER as supports, building upon the success demonstrated thus far by MFI, BEA and CHA structures. The precisely sized cages, channels and intersections characteristic of zeolite frameworks allow for exquisite control in localizing desired active site motifs. Hence continued research into novel zeolite compositions and topologies may unravel additional productive supports that provide optimal confinement environments. Investigation into mesoporous zeolites can also overcome micropore diffusional limitations while retaining advantages like metal-support interactions. Progress in these research trajectories could enable enhanced stabilization of isolated metal sites, permitting augmented catalyst stability, activity and selectivity.

分子筛和介孔支持——像MFI,FER,BEA这样的分子筛拓扑结构展现出什么样的支持应用前景?

文件透露,MFI、BEA和CHA分子筛框架已展现出巨大的潜力,可作为丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂的有前景的支持体。

具体而言,纯二氧化硅MFI分子筛硅英石-1可以为其10R正弦波通道内的亚纳米PtSn簇提供理想的支持,使PDH催化剂表现超群[10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7]。MFI结构提供的微孔约束环境可能有助于这种增强的稳定性。类似地,去除铝的铁硅酸盐MFI分子筛也可以固定隔离的Pt原子,其稳定性通过掺入Fe而得到加强[10.1021/acscatal.2c05042]。介孔MFI纳米片也可稳定PtZn纳米颗粒,引发卓越的稳定性[10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]。

对于BEA拓扑,许多案例利用了BEA分子筛,无论是纯的二氧化硅的还是去铝的,来定位单一Pt位点。将Pt原子封装在去铝BEA分子筛孔内的Zn笼中,可以形成一个独特的、稳定的和选择性好的催化剂[10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。去铝BEA分子筛上的羟基巢也可用于固定双金属PtM合金[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]。将Pt簇稳定在BEA分子筛框架上隔离的Sn位点上,也可提高其热稳定性[10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]。BEA分子筛框架所具有的通道结构和侵入性空隙显然有助于锚定隔离的金属位点。

此外,Ga-交换的CHA沸石也显示了很有前景的PDH催化能力,其中Ga+作为主要的活性中心[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]。

因此,具有MFI、BEA和CHA拓扑结构的分子筛已展现出作为支持体来定位超细金属种的有前景的支持体的证据。进一步研究其他分子筛的不同框架和组成调控可能会发现其他新型的可行支持体,以推动PDH催化剂的进一步发展。

※※※  

综合理解与未来展望:考虑到文件中讨论的进展和发现,我们有机会去探索像FER这样的新兴分子筛拓扑作为支持体,在MFI、BEA和CHA结构所展现的成功基础上进行拓展。分子筛框架所特有的大小精确的笼子,通道和交叉都允许精细定位所需的活性位点基团。因此,继续研究新型分子筛的组成和拓扑可能会发现额外的生产性支持体,为优化限域环境提供最佳条件。调查介孔分子筛也可以克服微孔扩散限制,同时保留诸如金属-载体相互作用等优点。这些研究轨迹的进展可能会使隔离金属位点的稳定性得到增强,从而允许提高催化剂的稳定性、活性和选择性。
