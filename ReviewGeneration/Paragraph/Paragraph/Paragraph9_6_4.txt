Based on the details provided in the file, several studies investigate how tuning oxidation states, oxygen mobility, and acid-base properties of metal oxides affects propane dehydrogenation performance. 

For vanadium oxide catalysts, partly reduced V3+/V4+ species have been identified as the active sites for propane dehydrogenation without oxygen introduction [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]. The chemical states of bridge oxygen play critical roles, with weaker VOx-support interactions leading to lower electron density on bridge oxygen and enhanced capability to activate the C-H bond. Introducing CO2 facilitates propane activation by re-oxidizing VOx to generate more active lattice oxygen species.

On alumina-supported chromium oxide catalysts, tuning the oxidation state of Cr is shown to dramatically influence activity and selectivity [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]. Reduction generates Cr3+ and Cr2+ sites which are the active sites for propane dehydrogenation. The dispersion and evolution of these partially reduced species with Cr loading determines catalytic performance. Maintaining the Cr3+/Cr2+ redox cycle via alternating redox treatments is essential for sustained high yield.

For gallium oxide, the polymorph β-Ga2O3 exhibits much higher activity than α-Ga2O3, attributed to a higher ratio of tetrahedral GaIV sites in the bulk structure [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]. Tuning the acidity also affects performance, with β-Ga2O3 showing more weak Lewis acid sites compared to γ-Ga2O3. Acidity correlates strongly with propane dehydrogenation activity over the Ga2O3 polymorphs.

Additionally, altering properties like Pt dispersion, metal-support interactions, oxygen mobility of the support, and acid/base site density has been demonstrated to dramatically impact conversion, selectivity, and stability for supported metal catalysts like Pt, Ru, Rh and bimetallics [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0001, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01580].

根据文件提供的详细信息,几项研究调查了调控金属氧化物的氧化态、氧迁移性和酸碱性如何影响丙烷脱氢反应性能。

对于钒氧化物催化剂,部分还原的V3+/V4+物种已被证明是无氧参与丙烷脱氢的活性位点 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]。桥氧的化学态起关键作用,VOx与支撑体的相互作用越弱,桥氧的电子密度越低,激活C-H键的能力越强。引入CO2有助于通过再氧化VOx生成更多活泼的晶格氧物种来激活丙烷。 

在氧化铝支撑的铬氧化物催化剂上,调控Cr的氧化态对活性和选择性的影响非常大 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]。还原生成Cr3+和Cr2+位点,这些位点是丙烷脱氢的活性位点。这些部分还原物种的分散和Cr负载量的演变决定了催化性能。通过交替的氧化-还原处理维持Cr3+/Cr2+红氧循环对于保持高收率至关重要。

对于氧化镓,β-Ga2O3的活性远高于α-Ga2O3,这是由于β-Ga2O3晶体结构中四面体配位的Ga位点的比例更高所致 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]。调节酸度也会影响性能,β-Ga2O3包含比γ-Ga2O3更多的弱路易斯酸位点。Ga2O3多形体上的丙烷脱氢活性与酸度有很强的相关性。 

此外,改变诸如Pt分散度、金属与支撑体的相互作用、支撑体的氧迁移性以及酸/碱位点的密度等性质已被证明会极大地影响Pt、Ru、Rh以及双金属支撑催化剂的转化率、选择性和稳定性 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0001, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01580]。
