		The article with DOI 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878 explores metal-support interfacial effects in VOx catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). It systematically investigates the effects of different oxide supports on PDH activity over VOx catalysts, finding VOx/ZrO2 to be 4 times more active [Placeholder_Of_DOI1]. Density functional theory calculations reveal this is due to weaker VOx-ZrO2 interactions leading to lower electron density on bridge oxygen atoms, enhancing their capability to rupture C-H bonds in propane. The principles established aim to help tune the VO active site microenvironment.
		
		Additionally, the article with DOI 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726 shows that dealumination of BEA zeolite support generates silanol groups that anchor isolated Co2+ cations in the zeolite micropores. These stable Co2+ sites selectively activate C-H bonds over C-C bonds in propane, leading to high propylene selectivity [Placeholder_Of_DOI2].
		
		Furthermore, the study with DOI 10.1021/acscatal.5b01679 finds that adding Na+ promotes more reactive SiONa+ sites on silica support, enhancing 2D vanadia dispersion to levels of other supports. This enhances activity while maintaining selectivity [Placeholder_Of_DOI3].

Key parameters identified include support oxygen mobility, metal-support bond strength and support surface chemistry.
		
		Building on these advancements, future efforts might focus on:

		DOI 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878的文章探究了VOx催化剂中金属支撑界面效应对丙烷脱氢的影响。它系统地研究了不同氧化物支撑物对VOx催化剂丙烷脱氢活性的影响,发现VOx/ZrO2的活性是其他样品的4倍[Placeholder_Of_DOI1]。第一性原理计算揭示,这是由于VOx-ZrO2相互作用较弱,导致桥氧原子上的电子密度较低,从而增强了它们断裂丙烷中C-H键的能力。建立的原理旨在帮助调谐VO活性位点的微环境。
		
		此外,DOI 10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726的文章表明,BEA分子筛支撑物的除铝化会产生硅醇基团,固定Co2+阳离子在分子筛的微孔内。这些稳定的Co2+位点选择性地活化丙烷中的C-H键而不是C-C键,导致高丙烯选择性[Placeholder_Of_DOI2]。
		
		而且,DOI 10.1021/acscatal.5b01679的研究发现,在二氧化硅载体上添加Na+促进了更具反应活性的SiONa+位点的形成,提高了钒的二维分散度至其他载体的水平。这增强了活性同时保持选择性[Placeholder_Of_DOI3]。

		综合理解与前景展望:讨论的发现表明,精心设计金属-支撑体界面可以有效调控活性位点的电子密度、稳定性和分散性,从而调谐丙烷脱氢反应的性能。确定的关键参数包括支撑体氧迁移性、金属-支撑体键强度和支撑体表面化学性质。
		
		在这些进展的基础上,未来的工作可能会关注:
