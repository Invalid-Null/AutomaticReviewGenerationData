Based on the details extracted from the file, some articles describe techniques for synthesizing isolated single sites in zeolitic supports for propane dehydrogenation catalysts. 

One method is dealumination to generate vacant framework Al sites and hydroxyl nests that can stabilize isolated metal species. For example, Co2+ species were successfully confined into the silanol groups of dealuminated SiBeta zeolite [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]. Isolated Ga+ sites can also be introduced by exchanging Brønsted protons in the zeolite [10.1002/anie.200702463].

Using structure-directing agents or ligands during hydrothermal zeolite synthesis is another technique to coordinate and disperse metal ions while preventing aggregation. Ammonia and ethylenediamine are examples that produced isolated Co2+ in S-1 zeolite and stabilized Zn cations in S-1, respectively [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816].

Chemical vapor deposition (CVD) introduces volatile organometallic precursors like dimethylzinc that get incorporated as isolated metal-methyl species. Subsequent high temperature hydrogen treatment generates isolated Zn2+ sites [10.1021/cs200441e].

Overall, advanced spectroscopic and microscopic methods like XAS, FTIR, and aberration-corrected STEM enable definitive identification of the isolated sites.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The examined work elucidates helpful pathways for designing isolated single-site zeolite catalysts via techniques like dealumination, ligand protection, and CVD. However, widespread adoption necessitates enhancing stability and accessibility of the single sites. Exploring novel zeolite topologies with intrinsically higher hydrothermal resilience could be one future research direction. There are also opportunities to employ computational screening in conjugation with automated synthesis robots to accelerate discovery of optimized sites, ligands, and frameworks tailored for the desired reaction.

一种方法是去铝化以生成空位的框架Al位点和羟基巢,这可以稳定分离的金属物种。例如,Co2+物种成功限制在部分去铝SiBeta沸石的硅醇基团中[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]。也可以通过交换沸石中的Brønsted质子引入分离的Ga+位点[10.1002/anie.200702463]。

在沸石水热合成过程中使用结构引导剂或配体是另一种技术,用于协调和分散金属离子,同时防止聚集。氨和乙二胺是生成S-1沸石中分离Co2+和稳定Zn阳离子的例子[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。

化学气相沉积(CVD)引入挥发性有机金属前体如二甲基锌,它们被并入为分离的金属-甲基物种。随后的高温氢处理生成分离的Zn2+位点[10.1021/cs200441e]。

总体而言,XAS、FTIR和球差校正STEM等先进的光谱学和显微技术可以明确鉴定分离的位点。
