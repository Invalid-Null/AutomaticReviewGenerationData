Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Several studies have evaluated the effect of alloying platinum with metals like tin (Sn), gallium (Ga), and zinc (Zn) for propane dehydrogenation catalysis. Alloying helps modulate the geometric and electronic properties of Pt active sites. 

Pt-Sn alloys are widely investigated and utilized commercially [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018, 10.1038/s41467-022-30522-1]. Adding Sn changes the electron density of Pt sites, improving selectivity towards propylene and stability by suppressing coke formation and hydrogenolysis reactions [10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63395-4, 10.1007/s11244-016-0757-0]. However, excessive Sn can overly dilute Pt ensembles and cause a trade-off between stability and activity [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]. An optimal Sn/Pt ratio around 1-1.5 is suggested [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]. Pt-Sn intermetallic nanoparticles with ordered Pt3Sn arrangements also boast remarkable durability [10.1021/acscatal.7b04123]. 

Incorporating Ga forms highly dispersed Pt-Ga sites that resist sintering and fouling [10.1002/anie.202107210, 10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129]. Electron donation from Ga to Pt facilitates selective C-H cleavage [10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084]. Stabilization of sub-nm Pt entities using [GaO4] units further unlocks previously unattainable stability [10.1021/jacs.8b05378].

Pt-Zn catalysts also achieve exceptional durability owing to very small, isolated Pt sites decorated by Zn [10.1021/jacs.1c10261]. The Zn isolates Pt, impedes agglomeration, and tunes geometric and electronic properties [10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]. DFT models indicate Zn weakens propane adsorption strength, improving propylene selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]. 

In summary, strategic incorporation of Sn, Ga, or Zn alters the Pt ensemble configuration and ligand environment synergistically. This modulation governs selectivity patterns, lifetimes, and turnover capabilities. While tradeoffs between activity and resilience manifest at times, breakthroughs in synthetic techniques to isolate ultra-stable alloy sites promise further advancements.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The detailed investigations compiled in this file offer valuable insights into how introducing secondary metals like Sn, Ga and Zn can transform the performance of Pt catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. Key mechanisms like electronic structure alterations, geometric isolation of Pt sites, and mitigation of deactivation pathways are elucidated. 

Building on these fundamental understandings, future explorations might focus on extending this modification approach to other promising bimetallic and intermetallic combinations. New spectroscopic, microscopic and computational methodologies could also help achieve greater precision in synthesizing and probing optimized alloy configurations. Translating ultra-stable single site catalysts from academic studies to large-scale implementation remains an open challenge. Overall, strategic alloying founded on mechanistic comprehension provides a fertile platform for designing next-generation propane dehydrogenation systems.

双金属和合金催化剂 - Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

几项研究评估了将铂与类似锡(Sn)、镓(Ga)和锌(Zn)合金化用于丙烷脱氢催化的效果。 合金化有助于调控Pt活性位点的几何和电子性质。

Pt-Sn合金广泛调查和商业利用[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018,10.1038/s41467-022-30522-1]。 添加Sn改变了Pt位点的电子密度,通过抑制焦炭形成和加氢裂解反应改善了丙烯选择性和稳定性[10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63395-4,10.1007/s11244-016-0757-0]。 然而,过量的Sn会过度稀释Pt配合位,在稳定性和活性之间造成权衡[10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。 建议Sn/Pt比约为1-1.5最佳[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]。 Pt-Sn金属间化合物纳米颗粒与有序Pt3Sn排列也拥有卓越的耐久性[10.1021/ acscatal.7b04123]。

掺入Ga形成高度分散的Pt-Ga位点,抵抗烧结和污染[10.1002/anie.202107210,10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129]。 Ga对Pt的电子供体有助于选择性C-H键裂解[10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084]。 使用[GaO4]单元稳定亚纳米Pt实体,进一步释放以前难以达到的稳定性[10.1021/jacs.8b05378]。

Pt-Zn催化剂也由于非常小的、被Zn隔离的Pt位点而获得了异常的稳定性[10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。 Zn隔离Pt,抑制团聚,并调谐几何和电子性质[10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]。 DFT模型表明Zn削弱丙烷吸附强度,提高丙烯选择性[10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]。

总之,Sn、Ga或Zn的战略掺入协同地改变了Pt配位构型和配体环境。这种调节控制了选择性模式、使用寿命和周转能力。尽管在某些时候会出现活性和韧性之间的权衡,但在合成技术方面的突破为隔离超稳定的合金位点提供了进一步的改进空间。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:本文件汇编的详细研究对Sn、Ga和Zn等第二金属引入如何改变丙烷脱氢催化剂Pt的性能提供了宝贵的洞见。阐明了关键机制,如电子结构改变、Pt位点的几何隔离以及减缓失活途径。

在这些基础理解的基础上,未来的探索可能会关注将这种改性方法扩展到其他有前景的双金属和金属间化合物。新型光谱、显微和计算方法也有助于在合成和探测优化合金配置方面实现更高的精度。 将超稳定的单一位点催化剂从学术研究转化为大规模实施仍然是一个悬而未决的挑战。 总的来说,以机制理解为基础的战略合金化为设计下一代丙烷脱氢系统提供了肥沃的平台。
