**Metal Oxide Catalysts - What metal oxides like Cr2O3, Ga2O3 have shown promising propane dehydrogenation activity?**

Based on the details extracted from the file, some metal oxide catalysts that have demonstrated promising propane dehydrogenation (PDH) activity include:

- Chromium oxide (Cr2O3): Cr-based catalysts are reported to be among the most active catalysts for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane [\[10.1021/acscatal.2c05286\]\]. Mesoporous silica supported Cr oxide exhibits higher PDH activity compared to other metal oxides like Ga, Ni, V, Fe, Mn, and Co [\[10.1021/acscatal.2c05286\]\]. Reduced α-Cr2O3(0001) surfaces have also been computationally investigated for non-oxidative PDH using kinetic Monte Carlo simulations [\[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814\]\].

- Gallium oxide (Ga2O3): Among different polymorphs of Ga2O3 tested for PDH, β-Ga2O3 showed the highest initial activity correlating with its surface acid site density [\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999\], \[10.1021/acscatal.0c05009\]\]. Ga2O3/SiO2 exhibited comparable or higher C3H6 yield than Fe2O3 and Cr2O3/SiO2 \[\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999\]\].

- Platinum-tin oxide (Pt-Sn): PtSn catalysts modified with promoters have been commercialized for PDH. PtSn/SBA-15 synthesized by coimpregnation demonstrated 40% propane conversion and 92% propylene selectivity attributed to geometric and electronic effects \[\[10.1021/acscatal.1c00639\]\]. PtSn clusters stabilized on isolated Sn sites in zeolite Beta gave 50% conversion and 99% selectivity \[\[10.1021/acscatal.9b03527\]\].

However, deactivation via coking and poor hydrothermal stability remain key challenges with metal oxide catalysts that need further improvement.

※※※ 

**Integrative Understanding and Future Outlook**

The findings reveal Cr, Ga, and PtSn based metal oxides as state-of-the-art catalysts for commercializing PDH technology. While significant advances have been made, unraveling the intricate structure-activity relationships and reaction mechanisms governing these complex oxides presents an open frontier. 

Elucidating the nature of active sites, kinetic bottlenecks and deactivation pathways through advanced in-situ spectroscopy and theoretical simulations will provide vital insights to design enhanced next-generation catalysts. Incorporating these oxides into dynamic flow systems that prevent coking could enable breakthroughs. 

Expanding the search space to undiscovered compositional spaces using high-throughput methods may uncover revolutionary new metal oxide chemistries for PDH. Machine learning models trained on accumulating data could accelerate this discovery. Finally, scaling up lab-scale methods while retaining catalyst performance remains an engineering challenge. 

Overall, strategic fundamental and applied research at the intersection of characterization techniques, reactor engineering and computational methods will accelerate the actualization of selective and robust metal oxide catalysts—ushering PDH technology into widespread large-scale implementation.

**金属氧化物催化剂——Cr2O3、Ga2O3等金属氧化物催化剂在丙烷脱氢反应中表现出色**

根据文件提取的详细信息,一些在丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应中表现出色的金属氧化物催化剂包括:

- 铬氧化物(Cr2O3):文献报道,Cr基催化剂是CO2辅助氧化脱氢丙烷反应中最活性的催化剂之一[\[10.1021/acscatal.2c05286\]]。硅酸盐支持的Cr氧化物催化剂比Ni、V、Fe、Mn和Co等其他金属氧化物表现更好[\[10.1021/acscatal.2c05286\]]。还使用动力学Monte Carlo模拟计算研究了还原α-Cr2O3(0001)表面催化非氧化脱氢丙烷的性能[\[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814\]]。


- 镓氧化物(Ga2O3):在测试过的不同Ga2O3多晶体中,β-Ga2O3的初始活性最高,与其表面酸中心的密度有关[\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999\], \[10.1021/acscatal.0c05009\]]。Ga2O3/SiO2的C3H6产率比Fe2O3 和Cr2O3/SiO2高[\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999\]]。

- 铂锡氧化物(Pt-Sn):添加了助剂的PtSn催化剂已经商业化用于PDH。共浸渍合成的PtSn/SBA-15催化剂的丙烷转化率为40%,丙烯选择性达92%,归因于几何和电子效应[\[10.1021/acscatal.1c00639\]]。PtSn簇稳定在分子筛Beta中的隔离Sn位点上,丙烷转化率可达50%,选择性大于99%[\[10.1021/acscatal.9b03527\]]。


然而,金属氧化物催化剂依然面临着结烃和水热稳定性差等关键挑战,需要进一步改进。

※※※

**整体理解与展望**

研究结果揭示了Cr、Ga和PtSn基金属氧化物是实现PDH商业化的最新催化剂。尽管已取得重大进展,但揭开调控这些复杂氧化物的精细结构-性能关系和反应机制仍是一个新的研究前沿。

使用先进的原位表征和理论模拟来阐明活性位点、动力学瓶颈和失活途径的本质,将提供关键性启示以设计增强的新一代催化剂。将这些氧化物掺入可以防止结焦的动态流化床反应器中可能取得突破。

使用高通量方法扩大尚未发现的组分空间的搜索范围,可能会发现划时代的新型PDH金属氧化物化学体系。训练在积累数据上获得经验的机器学习模型可以加速这种发现。最后,在保持催化剂性能的同时放大实验室级方法至大规模仍是一个工程挑战。

总的来说,在表征技术、反应工程和计算方法交叉融合的战略基础和应用研究的推动下,选择性强和稳定性金属氧化物催化剂的实现将加速PDH技术向大规模应用的推广。
