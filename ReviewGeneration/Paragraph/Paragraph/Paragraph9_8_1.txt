Based on the details extracted from the references, there is evidence showing that tuning oxidation states, oxygen mobility, and acid-base properties of metal oxides can affect propane dehydrogenation performance. 

Specifically, creating oxygen vacancies on metal oxides like alumina reduces metal coordination and generates active sites like Alcu-O acid-base pairs, which facilitate propane activation by tuning adsorption energies and reaction barriers [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033]. The chemical states and mobility of lattice oxygen also play critical roles, with weaker metal-oxygen bonds correlated with higher propane conversion [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01580]. 

Furthermore, the acid strength and basic site density of supports directly impact selectivity, as evidenced by increased propylene yields for ZrO2-based catalysts with tuned Zr content that modifies surface acid-base properties [DOI: 10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074]. Dispersion and isolation of active metal sites like Cr and V species through interactions with the support additionally prevent over-reduction and minimize secondary reactions leading to coke formation [DOI: 10.1016/j.apcatb.2020.119089, 10.1021/jacs.9b09235].

Overall, advances in characterization techniques have enabled precise control over metal oxidation states, oxygen defects, metal-support interfaces, and acid-base properties, paving the way for rational design of optimized propane dehydrogenation catalysts. Further research might focus on multi-functional single atom alloy catalysts with balanced redox properties to push propane conversion and propylene selectivity limits.

根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,有证据表明调节金属氧化物的氧化态、氧活性和酸碱性能影响丙烷脱氢反应的性能。

具体而言,在氧化铝等金属氧化物上创建氧空位会降低金属的配位数,并生成像Alcu-O酸碱成对等活性位点,这会通过调节吸附能和反应屏障来促进丙烷的活化[DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033]。 晶格氧的化学状态和活性也起关键作用,金属氧键越弱,丙烷转化率越高[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01580]。

此外,载体的酸强度和碱性位点密度直接影响选择性,正如Zr含量调节过的ZrO2基催化剂所证明的那样,这会改变表面酸碱性质从而提高丙烯产率 [DOI: 10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074]。 通过与载体的相互作用来分散和隔离Cr和V等活性金属位点,还可以防止过度还原并最大限度地减少导致焦炭形成的副反应[DOI: 10.1016/j.apcatb.2020.119089, 10.1021/jacs.9b09235]。

总体而言,先进的表征技术的发展使得人们能够精确控制金属的氧化态、氧缺陷、金属载体界面以及酸碱性质,为设计优化的丙烷脱氢催化剂铺平了道路。 未来的研究可能会关注具有平衡氧化还原性能的多功能单原子合金催化剂,以推动丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性的极限。
