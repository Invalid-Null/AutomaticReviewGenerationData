 <English>
Several of the studies provided evaluate supported mono-metallic platinum (Pt) catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). One study shows Pt/alumina catalysts promoted with sulfur or boron can achieve improved selectivity and stability compared to unpromoted Pt [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. Other works demonstrate specialized Pt catalysts like sub-nanometric clusters in Ge-UTL zeolites [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7] or single atoms stabilized on silica [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389] that boast ultra-high selectivity and resistance to deactivation. Some comparisons reveal Pt often outperforms other metals like Ru [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667], but suffers from limitations like sintering and coking that bimetallics can overcome [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]. While promising, most industrial Pt catalysts still require improvements to stability. Continued research into tuned metal-support interactions, promoters, and controlled metal ensembles offers paths to enhanced mono- and multi-metallic PDH catalysts.
※※※
The insights on Pt catalysis provide direction on crafting more optimal PDH systems. Beyond platinum, expanding materials search and in-depth studies of other transition metals may uncover alternative active phases with favorable kinetics and resilience to poisons. Controlled deposition methods that isolate single sites in zeolites could apply broadly across metals. And support effects should remain an area of emphasis, as changes in acidity, porosity, and metal-support bonding remediate limitations of existing catalysts. The field would also benefit from advanced operando techniques to obtain deeper understandings of working catalysts. By integrating these approaches, next-generation catalysts may push PDH performance boundaries.

<Chinese>
几项研究评价了单金属铂(Pt)负载催化剂用于丙烷脱氢的性能。一项研究表明,使用硫或硼提纯的 Pt/氧化铝催化剂比未提纯的 Pt 催化剂有更高的选择性和稳定性 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。其他研究表明,嵌入 Ge-UTL 分子筛的亚纳米 Pt 簇 [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7] 或受控制地分散在二氧化硅上的 Pt 单原子 [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389] 催化剂展现出极高的选择性和抗脱活性。一些比较研究表明,Pt 的性能通常优于其他金属如 Ru [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667],但 Pt 催化剂还是会遭受烧结和积炭导致的失活。所以大多数工业 Pt 催化剂仍需进一步提高稳定性。继续研究调控金属与载体间相互作用、添加剂以及调控金属活性部位的方法,这些都是取得更优异单金属和多金属丙烷脱氢催化剂的途径。  
※※※
这些关于 Pt 催化剂的理解为优化丙烷脱氢体系提供了指导方向。除铂以外,扩大材料筛选的范围和对其他过渡金属的深入研究可能会发现具有良好活性和抗中毒性的备选活性相。将单一活性中心隔离掺入分子筛的方法可能会广泛应用于不同金属。此外,载体效应也应该是关注重点,因为改变酸度、孔结构和金属与载体间的键结都可以缓解现有催化剂的局限性。该领域也会从先进的原位表征技术中获益,这可以获得对工作状态催化剂的更深入理解。通过整合这些方法,新一代催化剂可能会推进丙烷脱氢性能的极限。
