Based on the quotes provided, catalyst preparation methods aimed at optimizing active phase dispersion that are investigated include:

1. Vacuum impregnation to achieve isolated Co2+ sites and higher Co dispersion in Co@Si-BEA catalysts [DOI1]. Dealumination also produced Co@Si-BEA catalyst with less graphitic coke compared to Co@BEA [DOI1].

2. Creating defects and holes in BN sheets, controlling pore structure, and loading/doping B species on 3D porous supports to increase dispersion and number of active sites [DOI2]. 

3. Impregnation, ion exchange, and reductive treatments to optimize dispersion of metals like Ga, Zn, and In in zeolite catalysts [DOI3].

4. Using ligand-protected hydrothermal synthesis to construct isolated Co-motifs in MFI, avoiding CoOx cluster formation [DOI4].

5. Varying impregnation vanadia content and density on oxide supports to control dispersion. Higher V content on HMS gave better propylene yield vs SBA-15 [DOI5].

6. Hydrothermal synthesis produced sheet-Co/Al2O3 with better dispersion vs impregnation nanoparticles [DOI6].

7. Encapsulating subnanometric Pt clusters in zeolites via hydrothermal synthesis with capping agents [DOI7].

8. Tuning alumina rod synthesis and varying calcination gave controlled acidity and Cr dispersion [DOI8].

9. CVD of precursors followed by H2 reduction gave dispersed cationic Ga species stabilized by framework Al [DOI9]. Care taken to prevent air exposure during loading.

10. Impregnation to add In and Sn improved Pt dispersion on Al2O3 from 10 nm to 1 nm due to dilution effects and alloying [DOI10].

※※※

The methods investigated demonstrate the critical role that tailored synthetic techniques play in optimizing metal dispersion, stabilizing isolated active sites, and controlling the metal-support interface. Achieving isolated, highly dispersed active sites prevents unwanted consecutive reactions and coking while maximizing desired dehydrogenation activity.

As research continues to uncover promising compositional and geometric configurations for PDH catalysts, it will be imperative to pair these insights with preparative innovation. Advanced characterization techniques should further our understanding of active site genesis and transformation. New methods that exercise precision control over dispersion and stabilization under realistic operating environments may enable enhanced productivity.

<Chinese>

根据提供的引文,调查的旨在优化活性相分散的催化剂制备方法包括:

1. 使用真空浸渍法实现 Co@Si-BEA 催化剂中隔离的 Co2+ 位点和更高的 Co 分散度 [DOI1]。硅 extraction 也制备出比 Co@BEA 催化剂具有更少石墨烯类焦炭的 Co@Si-BEA 催化剂 [DOI1]。

2. 在 BN 片中创建缺陷和孔洞,控制孔结构,并在三维多孔支撑体上加载/掺杂 B 物种以增加分散度和活性位点的数量 [DOI2]。

3. 浸渍、离子交换和还原处理以优化分子筛催化剂中 Ga、Zn 和 In 等金属的分散度 [DOI3]。

4. 使用配体保护的水热合成方法在 MFI 框架中构建隔离的 Co-motifs,避免形成 CoOx 簇或纳米粒子 [DOI4]。

5. 调节氧化物载体上的钒浸渍量和密度以控制分散度。 HMS 上的更高钒含量比 SBA-15 具有更好的丙烯收率 [DOI5]。

6. 水热合成产生片状 Co/Al2O3,分散度优于浸渍纳米粒子 [DOI6]。

7. 通过水热合成和带有包裹剂的方法封装亚纳米 Pt 簇 [DOI7]。

8. 调节氧化铝棒合成和变化煅烧温度以控制酸度和铬分散 [DOI8]。

9. 先驱体的 CVD 随后 H2 还原形成由框架 Al 稳定的离散的阳离子态 Ga 物种 [DOI9]。 在加载期间小心防止曝露于空气。

10. 添加 In 和 Sn 改善了 Al2O3 上 Pt 的分散度,从 10 nm 减少到 1 nm,这是由于稀释效应和合金形成的结果 [DOI10]。

※※※

调查的方法证明了量身定制的合成技术在优化金属分散度、稳定隔离的活性位点和控制金属载体界面方面发挥的关键作用。实现活性位点的隔离和高度分散可以防止不必要的连续反应和结焦,同时最大限度地提高所需的脱氢活性。

随着研究继续揭示 PDH 催化剂的有希望的组成和几何配置,将这些见解与制备创新结合起来将至关重要。先进的表征技术应进一步深入我们对活性位点起源和转化的理解。在实际工作环境下精确控制分散度和稳定性的新方法可能实现增强的生产力。
