 <English>

Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts facilitate the reaction by lowering activation energy barriers, enabling an economically competitive and environmentally friendly route for on-purpose production of propylene. Industrial PDH processes utilize supported metal catalysts like Pt/Al2O3 and CrOx/Al2O3, where the metal sites activate C-H bonds in propane through mechanisms such as the Horiuti-Polanyi steps of alkane dissociative adsorption, β-H elimination, and alkene desorption [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]. DFT calculations have elucidated how the metal site geometry impacts selectivity, with under-coordinated atoms on corners and steps promoting high activity but increased cracking [10.1021/acscatal.5b01423]. Alternative catalysts like Ga2O3, ZrO2, and metal-exchanged zeolites are also being explored to improve selectivity and stability [10.1021/acscatal.0c05454, 10.1021/advs.202207756].

<Chinese>

丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂通过降低活化能垒促进反应,实现了一种经济实惠且环保的目的丙烯生产途径。工业PDH过程使用Pt/Al2O3和CrOx/Al2O3等支持金属催化剂,其中的金属位点通过Horiuti-Polanyi机制等机制活化丙烷中的C-H键,包括烷烃解离吸附、β-H消除和烯烃解吸[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]。DFT计算阐明了金属位点几何形状如何影响选择性,角点和台阶上的未配位原子促进高活性但会增加裂化反应[10.1021/acscatal.5b01423]。也在探索Ga2O3、ZrO2和金属交换沸石等替代催化剂以提高选择性和稳定性[10.1021/acscatal.0c05454, 10.1021/advs.202207756]。
