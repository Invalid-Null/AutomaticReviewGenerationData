Several studies in the file evaluate supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, or Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts, employed in processes like UOP's Oleflex [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]. For example, an alumina-supported Pt catalyst shows good propane conversion and propylene selectivity, which is attributed to Pt's ability to activate C-H bonds while minimizing C-C cleavage [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. Adding promoters like boron [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548] or metal oxide overcoats [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391] are found to enhance Pt/alumina stability and yield. Optimizing the dispersion of Pt clusters [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631] or the Pt-support interface, via bonds between Pt and Ge atoms in zeolite UTL pores [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7], also improves performance.

Compared to Pt/alumina, supported RhCu single atom alloys demonstrate higher intrinsic activity and 100% propene selectivity [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]. But Pt still outperforms monometallic Ru nanoparticles, which have very low selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]. Adding Sn generally enhances Pt performance for PDH by modifying geometric and electronic properties [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639, DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119].

Several studies also explore deactivation mechanisms. Temperature-programmed oxidation reveals multiple types of coke on spent Pt catalysts [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]. And sulfur poisons Pt/spinel catalysts by accelerating metal agglomeration [DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X].

Overall, supported mono-metallic Pt catalysts demonstrate leading PDH performance in terms of activity, selectivity and commercial viability. But stability remains a key challenge, and promoters, overcoats and optimized metal-support interfaces aim to address this.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings presented underscore supported Pt catalysts' proficiency for selective propane dehydrogenation, substantiated by real-world commercial implementations. However, gradual deactivation prompted by coking, agglomeration and poisoning necessitates ongoing stabilization endeavors – whether via promoters, overcoats or interfacial modulations. 

An expansive perspective would also encompass emerging opportunities in C1 chemistry through C3 upgrading. Propane dehydrogenation constitutes but one pathway to harness abundant, stranded natural gas and light alkane reserves. Innovative hybrid processes interlinking chemical looping reforming, oxy-cracking and dehydrogenation with C1 utilizations could hold promise. C1 conversion to value-added chemicals, fuels and materials remains an active domain. Creative catalyst and process design marrying selective dehydrogenation with C1 activation may proffer system-level solutions.

Researchcould focus on developing robust multifunctional catalysts that selectively transform propane while utilizing released C1s. Or designing reactors that spatiotemporally couple and optimize sequential PDH and C1 conversion steps. Such explorations at the confluence of light alkane activation, dehydrogenation and C1 chemistry may further propane resource development.

文件中评价了几种负载的单金属 Pt、Pd 或 Ir 催化剂用于丙烷脱氢。

Pt 基催化剂被报道是最广泛使用和商业上最成功的 PDH 催化剂之一,应用于UOP公司的 Oleflex 工艺中[DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。例如,氧化铝支持的 Pt 催化剂显示了良好的丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性,这是由于 Pt 能激活 C-H 键而最小化 C-C 键的断裂[DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。添加促进剂如硼[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]或金属氧化物涂层[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]发现可以增强 Pt/氧化铝的稳定性和产率。优化 Pt 簇的分散[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631]或 Pt 与硅沸石 UTL 微孔内的 Ge 原子之间的界面相互作用,通过 Pt-O-Ge 键[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7],也可以改善性能。

与 Pt/氧化铝相比,支持的 RhCu 单原子合金表现出更高的内在活性和 100% 的丙烯选择性[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。但 Pt 的性能仍优于单金属 Ru 纳米粒子,后者选择性非常低[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]。添加 Sn 通常可以通过改变 Pt 的几何和电子性质来增强其 PDH 性能[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639, DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。

几项研究还探索了失��机制。程序升温氧化显示Pt催化剂脱活后的多个焦炭类型 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]。而硫通过加速金属聚集毒害了 Pt/磁铁矿催化剂 [DOI: 10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]。

总体而言,负载的单金属 Pt 催化剂在活性、选择性和商业可行性方面显示了领先的 PDH 性能。但是稳定性仍然是一个关键挑战,促进剂、涂层和优化的金属-支持体界面旨在解决这个问题。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:所呈现的研究结果强调了负载Pt催化剂在选择性丙烷脱氢方面的专业性,这是由实际的商业应用所证实的。然而,焦炭、聚集和中毒导致的逐渐失活必需进行稳定化的努力,无论是通过促进剂、涂层还是界面调控。 

一个扩展的视角还将包括通过C3升级获得C1化学的新兴机会。丙烷脱氢只是利用丰富的、困难的天然气和轻烷类储备的途径之一。创新将化学循环重整、氧裂解和脱氢与C1利用相结合的工艺可能很有前途。 C1转化为高附加值化学品、燃料和材料仍是一个活跃的领域。创造性地催化剂和工艺设计,结合选择性脱氢和C1激活,可能提供系统级的解决方案。

研究可能集中在开发稳定的�功能催化剂,能选择性转化丙烷的同时利用释放出来的C1。或者设计反应器,时空耦合和优化顺序PDH和C1转化步骤。这些在轻烷类活化、脱氢和 C1化学交汇点的探索可能进一步推动丙烷资源的开发。
