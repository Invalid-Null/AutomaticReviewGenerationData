 <English>

The article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816] investigates tuning zeolite Si/Al ratio by dealumination of Beta zeolites to create silanol nests, which enhances metal dispersion and stabilization.

Another article (DOI: [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]) evaluates propane dehydrogenation performance over CHA zeolites with varying Si/Al ratios of 5, 12 and 25. It shows that extra-framework Ga+ sites are formed regardless of Si/Al ratio. While reaction rates decrease over time, propylene selectivity remains stable at 96% when Ga/Al ratio is properly tuned.

Additionally, the article with DOI [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024] studies the effects of tuning TS-1 zeolite crystal size from 100 nm to 5.7 μm. Smaller crystals introduce mesoporosity, enhancing diffusion and avoiding coking. This significantly improves catalytic conversion, selectivity and lifetime. 

Lastly, DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808] incorporates mesoporosity into MFI zeolite nanosheets to synthesize PtZn nanoparticle catalysts. The mesoporous structure and surface silanol groups promote intermetallic alloy formation.

※※※

The investigations reveal complex structure-function relationships in tuning zeolite properties for propane dehydrogenation. Strategies like dealumination, nanosizing and mesopore incorporation impact critical aspects including metal dispersion, selectivity and catalyst lifetime. However, reactions are sensitive to precise tuning of not just Si/Al ratio but also properties like crystal size and metal loading. 

As zeolite catalyst design advances, future work should systematically explore these high-dimensional complexity in property optimization. Combined theoretical and experimental efforts spanning multiple length and time scales can uncover more intricate descriptors. Rapid screening enabled by automation and in situ characterization can also accelerate optimal material discovery. Broader implementation necessitates addressing scalability challenges early during development as well.

<Chinese>

DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]的文章通过Beta分子筛的脱铝来调节Si/Al比,形成硅醇巢,增强了金属的分散和稳定性。

另一篇文章(DOI:[10.1021/jacs.2c03941])评估了Si/Al比为5、12和25的CHA分子筛在丙烷脱氢反应中的性能。它表明,无论Si/Al比如何,都会形成额外的Ga+位点。尽管反应率随时间下降,但在Ga/Al比正确调节的条件下,丙烯选择性保持在96%。

此外,DOI [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024] 的文章研究了调节TS-1沸石晶体大小从100 nm到5.7 μm的效果。较小的晶体引入了介孔,增强了扩散并避免了结焦。这显著改善了催化转换率、选择性和使用寿命。

最后,DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]在MFI分子筛纳米片中引入介孔,合成了PtZn纳米粒子催化剂。介孔结构和表面硅醇基团促进了金属间化合物的形成。

※※※

这些研究揭示了调节分子筛性质用于丙烷脱氢反应的复杂结构-性能关系。脱铝、纳米化和介孔引入等策略影响金属分散度、选择性和催化剂使用寿命等关键方面。但是,反应对Si/Al比以及晶体大小和金属负载等性质的精确调节非常敏感。

随着分子筛催化剂设计的进步,未来的工作应该系统地探索这些高维复杂性中的性能优化。跨越多个长度和时间尺度的理论和实验相结合的努力可以揭示更复杂的描述符。自动化和原位表征ENABLED的快速筛选也可以加速优化材料的发现。在开发早期解决可扩展性挑战也必不可少。
