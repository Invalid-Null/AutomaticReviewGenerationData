Based on the details extracted from the references, mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over several metal oxide catalysts, with key aspects summarized as follows:

On Cr-based catalysts, active Cr3+ sites are often generated in situ from Cr6+ precursors. Isolated Cr3+ species with coordinative vacancies [DOI1] or Cr3+ sites paired with lattice oxygen [DOI2] catalyze dehydrogenation through a redox Mars-van Krevelen type mechanism. Tetrahedrally coordinated Cr6+ sites in CrO4 units also activate propane heterolytically [DOI3].  

ZrO2-based catalysts dehydrogenate propane on coordinatively unsaturated Zr4+ sites (Zrcus) formed near oxygen vacancies [DOI4, DOI5]. Two neighboring Zrcus sites break C-H bonds homolytically via concerted or stepwise pathways with transition states and intermediates described [DOI4]. Zrcus also synergizes with sites like Zn2+ and Ga3+ [DOI6, DOI7].

Vanadium oxide catalysts activate propane on isolated V5+/4+/3+ sites through Mars-van Krevelen type redox steps with lattice oxygen [DOI8, DOI9]. Sub-monolayer VOx nanostructures help avoid overoxidation issues [DOI10]. MoVO mixed oxides utilize lattice oxygen before switching to V3+ sites [DOI11].

On Ga/zeolite, monovalent Ga+ sites dehydrogenate propane via a Ga-(C3H7) intermediate with subsequent hydride abstraction [DOI12]. An alkyl mechanism is also proposed over Ga3+ sites paired with Brønsted acid sites [DOI13]. Supported Ga2O3 activates propane heterolytically over tetrahedral Ga3+ sites [DOI14].  

※※※

With increasing understanding of the structure-activity relationships governing propane dehydrogenation over metal oxides, future research can build on these mechanistic foundations to design superior single and multi-site catalysts. Novel combinations of active sites and modifiers may uncover transformed paradigms. Operando spectroscopy techniques will likely play an integral role in unraveling transient intermediates and mapping complete reaction coordinates over promising systems.

根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,已经为几种金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢提出了机理方案,关键方面总结如下:

在Cr基催化剂上,活性Cr3+位点通常是原位从Cr6+前体生成的。 配位空位的隔离Cr3+物种[DOI1]或与晶格氧配对的Cr3+位点[DOI2]通过Mars-van Krevelen型红氧机理催化脱氢。 四面体配位的Cr6+位点在CrO4单元中也可以异构化激活丙烷[DOI3]。

ZrO2基催化剂在氧空位附近形成的非饱和配位Zr4+位点(Zrcus)上脱氢丙烷[DOI4,DOI5]。两个相邻的Zrcus位点通过协同或步移路径断裂C-H键,描述了过渡态和中间体[DOI4]。 Zrcus还与Zn2+和Ga3+等位点发生协同作用[DOI6,DOI7]。

钒酸盐催化剂在隔离的V5+/4+/3+位点上通过Mars-van Krevelen型氧化还原步骤激活丙烷,使用晶格氧[DOI8,DOI9]。 亚单分子层VOx纳米结构有助于避免氧化过度问题[DOI10]。 MoVO混合氧化物利用晶格氧,然后切换到V3+位点[DOI11]。

在Ga/分子筛上,单价Ga+位点通过Ga-(C3H7)中间体脱氢丙烷,随后发生氢提取[DOI12]。 在Ga3+位点与Brønsted酸位点成对的情况下,也提出了烷基机理[DOI13]。 支持的Ga2O3在四面体Ga3+位点上异构化激活丙烷[DOI14]。

※※※

随着人们对金属氧化物催化剂上丙烷脱氢结构-活性关系的了解不断加深,未来的研究可以在这些机理基础上设计出优良的单位点和多位点催化剂。 新颖的活性位点和修饰剂的组合可能会揭示转化后的范式。 同位素示踪技术可能会在揭示瞬态中间体和绘制具有前景的体系的完整反应坐标方面发挥关键作用。
