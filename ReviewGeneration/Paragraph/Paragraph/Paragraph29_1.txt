Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The thorough investigation reveals that existing literature on propane dehydrogenation has concentrated considerably on enhancing catalysts through diverse compositions, nanostructures, and synthetic techniques. However, process intensification principles remain largely unexplored in this context. The amalgamation of cutting-edge catalysts and intensified reactors can potentially stimulate a new wave of research and push the boundaries of current chemical conversion boundaries. Overall, while tangible advancements have arisen via catalytic routes, process-centric intensification now warrants attention to foster additional efficiency gains in propane dehydrogenation.
