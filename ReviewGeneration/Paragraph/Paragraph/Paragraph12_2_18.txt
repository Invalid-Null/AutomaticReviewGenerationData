 <English>
Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have evaluated supported mono-metallic platinum (Pt), palladium (Pd), and iridium (Ir) catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts appear to be the most widely explored. One study showed an alumina-supported Pt catalyst maintains 58% propane conversion for 25 hours, with the addition of 1.5 wt% Ca markedly improving stability [DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044]. Another demonstrated that confining sub-nanometric Pt clusters inside the micropores of a germanosilicate zeolite via Pt-O-Ge bonds gave a catalyst with excellent activity, selectivity and stability [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. Adding promoters is a common strategy to enhance Pt catalysts - 1 wt% boron on Pt/γ-Al2O3 was found to increase selectivity from 90% to 98% while reducing coking 3-fold [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. 

Data also shows supported Pt can be far inferior to other metals in some cases. One study comparing Pt/Al2O3 to a RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst found the RhCu had higher activity and 100% propene selectivity versus only 80% for Pt [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]. Another showed that at similar conditions, monometallic Ru nanoparticles had extremely poor 4.5% selectivity compared to Pt (46%) and optimized Ru phosphide (91%) [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667].

Several works provide insights into deactivation mechanisms of supported Pt catalysts during PDH. Temperature-programmed oxidation revealed three types of coke on PtSn/Al2O3 - on the metal, support, and graphitic support coke - which hydrogen suppresses [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]. Another showed sulfur accelerates agglomeration-linked deactivation of Pt/MgAl2O4 spinel [DOI: 0021-9517(86)90312-X].

Less information is available on supported Pd and Ir catalysts. One study noted they can activate propane C-H bonds similar to Pt, but did not provide data [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. No evaluations of supported Pd or Ir catalysts are explicitly mentioned.

Overall, Pt-based catalysts appear to be the most extensively studied supported mono-metallic catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. Key areas needing further investigation are elucidating metal-support interactions that enhance stability and performance as well as optimizing promoters, modifiers and preparation methods to mitigate deactivation. Exploring other Group VIII-X transition metals like Pd and Ir may also uncover promising alternatives to Pt.

※※※ 
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings reveal supported mono- and bi-metallic Pt catalysts are at the forefront of PDH technology, but suffer drawbacks like high cost and propensity for deactivation. This underscores opportunities to develop lower-cost metallic alternatives or even metal-free catalysts leveraging advanced support materials. Recent works on single atom alloy catalysts and stabilized zeolite-confined clusters also showcase promising directions to maximize precious metal efficiency. Moreover, the critical role of metal-support interactions in modifying stability and selectivity motivates research on novel support materials that strongly couple with metals. Advancing in situ/operando characterization techniques to probe these interactions could provide vital feedback to guide support design. Overall, rising above incremental improvements in existing catalysts to explore completely new compositions and concepts may hold the key to transform PDH technology.

<Chinese>
根据文件提供的详细信息,已有几项研究对载体单组分铂(Pt)、钯(Pd)和钌(Ir)催化剂进行了丙烷脱氢(PDH)方面的评估。

以Pt为基础的催化剂似乎是研究最广泛的。一项研究表明,加入1.5 wt% Ca的AlaO3担载Pt催化剂可以将丙烷转化率维持在25小时58%以上,Ca的加入显著提高了稳定性[DOI: 10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044]。另一项研究证明,将亚纳米Pt簇控制在一种硅锗分子筛的微孔内通过Pt-O-Ge键实现高性能催化剂设计,展现了优异的活性、选择性和稳定性 [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。加入助剂是提高Pt催化剂性能的常用策略——在1 wt%硼改性Pt/g-AlaO3上,选择性从90%提高到98%,同时焦炭沉积减少了3倍 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。

数据还显示,与其他金属相比,某些情况下载体Pt的性能可能差很多。一项研究比较了Pt/AlaO3和RhCu单原子合金催化剂,发现RhCu催化剂活性更高,丙烯选择性达到100%,而Pt仅为80% [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。另一项研究表明,在相似条件下,单组分Ru纳米粒子的选择性仅为4.5%,远低于Pt (46%)和优化的Ru磷化物(91%) [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]]。

几项研究提供了PDH过程中Pt基催化剂失活机理的信息。程序升温氧化实验揭示PtSn/AlaO3上存在三种焦炭——金属上、载体上和载体上的图ite焦炭,氢气抑制其形成[DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1996.0361]。另一项研究表明,硫促进了Pt/MgAlaO4 spinal型担载物的团聚失活[DOI: 0021-9517(86)90312-X]]。

关于Pd和Ir载体催化剂的信息较少。一项研究指出它们也能激活丙烷的C-H键,类似Pt,但未给出数据[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。文中没有明确提到对Pd或Ir载体催化剂评估的内容。

总的来说,Pt基催化剂似乎是研究最广泛的单组分丙烷脱氢载体催化剂。亟需深入研究的关键领域是阐明增强稳定性和性能的金属-载体相互作用以及优化助剂、修饰剂和制备方法以减轻失活。探索钯和铱等VIII-X族过渡金属也可能发现Pt的有前景替代品。


※※※
综合理解与未来展望:研究结果显示,单组分和双组分Pt载体催化剂处于PDH技术的前沿,但也存在高昂成本和易失活等缺点。这突显了开发低成本金属替代品乃至金属游离催化剂的机遇,可利用先进的载体材料。最近关于单原子合金催化剂和稳定的分子筛限制簇催化剂的研究也展现了很有前景的方向,可以最大限度地提高贵金属的利用效率。此外,金属-载体相互作用在调控稳定性和选择性方面发挥的关键作用,激发了对能与金属产生强相互作用的新型载体材料的研究兴趣。推进原位/操作条件下表征技术来探测这些相互作用,可以提供关键的反馈来指导载体设计。总体而言,超越现有催化剂的渐进改进,全新的组合和概念的探索可能是改变PDH技术的关键。
