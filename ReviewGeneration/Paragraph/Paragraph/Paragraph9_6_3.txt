Based on the details extracted from the literature, tuning oxidation states, oxygen mobility, and acid-base properties of metal oxides can have significant effects on propane dehydrogenation performance. 

For vanadium oxide catalysts, creating oxygen vacancies and generating coordinatively unsaturated V3+/V4+ species leads to enhanced reducibility and higher activity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]. The chemical states of bridge oxygen also play critical roles, with weaker V-O interactions and lower oxygen electron density improving propane activation capability. Introducing dopants like Mo increases V-O bond strength, reducing overoxidation and improving selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/jacs.9b09235].

On supported chromium oxide catalysts, sustained regeneration of lower oxidation state Cr3+/Cr2+ species via redox cycling with O2 or CO2 is crucial for maintaining high activity [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1183, DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]. Dispersion of these species also determines performance.

For gallium oxide, the presence of tetrahedral Ga3+ sites generates Lewis acidity that correlates with propane activation rates [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c05454]. Acidity tuning by doping with metals like Ir also enhances catalytic performance. Among Ga oxide polymorphs, β-Ga2O3 with the highest concentration of medium-strong Brønsted acid sites shows superior activity [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001].  

On titania and alumina, creating oxygen vacancies and coordinatively unsaturated Ti4+ or Al3+ sites through high temperature H2 reduction also effectively activates propane dehydrogenation [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00818, DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033].

For Pt-based catalysts, optimizing metal particle size, dispersion across supports, and formation of PtSn alloy nanoparticles leads to enhanced propane conversion and propylene selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.6b00893].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The studies reveal that metal oxides can activate propane dehydrogenation through tunable redox properties and synergistic acid-base functionalities. Key descriptors like oxygen mobility, surface Lewis acidity, redox species dispersion, and metal-support interfacial sites critically impact activity, selectivity and stability. 

An expansive horizon encompassing additional characterization tools and multi-scale modeling is imperative to systematically unravel structure-functionality interplays. Operando spectroscopy can illuminate evolution of surface defects and adsorbates under working conditions. DFT calculations should further bridge atomic-level insights with experimentally observed catalytic phenomena. Efforts must underline rational catalyst design principles rooted in mechanistic comprehension, potentially expanding the portfolio of active and selective metal oxide materials. Continuous learning between computational screening and high-throughput experimentation could unveil unforeseen compositions and dopants that demonstrate enhanced propane conversion. Eventually, techno-economic analyses weighing capital investment, material criticality and sustainability factors should guide translation from promising lab-scale formulations to industry-ready alternatives, contributing to the global chemical value chain.

根据从文献中提取的详细信息,调控金属氧化物的氧化态、氧迁移性和酸碱性会对丙烷脱氢反应性能产生重大影响。

对于钒氧化物催化剂,创建氧空位和生成四配位的V3+/V4+物种会提高其可还原性和活性 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]。桥氧的化学态也发挥关键作用,较弱的V-O相互作用和较低的氧电子密度会提高丙烷活化能力。引入诸如Mo之类的掺杂剂会增加V-O键的强度,减少过氧化并提高选择性 [DOI: 10.1021/jacs.9b09235]。

在载体铬氧化物催化剂上,通过氧/CO2循环反应持续再生低氧化态的Cr3+/Cr2+物种对维持高活性至关重要 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1183, DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]。这些物种的分散度也决定了催化性能。  

对于氧化镓,四配位Ga3+位点的存在产生刘易斯酸性,与丙烷活化速率相关 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c05454]。用Ir等金属掺杂调控酸性也可以增强催化性能。在氧化镓多晶型中,硷基强度最高的β-Ga2O3表现出最高的活性 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2005.03.001]。

在二氧化钛和氧化铝上,通过高温H2还原生成氧空位和四配位的Ti4+或Al3+位点也可以有效激活丙烷脱氢反应 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00818, DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.033]。

对于Pt基催化剂,优化金属颗粒大小、在载体上的分散度以及PtSn合金纳米粒子的形成,会提高丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639, DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.6b00893]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:这些研究表明,金属氧化物可以通过可调节的氧化还原性质和协同的酸碱功能来激活丙烷脱氢反应。诸如氧迁移性、表面刘易斯酸性、还原物种的分散度和金属-载体界面的位点等关键描述子对活性、选择性和稳定性有关键的影响。  

需要更加广阔的视野,包括额外的表征工具和多尺度建模,以系统地揭示结构-功能关系。原位光谱可以阐明工作条件下表面缺陷和吸附物的演变。DFT计算应进一步桥接原子水平的洞察与实验观察到的催化现象。这些工作必须强调以机制理解为基础的合理催化剂设计原则,潜在地扩大活性和选择性金属氧化物材料的组合。计算筛选和高通量实验之间的持续学习可以发现意想不到的组分和掺杂剂,展示出增强的丙烷转化率。 最终,考虑资本投资、材料稀缺性和可持续性因素的技术经济分析应指导从有前景的实验室缩放配方向工业可行的替代品的转化,为全球化学价值链做出贡献。
