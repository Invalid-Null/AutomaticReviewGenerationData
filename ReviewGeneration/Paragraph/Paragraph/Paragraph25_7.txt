Based on the details extracted from the references \[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726\], \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00939\], \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728\], \[10.1002/advs.202207756\], \[10.1021/jacs.2c02636\], \[10.1006/jcat.2001.3372\], \[10.1038/s41467-022-35698-0\], several in-situ characterization techniques have been utilized to study working propane dehydrogenation catalysts and gain insights into the nature of active sites.

Temperature programmed surface reaction (TPSR) between 300-650°C was used to monitor product formation over MgO-SiO2, showing high propylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726\]. In-situ DRIFTS coupled with a Harrick cell probed propylene adsorption/desorption during temperature ramping, revealing differences between Co-modified and unmodified catalysts \[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726\]. Quantitative in-situ FTIR spectroscopy and pulse titration determined the density of active Ga species in Ga/H-ZSM-5, finding isolated Ga+ sites displace Brønsted protons upon H2 reduction \[10.1021/acscatal.1c05724\]. In-situ XAS showed changes in metal coordination environments in Ga/H-ZSM-5 at propane dehydrogenation conditions \[10.1002/advs.202207756\]. Online MS and in-situ FTIR over Co-MFI confirmed OH groups participate in dehydrogenation, also detecting H2 and HD formation \[10.1021/jacs.2c02636\]. UV-Vis diffuse reflectance spectroscopy using an optical fiber placed inside the catalyst bed monitored reduction of Cr6+ during reaction over Cr/Al2O3 \[10.1006/jcat.2001.3372\]. In-situ DRIFTS revealed transformation of inactive ZnOH to active ZnH species over ZnO/SBA-15 during reaction, correlating with induction periods \[10.1038/s41467-022-35698-0\].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The in-situ techniques provided molecular-level insights into working catalysts, tracking changes in active sites, oxidation states, coordination environments, and surface species during propane dehydrogenation. This understanding of structure-function relationships and deactivation pathways can guide the rational design of more stable and selective catalysts. Emerging operando spectroscopies under industrially-relevant conditions combined with computational modeling may further bridge pressure and materials gaps. New structural, surface, and single-atom propane dehydrogenation catalysts could also be explored by harnessing multi-technique operando studies in tandem with high-throughput screening and machine learning. Ultimately, deeper comprehension of catalytic mechanisms secured via cutting-edge in-situ metrologies may catalyze transformative propane-to-propylene technologies.

根据从参考文献\[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726\]、\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00939\]、\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728\]、\[10.1002/advs.202207756\]、 \[10.1021/jacs.2c02636\]、\[10.1006/jcat.2001.3372\]和 \[10.1038/s41467-022-35698-0\]中提取的详细信息,已经利用几种原位表征技术来研究正在工作的丙烷脱氢催化剂,并深入了解活性位点的本质。


在MgO-SiO2上,进行了300-650°C范围内的温度程序表面反应(TPSR),以监测产物的形成,发现高丙烯选择性\[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726\]。采用Harrick原位DRIFTS结合温度扫描技术,研究了钴改性和未改性催化剂上的丙烯吸附/解吸过程,揭示了两者之间的差异\[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726\]。定量原位FTIR光谱技术和脉冲滴定技术确定了Ga/H-ZSM-5中活性Ga化合物的密度,发现H2还原后,Ga+被分离位点置换了质子\[10.1021/acscatal.1c05724\]。原位XAS表明,在丙烷脱氢反应条件下,Ga/H-ZSM-5中的金属配位环境发生了变化\[10.1002/advs.202207756\]。在Co-MFI上使用在线MS和原位FTIR确认了OH基团参与了脱氢反应,还检测到了H2和HD的形成\[10.1021/jacs.2c02636\]。使用放置在催化剂床内的光纤进行UV-Vis漫反射光谱,监测了Cr/Al2O3上反应过程中Cr6+的还原\[10.1006/jcat.2001.3372\]。原位DRIFTS显示了ZnO/SBA-15上非活性ZnOH向活性ZnH的转变,与诱导期相关\[10.1038/s41467-022-35698-0\]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:原位技术在分子层面上洞察了工作状态下的催化剂,跟踪了丙烷脱氢过程中活性位点、氧化态、配位环境和表面种类的变化。这种对结构-功能关系和失活途径的理解,可以指导设计出更稳定和选择性更高的催化剂。新出现的模拟工业相关条件下的同位素光谱技术,结合计算模型建立,可进一步弥合压力差和材料差。利用多技术同位素研究与高通量筛选和机器学习技术的配合,也可以开拓结构性的、表面性的和单原子丙烷脱氢催化剂。最终,通过尖端原位测量技术获得的更深入的催化机理理解,可能催生丙烷转丙烯技术的革新。
