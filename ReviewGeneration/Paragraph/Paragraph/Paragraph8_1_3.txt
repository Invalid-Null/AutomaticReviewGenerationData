<English>
Metal oxides have been extensively explored as catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) due to their potential for high activity, selectivity, and stability [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Among the most promising metal oxide catalysts investigated are chromium oxide (Cr2O3), gallium oxide (Ga2O3), and platinum-tin (Pt-Sn) oxides. 

Chromium oxide, in particular α-Cr2O3, has shown high intrinsic activity for propane dehydrogenation. CrOx/SiO2, CrOx/Al2O3, and unsupported α-Cr2O3 catalysts were all found active for PDH at 723-823 K [10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]. Weak metal-support interactions with SiO2 improved propylene selectivity for Cr2O3/SiO2 compared to Cr2O3/Al2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Doping Cr2O3 with Pt and using Cr2O3/SiO2 also enhanced performance [10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]. However, Cr2O3 catalysts still suffer from rapid deactivation. 

Gallium oxide, especially in the β-Ga2O3 polymorph, exhibited even higher initial activity than Cr2O3 for PDH, particularly under CO2 co-feeding. This was attributed to the abundant medium strength acid sites on β-Ga2O3 surfaces [10.1021/jacs.5b00225]. Unsupported nanocrystalline β-Ga2O3/SiO2 and γ-Ga2O3/SiO2 also showed good propane conversion and propylene selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]. The PDH performance of Ga2O3 could be further improved by creating mixed Ga2O3-ZrO2 metal oxides, which increased the Lewis acidity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. However, stabilization of highly active gallium oxide surfaces remains an ongoing challenge.

Among supported Pt catalysts, PtSn bimetallics have shown particularly promising results for PDH. Pt-Sn/SiO2, Pt-Sn/Al2O3 and Pt-Sn/zeolite catalysts achieved over 90% propylene selectivity and 40-50% propane conversion [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527, 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]. The formation of PtSn intermetallic compounds modifies the Pt electronic structure, weakens propane adsorption strength, and suppresses hydrogenolysis/coking pathways - all of which improve selectivity [10.1021/jacs.6b08705]. However, sintering of the PtSn active phase still leads to gradual activity loss.

Overall, while metal oxides like Cr2O3 and Ga2O3 exhibit high intrinsic activity for propane dehydrogenation, their practical stability and selectivity remains inferior to supported PtSn catalysts. However, opportunities exist to stabilize highly active Cr2O3 and Ga2O3 surfaces via new synthetic strategies and metal-support interfaces. This could enable metal oxides to approach the performance of PtSn while avoiding the high cost and scarcity of noble metals.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and results surveyed, further research could focus on:

1) New synthetic methods to stabilize intrinsically active Cr2O3 and Ga2O3 surfaces, such as morphology control, surface passivation, or anchoring on protective supports.

2) Designing bifunctional metal oxide catalysts that combine highly active sites (e.g. Cr, Ga) with selectivity-enhancing sites (e.g. Sn, Zr). This may achieve PtSn-level performance through precise optimization of composition and structure. 

3) Operando spectroscopy and microkinetic modeling to provide molecular-level insights into active sites, reaction intermediates, and deactivation mechanisms on promising metal oxides. This can guide the rational design of optimized PDH catalysts.

4) Development of novel support materials such as metal-organic frameworks or nanocarbons to interact synergistically with active metal oxide phases for enhanced thermal and hydrothermal stability.

5) Economic and environmental assessment of PDH metal oxide catalysts to determine technical and commercial viability compared to existing systems.

<Chinese>
金属氧化物广泛地被研究作为丙烷脱氢反应的催化剂,由于它们具有潜在的高活性、选择性和稳定性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。研究得最多的有前景的金属氧化物催化剂包括氧化铬(Cr2O3)、氧化镓(Ga2O3)和铂锡(Pt-Sn)氧化物。

氧化铬,特别是α-Cr2O3,表现出很高的固有丙烷脱氢活性。CrOx/SiO2、CrOx/Al2O3和未负载的α-Cr2O3催化剂在723-823 K条件下都表现出PDH活性[10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]。与Al2O3相比,SiO2与Cr2O3的弱金属支撑相互作用提高了Cr2O3/SiO2的丙烯选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。用Pt掺杂Cr2O3和使用Cr2O3/SiO2也增强了表现[10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]。然而,Cr2O3催化剂仍然存在快速失活的问题。

氧化镓,特别是β-Ga2O3多形,表现出比Cr2O3更高的丙烷脱氢初始活性,特别是在CO2共饲的条件下。这被归因于β-Ga2O3表面的丰富中等强度酸位[10.1021/jacs.5b00225]。无载纳米β-Ga2O3/SiO2和γ-Ga2O3/SiO2也表现出良好的丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]。Ga2O3的PDH性能可以通过创建Ga2O3-ZrO2混合氧化物进一步提高,这增加了路易斯酸性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。但是,高活性的氧化镓表面稳定仍是一个持续的挑战。

在负载Pt催化剂中,PtSn双金属展现出特别有前景的PDH结果。Pt-Sn/SiO2、Pt-Sn/Al2O3和Pt-Sn/分子筛催化剂达到90%以上的丙烯选择性和40-50%的丙烷转化率[10.1021/acscatal.9b03527, 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]。 PtSn金属间化合物的形成改变了Pt的电子结构,削弱了丙烷的吸附强度,抑制了氢化裂解/焦化途径 - 所有这些都提高了选择性[10.1021/jacs.6b08705]。然而,PtSn活性相的烧结仍然导致活性逐渐损失。

总的来说,虽然Cr2O3和Ga2O3等金属氧化物表现出高的丙烷脱氢固有活性,但它们的实际稳定性和选择性仍然低于负载PtSn催化剂。然而,通过新的合成策略和金属支撑界面稳定高活性的Cr2O3和Ga2O3表面存在机会。这可能使金属氧化物接近PtSn的性能,同时避免了贵金属的高成本和稀缺。

综合理解与前景展望:鉴于所调研的进展和结果,进一步的研究可以关注:

1) 新的合成方法来稳定本质活泼的Cr2O3和Ga2O3表面,如形貌控制,表面钝化或固定在保护性支撑物上。

2) 设计具有双功能的金属氧化物催化剂,将高活性位点(如Cr,Ga)与提高选择性位点(如Sn,Zr)相结合。这可能通过精确优化组成和结构来实现PtSn级别的性能。

3) 原位光谱和微观动力学建模,以提供有前景的金属氧化物上活性位点、反应中间体和失活机理的分子水平信息。这可以指导优化的PDH催化剂的合理设计。

4) 开发诸如金属有机框架或碳纳米材料等新型支撑材料,与活性金属氧化物相协同增强热稳定性和水解稳定性。

5) 对PDH金属氧化物催化剂进行经济性和环境影响评估,以确定与现有体系相比的技术和商业可行性。
