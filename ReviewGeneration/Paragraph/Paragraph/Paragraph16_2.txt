		Based on the detailed analysis of the references provided [10.1021/acscatal.3c00548, 10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, 10.1021/acscatal.1c04092, 10.1016/j.jechem.2020.08.058, 10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1021/jacs.1c10261, 10.1016/j.jechem.2017.09.035, 10.1038/s41467-022-30522-1], bimetallic and alloy Pt catalysts with Sn, Ga, Zn have demonstrated improved propane dehydrogenation performance in terms of activity, selectivity and stability compared to monometallic Pt catalysts. Adding Sn helps suppress hydrogenolysis and coke formation, thereby enhancing selectivity [10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00548]. Pt-Ga forms highly stable intermetallic compounds, resisting sintering and coking over extended reaction periods [10.1016/j.jechem.2020.08.058]. Alloying with Zn allows tuning of the Pt ensemble size and electronic structure to achieve optimal sites for C-H activation while avoiding overbinding C3 species [10.1021/jacs.1c10261].  
		
		Key structural characteristics influencing propane dehydrogenation performance have been identified. Subnanometric PtSn clusters in MFI zeolite channels give 10 times higher activity than PtSn nanoparticles, attributed to optimized Pt-Sn interactions [10.1021/acscatal.3c00548]. Isolated Pt atoms stabilized by Zn complexes in the framework of dealuminated zeolite Beta achieve remarkable stability and near equilibrium conversion [10.1021/jacs.1c10261]. PtZn intermetallic nanoparticles in mesostructured MFI nanosheets allow breaking diffusion limitations to attain high productivity [10.1021/acscatal.1c04092].
		
		The appropriate Sn/Pt ratio has also been determined to achieve optimal dehydrogenation activity and propylene selectivity. For PtSn clusters on alumina, Pt/Sn ~ 3 gave moderate turnover frequencies; equal Pt/Sn ratios resulted in negligible activity [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]. Increasing Sn/Pt from 0 to 1.5 increased propane conversion over PtSn/Al2O3 before excess Sn became detrimental [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]. These trends confirm computationally predicted volcano-type dependence of rate on Sn content [10.1016/j.jcat.2018.09.006].  
		
		※※※
		Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The collected insights on bimetallic Pt catalysts highlight promising design paradigms that may stimulate further advancements. Creating isolated single-sites in zeolites, strategic selection of secondary metals, compartmentalizing ultrasmall nanoclusters, and interface engineering with unconventional supports each offer tunable modules to continue optimizing propane dehydrogenation systems. Additionally, operando spectroscopies [10.1038/s41467-022-30522-1] and multi-scale modeling may facilitate fundamental comprehension to inform rational catalyst formulation targeting industrially relevant process intensification. Broader deployment of high-throughput experimentation and data-driven approaches can also accelerate identification of novel compositions and metastable phases with targeted functionality.

<Chinese>
		根据所提供参考文献的详细分析[10.1021/acscatal.3c00548, 10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, 10.1021/acscatal.1c04092, 10.1016/j.jechem.2020.08.058, 10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1021/jacs.1c10261, 10.1016/j.jechem.2017.09.035, 10.1038/s41467-022-30522-1],与单金属Pt催化剂相比,双金属和合金Pt催化剂与Sn、Ga、Zn的活性、选择性和稳定性方面在丙烷脱氢反应中表现出了改进。添加Sn有助于抑制氢解和焦炭形成,从而提高选择性[10.1016/j.cej.2014.02.084, 10.1021/acscatal.3c00548]。 Pt-Ga形成高度稳定的金属间化合物,能够抵抗烧结和结焦,延长反应周期[10.1016/j.jechem.2020.08.058]。与Zn合金允许调节Pt组装体大小和电子结构,以实现C-H活化的最佳位点,同时避免过度结合C3物种[10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。

		确定了影响丙烷脱氢性能的关键结构特征。 MFI分子筛渠道中的亚纳米PtSn簇,活性比PtSn纳米粒子高10倍,这是由于Pt-Sn相互作用的优化所致[10.1021/acscatal.3c00548]。脱硝硅胺Beta分子筛框架中Zn复合物稳定的隔离Pt原子实现了显著的稳定性和接近平衡的转化率[10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。 中孔MFI纳米片支撑的PtZn金属间化合物纳米粒子允许突破扩散限制,实现高生产力[10.1021/acscatal.1c04092]。

		Sn/Pt的适当比率也已确定为实现最佳脱氢活性和丙烯选择性。 对于氧化铝上PtSn簇,Pt/Sn~3给出了适中的周转频率; 相等的Pt/Sn比率导致可忽略的活性[10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。 在PtSn/Al2O3上,Sn/Pt从0增加到1.5,丙烷转化率增加,过量的Sn则不利[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.09.016]。 这些趋势证实了Sn含量与速率之间依赖关系的计算机预测火山式依赖关系[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.09.006]。

		※※※
		综合理解和前瞻展望:这些关于双金属Pt催化剂的见解强调了有前景的设计范式,这些范式可能刺激进一步的发展。 在分子筛中创建隔离的单一位点,战略性选择次要金属,隔间纳米簇,以及与非传统载体的界面工程,都提供了可调模块,以继续优化丙烷脱氢系统。 此外,原位光谱技术[10.1038/s41467-022-30522-1]和多尺度建模可以促进基本理解以指导用于工业相关过程强化的合理催化剂配方的选择。 高通量实验和数据驱动方法的更广泛部署也可以加快鉴定具有目标功能的新组成和亚稳态相的速度。
