Based on the details extracted from the various files, several studies employ advanced microscopy techniques to gain insights into metal-support interfaces in propane dehydrogenation catalysts.

One study utilizes iDPC-STEM imaging to reveal isolated cobalt species embedded within T1 and T3 sites of the MFI framework, confirming their distorted tetrahedral coordination environment [10.1021/jacs.2c02636]. Another work combines HR-HAADF-STEM and iDPC to precisely locate subnanometric platinum clusters confined within the sinusoidal channels of purely siliceous MFI zeolite [10.1038/s41563-019-0430-4]. Aberration-corrected HAADF-STEM enables direct visualization of periodic [PtZn4] ensembles on the PtZn intermetallic (110) facet, exposing isolated single Pt sites [10.1016/j.chempr.2020.10.008]. Isolated nickel atoms are also imaged by HAADF-STEM, appearing as bright dots uniformly dispersed on the alumina surface [10.1021/acscatal.2c03240].

Additional examples showcase the ability of electron microscopy methods to probe metal-support interactions. TEM reveals differences in platinum particle size, dispersion and distribution on zeolites prepared by conventional versus ultrasound-assisted impregnation [10.1016/j.ultsonch.2010.04.005]. STEM-EDX documents the migration of gallium from the support to alloy with platinum nanoparticles [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.017]. SEM-EDX maps a homogeneous distribution of boron on the zeolite, even at 8 wt% loading without detectable aggregates [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034].

The stability afforded by strong metal-support anchoring is also evidenced. HR-TEM shows encapsulated platinum clusters within zeolites maintain 1 nm size after reaction, while platinum on silica aggregates from 2.3 to 6.1 nm [10.1002/anie.201805150]. Aberration-corrected STEM reveals negligible changes in size or morphology of 0.9-1.6 nm Pt-Sn clusters on alumina nanosheets after propane dehydrogenation [10.1002/anie.201507119].

Collectively, these works highlight the ability of cutting-edge electron microscopy tools to extract specific structural details related to active site location, morphology, dispersion and stability in working catalysts. The insights gained contribute to rational design strategies focused on optimizing metal-support interfaces.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Advanced microscopy has clearly demonstrated its potential to extract precise mechanistic details regarding metal-support interfacial sites in propane dehydrogenation catalysts. However, we are only beginning to scratch the surface of what is possible. Emerging techniques like 4D-STEM with simultaneous spatial and spectroscopic information hold promise for even greater revelations [10.1021/acscatal.1c02553]. Machine learning integration also portends more automated analysis of complex multi-modal data [10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7]. 

An expansive perspective should also consider research directions focused on interfacial characterization under realistic working environments. In one example, AC-HAADF-STEM images track changes to atomically dispersed tin and platinum on alumina from ambient conditions through reduction and catalytic cycling [10.1016/S1872-2067(23)64402-X]. We must seek out additional opportunities to probe interfacial structure-function relationships in situ. New discoveries along these frontiers will undoubtedly provide profound clarity intodegredation pathways and open new stability engineering strategies.

根据从各种文件中提取的详细信息,几项研究采用先进的显微技术来深入了解丙烷脱氢催化剂中的金属载体界面。

一项研究利用 iDPC-STEM 成像技术显示出嵌入 MFI 框架内 T1 和 T3 位点的隔离钴种,确认它们扭曲的四面体配位环境 [10.1021/jacs.2c02636]。另一篇论文将 HR-HAADF-STEM 和 iDPC 技术结合使用,精确定位纯二氧化硅 MFI 分子筛内部正弦波通道内的亚纳米白金簇 [10.1038/s41563-019-0430-4]。 spherical aberration 校正 HAADF-STEM 使人们能够直接观察 PtZn 金属间化合物 (110) 表面上的周期性 [PtZn4] 集合体,暴露出隔离的单原子 Pt 位点 [10.1016/j.chempr.2020.10.008]。 HAADF-STEM也成像了光滑的镍原子,显示它们均匀分散在氧化铝表面上 [10.1021/acscatal.2c03240]。

其他实例展示了电子显微技术探测金属载体相互作用的能力。TEM 揭示了采用常规方法与超声辅助浸渍制备的沸石上铂颗粒大小、分散性和分布的差异
[10.1016/j.ultsonch.2010.04.005]。 STEM-EDX 记录了载体上镓的迁移与铂纳米颗粒的合金形成 [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.017]。 SEM-EDX 成像显示硼在沸石载体上有均匀分布,甚至在 8 重量%的负载量下也没有检测到团聚体 [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.04.034]。

强大的金属载体锚定提供的稳定性也得到了证明。 HR-TEM显示铂簇在分子筛内保持 1 nm 的尺寸稳定,而二氧化硅上的铂团聚体从
2.3 nm 增长到 6.1 nm [10.1002/anie.201805150]。spherical aberration校正STEM显示 0.9-1.6 nm 的 Pt-Sn 簇在氧化铝纳米片上经过丙烷脱氢后几乎没有发生大小或形态变化 [10.1002/anie.201507119]。

总的来说,这些研究强调了尖端电子显微技术的能力,可以从反应条件下的催化剂中提取与活性位点位置、形态、分散性和稳定性有关的具体结构细节。获得的见解有助于确定针对优化金属载体界面而设计合理方法。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:高级显微技术在从丙烷脱氢催化剂的金属载体界面位点提取精密的机理细节方面已经证明了其潜力。 然而我们刚刚开始揭开可能性的一角。 像4D-STEM这样同时具有空间和光谱信息的新出现技术预示着取得更大的信息[10.1021/acscatal.1c02553]。 机器学习的应用也预示着对复杂多模式数据进行更加自动化的分析[10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7]。

我们还应该考虑到关注在真实工作环境下界面的表征的研究方向。 在一个例子中,AC-HAADF-STEM 图像跟踪氧化铝上原子分散锡和铂从环境条件到还原和催化循环过程中的变化[10.1016/S1872-2067(23)64402-X]。 我们必须寻找更多的机会,原位探索催化剂的结构-功能关系。 在这些前沿领域的新发现无疑会对降解途径提供深刻的理解,并开辟新的稳定性工程策略。
