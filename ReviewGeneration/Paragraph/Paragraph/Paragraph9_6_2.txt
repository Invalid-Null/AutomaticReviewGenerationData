Based on the details extracted from the file, there is evidence showing how tuning oxidation states, oxygen mobility, and acid-base properties of metal oxides affects propane dehydrogenation performance. 

For vanadium oxide catalysts, partly reduced V3+/V4+ species have been identified as the active sites for propane dehydrogenation without oxygen introduction [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]. The chemical states of bridge oxygen in the VO bond also play critical roles, with weaker VOx-support interactions leading to lower electron density on bridge oxygen and enhanced capability to rupture the C-H bond [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]. Additionally, CO2 facilitates propane activation by re-oxidizing VOx to create more active lattice oxygen species [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878].

For chromium oxide catalysts, tuning the oxidation state between Cr3+ and Cr6+ affects performance. Active Cr3+ sites are generated during the reaction, and can be reoxidized to Cr6+ by O2 or CO2 treatment to regenerate catalyst activity [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014]. The dispersion and evolution of Cr3+/Cr2+ species with loading also determines activity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999].

For gallium oxide catalysts, the surface acid site density correlates with activity, with β-Ga2O3 showing the highest density and activity [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017]. Tetrahedrally coordinated Ga3+ sites have also been identified as the active Lewis acid sites [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999].

For titania catalysts, oxygen vacancies and tetracoordinated Ti4c sites generated during H2 reduction boost catalytic activity by altering the binding state of propane molecules during dehydrogenation [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00818].

For zinc-zirconia catalysts, increased oxygen mobility from synergistic effects between ZnO and ZrO2 facilitates removal of lattice oxygen to generate active sites like Zncus and Zrcus [DOI: 10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074]. Acidity and basicity also affect selectivity.

根据文件中提取的详细信息,有证据表明控制金属氧化物的氧化态、氧迁移性和酸碱性如何影响丙烷脱氢反应性能。

对于钒氧化物催化剂,部分还原的V3+/V4+物种已被确定为无氧参与下丙烷脱氢反应的活性位点 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]。 VO键中的桥氧的化学态也发挥关键作用,VOx与支撑体的相互作用越弱,桥氧上的电子密度越低,断裂C-H键的能力就越强 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]。 此外,CO2通过使VOx再氧化来产生更多活性晶格氧物种,促进丙烷的活化[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c00878]。

对于铬氧化物催化剂,在Cr3+和Cr6+之间调谐氧化态会影响性能。 反应过程中产生活性Cr3+位点,可以通过O2或CO2处理将其再氧化为Cr6+,以恢复催化剂活性[DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014]。 Cr3+/Cr2+物种的分散和载量变化也决定了活性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。

对于镓氧化物催化剂,表面酸中心密度与活性有关,β-Ga2O3表面酸中心密度最高,活性也最高[DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017]。 正四面体配位的Ga3+位点也已被确定为活性路易斯酸位点[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。

对于二氧化钛催化剂,H2还原过程中生成的氧空位和四配位Ti4c位点通过改变丙烷分子在脱氢过程中的结合状态来增强催化活性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00818]。

对于氧化锌-氧化锆催化剂,ZnO与ZrO2之间的协同作用增加了氧的迁移性,有利于去除晶格氧以生成活性位点,如Zncus和Zrcus [DOI: 10.1021/acssuschemeng.1c03074]。酸碱性也会影响选择性。
