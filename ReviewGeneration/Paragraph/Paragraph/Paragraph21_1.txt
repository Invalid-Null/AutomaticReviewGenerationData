 <English>

The article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816] investigates tuning zeolite Si/Al ratio by dealumination to create silanol nests in Beta zeolites, enhancing metal dispersion and stabilization.

Additionally, the study with DOI [10.1021/jacs.2c03941] evaluates propane dehydrogenation over CHA zeolites with varying Si/Al ratios of 5, 12 and 25. It demonstrates that extra-framework Ga+ sites are formed regardless of Si/Al ratio. While reaction rates decrease over time, propylene selectivity remains stable at 96% when the Ga/Al ratio is properly tuned.

Furthermore, the research published in [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024] explores the effects of tuning TS-1 crystal size from 100 nm to 5.7 μm. Smaller sizes introduce mesoporosity, improving diffusion and avoiding coking. This significantly enhances conversion, selectivity and catalyst lifetime. 

Finally, the article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808] investigates mesoporous MFI zeolites. The mesoporous structure and abundant silanol groups are shown to facilitate intermetallic alloy nanoparticle formation.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The examined studies reveal promising directions in tuning zeolite properties to enhance propane dehydrogenation performance. Strategies like mesoporosity incorporation, crystal size reduction, and dealumination demonstrate clear structure-function relationships in improving selectivity, conversion, and stability. 

Future research might explore combining these strategies to optimize multifunctional zeolite catalysts. For example, nanostructured mesoporous zeolites with optimized acidity could balance activity, selectivity and longevity. High-throughput computational screening could accelerate identification of optimal zeolite topologies, compositions and textures. Operando spectroscopy and microscopy could provide fundamental insights to guide rational design. Cross-laboratory collaborative efforts, coupled with industry partnerships and pilot-scale testing, can facilitate translation of promising laboratory developments into commercial implementations.

<Chinese>

DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]的文章通过脱铝来调节Beta分子筛的Si/Al比,在Beta分子筛中创建硅醇巢,增强了金属的分散和稳定性。

此外,DOI [10.1021/jacs.2c03941] 的研究评估了Si/Al比为5、12和25的CHA分子筛上的丙烷脱氢反应。它证明,不管Si/Al比如何,额外的Ga+位点都会形成。尽管反应速率随时间下降,但在Ga/Al比适当调节的条件下,丙烯选择性保持在96%。

而且,发表在[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024]的研究探索了调节TS-1晶体大小从100纳米到5.7微米的效果。较小的尺寸引入了介孔,改善了扩散,避免了结焦。这显著提高了转化率,选择性和催化剂的使用寿命。

最后,DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]的文章研究了介孔MFI分子筛。 介孔结构和丰富的硅醇基团被证明有利于金属间化合物纳米粒子的形成。

※※※

综合理解和未来展望:所检查的研究揭示了调节分子筛性质以提高丙烷脱氢性能的有前途的方向。介孔引入、晶体大小缩小和脱铝等策略明确了结构与性能之间的关系,改善了选择性、转化率和稳定性。

未来的研究可能会探索将这些策略组合以优化多功能分子筛催化剂。例如,纳米结构介孔分子筛与优化的酸度可能会平衡活性、选择性和长久性。高通量计算筛选可以加速识别优化的分子筛拓扑结构、组成和纹理。原位光谱和显微技术可以提供指导理性设计的基本见解。跨实验室的协作努力,以及与工业合作伙伴的伙伴关系和试验规模的测试,可以促进有前途的实验室开发成果向商业实现的转化。
