Three major catalyst classes explored for propane dehydrogenation are introduced, including metal oxides, supported metals, and zeolites. 

Metal oxides such as VOx/CrOx supported on MCM-41 and SBA-15 mesoporous silica have been widely investigated for propane dehydrogenation ([Placeholder_of_DOI2]). An example is Sn-incorporated hexagonal mesoporous silica which achieved 40% propane conversion with 90% propylene selectivity ([Placeholder_of_DOI2]). Optimized Co-incorporated mesoporous silica attained 37% propane conversion and 96% propylene selectivity ([Placeholder_of_DOI2]).

Supported metals catalysts like PtSn alloys have also shown promise, with examples like silica-supported PtSn ([Placeholder_of_DOI6]). Traditional supported Pt and Cr oxide catalysts have multiatom sites leading to strong propylene adsorption, prompting recent interest in single-atom catalysts ([Placeholder_of_DOI7]). 

Zeolites such as H-Beta and iron-silicate zeolites have been explored. H-Beta activates propane to propylene and other products via an acid site redox cycle ([Placeholder_of_DOI4]). Isolated Fe sites in iron-silicates are suggested to facilitate propane dehydrogenation through a redox mechanism generating radical cations ([Placeholder_of_DOI4]).

The progress reviewed reveals metal oxides, supported metals, and zeolites as promising catalyst classes for propane dehydrogenation, with recent attention on single-atom and multifunctional systems. However, widespread utilization is hampered by stability and selectivity challenges. Further research could focus on novel synthetic strategies to optimize active site isolation and precision in multifunctional catalysts. Operando spectroscopic techniques probing reaction intermediates at these sites could offer insights to guide rational design. Machine learning methods may also accelerate identification of optimized compositions and architectures. Broader adoption will necessitate addressing scalability and economic considerations.

文章介绍了三大类烷烃脱氢催化剂,包括金属氧化物、载体金属和沸石。

如载在MCM-41和SBA-15介孔硅上的VOx/CrOx金属氧化物,在丙烷脱氢反应中有广泛研究([占位符_DOI2])。例子有六方介孔硅嵌入锡,获得了40%的丙烷转化率和90%的丙烯选择性([占位符_DOI2])。优化的钴掺杂介孔硅达到了37%的丙烷转化率和96%的丙烯选择性([占位符_DOI2])。


PtSn合金等载体金属催化剂也展现了前景,例如硅载PtSn ([占位符_DOI6])。传统的Pt和Cr氧化物载体催化剂具有多原子活性位点,会导致丙烯强吸附,最近开始关注单原子催化剂([占位符_DOI7])。


如H-Beta和铁硅酸盐沸石等沸石也有研究。H-Beta可以通过氧化还原循环将丙烷转化为丙烯和其他产物([占位符_DOI4])。铁硅酸盐中隔离的铁位点被认为可以通过生成自由基阳离子的氧化还原机理促进丙烷脱氢([占位符_DOI4])。


※※※

文献综述了金属氧化物、载体金属和沸石等有前景的丙烷脱氢催化剂类别,最近关注单原子和多功能体系。但是,稳定性和选择性挑战限制了其广泛应用。未来研究可以关注新合成策略,优化活性位点的隔离和多功能催化剂的精准设计。原位光谱技术可以检测这些位点上的中间体,为理性设计提供信息。机器学习方法也可以加速优化组成和结构的确定。更广泛的采用还需要解决可扩展性和经济性问题。
