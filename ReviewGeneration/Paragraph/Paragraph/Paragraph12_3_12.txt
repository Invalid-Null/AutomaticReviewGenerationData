 <English>
Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most successful and widely used catalysts for commercial PDH processes [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]. An alumina-supported Pt catalyst is considered vital for its superior ability to activate paraffinic C-H bonds while minimizing C-C bond cleavage [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. Data shows Pt/Al2O3 catalysts modified with metal oxide overcoats applied by atomic layer deposition can increase PDH yield and selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. Other studies found supported Pt on boron nitride [DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008] and Ge-UTL zeolite [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7] also demonstrate excellent PDH performance. Additionally, the effect of cluster size, Sn alloying, and supports have been investigated for supported Pt clusters [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527].

A few studies have explored Pd-based catalysts. Pd is reported to have high intrinsic activity for C-H bond activation but the catalysts remain expensive [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. No specific PDH performance data is provided.

Similarly, Ir is stated to be intrinsically active but expensive. No Ir PDH data is given [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002].

In summary, Pt-based catalysts, especially on supports like Al2O3, BN, and zeolites have been widely studied for PDH. Pd and Ir are theoretically active but likely cost prohibitive. Further insights into optimizing Pt dispension, stability, and selectivity are still needed.

※※※ 
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings discussed in the file highlight supported Pt catalysts as the most promising option for propane dehydrogenation among the mono-metallic precious metals. However, deactivation issues due to coking and agglomeration persist. This suggests an opportunity to explore novel supports, promoters, and process intensification strategies to further enhance stability. Additionally, fundamental investigations into metal-support interfacial effects could offer new pathways to tune selectivity. Broader incorporation of operando spectroscopic techniques paired with kinetic and computational modeling will likely provide crucial molecular-level insights to guide design of optimized PDH catalysts. Machine learning methods also show promise to accelerate identification of active sites and optimal catalyst architectures.

<Chinese>
根据文件提供的详细信息,已经有几项研究探索了支持单金属Pt、Pd和Ir催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

报道称,Pt基催化剂是商业PDH过程中最成功和最广泛使用的催化剂之一[DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。氧化铝支持的Pt催化剂因其优越的抗碳烃C-H键的激活能力和抑制C-C键断裂的性能而被认为是必不可少的[DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。数据显示,通过原子层沉积法施加金属氧化物涂层的改性Pt/Al2O3催化剂可以提高PDH的收率和选择性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。其他研究还发现,氮化硼[DOI: 10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]和Ge-UTL沸石[DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]支撑的Pt也表现出优异的PDH性能。 此外,还研究了Pt簇大小、Sn合金化和载体对支撑Pt簇的影响[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c05671, 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]。

少数几项研究探索了Pd基催化剂。报道Pd具有高的本征C-H键激活活性,但催化剂成本仍然高昂[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。没有提供PDH性能的具体数据。

同样,文献报道Ir也具有高的内在活性,但成本高昂。没有提供Ir PDH数据[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。

总之,Pt基催化剂,特别是在Al2O3、BN和沸石等载体上的催化剂已经被广泛研究用于PDH。Pd和Ir在理论上是活性的,但可能由于成本过高而限制其应用。 Pt分散度、稳定性和选择性的优化仍需要进一步的研究。


※※※
综合理解与未来展望:文件中讨论的结果突出了Pt基催化剂在单金属贵金属中是最有前景的PDH催化剂选择。 但是,由于炭化和团聚引起的失活问题仍然存在。 这表明有机会探索新的载体、助剂和过程强化策略来进一步增强稳定性。 此外,关于金属载体界面效应的基础研究可以提供调节选择性的新途径。 更广泛地采用工作体系光谱技术与动力学和计算建模相结合,可能会提供引导优化PDH催化剂设计的关键分子水平的见解。 机器学习方法在加速识别活性位点和优化催化剂体系结构方面也显示出很大的前景。
