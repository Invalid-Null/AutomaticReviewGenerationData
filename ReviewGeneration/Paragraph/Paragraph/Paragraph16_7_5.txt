Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

It is well established that alloying Pt with secondary metals like Sn, Ga, and Zn can significantly improve the performance of catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018]. The synergistic effects arising from the bi-functional nature of these alloy combinations enhance activity, selectivity, and stability compared to monometallic Pt catalysts.

Pt-Sn alloy catalysts are the most extensively studied system for PDH. Numerous studies have demonstrated that incorporating Sn serves to depress hydrogenolysis and coke formation side reactions while minimizing Pt ensemble sizes, thereby boosting propylene selectivity [10.1021/science.abg7894, 10.1016/S1872-2067(23)64402-X, 10.1002/anie.201507119]. For instance, PtSn intermetallic nanoparticles supported on silica achieved >99% propylene selectivity without the need for hydrogen dilution, offering a major advance over conventional Pt catalysts [10.1126/science.abg7894]. The performance was found to depend critically on the formation of ordered PtSn sites. Other work showed that an optimal Pt:Sn ratio of 2:1 maintains good dehydrogenation activity while a 1:1 ratio leads to catalyst inactivation [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]. The Pt-Sn system has been commercialized for PDH in the Oleflex process developed by UOP [10.1016/j.jechem.2020.08.058].

Investigations on Ga and Zn promoted Pt catalysts are relatively nascent but display encouraging potential. Pt-Ga intermetallic nanoparticles in hierarchical zeolites maintained 8% propane conversion for over 30 days on-stream [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. Isolated PtZn4-6 sites stabilized by grafting Zn into the framework of dealuminated Beta zeolite also delivered remarkable stability alongside high initial activity and >90% propylene selectivity [10.1021/jacs.1c10261]. The appropriately configured PtM alloy ensemble serves to tune the geometric and electronic environment of Pt active sites. Furthermore, metal-support interactions help disperse the alloy nanoparticles and stabilize them against sintering during prolonged reaction [10.1016/j.fuproc.2009.07.019, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01808].

In summary, alloying Pt with promoters like Sn, Ga and Zn in the optimal stoichiometry modifies the electron density, ensemble requirements and binding strengths of surface intermediates in a manner conducive for selective alkane dehydrogenation. The resultant structural and electronic perturbations mitigate side reactions while sustaining dehydrogenation turnover. Further work is still needed to fully elucidate the intricacies of these bimetallic combinations and translate the fundamental advances into large-scale PDH processes.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Taking into consideration the advancements and findings discussed in the file, there lies an opportunity to explore emerging fields and innovative methodologies. Future research endeavors might focus on:

- Operando spectroscopic techniques to track dynamic changes in alloy nanoparticle speciation and correlate with catalytic function
- Machine learning based high-throughput computational screening of vast composition-structure-function phase space to discover optimal catalyst formulations

The cutting-edge insights into tailored bimetallic catalyst synthesis provide a foundation to actualize next-generation PDH processes through persisted scientific inquiries across interdisciplinary horizons.

双金属和合金催化剂—— Pt与Sn、Ga、Zn合金化如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

已经确立的事实是,Pt与Sn、Ga和Zn等辅助金属合金化可以显着提高丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂的性能[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816, 10.1016/j.jcat.2010.09.018]。这些双金属组合所产生的协同效应提高了活性、选择性和稳定性,与单金属Pt催化剂相比。

Pt-Sn合金催化剂是PDH中研究最广泛的体系。许多研究表明,掺入Sn有助于抑制氢化和焦炭生成副反应,同时最小化Pt团簇大小,从而提高丙烯选择性[10.1021/science.abg7894,10.1016/S1872-2067(23)64402-X,10.1002/anie.201507119]。例如,负载在二氧化硅上的PtSn间金属纳米粒子实现了>99%的丙烯选择性,无需氢稀释,这代表了对传统Pt催化剂的重大进步 [10.1126/science.abg7894]。发现性能的提高与有序PtSn位点的形成紧密相关。其他工作表明,Pt:Sn = 2:1的最佳比例维持了较好的脱氢活性,而1:1的比例导致催化剂失活[10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。Pt-Sn体系已经被UOP公司商业化用于PDH,即Oleflex过程[10.1016/j.jechem.2020.08.058]。

使用Ga和Zn掺杂的Pt催化剂的研究相对较新,但显示了令人鼓舞的潜力。 层状硅酸盐支持的Pt-Ga间金属纳米粒子保持了30天内8%的丙烷转化率[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。通过在去铝Beta分子筛的框架中掺入Zn稳定的PtZn4-6孤立活性中心也展示出色的稳定性和较高的初始活性及>90%的丙烯选择性[10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。 适当构型的PtM合金集群有助于调节Pt活性位点的几何和电子环境。 此外,金属与载体之间的相互作用有助于分散合金纳米粒子并在长时间反应中抑制结焦[10.1016/j.fuproc.2009.07.019, 10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]。

总之,在最佳化学计量比下,Pt与Sn、Ga和Zn等助剂形成合金会改变表面中间体的电子密度、集团要求和结合力,这种改变有利于选择性碳氢键脱氢。所产生的结构和电子扰动可以抑制副反应,同时维持脱氢转化。为了全面阐明这些双金属催化剂组合的微妙性质,并将这些基础发现转化为大规模PDH工艺,还需要进一步的工作。

综合理解与未来展望:考虑到文件中讨论的进步和发现,我们有机会探索新兴领域和创新方法。未来的研究工作可能会集中在:

- 设计三元和四元Pt合金组合以实现高于双金属体系的协同助剂效应

- 研究MOF和共价有机框架等新型载体,这些载体允许对活性位点的隔离和稳定性进行精确控制

- 操作光谱技术跟踪合金纳米粒子规iation的动态变化并与催化功能相关

- 基于机器学习的高通量计算筛选广泛的成分-结构-性能相图,发现优化的催化剂配方

- 开发具有超高灵敏度和分辨率的新型表征工具,深入探究隔离的合金活性位点

定制双金属催化剂合成的前沿见解为通过跨学科方式进行科学探询,实现下一代PDH工艺奠定了基础。
