 <English>

The article with DOI 10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008 presents microkinetic modeling efforts that have provided insights into propane dehydrogenation kinetics. Specifically, it used descriptors based on formation energies of key surface species to screen and rank intermetallic compound catalysts for predicted propane dehydrogenation activity and propylene selectivity [1]. Rate equations were also utilized to construct selectivity maps as the ratio of propylene production rate to propane consumption rate [1]. 

Additional microkinetic modeling insights were presented in the article with DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00939. This study used mean-field microkinetic models along with DFT-calculated parameters to simulate propane dehydrogenation over PtSn surfaces. It discussed rate equations, coverages, turnover frequencies, selectivities, and identified the rate-controlling steps [1][2][3].

The article with DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00889 also provided microkinetic modeling results for single-atom catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. It carried out microkinetic simulations with the CATKINAS package based on DFT data to evaluate the practical applicability and kinetics of several catalysts under industrial conditions [1][2][3].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The microkinetic modeling efforts discussed have offered valuable glimpses into propane dehydrogenation mechanisms, kinetics and catalyst screening. Going forward, integration of machine learning with microkinetic simulations could further enhance predictive capabilities and catalyst design. Exploring more complex models accounting for lateral interactions and multiscale simulations may also advance fundamental insights.

<Chinese>
DOI 10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008的文章通过使用基于关键表面物种的形成能的描述符来屏幕和排名互金属化合物催化剂,为预期的丙烷脱氢活性和丙烯选择性提供了微动力学建模的见解[1]。速率方程也被利用来构建选择图,作为丙烯生产速率与丙烷消耗速率的比率[1]。

DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00939的另一篇文章中也提供了额外的微动力学建模见解。这项研究使用平均场微动力学模型以及DFT计算的参数来模拟PtSn表面上的丙烷脱氢。它讨论了速率方程、覆盖率、周转频率、选择性,并确定了控制速率的步骤[1][2][3]。

DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00889的文章还提供了单原子催化剂丙烷脱氢的微动力学建模结果。它使用CATKINAS软件包根据DFT数据进行了微动力学模拟,以评估几种催化剂在工业条件下的实际适用性和动力学[1][2][3]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:讨论的微动力学建模工作对丙烷脱氢机理、动力学和催化剂筛选提供了宝贵的见解。展望未来,机器学习与微动力学模拟的整合可以进一步增强预测能力和催化剂设计。探索更复杂的模型以考虑侧向相互作用和多尺度模拟也可以推进基本见解。
