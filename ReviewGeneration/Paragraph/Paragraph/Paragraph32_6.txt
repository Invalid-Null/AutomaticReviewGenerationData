<English>
Thermally induced deactivation remains a major challenge for propane dehydrogenation catalysts despite decades of commercialization, as noted by reference [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]. Exploring catalysts with improved stability and propylene selectivity is still an urgent need to meet industrial demand. Replacing expensive platinum-based catalysts with cost-effective alternatives is also a key long-term goal, though the intrinsic mechanism understanding how promoters and modifiers affect performance requires further research [10.1021/acscatal.1c02621, 10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1].

Definitively characterizing the identity and geometry of active sites in promising catalysts like gallium-modified zeolites remains an open challenge, limited by an ability to probe real-world low metal loadings [10.1021/acscatal.1c05724]. Coke formation kinetics, confinement effects, and side reaction suppression also require better fundamental understanding to guide next-generation catalyst architectural designs [10.1021/acscatal.1c00331]. 

Computational screening of unprecedented single atom and cluster configurations could uncover innovative pathways beyond incremental improvements [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. Operando spectroscopy harnessing advanced synchrotron radiation sources may finally bridge structure-activity relationships that to date have relied extensively on ex situ techniques.

Future research endeavors might focus on in silico catalyst screening leveraging machine learning, operando characterization of working catalysts, and bioinspired approaches mimicking enzymatic function.

尽管商业化几十年,但热诱导的失活仍然是丙烷脱氢催化剂面临的主要挑战[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103]。 为满足工业需求,继续探索稳定性高和丙烯选择性高的催化剂仍然迫在眉睫。 用成本效益高的替代品取代昂贵的铂基催化剂也是一个关键的长期目标,尽管促进剂和修饰剂如何影响性能的本质机理还需要进一步研究[10.1021/acscatal.1c02621,10.1038/s41929-022-00885-1]。

确定有前景的催化剂(如改性沸石中的镓)中活性位点的身份和几何构型仍然是一个未解决的挑战,目前主要受制于无法在实际低金属负载条件下进行探测的能力 [10.1021/acscatal.1c05724]。 焦炭生成动力学、限域效应以及副反应抑制也需要更好的基本理解,以指导下一代催化剂体系结构设计[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331]。

计算筛选前所未有的单原子和簇配置可以发现超越渐进改进的创新途径[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。 利用先进的同步辐射光源进行的原位光谱技术或许终于可以构建迄今主要依赖于体外技术的结构-活性关系。
