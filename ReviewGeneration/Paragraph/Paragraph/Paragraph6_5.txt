 <English>

The article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939] presents microkinetic models of propane dehydrogenation using DFT-derived parameters. It discusses rate equations, surface coverages, turnover frequencies, selectivities, and rate-controlling steps.

Additionally, the study with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889] presents microkinetic modeling efforts using the CATKINAS package to evaluate the kinetics and practical applicability of single-atom catalysts for propane dehydrogenation under industrial conditions. 

Moreover, the research published in [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916] indicates that microkinetic modeling on Pt alloy surfaces has assessed propane dehydrogenation barriers as a proxy for activity, along with a selectivity descriptor, revealing trade-offs useful for identifying optimal catalysts.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The microkinetic modeling efforts discussed have provided valuable insights into propane dehydrogenation kinetics over different catalysts. Building on these advancements, future work could further optimize model parameters, incorporate additional descriptors, and explore the kinetics for emerging catalyst classes. Opportunities also exist to couple modeling with in situ/operando studies to refine model predictions and elucidate complex transient phenomena during propane dehydrogenation. Overall, microkinetic modeling will continue serving as an indispensable tool guiding the design and development of superior catalysts.

<Chinese>

DOI为[10.1021/acscatal.3c00939]的文章使用DFT导出的参数提出了丙烷脱氢的微观动力学模型。它讨论了速率方程、表面覆盖率、周转频率、选择性和速控步骤。

此外,DOI为[10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]的研究利用CATKINAS软件包对工业条件下单原子催化剂丙烷脱氢的动力学及实际适用性进行了微观动力学建模。

而DOI为[10.1021/acscatal.1c01916]的研究表明,对Pt合金表面进行的微观动力学建模评估了丙烷脱氢的活性指标,以及选择性描述符,揭示了有利于识别最佳催化剂的权衡关系。


※※※
综合理解与未来展望:所讨论的微观动力学建模为丙烷在不同催化剂上的脱氢动力学提供了宝贵的洞见。在这些进展的基础上,未来的工作可以进一步优化模型参数,结合更多描述符,并探索新兴催化剂的动力学。同时还有机会将建模与原位/操作过程研究相结合,以提高模型预测的准确性,阐明丙烷脱氢过程中的复杂瞬态现象。总的来说,微观动力学建模将继续发挥不可或缺的作用,以指导设计和开发更优异的催化剂。
