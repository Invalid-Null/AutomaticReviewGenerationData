Based on the details extracted from the references, mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over certain metal oxide catalysts, while several studies did not investigate reaction mechanisms. 

For Cr-based oxides, isolated Cr3+ sites [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1183], Cr3+/Cr2+ sites [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999], and Cr6+/Cr3+/Cr2+ redox cycles [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999] have been suggested as active sites. Coordinatively unsaturated Cr3+ was also proposed as the active site, getting reduced to Cr/Cr2+ [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(15)61042-7]. In situ studies showed Cr(III) generated in-situ from Cr(VI) during reaction over xCrSBA-15 and xCrAl2O3 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014].

For VOx catalysts, monomeric [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.8b04701], dimeric [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.8b04701], and trimeric [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.8b04701] vanadyl, V3+ [DOI: 10.1021/jacs.9b09235], and isolated V5+ reduced to V4+/V3+ [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.02.028] have been proposed as active sites. Lattice oxygen enables initial ODH phase before getting depleted, allowing V3+ to catalyze non-oxidative PDH [DOI: 10.1021/jacs.9b09235]. Sub-monolayer VOx on TiO2 suppresses overoxidation by avoiding bulk lattice oxygen diffusion [DOI: 10.1002/anie.202005968].

Isolated Ga3+ [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.004], Ga+/H+ pairs [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c01497], and tetrahedral Ga3+ [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999] were suggested for Ga/zeolite catalysts, going through propyl and alkoxide intermediates. Heterolytic dissociation pathways were preferred over redox routes [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017].

For ZrO2, two neighboring unsaturated Zr (Zrcus) sites at oxygen vacancies [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012], as well as Zrcus with neighboring lattice oxygen [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201508731] have been proposed. Adding Ga changes it from a stepwise path on ZrO2 to a concerted path with lower barrier on Ga-ZrOx [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002].

MnOx-CeO2 interfaces were suggested to activate propane on MnOx/CeO2 catalysts [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c02782]. Tetracoordinated Ti4c sites adjacent to oxygen vacancies were proposed as key sites for TiOx nanosheets, activating propane dehydrogenation through hydride intermediates [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00818].

Pt/Sn-Beta was found to have isolated Pt clusters stabilized on Sn as active sites [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]. A Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism was described for Pt catalysts with first CH cleavage as rate-limiting step [DOI: 10.1002/advs.202207756].

Several other mechanistic proposals have also been made over specific metal oxide catalysts. However, many studies focused solely on characterization without investigating detailed reaction mechanisms.

根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,已经为某些金属氧化物催化剂上的丙烷脱氢提出了机理场景,而一些研究并没有调查反应机理。

对于以铬为基础的氧化物, 已经提出隔离的 Cr3+ 位点 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1183]、Cr3+/Cr2+ 位点 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999] 和 Cr6+/Cr3+/Cr2+ 氧化还原循环 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999] 是活性位点。非配位饱和的 Cr3+ 也被提议为活性位点,被还原为 Cr/Cr2+ [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(15)61042-7]。原位研究显示,Cr(III) 是在 xCrSBA-15 和 xCrAl2O3 反应过程中从 Cr(VI)生成的 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014]。

对于 VOx 催化剂,单体 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.8b04701]、二聚体 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.8b04701]和三聚体 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.8b04701] 铵盐基团、V3+ [DOI: 10.1021/jacs.9b09235] 和隔离的 V5+ 还原为 V4+/V3+ [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2008.02.028] 已被提出为活性位点。晶格氧使初始的氧化脱氢(ODH)阶段成为可能,在耗尽之前允许 V3+ 催化非氧化脱氢(PDH)[DOI: 10.1021/jacs.9b09235]。载在 TiO2 上的亚单层 VOx 通过避免晶格氧的扩散抑制过氧化 [DOI: 10.1002/anie.202005968]。

在沸石催化剂上建议使用单独的 Ga3+ [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.004]、Ga+/H+ 对 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.1c01497] 和四面体 Ga3+ [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999], 通过丙基和醇根中间体。优选杂解裂合路径而不是氧化还原路径 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2006.02.017]。

对于 ZrO2,已经提出两个相邻的不饱和锆(Zrcus)位点位于氧空位 [DOI: 10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012],以及相邻晶格氧的 Zrcus [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201508731]。添加镓使它从 ZrO2 上的一步机理变为镓掺杂的 ZrOx 上的协奏机理,具有更低的屏障 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。

已经建议 MnOx-CeO2 界面的作用是激活 MnOx/CeO2 催化剂上的丙烷 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c02782]。四配位的 Ti4c 位点毗邻氧空位被提出为 TiOx 纳米片的关键位点,通过氢化物中间体激活丙烷脱氢 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c00818]。

发现 Pt/Sn-Beta 具有稳定在锡上的隔离 Pt 簇作为活性位点 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]。对 Pt 催化剂描述了 Horiuti-Polanyi 机理,第一个 CH 键裂解作为速率控制步骤 [DOI: 10.1002/advs.202207756]。

在特定金属氧化物催化剂上也提出了其他一些机理建议。但是,许多研究只关注特征而不调查详细的反应机理。
