 <English>

Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) is an important industrial process for on-purpose propylene production. Catalysts play a crucial role in enabling this reaction by lowering activation energy barriers. According to the analysis based on references [10.1021/acs.jpclett.1c00331], [10.1021/acscatal.2c01374], and [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889], platinum-based catalysts are widely employed in commercial PDH processes due to their high C-H bond scission activity and relatively low C-C bond cleavage activity. The specific mechanism described is that Pt sites preferentially break C-H bonds over C-C bonds during propane activation steps. 

Industrial PDH catalysts also utilize chromium and other metal oxides that provide alternative pathways with lower barriers around 1.76 eV [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Additional insights from first-principles DFT calculations explain how smaller Pt clusters with more coordinatively unsaturated sites promote high catalytic activity [10.1021/acscatal.5b01423].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The reviewed analyses clearly demonstrate how various catalyst compositions facilitate propane dehydrogenation through mechanisms like metal-alkane bond formation, hydrogen transfer, and redox cycles. As shale gas resources expand propane feedstocks, further developments in PDH catalyst engineering can focus on optimizing selectivity, stability, and regenerability. This might encompass novel multidimensional zeolites, single-atom alloy configurations, and machine learning for predictive catalyst screening.

丙烷脱氢(PDH)是一种重要的工业过程,用于专用丙烯生产。催化剂通过降低激活能垒起着关键作用,使这一反应成为可能。 根据参考文献[10.1021/acs.jpclett.1c00331]、[10.1021/acscatal.2c01374]和[10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]的分析,铂基催化剂由于其高的C-H键断裂活性和相对较低的C-C键断裂活性而被广泛应用于商业PDH过程。 描述的具体机制是Pt位点在丙烷激活步骤中优先断裂C-H键而不是C-C键。

工业PDH催化剂也利用铬和其他金属氧化物,提供约1.76 eV更低能垒的替代途径[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。 来自第一性原理DFT计算的额外见解解释了具有更多无配位饱和位点的较小Pt簇如何促进高催化活性[10.1021/acscatal.5b01423]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:评论分析清楚地展示了各种催化剂组成如何通过金属-烷基键形成、氢转移和氧化还原循环等机制促进丙烷脱氢。随着页岩气资源扩大丙烷馏分,PDH催化剂工程的进一步发展可以关注优化选择性、稳定性和再生能力。这可能包括新的多维分子筛、单原子合金配置和催化剂筛选的机器学习预测。
