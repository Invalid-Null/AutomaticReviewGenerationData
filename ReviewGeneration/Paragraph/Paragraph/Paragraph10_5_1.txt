Based on the details extracted from the references, mechanistic scenarios have been proposed for propane dehydrogenation over certain metal oxide catalysts, while others do not describe specific pathways. 

For Cr-based oxides, active sites consisting of coordinatively unsaturated Cr3+ species [DOI1], Cr3+/Cr2+ redox pairs [DOI2], or mononuclear Cr(III) sites [DOI3] have been suggested. Key steps involve CH bond activation via hydrogen abstraction to form propyl intermediates, followed by β-H elimination to release propylene. 

VOx catalysts are proposed to follow Mars-van Krevelen type pathways, using lattice oxygen for alkane activation and gas phase O2 for reoxidation [DOI4, DOI5]. Isolated V5+ sites reduce to V3+ upon dehydrogenation and regain activity when reoxidized. Sub-monolayer VOx structures help avoid overoxidation [DOI6].

For ZrO2, dual Zrcus sites neighboring an oxygen vacancy activate propane through homolytic or heterolytic routes [DOI7, DOI8]. Ga-doping facilitates a concerted pathway with lower apparent barrier than ZrO2 [DOI9].  

On Ga/zeolites, monovalent Ga+ sites activate propane via oxidative addition, elimination, and C3H6 desorption steps [DOI10]. Lewis acid Ga3+ sites on oxides abstract hydride to form alkyl species [DOI11].

※※※

The findings reveal metal cations at oxygen defects or with unsaturated coordination generate active sites for propane dehydrogenation. Tuning the oxidation state and coordination environment facilitates CH activation steps. Further research could focus on controlling surface oxygen species to promote selective dehydrogenation while preventing complete combustion. Operando spectroscopic studies can also elucidate transient intermediates involved in key C-H scission and H2 recombination events.

<Chinese>
根据从参考文献中提取的详细信息,氧化物催化剂的丙烷脱氢反应的机理途径已经得到了一定的提出,而其他一些文献没有描述具体的机理路径。

对于Cr基氧化物催化剂,活性位点可能由无配位的Cr3+物种[DOI1]、Cr3+/ Cr2+氧化还原对[DOI2]或单核Cr(III)位点[DOI3]组成。关键步骤包括通过氢原子移除活化丙烷形成丙基中间体,随后β-H消除生成丙烯。

 VOx催化剂被提出遵循Mars-van Krevelen机理,使用晶格氧活化烷烃和气相O2复氧[DOI4,DOI5]。 隔离的V5+位点在脱氢过程中被还原为V3+,当被氧化时恢复活性。 亚单层VOx 结构有助于避免过氧化[DOI6]。

对于ZrO2,邻近氧空位的两组Zrcus位点可以通过均裂或异裂途径活化丙烷[DOI7,DOI8]。 Ga掺杂能够促进协同途径,表观活化栏较ZrO2更低[DOI9]。

在Ga/分子筛上,单价Ga+位点通过氧化加成、消除和C3H6解吸步骤活化丙烷[DOI10]。 氧化物上的Lewis酸Ga3+位点能够移除氢来形成烷基物种[DOI11]。


※※※

研究结果揭示了金属阳离子在氧缺陷或无配位配位环境下生成的活性位点用于丙烷脱氢。调节氧化态和配位环境有利于促进C-H活化步骤。进一步的研究可以关注控制表面氧物种以选择性地促进脱氢同时防止完全燃烧。操作谱学研究也可以阐明关键的C-H断裂和H2重组事件中的瞬态中间体。
