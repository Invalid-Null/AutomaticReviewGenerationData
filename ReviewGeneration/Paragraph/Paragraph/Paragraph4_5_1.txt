Based on the details extracted from the references, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) faces considerable thermodynamic constraints due to being a highly endothermic process. As stated in [10.1021/acscatal.0c01455], [10.1016/j.jechem.2023.01.055], [10.1021/acscatal.3c00103], and [10.1021/jacs.2c03941], the positive reaction enthalpy of 124.3 kJ/mol necessitates high operating temperatures above 500-600°C to achieve reasonable equilibrium conversion. This also makes PDH prone to side reactions like cracking, coking, and deep dehydrogenation according to [10.1021/acscatal.1c01916] and [10.1021/jechem.2023.04.019]. 

Some articles like [10.1021/jechem.2023.04.019] and [10.1021/acscatal.0c01455] note that oxidative PDH using O2 or CO2 co-feeds can provide exothermic oxidation reactions to improve the thermodynamic limitations. As explained in [10.1002/advs.202207756], this makes oxidative PDH processes less constrained by equilibrium conversion.

Regarding kinetics, a few articles estimate apparent activation energies between 159-200 kJ/mol for PDH on zeolite catalysts based on Arrhenius plots, as noted in [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]. Other kinetic factors discussed include turnover frequencies, selectivities, and reaction orders in [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939], as well as qualitative mechanistic details like the rate-limiting H2 formation step in [10.1021/acscatal.0c01455]. However, comprehensive quantitative details on PDH kinetics remain lacking.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Taking into consideration the advancements and findings discussed in the references, future research directions might focus on:

1) Overcoming thermodynamic constraints via process intensification strategies - Novel reactor designs like membrane reactors, sorption-enhanced systems, or chemical looping methods could push equilibrium conversion higher.

2) Elucidating detailed PDH kinetics and mechanisms - More quantitative kinetics encompassing surface reaction networks based on operando spectroscopy studies and microkinetic modeling will enable better catalyst design. 

3) Designing multifunctional catalysts - Tailoring sites for desired dehydrogenation activity while inhibiting coking/cracking side reactions through smart material choice and careful tuning of porosity, composition and architecture.

Overall there are opportunities to push forward PDH technology by tackling limitations rooted in thermodynamics and kinetics via creative catalyst, sorption media, and reactor enhancements guided by deeper mechanistic understanding.

一些文章比如[10.1021/jechem.2023.04.019]和[10.1021/acscatal.0c01455]指出,使用 O2 或 CO2 共馈氧化PDH可以提供放热的氧化反应来改善热力学限制。正如[10.1002/advs.202207756]中解释的,这使氧化PDH过程的平衡转化率不再受到约束。

关于动力学方面,一些文章根据Arrhenius曲线在沸石催化剂上估算了159-200 kJ/mol 左右的表观活化能,如[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]中提到的。其它动力学因素还包括[10.1021/acscatal.3c00939]中讨论的周转数、选择性和反应级数,以及诸如[10.1021/acscatal.0c01455]中提到的限速步骤H2形成等定性机理细节。然而,对PDH动力学的全面定量细节仍然缺乏。
