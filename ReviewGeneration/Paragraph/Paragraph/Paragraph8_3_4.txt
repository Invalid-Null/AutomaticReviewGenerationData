# Metal Oxide Catalysts - What metal oxides like Cr2O3, Ga2O3 have shown promising propane dehydrogenation activity?

Based on the in-depth details extracted from the file related to metal oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, the following key points can be synthesized:

Chromium oxide (Cr2O3) is one of the most promising and widely studied metal oxide catalysts for this reaction. Cr-based catalysts exhibit high activity for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane [1‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]]. The CrOx component is also used in the commercial CATOFIN process by CB&I [2‱[DOI:10.1021/acssuschemeng.9b07224]]. DFT calculations and kinetic modeling have provided insights into non-oxidative dehydrogenation mechanisms on Cr2O3 surfaces [3‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]]. Weakening the Cr-support interaction, such as using SiO2, can further enhance propylene selectivity [4‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]]. 

Gallium oxide (Ga2O3), especially the β-Ga2O3 polymorph, is another promising metal oxide catalyst. Among different Ga2O3 crystal phases, β-Ga2O3 showed the highest propane conversion and propylene yield, correlated to its surface acidity [5‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]]. In situ IR results confirmed intrinsic activity of Ga2O3/SiO2, with promoted versions outperforming PtSn and CrOx/Al2O3 references [6‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.1c02621]]. The strong interaction between Ga and supports like ZrO2 also increased Lewis acid sites, a proposed active site [7‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]].

Other metal oxides like Fe2O3 showed moderate propane dehydrogenation activity, while Pt and Sn additions were found to enhance Cr2O3 and ZnO performance [8‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]]. Overall,metal oxides remain an interesting class of propane dehydrogenation catalysts worthy of continued research.

# 金属氧化物催化剂 - Cr2O3、Ga2O3等金属氧化物在丙烷脱氢反应中表现出了哪些有前景的催化活性?

根据文件中关于丙烷脱氢反应金属氧化物催化剂的详细信息,可以归纳出以下关键点:

铬氧化物(Cr2O3)是这一反应中研究最广泛、最有前景的金属氧化物催化剂之一。Cr基催化剂在CO2辅助氧化脱氢丙烷反应中表现出高活性[1‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]]。CrOx组分也被用在CB&I的商业CATOFIN工艺中[2‱[DOI:10.1021/acssuschemeng.9b07224]]。DFT计算和动力学建模已经对Cr2O3表面的非氧化脱氢机制提供了见解[3‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]]。削弱Cr与载体的相互作用,如使用SiO2,可以进一步提高丙烯选择性[4‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]]。

钒酸盐(Ga2O3),尤其是β-Ga2O3多晶型,是另一种有前景的金属氧化物催化剂。在不同的Ga2O3晶体相中,β-Ga2O3表现出最高的丙烷转化率和丙烯收率,这与其表面酸度有关[5‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]]。原位红外光谱结果证实了Ga2O3/SiO2的固有活性,掺杂版本的性能优于PtSn和CrOx/Al2O3参考催化剂[6‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.1c02621]]。Ga与ZrO2等载体的强相互作用也增加了刘易斯酸位点,这是拟定的活性位点[7‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]]。

其他金属氧化物如Fe2O3在丙烷脱氢反应中表现出中等活性,而Pt和Sn的添加能增强Cr2O3和ZnO的性能[8‱[DOI:10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]]。总体而言,金属氧化物仍然是丙烷脱氢反应值得继续研究的有趣催化剂类别。
