Based on the details extracted from the file, several studies have evaluated supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH).

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts. For example, Pt/Al2O3 is a vital PDH catalyst component due to platinum's ability to activate C-H bonds while minimizing C-C bond cleavage [10.1002/anie.201507119]. One study showed an alumina-supported Pt catalyst maintained high initial activity, before deactivating over multiple cycles due to platinum agglomeration accelerated by sulfur [10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]. Adding metal oxide overcoats to supported Pt catalysts via atomic layer deposition (ALD) increased PDH yield and selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. Doping Pt/γ-Al2O3 with 1 wt% boron was also found to decrease coke formation 3-fold and increase propylene selectivity from 90% to 98% [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548].

Several studies have explored the impact of Pt particle size, metal-support interactions, and promoters on activity and stability. One report showed supported sub-nanometric Pt clusters stabilized inside zeolite pores via Pt-O-Ge bonds gave an ultrastable PDH catalyst [10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]. Another study demonstrated a volcano-shaped curve for Pt cluster size effects, with Pt2 clusters being the most active [10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]. Meanwhile, metal-support interactions between Pt and boron nitride (BN) were speculated to improve catalyst stability compared to Pt/Al2O3 [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008].

Besides Pt, a few studies have explored Pd, Rh, and Ir, but note they remain expensive options [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. One report showed supported RhCu single atom alloy (SAA) catalysts exhibited higher PDH activity, 100% propene selectivity, and coking resistance compared to a standard Pt/Al2O3 catalyst [10.1126/science.abg8389].

Overall, Pt-based catalysts, especially when enhanced via promoters and stabilization methods, remain the predominant supported metal catalysts for commercial PDH processes. But new compositions and configurations continue being explored to further improve catalytic performance.

※※※
Integr understanding and prospective outlook: The studies reveal platinum's unique ability to selectively activate propane while minimizing activity for undesired reactions. This establishes Pt-based catalysts as prime options for commercial PDH deployment. However, stability issues due to agglomeration and coking motivate the development of advanced synthesis routes and promoters to further enhance durability. Stabilizing ultra-small sub-nanometric clusters or single atoms may offer a pathway for exceptional catalyst longevity. Beyond platinum, expanding investigations into affordable Pd, Rh, and Ir alternatives is warranted. The possibility of cheaper non-precious metal catalysts also deserves more attention. Overall, continuing explorations at the frontier of nanoscale design, advanced characterization, and mechanistic examination will disseminate future innovations to make PDH a more viable and sustainable process.

根据文件提取的详细信息,几项研究已经评价了支撑的单金属Pt、Pd和Ir催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

Pt基催化剂被报道是最广泛使用和商业上最成功的PDH催化剂之一。例如,Pt/Al2O3是一种重要的PDH催化剂组分,因为铂能激活C-H键而最小化C-C键的断裂[10.1002/anie.201507119]。一项研究表明,氧化铝支撑的Pt催化剂保持了高初始活性,然后在多周期内失活,因为硫促进了铂的聚集[10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X]。通过原子层沉积(ALD)向支撑的Pt催化剂添加金属氧化物涂层增加了PDH产率和选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。掺杂1 wt%硼到Pt/γ-Al2O3也被发现可以使焦炭形成降低3倍,并将丙烯选择性从90%提高到98% [10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]。

几项研究探讨了Pt颗粒大小、金属支撑物间相互作用和促进剂对活性和稳定性的影响。一份报告显示,通过Pt-O-Ge键将纳米Pt簇稳定在分子筛孔内,可以获得超稳定PDH催化剂[10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。另一项研究证明,Pt簇的大小效应呈火山形关系,Pt2簇具有最高活性[10.1021/acscatal.2c05671]。同时,推测Pt与氮化硼(BN)之间的金属支撑物相互作用相比Pt/Al2O3改善了催化剂的稳定性 [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]。

除Pt外,少数几项研究探索了Pd、Rh和Ir,但指出它们仍然是昂贵的选择[10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。一份报告显示,支撑的RhCu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂展示了比标准Pt/Al2O3催化剂更高的PDH活性、100%丙烯选择性和抗焦化性[10.1126/science.abg8389]。

总的来说,尤其是在促进剂和稳定方法的增强下,Pt基催化剂仍然是商业PDH工艺中占主导地位的支撑金属催化剂。但是人们继续探索新的组成和配置以进一步改善催化性能。  

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:这些研究揭示了铂选择性激活丙烷而最小化非期望反应的独特能力。这使得Pt基催化剂成为商业PDH应用的首选。然而,由于团聚和焦化导致的稳定性问题,推动了使用先进的合成路线和促进剂来进一步增强耐久性的发展。稳定超小亚纳米簇或单原子可能提供催化剂异常长期稳定性的途径。除铂外,扩大对可负担的Pd、Rh和Ir替代品的研究是有必要的。廉价的非贵金属催化剂的可能性也值得更多的关注。总的来说,继续开拓纳米设计、先进表征和机制检查的前沿,会促进未来的创新,使PDH成为一个更可行和更可持续的工艺。
