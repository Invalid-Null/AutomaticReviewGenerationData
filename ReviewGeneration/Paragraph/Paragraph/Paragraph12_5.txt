Based on the details extracted from the file, several studies have evaluated supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, or Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

A study by Burch and Watling [10.1006/jcat.1994.1333] investigated the effects of adding sulfur during preparation or to the feed on Pt/alumina catalysts for PDH. Key findings were that predosing with H2S at 873 K greatly improved selectivity to propene, and sulfur incorporation during preparation was more effective than during reaction.

Other works evaluated Pt supported on boron nitride (Pt/BN) [10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008], SBA-15 (Pt/SBA-15) [10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005], Al2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391, 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548], and zeolites like Ge-UTL (Pt/Ge-UTL) [10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7] and Zn-ZSM-5 (Pt/Zn-ZSM-5) [10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008]. Effects of promoters like Ca [10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044], Sn [10.1021/acscatal.2c01631, 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639], and Pb [10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9] on Pt catalysts were also studied. These works provided insights into metal-support interactions, alloying effects, and strategies to enhance Pt stability and selectivity.

A few studies also looked at supported Pd [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002], Rh [10.1126/science.abg8389], Ru [10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X, 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667], and Ir [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002], but data was more limited compared to Pt catalysts.

Overall, Pt-based catalysts remain the most widely studied mono-metallic systems for PDH, and detailed mechanistic insights have been gained on supported Pt catalysts through advanced characterization techniques. However, expanding research to other noble metals and modifiers could uncover further opportunities to enhance catalytic performance.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The comprehensive analysis of supported metal catalysts for propane dehydrogenation provides a robust baseline understanding centered chiefly on Pt-based materials. Nonetheless, broader avenues may be charted to diversify the portfolio of investigated metals, supports, and characterization methods:

1) Evaluate second-row transition metals like Ru, Rh, Pd more extensively as alternatives to scarce Pt. Layered materials like MXenes could stabilize these metals in single sites. Operando spectroscopies during PDH could give mechanistic clarity.

2) Survey metal pairings on various supports by high-throughput experimentation and computations to determine unconventional, high-performance compositions. Metal-support interfacial engineering could bestow stability.

3) Implement novel architectures like single atom alloys and confined cluster catalysts to limit undesirable dehydrogenation pathways. In tandem, employ spectro-microscopic tools like AP-XPS to impart views of working catalysts. 

4) Utilize the accrued wisdom from fundamental catalytic studies to guide process intensification of PDH through microreactor prototyping, membrane separations, and heat integration. 

Purposeful endeavors at the confluence of these frontiers might synergize into efficient and sustainable PDH technologies for propylene production.

<Chinese>
根据文件提取的详细信息,已有几项研究评价了丙烷脱氢反应(PDH) 中载体化的单金属 Pt、Pd 或 Ir 催化剂。

Burch 和 Watling [10.1006/jcat.1994.1333]的研究调查了在制备期间或给料中加入硫对Pt/氧化铝催化剂的影响。关键发现是先在 873 K 下用 H2S 处理大大改善了丙烯选择性,制备期间引入硫比反应期间加入更有效。

其他工作评价了负载在氮化硼(Pt/BN)[10.1016/j.jechem.2021.10.008]、SBA-15(Pt/SBA-15)[10.1016/j.apcatb.2012.11.005]、 Al2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03391, 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]和沸石如Ge-UTL(Pt/Ge-UTL)[10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7] 和Zn-ZSM-5(Pt/Zn-ZSM-5)[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008]上的Pt。Ca[10.1016/j.cej.2014.07.044]、Sn[10.1021/acscatal.2c01631, 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]和Pb[10.1038/s41467-020-16693-9]等助剂对Pt催化剂的影响也有研究。这些工作提供了有关金属载体相互作用、合金效应和提高Pt稳定性和选择性策略的见解。

还有几项研究关注载体化的Pd [10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]、Rh [10.1126/science.abg8389]、Ru [10.1016/0021-9517(86)90312-X, 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]和Ir[10.1021/acscatal.3c01002],但与Pt催化剂相比数据更有限。

总体而言,Pt基催化剂仍是PDH中研究最广泛的单金属体系,并且通过先进的表征技术对载体化Pt催化剂获得了详细的机理见解。 然而,扩大研究范围至其他贵金属和修饰剂可以让我们有机会进一步提高催化性能。


※※※

综合理解和未来展望: 对丙烷脱氢反应中载体金属催化剂的全面分析为我们奠定了一个以Pt基材料为核心的深厚基础。不过,有望盘活更多种类的金属、载体材料、表征手段来拓宽研究路径: 

1) 大量评估第二过渡系列金属如Ru、Rh、Pd作为Pt的备选。二维层状物如MXene可能稳定这些金属位于单一位点。利用反应过程中的光谱技术可以明确反应机理。

2) 通过高通量实验和计算对多种载体上的金属组合进行大规模筛查,以确定非传统的高性能组成。金属-载体界面工程可赋予其稳定性。

3) 利用单原子合金和受限集群催化剂等新颖构型,限制不利的脱氢途径。同时,采用分光-显微技术如AP-XPS来解析工况下的催化剂。

4) 运用基础催化研究积累的智慧指导丙烷脱氢工程化过程的新工艺地开发,如微反应器、膜分离和热集成等。

在这些前沿交汇的精心努力下,丙烷脱氢技术有望向高效可持续的方向升华,满足丙烯生产的需求。
