 <English>

Based on the analysis of the file contents, the article with DOI 10.1021/acscatal.0c05454 describes using degree of rate control (DRC) analysis on DFT-calculated transition states to identify the rate-limiting steps in propane dehydrogenation. It establishes relationships between the energies of these steps and adsorbed species to identify descriptors that correlate to propane dehydrogenation activity [DOI 10.1021/acscatal.0c05454]. 

Additionally, the article with DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00889 reveals a volcano-type activity trend with respect to the propylene adsorption energy Ead(C3H6) through computational modeling of single-atom catalysts. This structure-activity relationship demonstrates tuning catalyst binding strength can optimize dehydrogenation performance [DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00889].

The article with DOI 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019 shows computationally and experimentally that separating Pt atoms and increasing interatomic distance on a Pt-In intermetallic catalyst correlates with enhanced stability by weakening propylene binding strength. An optimal Pt-Pt distance of 3.25 Angstroms gave excellent performance [DOI 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019]. 

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The analysis reveals promising pathways for designing optimized PDH catalysts through computational modeling to identify descriptors and structure-activity relationships correlating binding energies, geometric configurations, and electronic properties to performance. This knowledge can guide rational catalyst screening and development. Future horizons may integrate high-throughput DFT screening, machine learning models, and combinatorial synthesis to accelerate optimization. Operando spectroscopy and multi-scale modeling can provide insights into deactivated states.

<Chinese>

根据文件内容的分析,DOI 10.1021/acscatal.0c05454的文章描述了使用DFT计算的转化态上的速率控制度(DRC)分析来识别丙烷脱氢反应中的速率控制步骤。它建立了这些步骤的能量与吸附种的关系,以识别与丙烷脱氢活性相关的描述符[DOI 10.1021/acscatal.0c05454]。

此外,DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00889的文章通过计算模拟单原子催化剂,揭示了与丙烯吸附能Ead(C3H6)相关的火山型活性趋势。这种结构-活性关系证明了调谐催化剂的结合强度可以优化脱氢性能[DOI 10.1021/acscatal.3c00889]。

DOI 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019的文章计算和实验表明,分离Pt原子并增加Pt-In互金属催化剂上的原子间距离,与通过削弱丙烯的结合强度来提高稳定性相关。3.25埃的Pt-Pt距离实现了优异的性能[DOI 10.1016/j.jechem.2023.04.019]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望:分析揭示了通过计算建模识别描述符和结构-活性关系的有前景的途径,这些描述符和关系将结合能量、几何配置和电子属性与性能相关联,以设计优化的PDH催化剂。这些知识可以指导合理的催化剂筛选和开发。未来的发展方向可能集成高通量的DFT筛选、机器学习模型和组合合成来加速优化。同位素谱和多尺度建模可以对失活状态提供洞见。
