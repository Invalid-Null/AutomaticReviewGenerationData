	<English>
		The article introduces several major catalyst classes explored for propane dehydrogenation, including metal oxides like VOx/CrOx supported on mesoporous silica MCM-41 and SBA-15 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816], Sn-incorporated hexagonal mesoporous silica (HMS) [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816], and Co-incorporated mesoporous silica [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. It also discusses Pt- and Cr-based catalysts used industrially [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008], as well as intermetallic compounds [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]. Additionally, H-Beta zeolite [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019] and iron-silicate zeolites [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019] are introduced as catalysts that can activate propane and convert it to propylene through proposed redox cycles. Supported metals like PtSn [10.1021/acscatal.3c00939] and single-atom catalysts [10.1021/acscatal.3c00889] are also mentioned as promising options.
		※※※
		Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The major catalyst classes studied for propane dehydrogenation encompass a diverse range of materials including metal oxides, supported metals, intermetallics, and zeolites. As research continues to uncover improved catalyst formulations and mechanistic insights, there are opportunities to explore emerging preparation methods like single-atom synthesis as well as novel supports like metal-organic frameworks. Advanced characterization techniques could also elucidate catalytic active sites and deactivation pathways. Overall, further developments promise to enhance catalytic efficiency while reducing costs and environmental impacts.

		综合理解与未来展望:用于丙烷脱氢的主要研究催化剂类别包括各种材料,如金属氧化物、支持金属、金属间化合物和沸石。随着研究继续发现改进的催化剂配方和机理见解,有机会探索新兴的制备方法,如单原子合成以及金属有机框架等新型载体。先进的表征技术也可以阐明催化活性位点和失活途径。总体而言,进一步的发展有助于提高催化效率,同时降低成本和环境影响。
