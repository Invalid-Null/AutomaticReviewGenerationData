 <English>

The article investigates catalyst preparation methods aimed at optimizing active phase dispersion. Specifically, it prepared Co@Si-BEA catalysts with Co loadings from 0.1-5 wt% by vacuum impregnation and showed the 0.1-0.5 wt% samples had the highest intrinsic activity due to well dispersed isolated Co2+ sites \[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726\]. It also showed the dealuminated Co@Si-BEA catalyst produced less graphitic coke than non-dealuminated Co@BEA \[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726\].

※※※

The findings showcase techniques like controlled impregnation loading and dealumination treatment to achieve optimized dispersion of active Co species in zeolite frameworks. Isolated Co2+ sites demonstrate superior intrinsic activity compared to larger CoOx clusters. Future research could explore extending these strategies to other crucial propane dehydrogenation components like gallium, chromium, and vanadium. Advanced characterization methods should also elucidate active site isolation mechanisms. Overall, rationally tailoring support interactions, site isolation, and electron density modulation emerge as pivotal areas warranting further exploration.

<Chinese>

该文探究了旨在优化活性相分散的催化剂制备方法。具体来说,它通过真空浸渍法制备了Co@Si-BEA催化剂,Co含量从0.1-5 wt%,结果表明0.1-0.5 wt%的样品由于分散良好的隔离Co2+活性位点\[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726\]而具有最高的固有活性。它还表明脱铝Co@Si-BEA催化剂生成的焦炭比未脱铝的Co@BEA少\[10.1016/j.cej.2022.140726\]。

※※※

研究结果展示了采用控制浸渍载量和脱铝处理等技术实现活性Co物种在分子筛框架中的优化分散。 与较大的CoOx簇相比,隔离的Co2+位点表现出优异的固有活性。 未来的研究可以探索将这些策略扩展到丙烷脱氢反应的其他关键组成部分,如钒、铬和镓。 先进的表征方法也应该阐明活性位点隔离机制。 总体而言,合理定制支持相互作用、位点隔离以及电子密度调控成为值得进一步探索的关键领域。
