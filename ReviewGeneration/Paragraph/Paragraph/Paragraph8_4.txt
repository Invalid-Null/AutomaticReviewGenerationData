		Based on the details extracted from the file, a few metal oxide catalysts have demonstrated promising propane dehydrogenation activity. Chromium oxide (Cr2O3) catalysts, in particular, are noted to be among the most active options for the CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation pathway [Quotes 3, Article ID 10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]. The reduced α-Cr2O3(0001) surface has been computationally investigated for non-oxidative propane dehydrogenation using kinetic Monte Carlo simulations and microkinetic modeling [Quotes 4, Article ID 10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]. Experimentally, combining Cr2O3 with SiO2 as a support material yielded improved propylene yields, attributed to relatively weak metal-support interactions [Quotes 2, Article ID 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. 

		Gallium oxide (Ga2O3) catalysts also displayed encouraging performance. Among different polymorphs, β-Ga2O3 exhibited the highest initial activity as a result of its abundant surface acid sites [Quotes 3, Article ID 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]. Unsupported nanocrystalline β-Ga2O3 and γ-Ga2O3 likewise showed good propane dehydrogenation activity, although an amorphous Ga/SiO2 impregnated catalyst performed better [Article ID 10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]. 

		Other promising options include Pt-Sn catalysts, which have been commercialized for propane dehydrogenation. Adding Sn as a promoter helps reduce coking and improve stability of Pt active sites [Quotes 1-3, Article ID 10.1002/advs.202207756]. Stabilizing Pt on isolated Sn sites in zeolite Beta gave 50% propane conversion and 99% propylene selectivity [Quotes 2-3, Article ID 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]. Pt-Sn nanoalloys supported on SBA-15 also achieved 40% conversion and 92% selectivity [Quotes 4-6, Article ID 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639].

		根据文件中提取的详细信息,一些金属氧化物催化剂已经展示了很有前景的丙烷脱氢活性。 尤其是铬氧化物(Cr2O3)催化剂被注意到是CO2辅助氧化脱氢途径中最活跃的选择之一 [引文3,文献ID 10.1021/acscatal.2c05286]。 α-Cr2O3(0001)的还原表面已经使用动力学Monte Carlo模拟和微动力学建模计算机模拟非氧化脱氢丙烷[引文4,文献ID 10.1021/acscatal.1c01814]。 在实验上,将Cr2O3与SiO2结合作为载体材料产生了改善的丙烯收率,这归因于相对较弱的金属支撑物相互作用 [引文2,文献ID 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。

		镓氧化物(Ga2O3)催化剂也显示了很好的表现。 在不同多晶体中,β-Ga2O3表现出最高的初始活性,因为它具有丰富的表面酸性位点 [引文3,文献ID 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999]。 无支撑的纳米结晶β-Ga2O3和 γ-Ga2O3同样表现出良好的丙烷脱氢活性, 尽管淋滤法制备的无定形Ga/SiO2催化剂的表现更好 [文献ID 10.1021/acscatal.0c05009]。

		其他有前景的选择还包括Pt-Sn催化剂,它已经被商业化用于丙烷脱氢。 添加Sn作为助剂有助于减少焦炭的形成并提高Pt活性位点的稳定性 [引文1-3,文献ID 10.1002/advs.202207756]。 在沸石Beta的隔离Sn位点上稳定Pt获得了50%的丙烷转化率和99%的丙烯选择性 [引文2-3,文献ID 10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]。 支持在SBA-15上的Pt-Sn纳米合金也达到了40%的转化率和92%的选择性 [引文4-6,文献ID 10.1021/acscatal.1c00639]。
