Bimetallic and Alloy Catalysts - How does alloying Pt with Sn, Ga, Zn affect propane dehydrogenation performance?

Several studies have evaluated the effect of alloying platinum with secondary metals like tin (Sn), gallium (Ga), and zinc (Zn) for enhancing the performance of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts. 

Pt-Sn Alloys

Multiple investigations on supported Pt-Sn catalysts like PtSn/Al2O3, PtSn/MFI, PtSn/ZSM-5, PtSn/SBA-15 demonstrate that incorporating Sn helps increase propene selectivity and stability compared to monometallic Pt catalysts by modifying the electronic properties of Pt, inhibiting secondary reactions, and mitigating coke formation [Placeholder_Of_DOI1], [Placeholder_Of_DOI2], [Placeholder_Of_DOI3]. DFT and experimental findings suggest Pt-Sn intermetallic sites and alloys with moderate Sn content alter the d-band center of Pt, enabling selective C-H scission over C-C cleavage [Placeholder_Of_DOI4]. The Sn also produces a “drain-off” effect to remove coke precursors from Pt sites [Placeholder_Of_DOI5]. However, excessive Sn can overly suppress dehydrogenation activity [Placeholder_Of_DOI6]. An optimal Sn/Pt ratio around 1-1.5 is reported.

Pt-Ga Alloys  

Investigations on supported Pt-Ga nanoparticles and single atoms like Pt-Ga/Al2O3, Pt-Ga/zeolites highlight the efficacy of Ga promotion in improving dispersion, thermal stability and selectivity [Placeholder_Of_DOI7], [Placeholder_Of_DOI8]. This is attributed to the oxophilic nature and geometric effect of Ga which helps anchor and isolate Pt sites while blocking unwanted sites for secondary reactions like cracking. Pt-Ga intermetallic bonding also potentially modifies the electronic structure to promote selective dehydrogenation.   

Pt-Zn Alloys

Studies on Pt-Zn alloys and atomically dispersed Pt-Zn complexes stabilized by SiOZn-OH groups in zeolitic frameworks demonstrate Zn's role in limiting the size of Pt ensembles [Placeholder_Of_DOI9], [Placeholder_Of_DOI10]. This combined ensemble, ligand and metal-support interaction effect of Zn aids in achieving high PDH performance. The oxophilicity of Zn may additionally aid product desorption while the Lewis acidic sites contribute to dehydrogenation activity akin to Ga.

In summary, investigations overwhelmingly demonstrate that alloying Pt with Sn, Ga and Zn enables modulating the geometric and electronic properties of Pt active sites in a beneficial manner to enhance propane dehydrogenation selectivity, activity and stability. Each secondary metal contributes uniquely - Sn via electronic and coke inhibition effects, Ga by dispersion and oxophilic site isolation effects, Zn through ensemble size control. Future explorations might further fine-tune the synergism between these promotors and Pt, potentially leading to rational, multifunctional PDH catalyst designs.

双金属和合金催化剂 - 用锡、镓、锌合金化铂如何影响丙烷脱氢性能?

几项研究评估了使用锡(Sn)、镓(Ga)和锌(Zn)等第二金属与铂合金化,以提高丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂的性能。

Pt-Sn 合金

在 Al2O3、MFI、ZSM-5、SBA-15 等载体上支持的多种 Pt-Sn 催化剂如 PtSn/Al2O3、PtSn/MFI、PtSn/ZSM-5、PtSn/SBA-15 表明,引入 Sn 有助于通过修改 Pt 的电子性质、抑制次生反应和缓解焦炭生成来提高丙烯选择性和稳定性,相比单金属 Pt 催化剂[Placeholder_Of_DOI1],[Placeholder_Of_DOI2],[Placeholder_Of_DOI3]。DFT 和实验结果表明,Pt-Sn 间金属位和合金的适度 Sn 含量改变了 Pt 的 d 能带中心,促进选择性 C-H 裂解而不是 C-C 裂解[Placeholder_Of_DOI4]。Sn 还产生“引流”效应,去除 Pt 位点上的焦炭前体[Placeholder_Of_DOI5]。但是,过量的 Sn 会过度抑制脱氢活性[Placeholder_Of_DOI6]。文献报道 Sn/Pt 最佳比约为 1-1.5。

Pt-Ga 合金 

在 Al2O3、分子筛等载体上支撑的 Pt-Ga 纳米粒子和单原子如 Pt-Ga/Al2O3、Pt-Ga/分子筛的研究强调了 Ga 掺杂的效果,可改善 Pt 的分散性、热稳定性和选择性[Placeholder_Of_DOI7],[Placeholder_Of_DOI8]。这归因于镓的亲氧性质和几何效应,有助于锚定和隔离 Pt 位点,同时堵塞次生反应如裂化的不需要的位点。Pt-Ga 金属间化合物还可能通过改变电子结构来选择性促进脱氢。

Pt-Zn 合金

对沸石框架中由 SiOZn-OH 基团稳定的 Pt-Zn 合金和原子分散 Pt-Zn 络合物的研究证明,Zn 有助于限制 Pt 集合的大小[Placeholder_Of_DOI9],[Placeholder_Of_DOI10]。Zn 的这种集合效应、配体效应和金属-载体相互作用共同发挥了作用,有助于实现高的 PDH 性能。Zn 的亲氧性还可能有助于产物解吸,而 Lewis 酸性位点对脱氢活性的贡献类似于镓。

总而言之,研究压倒性地证明,Pt 与 Sn、Ga 和 Zn 的合金化可以以有益的方式调控 Pt 活性位点的几何和电子性质,以提高丙烷脱氢的选择性、活性和稳定性。每种第二金属的贡献都是独特的——Sn 通过电子和抑制焦炭生成的效应,Ga 通过分散和亲氧隔离 Pt 位点的效应,Zn 通过调控集合大小的效应。未来的探索可能会进一步优化这些助剂与 Pt 之间的协同作用,可能导致合理的多功能 PDH 催化剂设计。
