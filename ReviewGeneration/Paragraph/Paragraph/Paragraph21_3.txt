Based on the details extracted from the file, most of the articles do not investigate the effects of tuning zeolite Si/Al ratio or incorporating mesoporosity on propane dehydrogenation catalyst performance. However, a few studies provide some insights into structure-function relationships:

One article shows tuning of Si/Al ratio in dealuminated Beta zeolites creates abundant silanol nests that enhance metal dispersion and stabilization [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]. Another investigates CHA zeolites with varying Si/Al ratios from 5-25 and shows that proper tuning of Ga/Al ratio leads to stable 96% propylene selectivity, although rates decrease over time [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]. An additional study demonstrates complex dependences of propane dehydrogenation rate on both Si/Al and Ga/Al ratios, indicating interpreting data from a single Si/Al ratio can be misleading [10.1021/acscatal.1c01497]. Finally, decreasing TS-1 crystal size from 100 nm to 5.7 μm introduces mesoporosity, significantly improving catalytic performance by enhancing diffusion and avoiding coking [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The limited examples investigating zeolite tuning reveal optimizing Si/Al ratio and incorporating mesoporosity can impact metal dispersion, selectivity, lifetime and diffusion resistances. However, most studies did not systematically explore these structure-function relationships. Future research should focus on tuning zeolites across wider ranges of properties to provide more complete insights. Careful characterization identifying active sites and locations coupled with kinetic and spectroscopic studies can help reveal mechanistic dependences on structure. Expanding beyond empirical observations to fundamental understandings of these relationships may unlock new directions for rational catalyst design.

一篇文章显示,在经过除铝的Beta分子筛中调节Si/Al比可产生丰富的硅醇巢,这可增强金属的分散和稳定性 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]。另一篇文章研究了Si/Al比从5到25不同的CHA分子筛,结果显示适当调节Ga/Al比可使丙烯选择性保持在稳定的96%,尽管反应率随时间而降低 [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]。额外的一项研究证明,丙烷脱氢反应率对Si/Al比和Ga/Al比都有复杂的依赖性,这表明从单一Si/Al比的结果进行解释可能会产生误导 [10.1021/acscatal.1c01497]。最后,将TS-1晶体粒径从100纳米减小到5.7微米可引入介孔,通过增强扩散和避免焦炭显著提高催化性能 [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 少数调节分子筛性质的研究结果揭示,优化Si/Al比和引入介孔可能会影响金属分散度、选择性、使用寿命和扩散阻力。然而,大多数研究没有系统地探索这些结构-功能关系。未来的研究应该关注在更广泛的范围内调节分子筛性质,以提供更完整的认识。仔细的表征确定活性中心和位置,并配合动力学和光谱研究,可以帮助揭示结构对机理的影响。从经验观察上升到这些关系的基本理解,可能会打开制定合理催化剂设计的新方向。
