 <English>

The article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816] investigates tuning zeolite Si/Al ratio by dealumination to create silanol nests in Beta zeolites, enhancing metal dispersion and stabilization.

Additionally, the article with DOI [10.1021/jacs.2c03941] evaluates propane dehydrogenation over CHA zeolites with varying Si/Al ratios. It shows that extra-framework Ga+ sites form regardless of Si/Al ratio between 5-25. While rates decrease over time, propylene selectivity remains stable at 96% when Ga/Al ratio is properly tuned. This reveals the complex interplay between Si/Al and Ga/Al ratios.  

Moreover, the article with DOI [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024] studies the effects of tuning TS-1 crystal size from 100 nm to 5.7 μm. Smaller sizes introduce mesoporosity, enhancing diffusion and avoiding coking. This significantly improves conversion, selectivity and catalyst lifetime.

Additionally, the article with DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808] incorporates mesoporosity into MFI zeolite nanosheets to synthesize propane dehydrogenation catalysts. The mesoporous structure and silanol groups are suggested to facilitate intermetallic alloy nanoparticle formation.

※※※

The examined articles provide some initial examples of how modulating Si/Al ratio and introducing mesoporosity can impact propane dehydrogenation performance over zeolite-based catalysts. Dealumination creates open binding sites, while mesopores facilitate molecular transport. Appropriate Ga loading also plays a key role. However, most of the articles do not systematically investigate structure-function relationships. Future work should focus on narrowly targeted Si/Al ratios rather than broad ranges, coordinated with pore architecture tailoring. Advanced characterization techniques such as operando spectroscopy could shed light on binding site changes. Data-driven studies may also help identify optimal zeolite compositions and mesostructures.

<Chinese>

DOI [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816]的文章通过Beta分子筛的脱铝来调节Si/Al比,形成硅醇巢,提高了金属分散和稳定性。

此外,DOI [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]的文章评价了具有不同Si/Al比(5-25)的CHA分子筛上的丙烷脱氢反应。结果显示,无论Si/Al比如何,额外的Ga+位点都会形成。随着时间的推移,反应速率会下降,但当Ga/Al比正确调节时,丙烯选择性可保持在96%。这揭示了Si/Al比和Ga/Al比之间的复杂相互作用。

而且,DOI [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024] 的文章研究了调节TS-1晶体大小从100纳米到5.7微米的效果。较小的晶体尺寸引入了介孔,增强了扩散,避免了结焦,显著提高了转化率,选择性和催化剂的使用寿命。

此外,DOI [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]的文章在MFI纳米片中掺入了介孔,合成丙烷脱氢催化剂。介孔结构和硅醇基团有助于促进金属间化合物纳米粒子的形成。

※※※

检验过的文章提供了一些初�例子,说明了如何调控Si/Al比和引入介孔以影响基于分子筛的丙烷脱氢催化剂的性能。脱铝会产生新的结合位点,而介孔有助于分子运输。Ga负载量的适当调节也起关键作用。但是,大多数文章没有系统地研究结构与性能的关系。未来的工作应该更有针对性地关注特定的Si/Al比范围,而不是广泛的范围,并配合孔结构的调控。先进的表征技术(如原位光谱)可以揭示结合位点的变化。数据驱动的研究也可以帮助识别最佳的分子筛组成和介孔结构。
