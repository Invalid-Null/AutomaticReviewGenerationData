 Here is a draft review section on propane dehydrogenation catalysts with a focus on leveraging high-throughput computational methods to accelerate catalyst screening and design:

<English>
A recent study utilized a descriptor-based microkinetic analysis approach combined with DFT calculations to rapidly screen potential intermetallic compound catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]. By considering both terrace and step sites, over 2000 catalyst candidates were assessed. This high-throughput computational screening identified inexpensive and eco-friendly Fe-Ga intermetallics as promising PDH catalysts. Additional works have started leveraging machine learning to accelerate screening and design of metallic heterogeneous catalysts. With databases of computed descriptors continuing to grow, data-driven strategies can efficiently navigate vast materials spaces [10.1021/acscatal.0c05711]. Further expansion of this framework to screen trimetallic alloys may uncover even more active and selective PDH catalysts.  
※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The discussed advancements in high-throughput computational catalyst screening are poised to transform the PDH field. Rapid assessments of thousands of candidates using descriptors and machine learning models can guide experimental validation toward the most promising catalyst formulations. Moving forward, integration of multi-fidelity models, uncertainty quantification, and autonomous experimentation into the computational workflow could further enhance the rate and reliability of discoveries. Broader adoption of these techniques beyond metals to screen novel supports, additives, and processing methods may unravel generalizable design principles for superior PDH catalysts.
</English>

<Chinese>
最近的一项研究利用基于描述符的微动力学分析方法结合 DFT 计算,对丙烷脱氢(PDH)的潜在互金属化合物催化剂进行了快速筛选[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.008]。通过考虑台阶和阶梯位点,评估了2000多种催化剂候选材料。这种高通量计算筛选确定了廉价和环保的 Fe-Ga 互金属化合物是有前途的 PDH 催化剂。另一些工作开始利用机器学习来加速筛选和设计金属异质催化剂。随着计算描述符数据库的不断增长,数据驱动的策略可以高效地浏览大规模材料空间 [10.1021/acscatal.0c05711]。进一步扩展该框架以筛选三金属合金,可能会发现更加活跃和选择性的 PDH 催化剂。
※※※  
综合理解与未来展望:所讨论的高通量计算催化剂筛选的进步必将改变 PDH 领域。利用描述符和机器学习模型对数千种候选材料进行快速评估,可以指导实验验证最有前途的催化剂配方。展望未来,将多保真度模型、不确定性量化和自主实验平台集成到计算工作流程中,可以进一步提高发现的速度和可靠性。更广泛地采用这些技术,除金属外,还可以筛选新型支撑体、添加剂和处理方法,这可能会揭示用于设计优良 PDH 催化剂的普适原则。
</Chinese>
