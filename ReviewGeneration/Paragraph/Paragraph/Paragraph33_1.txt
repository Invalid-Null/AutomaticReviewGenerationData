Specifically, it uses a descriptor-based microkinetic analysis combined with DFT calculations to screen over 2000 intermetallic compound catalysts and identify inexpensive and environmentally friendly Fe-Ga candidates as promising options. 

The other references do not discuss utilizing high-throughput computations for catalyst screening and design. Most rely on experimental synthesis and characterization rather than computational techniques. ※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The analysis reveals that high-throughput computations like descriptor-based screenings and DFT calculations hold promise to accelerate identification of optimal catalysts, but experimental validation is still essential. Overall there is ample room for innovation in leveraging simulations to guide experiments.

其他参考文献没有讨论利用高通量计算进行催化剂筛选和设计。大多数依赖于实验合成和表征,而不是计算技术。 一些使用一些计算建模但没有具体的高通量方法论 [10.1016/S1872-2067(23)64402-X, 10.1021/acscatal.0c05711]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望:分析表明,基于描述符的筛选和DFT计算等高通量计算有望加速识别最佳催化剂,但实验验证仍然必不可少。随着计算能力和机器学习的进步,高通量虚拟筛选巨大的催化剂库可能会获得更多关注。未来的工作可以关注改进这些方法,并将它们与快速原型技术相结合,以简化经验证。总的来说,利用模拟来指导实验还有很大的创新空间。
