Based on the details extracted from the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most widely used and commercially successful PDH catalysts. For example, Pt/Al2O3 is a vital PDH catalyst component due to Pt's ability to activate paraffinic C-H bonds while minimizing C-C cleavage [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]. One study showed an ALD Al2O3 or TiO2 overcoat on Pt/Al2O3 extrudates increased PDH yield and selectivity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]. Sulfur addition also improved a Pt/alumina catalyst by increasing selectivity to 98% propylene [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333]. On other supports, isolated Pt atoms in Zn-exchanged zeolite ZSM-5 demonstrated high intrinsic activity [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631], while encapsulating Pt clusters in zeolite UTL pores via Pt-O-Ge bonds gave an ultrastable PDH catalyst [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7].

Some comparisons have been made between Pt and other metals. A supported RhCu single atom alloy catalyst showed higher activity, 100% propylene selectivity and coking resistance compared to a Pt/Al2O3 catalyst [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]. Under identical conditions, a NiMo/Al2O3 catalyst exhibited twice the selectivity of Pt/Al2O3 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c05711]. On the other hand, monometallic Ru nanoparticles had very low 4.5% propylene selectivity, far below Pt nanoparticles at 46% [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667].

Several studies also explored Pd- and Ir-based catalysts. Pd, Rh and Ir are noted to have high intrinsic activity, but are expensive compared to Pt [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. No specific PDH data or insights are provided for these metals.

In summary, Pt catalysts are the most extensively studied and utilized mono-metallic PDH catalysts, with demonstrated commercially viability. But opportunities exist to further improve Pt stability and selectivity, as well as investigate alternative metals like RhCu alloys that show promising capability.

根据从文件中提取的详细信息,已经有几项研究探索了载体支持的单金属 Pt、Pd 和 Ir 催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

报道称,Pt基催化剂是最广泛使用和商业上最成功的 PDH 催化剂之一。例如,Pt/Al2O3 是一种重要的 PDH 催化剂,因为 Pt 能激活烷烃 C-H 键而最小化 C-C 键的断裂 [DOI: 10.1002/anie.201507119]。一项研究表明,ALD Al2O3或 TiO2 超薄层涂覆的 Pt/Al2O3 挤出催化剂增加了 PDH 产率和选择性 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c03391]。添加硫也通过将选择性提高到 98% 丙烯改进了 Pt/氧化铝催化剂 [DOI: 10.1006/jcat.1994.1333]。在其他载体上,锌交换沸石 ZSM-5 中的隔离 Pt 原子表现出高的固有活性[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.2c01631],而通过 Pt-O-Ge 键将 Pt 簇封装在分子筛 UTL 的孔道中获得了超高稳定性的 PDH 催化剂 [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7]。

已经进行了 Pt 和其他金属之间的一些比较。与 Pt/Al2O3 催化剂相比,支持的 RhCu 单原子合金催化剂显示出更高的活性、100% 丙烯选择性和抗焦炭性 [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。在相同条件下,NiMo/Al2O3 催化剂的选择性是 Pt/Al2O3 的两倍 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c05711]。另一方面,单金属 Ru 纳米粒子的丙烯选择性非常低,只有 4.5%,远低于 Pt 纳米粒子的 46% [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.0c01667]。


还有几项研究探索了 Pd 和 Ir 基催化剂。Pd、Rh 和 Ir 具有高的固有活性,但与 Pt 相比昂贵 [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。没有提供这些金属的具体 PDH 数据或见解。


总的来说,Pt 催化剂是最广泛研究和利用的单金属 PDH 催化剂,并证明了其商业可行性。但仍有机会进一步改善 Pt 的稳定性和选择性,以及研究像 RhCu 合金等替代金属,这些金属显示了很有前景的能力。
