Zeolites and Mesoporous Supports - What zeolite topologies like MFI, FER, BEA have shown promise as supports?

Based on the detailed analysis from the provided references, several zeolite topologies have shown promise as supports for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) catalysts:

MFI Zeolite 
The purely siliceous MFI zeolite silicalite-1 provides an ideal support to anchor highly dispersed subnanometric PtSn clusters in the sinusoidal 10R channels, leading to outstanding PDH performance with 45.3% propane conversion and 99% propylene selectivity [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.02.019]. Its hierarchical micro-/mesoporous analog allows PtLa intermetallic nanoparticles to exhibit high initial activity (~40% conversion) and exceptional 30-day stability with &lt;10% deactivation [10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4]. Ferrosilicalite-1 (Fe-S-1-n) stabilizes isolated Pt atoms bonded to Si-O-Fe sites, boosting selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.2c05042]. Mesoporous MFI nanosheets strongly confine PtZn alloys, conferring remarkable durability [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808].

BEA Zeolite  
Sn-modified zeolite Beta (Sn-Beta) efficiently anchors isolated Pt atoms within Zn nests inside dealuminated BEA pores. The resulting single sites catalyze PDH with 90% propylene selectivity near equilibrium conversion [10.1021/jacs.1c10261]. Pt stabilized on isolated Sn sites in the Beta framework also demonstrates excellent catalytic performance [10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]. The Beta topology contributes a confinement effect to enhance PtSn particle dispersion [10.1021/acscatal.3c00548].

Other Zeolites
Chabazite zeolite (CHA) isolates extra-framework Ga+ active sites to achieve 96% propylene selectivity, albeit with some deactivation over time [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]. Titanosilicate TS-1 possesses weaker acid sites, improving propane conversion and catalyst stability [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024]. 

In summary, MFI and BEA zeolites have shown particular promise as PDH catalyst supports owing to properties like strong metal confinement, acid site isolation, and hierarchical porosity. However, opportunities exist to explore emerging zeolite and mesoporous architectures to further advance catalytic performance.     

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements reviewed in anchoring isolated metal sites and stabilizing subnanometric clusters on zeolites, future research might integrate cutting-edge support materials like phosphorus-modified zeolites and hierarchical metallorganic frameworks. Combined with novel in-situ characterization techniques, these next-generation supports could offer new insights into active site speciation and reaction mechanisms. Machine learning methods additionally show potential for accelerating the optimization of zeolite composition, porosity, acidity, and metal pairing. Advancing support design through these innovative directions could enable breakthroughs in commercial propane dehydrogenation.

分子筛和介孔材料支持 - MFI、FER、BEA等分子筛拓扑结构展现出哪些前景?

根据提供的参考文献的详细分析,几种分子筛拓扑结构展现出作为丙烷脱氢(PDH)催化剂载体的前景:

MFI分子筛
纯二氧化硅MFI分子筛硅沸石-1提供了理想的载体,可以锚定高度分散的亚纳米PtSn簇合物在10R正弦波通道中,导致卓越的PDH性能,丙烷转化率达到45.3%,丙烯选择性达到99% [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.02.019]。它的分层微-介孔类似物允许PtLa金属间化合物表现出高初始活性(约40%的转化率)和30天期间异常稳定,活性减少不到10% [10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4]。铁硅沸石-1 (Fe-S-1-n) 稳定了与 Si-O-Fe 位点相连的隔离Pt原子,提高了选择性 [10.1021/acscatal.2c05042]。介孔MFI纳米片强烈限制PtZn合金,赋予卓越的稳定性 [10.1021/acscatal.1c01808]。


BEA分子筛
在脱铝BEA分子筛孔内用Zn巢穴锚定隔离Pt原子的Sn改性沸石Beta (Sn-Beta) 有效地催化PDH反应,丙烯选择性接近平衡转化率的90% [10.1021/jacs.1c10261]。也表现出卓越的催化性能[10.1021/acscatal.9b03527]。 Beta拓扑结构为PtSn颗粒分散提供了限制作用 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00548]。


其他分子筛
方沸石(CHA)隔离了外框架Ga+活性位点,实现了96%的丙烯选择性,尽管随着时间的推移活性有所下降 [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]。硅钛酸TS-1具有较弱的酸位点,改善了丙烷转化率和催化剂稳定性 [10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.024]。


总的来说,MFI和BEA沸石由于其强大的金属限制作用、酸位点隔离以及分层孔隙等特性,已经展现出作为PDH催化剂载体的特殊前景。但是,探索新兴的分子筛和介孔体系以进一步提高催化性能仍然存在机会。


※※※
综合理解与未来展望:考虑到锚定隔离金属位和稳定亚纳米簇在分子筛上的进展,未来的研究可能会整合磷修饰分子筛和分层金属有机框架等前沿载体材料。结合新颖的原位表征技术,这些新一代载体材料能够提供关于活性位点规范和反应机理的新见解。此外,机器学习方法也表现出加速优化分子筛成分、孔隙度、酸性和金属配对的潜力。通过这些创新方向推进载体设计,能够实现商业丙烷脱氢反应的突破。
