 <English>
Based on the details provided in the file, several studies have explored supported mono-metallic Pt, Pd, and Ir catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH). 

Pt-based catalysts are reported to be among the most successful and widely used catalysts for commercial PDH processes [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]. For example, an alumina-supported Pt catalyst showed relatively poor performance for PDH with 15% propane conversion and &lt;80% propylene selectivity [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg7894]. Modifying the support or adding promoters has been investigated to enhance Pt catalyst stability and selectivity. A study found that incorporating 1 wt% boron into a Pt/γ-Al2O3 catalyst decreased coking by 3 times and improved propylene selectivity from 90% to 98% after 12 hours on stream [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.9b05548]. Encapsulating Pt clusters within the micropores of a Ge-UTL zeolite via Pt-O-Ge linkages also gave a highly stable and selective catalyst [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023-00968-7].

Besides Pt, a few studies have explored Pd- and Ir-based catalysts. One study mentions Pd, Rh, and Ir can activate propane C-H bonds, but does not provide any direct data [DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]. An innovative Rh-Cu single atom alloy (SAA) catalyst supported on SiO2 exhibited higher activity, 100% propylene selectivity, and coking resistance compared to a traditional Pt/Al2O3 catalyst [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389].

In summary, Pt-based catalysts, especially on modified supports, appear to be the most explored and optimized mono-metallic catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. Other metals like Pd, Rh, and Ir show potential but lack extensive evaluations. Further studies are needed to fully assess their viability and optimize their performance.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings reveal Pt-based catalysts currently dominate commercial PDH processes, with decades of research helping unlock their potential. However, opportunities exist to discover new active phase compositions and configurations that may outperform traditional Pt catalysts. Advances in characterization and computational screening can accelerate identification of novel single atoms, nanoclusters, and intermetallic compounds selective for PDH. The supports and promoters used with metal sites also deserve attention, as they govern dispersion and modulate metal-support interfacial sites influencing activity and selectivity. By combining established catalyst design principles with cutting-edge synthetic and analytical techniques, next-generation PDH catalysts may be uncovered.

<Chinese>
根据文件中提供的详细信息,几项研究已经探索了受支持的单金属Pt、Pd和Ir催化剂用于丙烷脱氢(PDH)。

报道称,Pt基催化剂是商业PDH过程中最成功和最广泛使用的催化剂之一 [DOI: 10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63360-7]。例如,一种氧化铝支持的Pt催化剂用于PDH的性能相对较差,丙烷转化率仅为15%,丙烯选择性低于80% [DOI: 10.1126/science.abg7894]。改性载体或添加促进剂已被研究以增强Pt催化剂的稳定性和选择性。一项研究发现,在Pt/γ-Al2O3催化剂中掺入1 wt%的硼会使结焦量降低3倍,并在流经12小时后将丙烯选择性从90%提高到98% [DOI: 10.1021/ acscatal.9b05548]。通过Pt-O-Ge连接将Pt簇封装在Ge-UTL分子筛的微孔内也获得了高度稳定和选择性的催化剂 [DOI: 10.1038/s41929-023- 00968-7]。

除Pt外,少数几项研究还探索了Pd和Ir基催化剂。一项研究提到Pd、Rh和Ir可以激活丙烷C-H键,但没有提供任何直接数据[DOI: 10.1021/acscatal.3c01002]。一种创新型的Rh-Cu单原子合金(SAA)催化剂(载体为SiO2)表现出比传统Pt/Al2O3催化剂更高的活性、100%的丙烯选择性和抗结焦性[DOI: 10.1126/science.abg8389]。

总的来说,Pt基催化剂,特别是改性载体的Pt基催化剂,似乎是用于丙烷脱氢最被广泛研究和优化的单金属催化剂。Pd、Rh和Ir等其他金属具有潜力,但缺乏广泛的评估。需要进一步的研究来全面评估其可行性并优化其性能。

※※※  
综合理解与未来展望:调查结果揭示,Pt基催化剂目前主导商业PDH过程,数十年的研究帮助释放了它们的潜力。然而,存在发现新活性相成分和配置的机会,这些成分和配置可能会优于传统的Pt催化剂。先进的表征和计算筛选可以加速识别用于PDH的新型单原子、纳米簇和互金属化合物。与金属位点一起使用的载体和促进剂也值得关注,因为它们控制了分散和调节金属-载体界面位点,从而影响活性和选择性。通过将建立的催化剂设计原理与尖端合成和分析技术相结合,下一代PDH催化剂可能会被发现。
