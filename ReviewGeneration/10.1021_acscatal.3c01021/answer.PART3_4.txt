6. The article does use X-ray based techniques to probe oxidation state changes during propane dehydrogenation catalysis. Specifically, it uses X-ray absorption spectroscopy (XAS) to study the oxidation states of gallium in Ga/HZSM-5 and Ga/γ-Al2O3 catalysts after high temperature H2 reduction. It provides criteria to identify Ga(I) sites based on XANES white line intensity and position. The data suggests Ga/HZSM-5 contains mostly Ga(I) sites after reduction, while Ga/γ-Al2O3 contains mostly Ga(III).
