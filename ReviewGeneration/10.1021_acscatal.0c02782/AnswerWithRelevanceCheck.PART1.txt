<English>
Answer: Yes, the article explores metal-support interfacial effects in the Mn-Ce oxide catalyst for propane dehydrogenation. It shows that depositing amorphous, layered MnOx on ceria leads to strong oxide-support interactions that modulate surface oxygen species. This tunes the reaction pathways from combustion to selective oxidative dehydrogenation by hindering propene overoxidation on oxygen-deficient Mn sites. In contrast, Mn oxides without ceria support like MnO2 show high combustion selectivity.
</English>
