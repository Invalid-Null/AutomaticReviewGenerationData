4. Answer: Yes, the article explores metal-support interfacial effects in manganese oxide catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. It shows that strong oxide-support interactions (SOSIs) between manganese oxide and ceria form active interfacial oxygen sites that activate propane and oxygen-deficient MnOx species that stabilize propene and prevent overoxidation. This boosts activity and selectivity, outperforming general MnOx and even promising VOx catalysts. The work provides insights into tuning performance via interfacial effects.
