4. Answer: The article explores metal-support interfacial effects in the MnOx/CeO2-SOSI catalyst for propane dehydrogenation. It shows that depositing amorphous, layered MnOx on ceria leads to strong oxide-support interactions that modulate surface oxygen species. This tunes the reaction pathways from combustion to selective oxidative dehydrogenation by hindering propene overoxidation on oxygen-deficient Mn sites. In contrast, Mn oxides without ceria support like MnO2 show high combustion selectivity.
</English version answer>
