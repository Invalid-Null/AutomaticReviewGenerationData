Answer: The article proposes mechanistic scenarios for propane dehydrogenation over monomeric, polymeric, and crystalline vanadium oxide catalysts. It evaluates monomer, dimer, and crystalline V2O5 slab models. The monomeric model shows low barriers (<0.15 eV) for initial CH bond activation on AlOV and VO sites. The dimeric model has higher barriers (~1.5 eV) for initial dehydrogenation. On crystalline V2O5, CH3CO*CH3 formation is easier than C3H6* formation, especially on VO sites.
