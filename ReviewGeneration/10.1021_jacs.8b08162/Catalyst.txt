The key scientific contribution of this work is the Pt3Mn bimetallic nanoparticle catalyst, which shows greatly improved propane dehydrogenation selectivity compared to Pt nanoparticle and Pt3Mn-s core-shell nanoparticle catalysts. The Mn in the subsurface of the Pt3Mn nanoparticle weakens the binding strength of reactants, leading to suppressed byproduct formation.
