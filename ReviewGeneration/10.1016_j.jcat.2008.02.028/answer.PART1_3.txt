It describes a reaction sequence where propane first reduces isolated V5+ sites to V4+ and then V3+, which are then reoxidized back to V5+ by gaseous oxygen. The nature and distribution of these V5+ sites as isolated vanadates or polymeric vanadates determines activity and selectivity.
