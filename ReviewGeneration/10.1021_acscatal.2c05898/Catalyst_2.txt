The main scientific contribution of this paper is on the Zn/H-ZSM-5 catalyst:

<Catalyst>

</Catalyst>

The paper investigates in detail the speciation of Zn in H-ZSM-5 and correlates the Zn sites to catalytic activity for propane dehydrogenation. Different Zn sites such as isolated Zn2+, [Zn-O-Zn]2+, [Zn-OH]+ and ZnOx clusters are identified and their properties and reactivity compared.
